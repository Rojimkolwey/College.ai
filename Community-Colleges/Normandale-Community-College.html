Normandale Community College

Bloomington, MN
9700 France Ave S, Bloomington, MN 55431-4399
952-358-8200

Founded in 1968, Normandale Community College is a comprehensive, two-year college located in Bloomington, Minnesota. The college offers more than 60 degree and certificate programs in liberal arts and sciences, business, health, and technology. Normandale also provides a variety of services to students, including academic advising, tutoring, career services, and disability services.

In addition to its credit programs, Normandale offers a variety of non-credit courses and programs for personal enrichment, professional development, and workforce training. The college also provides community education courses and programs, which are open to everyone in the community, regardless of age or educational background.

Normandale is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college is also accredited by the Minnesota State Colleges and Universities System.

Normandale Community College is committed to providing an accessible, affordable, and high-quality education to all members of the community. The college strives to create a supportive and inclusive environment that encourages all students to reach their full potential.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Normandale Community College, students must have a high school diploma or equivalent. They must also submit an application, which can be found on the college's website, and pay the application fee. Once the college has received the completed application and fee, the student's transcripts will be evaluated and they will be notified of their admission status.
