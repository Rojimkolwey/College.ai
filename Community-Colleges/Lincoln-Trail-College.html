Lincoln Trail College is a two-year community college in Robinson, Illinois. The college is part of the Illinois Community College System and offers associate degrees and certificate programs. The college also offers adult education and continuing education courses.

Lincoln Trail College was founded in 1965 as Lincoln Trail Junior College. The college was originally located in the city of Lawrenceville, Illinois. The college moved to its current location in Robinson, Illinois in 1968. The college was renamed Lincoln Trail College in 1971.

Lincoln Trail College offers associate degrees in arts, sciences, and applied sciences. The college also offers certificate programs in various fields. The college's adult education program offers courses for adults who want to earn a high school diploma or GED. The college's continuing education program offers courses for people who want to improve their job skills or learn new ones.

Lincoln Trail College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college's nursing program is accredited by the Accreditation Commission for Education in Nursing. The college's cosmetology program is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts and Sciences.

Lincoln Trail College has an enrollment of approximately 2,500 students. The college's student-to-faculty ratio is 20-to-1. The college's website is www.ltc.edu.
