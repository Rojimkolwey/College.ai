Ventura College

Ventura, CA
4667 Telegraph Rd, Ventura, CA 93003-3872

Ventura College is a public community college in Ventura, California. Founded in 1925, the college offers Associate's degrees and certificate programs in over 60 areas of study. The college is accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges.

Ventura College serves the cities of Ventura, Oxnard, and Port Hueneme, as well as the unincorporated areas of Ventura County. The college has an enrollment of over 10,000 students.

Ventura College offers a wide variety of academic programs. The college has programs in the arts, business, humanities, sciences, and social sciences. The college also offers vocational and technical programs.

Ventura College has a strong commitment to student success. The college offers a variety of services to help students succeed in their studies. These services include academic advising, tutoring, and career counseling.

Ventura College is a great place to start your college education. The college offers an affordable education and a wide variety of programs to choose from. The college also has a commitment to student success.

Admission Requirements

Ventura College is a public community college in Ventura, California. Established in 1925, the college offers Associate's degrees and certificate programs in a variety of subject areas.

To be eligible for admission to Ventura College, applicants must meet the following requirements:

1. Be a high school graduate or have a GED certificate.

2. Submit official transcripts from all previous colleges and universities attended.

3. Complete an online application.

4. Pay the non-refundable application fee.

5. Take the Ventura College Placement Test, if required.

6. Attend a mandatory new student orientation session.

7. Register for classes.

8. Pay tuition and fees.

9. Obtain a student ID card.

10. Complete the FAFSA, if applicable.
