Western Maricopa Education Center (WMEC) is a public high school located in Maricopa, Arizona. The school is part of the Maricopa Unified School District. WMEC was established in 1998 and serves grades 9-12. The school's mission is to provide a quality education that prepares all students for success in a global society.

WMEC offers a rigorous academic curriculum that includes Advanced Placement (AP) courses. The school also has a strong focus on career and technical education. Students can choose to focus on a particular area of interest, such as business, health care, or engineering. WMEC has a strong commitment to providing a well-rounded education, and offers a variety of extracurricular activities and clubs.

The school has a diverse student body, with students coming from a variety of backgrounds. WMEC is dedicated to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for all students. The school has a strong focus on college and career readiness, and offers a variety of resources to help students prepare for life after graduation.
