The University of Pittsburgh-Titusville (UPT) is a small, public university located in Titusville, Pennsylvania. It is part of the University of Pittsburgh system. UPT offers bachelor's and associate's degrees in a variety of majors. The most popular majors at UPT are Nursing, Business, and Education. The university has an active student life, with a variety of clubs and organizations. Greek life is also present on campus. UPT is known for its strong academics, and its students often go on to graduate or professional school. The university is located in a rural area, and its campus is spread out over several city blocks. The university is a commuter school, and most students live off campus.
