AACC is an open-admission college, meaning that any student who meets the basic requirements for admission will be accepted into the school. However, some programs may have additional requirements that must be met in order to be considered for admission.

The general requirements for admission to AACC are as follows:

• Must be a high school graduate or have earned a GED

• Must submit official transcripts from all previous colleges or universities attended

• Must take and pass the ACCUPLACER placement test, unless exempted

• Must complete a health screening and submit required immunization records

• Must submit a completed application for admission
