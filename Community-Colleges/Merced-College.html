Merced College

Merced, CA
3600 M St, Merced, CA 95348-2898
209-384-6000

Merced College is a community college located in Merced, California. It is part of the Yosemite Community College District and offers associate's degrees and certificate programs. The college opened in 1962 and serves the city of Merced and the surrounding areas. The college has an enrollment of over 10,000 students.

Merced College offers a variety of academic programs and services to its students. The college has a strong focus on career and technical education, offering programs in areas such as nursing, automotive technology, and welding. The college also offers a variety of general education courses, as well as a robust student support system that includes tutoring, counseling, and financial aid.

The college is committed to providing its students with a quality education that is affordable and accessible. Merced College is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Community and Junior Colleges of the Western Association of Schools and Colleges. The college has been recognized for its excellence in teaching, its innovative programs, and its commitment to student success.

Admission Requirements

Merced College is a two-year community college located in Merced, California. Admission to the college is open to all students who have a high school diploma or equivalent. There are no minimum GPA or test score requirements for admission.

Merced College offers a variety of programs and courses to meet the needs of its students. The college has over 60 associate degree and certificate programs, as well as a variety of transfer programs. Merced College also offers a number of non-credit and continuing education courses.

The college has an open-door policy, which means that any student who meets the minimum requirements for admission will be accepted. Merced College is committed to providing access to higher education for all students, regardless of their background or circumstances.
