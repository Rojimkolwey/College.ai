Amarillo College is a two-year community college located in Amarillo, Texas, United States. The college offers Associate in Arts, Associate in Science, and Associate in Applied Science degrees, as well as certificates in a variety of career and technical fields. Amarillo College is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. The college first opened its doors in 1929 as Amarillo Junior College, and was the first institution of higher education in the Texas Panhandle. The college has an enrollment of over 8,000 students, and is the largest provider of higher education and workforce training in the Texas Panhandle.
