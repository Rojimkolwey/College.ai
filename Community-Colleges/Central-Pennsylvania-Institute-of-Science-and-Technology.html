Central Pennsylvania Institute of Science and Technology (CPI) is a public, two-year technical college located in Summerdale, Pennsylvania. Founded in 1965, CPI is one of the oldest and largest technical colleges in the state. The school offers over 50 programs of study in a variety of technical and career fields, including engineering, business, health care, and information technology.

CPI is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education and is a member of the American Association of Community Colleges. The school has an enrollment of over 4,000 students, most of whom are Pennsylvania residents.

CPI offers a number of programs and services to help students succeed in their studies and careers. The school has an extensive career services department that provides job placement assistance, resume writing services, and career counseling. CPI also offers a number of academic support services, including tutoring, academic advising, and mentoring.

The school’s campus is located just minutes from Harrisburg, the state capital, and is easily accessible by car, bus, and train. CPI’s campus includes a variety of modern facilities, including classrooms, laboratories, and a library.

CPI is an affordable option for students seeking a quality education in a technical or career field. The school’s tuition and fees are among the lowest in the state, and CPI offers a number of financial aid options to help students cover the cost of their education.
