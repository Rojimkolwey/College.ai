Blue Mountain Community College (BMCC) is a public community college in Pendleton, Oregon. BMCC is one of the few community colleges in Oregon that offers on-campus housing. BMCC offers Associate of Arts (AA) and Associate of Science (AS) degrees, as well as professional certificates. The college has an enrollment of over 3,000 students.

BMCC was founded in 1963 as a two-year college. The college was originally located in the city of La Grande, Oregon. In 1965, the college moved to its current location in Pendleton. The college has undergone several name changes over the years, from Blue Mountain Community College to Eastern Oregon Community College to its current name.

BMCC is accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities. The college offers a variety of programs and courses, including business, education, health sciences, humanities, and social sciences. BMCC also offers online courses and distance learning opportunities.

BMCC is committed to providing affordable, quality education to its students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including grants, loans, and scholarships.
