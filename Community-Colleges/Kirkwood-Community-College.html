Kirkwood Community College

Cedar Rapids, IA
6301 Kirkwood Blvd SW, Cedar Rapids, IA 52406-2068
319-398-5411

Kirkwood Community College is a public two-year college in Iowa, United States. It is a member of the Iowa Association of Community College Presidents and the American Association of Community Colleges. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. Kirkwood has its main campus in Cedar Rapids and regional centers in Iowa City, Marion, and Belle Plaine. The college also offers online and distance learning courses.

Kirkwood was founded in 1966 and has since grown to become one of the largest community colleges in Iowa. The college offers over 100 programs of study, including Associate degrees, certificates, and continuing education courses. Kirkwood also has a strong commitment to workforce development, and offers many programs and services to help students find jobs and further their careers.

Kirkwood is a great choice for students looking for an affordable, high-quality education. The college has an excellent reputation and offers a wide variety of programs to choose from. Whether you're looking to transfer to a four-year university or enter the workforce, Kirkwood can help you reach your goals.

Admission Requirements

Kirkwood Community College is a public two-year institution of higher education located in the Cedar Rapids, Iowa area. The college offers a variety of programs and courses, and awards both associate degrees and certificates.

To be eligible for admission to Kirkwood Community College, applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent. GED scores are also accepted. Applicants must also take a placement test, which is used to determine course placement. The college has an open admissions policy, meaning that all applicants who meet the minimum requirements are accepted.

Once admitted, students must complete a new student orientation program before beginning their coursework. Kirkwood Community College also offers a variety of support services to help students succeed, including tutoring, academic advising, and career counseling.
