Heartland Community College

Normal, IL
1500 West Raab Road, Normal, IL 61761-9446
309-268-8000

Heartland Community College is a two-year community college in Normal, Illinois that serves over 12,000 students in its district. The college offers over 100 programs of study and awards associate degrees and certificates in a variety of fields.

Heartland Community College was founded in 1966 and is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college is a member of the National Junior College Athletic Association and offers a variety of varsity and club sports teams.

Heartland Community College is committed to providing accessible and affordable education to its students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans, to help students pay for their education.

Heartland Community College offers a variety of services to its students, including academic advising, career counseling, tutoring, and disability services. The college also has a robust student life program, with over 60 clubs and organizations for students to get involved in.

Heartland Community College is located in the twin cities of Bloomington-Normal, Illinois, which is home to over 85,000 people. The college is situated on a beautiful 200-acre campus and is within easy driving distance of Chicago, St. Louis, and Indianapolis.

Admission Requirements

Heartland Community College has an open admissions policy, meaning that any student who has a high school diploma or equivalent is welcome to enroll. There are no minimum GPA or standardized test score requirements. However, students are encouraged to complete the placement test before enrolling in classes to ensure they are placed in the appropriate level. Heartland also offers a variety of dual credit and early college programs for high school students.
