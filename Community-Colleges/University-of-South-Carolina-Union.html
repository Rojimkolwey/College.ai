The University of South Carolina-Union is a public, four-year liberal arts university located in Union, South Carolina, United States. It is one of four campuses of the University of South Carolina system. USC Union offers bachelor's degrees in 19 areas of study. The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award baccalaureate degrees. The school's colors are garnet and black and its mascot is the jaguar.

USC Union was established in 1873 as Union Academy. The school became a junior college in 1928 and a four-year institution in 1965.
