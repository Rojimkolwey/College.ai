Richland Community College is a public community college in Decatur, Illinois. The college offers associate's degrees and certificate programs in a variety of fields, as well as continuing education and adult education courses. Richland Community College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

Richland Community College was founded in 1972 and is a member of the Illinois Community College System. The college's main campus is located on 106 acres (43 ha) of land in Decatur, Illinois. The college also has a satellite campus in Springfield, Illinois.

Richland Community College offers over 50 associate's degree and certificate programs in a variety of fields, including business, education, healthcare, and liberal arts. The college also offers a number of continuing education and adult education courses.

Richland Community College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college's nursing program is accredited by the National League for Nursing Accreditation Commission.

Richland Community College has an enrollment of over 4,000 students. The college employs over 200 faculty and staff members.
