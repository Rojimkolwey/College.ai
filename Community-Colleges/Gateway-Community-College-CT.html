Gateway Community College is a public community college in New Haven, Connecticut. It is part of the Connecticut State Colleges and Universities system. The college was founded in 1992 and offers associate degrees and certificate programs in a variety of disciplines.

Gateway Community College offers a variety of programs and courses to its students. The college has over 50 academic programs, including programs in business, health care, and liberal arts. The college also offers a number of continuing education and adult education programs.

Gateway Community College is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges. The college is also a member of the American Association of Community Colleges.

Gateway Community College has an enrollment of over 5,000 students. The student body is diverse, with students from a variety of backgrounds and cultures. The college has a strong commitment to serving the needs of its students and the community.
