Anoka Technical College

Anoka, MN
1355 Highway 10 West, Anoka, MN 55303
763-433-1100

Anoka Technical College (ATC) is a public two-year technical college located in Anoka, Minnesota. The college is a member of the Minnesota State Colleges and Universities system. It was established in 1965 and is the oldest two-year technical college in the Twin Cities metropolitan area.

ATC offers more than 50 degree and certificate programs in a variety of fields, including business, health care, information technology, manufacturing, and transportation. The college also offers a number of non-credit courses and programs.

ATC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college is also accredited by the Minnesota Board of Nursing and the National League for Nursing Accreditation Commission.

ATC has an enrollment of more than 4,000 students, most of whom are Minnesota residents. The college employs more than 200 faculty and staff members.

ATC is located on a 72-acre campus in Anoka, Minnesota, a northern suburb of the Twin Cities. The campus includes a variety of buildings and facilities, including a library, a fitness center, and a child care center.

Admission Requirements

Anoka Technical College has an open-admission policy, meaning that any student who meets the minimum requirements for admission is welcome to enroll. The minimum requirements for admission are as follows:

1. Must be a high school graduate or have completed a GED/HiSET.

2. Must submit official high school and/or college transcripts.

3. Must complete an application for admission.

4. Must complete a placement test, unless exempt.

5. Must pay the required application fee.

6. Must meet any additional requirements for specific programs.

Anoka Technical College does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, creed, religion, national origin, sex, disability, age, veteran status, sexual orientation, or gender identity. The college is committed to providing equal access to all programs, services, and activities.
