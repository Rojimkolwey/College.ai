Stark State College

North Canton, OH
6200 Frank Ave NW, North Canton, OH 44720
330-494-6170

Stark State College is a public community college in North Canton, Ohio. Stark State College offers associate degrees and certificate programs in a variety of fields, including business, engineering, health care, and liberal arts. The college also offers online and distance learning opportunities.

Founded in 1960, Stark State College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college enrolls more than 20,000 students and employs more than 1,000 faculty and staff members.

Stark State College is committed to providing affordable, high-quality education and training opportunities for the people of Stark County and the surrounding region. The college offers a variety of financial aid options to help students pay for their education.

Stark State College is an active member of the community, partnering with local businesses and organizations to provide internships, job shadowing, and other opportunities for students. The college also offers a variety of cultural and recreational activities for students, faculty, and staff.

Admission Requirements

Stark State College is an accredited, public institution of higher learning located in North Canton, Ohio, United States. The college offers more than 200 associate degree and certificate programs in business, engineering, health, and liberal arts.

In order to be eligible for admission to Stark State College, applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent. Applicants must also submit all required application materials, including transcripts, test scores, and a non-refundable application fee.

Stark State College uses a holistic approach to admissions, and considers factors such as academic performance, leadership experience, and community involvement when making admissions decisions. The college also offers a number of programs and services to support students throughout their college experience, including academic advising, tutoring, and career services.
