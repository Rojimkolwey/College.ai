Rock Valley College is a public community college in Rockford, Illinois. It was founded in 1964 and is a member of the Illinois Community College System. The college serves students in the northern Illinois region and offers more than 100 programs of study in business, health, industrial technologies, and liberal arts. The college has an enrollment of more than 10,000 students and is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

Admission Requirements

Rock Valley College is a public community college in Rockford, Illinois. It is the largest community college in Illinois and the second-largest in the Midwest. The college offers more than 100 programs of study and awards associate degrees and certificates in various fields.

Admission to Rock Valley College is open to all students who have a high school diploma or equivalent. The college does not have any specific admission requirements, but all students are encouraged to submit an application and take placement tests.

Rock Valley College has a strong commitment to student success and offers a variety of services to help students succeed in college. The college has an extensive tutoring program, a writing center, and a math center. The college also offers career counseling and job placement services.
