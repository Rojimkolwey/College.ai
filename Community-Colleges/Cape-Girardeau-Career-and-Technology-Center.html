Cape Girardeau Career and Technology Center

Cape Girardeau, MO
1080 S. Silver Springs Rd., Cape Girardeau, MO 63703-7511
573-576-1149

The Cape Girardeau Career and Technology Center is a public, two-year career and technical education institution serving high school students and adults in southeast Missouri. The Center offers over 50 different programs in a variety of career fields, including agriculture, automotive technology, business and office technology, cosmetology, culinary arts, health care, information technology, manufacturing, and welding.

The Cape Girardeau Career and Technology Center is accredited by the Council on Occupational Education and is a member of the Missouri Association of Career and Technical Education. The Center is located on a 36-acre campus in Cape Girardeau, Missouri, and provides career and technical education programs and services to over 1,600 high school students and adults each year.

Admission Requirements

Cape Girardeau Career and Technology Center is a public, two-year career and technical education institution located in Cape Girardeau, Missouri. The center offers over 50 programs in a variety of career and technical fields. Admission to the center is open to all high school students in the Cape Girardeau area, as well as adults seeking to improve their career skills.

High school students wishing to attend the center must complete an application and submit transcripts from their previous school. Adult students must submit a high school transcript or GED certificate, as well as transcripts from any previous college coursework. All students must also take a placement test to determine their level of proficiency in Math and English.

Once admitted, students will work with an advisor to develop a plan of study based on their career goals. Programs at the center range from one semester to two years in length, and most programs offer both classroom and hands-on training. Upon completion of their program, students will earn a certificate or degree that will help them pursue their chosen career.
