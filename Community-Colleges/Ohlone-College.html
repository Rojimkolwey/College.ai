Ohlone College

Fremont, CA
43600 Mission Blvd, Fremont, CA 94539-0390
510-659-6000

Ohlone College is a public community college in Fremont, California. It is part of the Ohlone Community College District and is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Community and Junior Colleges. The college offers Associate's degrees and certificate programs in a variety of disciplines, including business, engineering, fine arts, and liberal arts.

Ohlone College was founded in 1965 as a part of the California Community College system. The college was originally located in the city of Newark, but moved to its current location in Fremont in 1974. The college serves the cities of Fremont, Newark, and Union City, as well as the unincorporated community of Sunol.

Ohlone College offers a variety of academic programs leading to an Associate's degree or certificate. The college also has a strong commitment to teaching English as a Second Language (ESL) and offers a variety of ESL courses.

Ohlone College is home to a number of student clubs and organizations, including the Ohlone College Anime Club, the Ohlone College Art Club, and the Ohlone College Chess Club. The college also has a number of athletic teams, including men's and women's basketball, cross country, and soccer teams.

Ohlone College is located on a beautiful campus in Fremont, California. The campus is situated on a hilltop with sweeping views of the Bay Area. The college is just a short drive from San Francisco, Oakland, and Silicon Valley.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Ohlone College is open to all students who meet the following minimum requirements:

1. Be at least 18 years of age, or have a high school diploma or equivalent, or be a currently enrolled high school student in good standing

2. Complete an online application

3. Submit official transcripts from all previous colleges and universities attended

4. Pay the non-refundable $46 application fee

5. Complete the required assessment tests

6. Attend an information session

7. Meet with a counselor

8. Register for classes

Ohlone College welcomes applications from all students who meet the minimum requirements for admission. The admissions process is designed to be simple and straightforward, and our admissions counselors are here to help you every step of the way.

Once you have submitted your application, our admissions team will review your transcripts and test scores to determine your eligibility for admission. If you meet the minimum requirements, you will be invited to attend an information session, where you will learn more about the college and the programs we offer.

After attending an information session, you will meet with a counselor to discuss your educational goals and develop a plan of study. Once you have registered for classes, you will be on your way to earning your Ohlone College degree!
