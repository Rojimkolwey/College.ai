Gaston College is a two-year community college located in Gastonia, North Carolina. The college offers a wide variety of programs and courses, including associate degrees, diploma and certificate programs, continuing education, and workforce development.

Gaston College was established in 1964 as a part of the Gaston County public school system. The college originally operated out of a single building in downtown Gastonia. In its early years, the college offered programs in business, health occupations, and secretarial science. In the 1970s, the college began to expand its program offerings, adding programs in engineering, computer science, and other technical fields.

Today, Gaston College enrolls over 6,000 students each year. The college offers over 60 degree and certificate programs. Gaston College is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges.

Gaston College is committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality education to its students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans. The college also offers a variety of services to support students, including tutoring, career counseling, and disability services.

Gaston College is an active member of the community, partnering with local businesses and organizations to provide workforce training and development. The college also offers a variety of community education programs, including adult basic education, English as a second language, and GED preparation.
