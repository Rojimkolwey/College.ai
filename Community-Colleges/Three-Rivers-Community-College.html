Three Rivers Community College

Norwich, CT
574 New London Turnpike, Norwich, CT 06360
860-215-9000

Three Rivers Community College (TRCC) is a public, two-year college located in Norwich, Connecticut. The college is part of the Connecticut State Colleges and Universities system. TRCC was founded in 1992 and offers over 60 associate degree and certificate programs. The college also has a campus in Willimantic, Connecticut.

TRCC serves the eastern region of Connecticut and enrolls over 4,000 students annually. The college offers a variety of academic programs, including business, liberal arts, health sciences, and STEM (science, technology, engineering, and math). TRCC also offers a number of non-credit courses and programs, including adult education, continuing education, and workforce development.

TRCC is accredited by the New England Commission of Higher Education (NECHE). The college is also a member of the American Association of Community Colleges (AACC) and the Association of Connecticut Community Colleges (ACCC).

TRCC has a strong commitment to student success and offers a variety of services and resources to support students. The college has an extensive tutoring program, and all students have access to academic advising and career counseling. TRCC also offers a number of scholarships and financial aid programs to help students cover the cost of tuition and other expenses.

TRCC is an affordable option for students seeking a high-quality education. The college offers a variety of programs and services to support student success, and the faculty and staff are dedicated to helping students reach their academic and career goals.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Three Rivers Community College, applicants must submit a completed application form, high school transcripts, and SAT or ACT scores. Once the admissions office has received all required materials, they will review the file and make a decision on whether or not to offer the student admission to the college.
