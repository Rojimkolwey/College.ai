Oconee Fall Line Technical College

Sandersville, GA
1189 Deepstep Rd, Sandersville, GA 31082
478-553-2050

Oconee Fall Line Technical College (OFTC) is a public, two-year technical college serving a ten-county area in east central Georgia. The College offers over 50 programs of study leading to technical certificates, diplomas, and associate degrees. Many of the programs are designed to transfer credits to four-year colleges and universities.

OFTC is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award associate degrees, diplomas, and technical certificates of credit. The College is also accredited by the Council on Occupational Education.

OFTC opened its doors in September 1987 as a state-of-the-art technical college serving the needs of a ten-county area in east central Georgia. The College was created by the Technical College System of Georgia (TCSG) to provide quality technical education and training that would meet the needs of business and industry and to help create a better-educated workforce.

Since its inception, OFTC has experienced tremendous growth. The College now serves over 4,000 students annually and has a faculty and staff of over 200. OFTC has become a leader in providing quality technical education and training.

The College offers a variety of programs leading to technical certificates, diplomas, and associate degrees. Many of the programs are designed to transfer credits to four-year colleges and universities. The College also offers a number of non-credit, continuing education courses and programs.

OFTC is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award associate degrees, diplomas, and technical certificates of credit. The College is also accredited by the Council on Occupational Education.

OFTC is committed to providing quality technical education and training that meets the needs of business and industry and helps create a better-educated workforce. The College offers a variety of programs that prepare students for successful careers in a variety of fields.

Admission Requirements

Oconee Fall Line Technical College (OFTC) is a public, two-year technical college serving a ten-county area in east central Georgia. The College offers career and technical education leading to the achievement of a diploma, certificate, or associate degree.

In order to be eligible for admission to OFTC, students must:

1. Be a high school graduate or have a GED certificate.

2. Complete an application for admission.

3. Submit official high school and/or college transcripts.

4. Take the College Placement Test (CPT) and achieve minimum scores.

5. Complete any additional requirements for specific programs.

6. Pay the required application fee.

Once students have met all of the admission requirements, they will be notified of their acceptance to the College.
