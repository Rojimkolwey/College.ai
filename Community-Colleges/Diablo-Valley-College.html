Diablo Valley College (DVC) is a public community college in Pleasant Hill, California. The San Ramon campus houses the college's administrative offices and the Contra Costa County Sheriff's Academy, while the Walnut Creek campus is home to the college's culinary arts program.

DVC is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Community and Junior Colleges of the Western Association of Schools and Colleges. The college's athletic teams are members of the Bay Valley Conference of the California Community College Athletic Association.
