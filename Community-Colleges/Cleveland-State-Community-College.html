Admission Requirements

Cleveland State Community College (CSCC) is a public community college in Cleveland, Tennessee. CSCC is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award associate degrees. The college offers over 50 programs of study leading to an associate degree, diploma, or certificate.

Admission to CSCC is open to any student who has a high school diploma or equivalent. Students must complete an application for admission and submit all required transcripts and test scores. CSCC does not have an application fee.

Once admitted, students must complete a new student orientation and meet with a counselor to develop a degree plan. First-time, full-time students are also required to participate in the College Success Seminar, a one-credit course that helps students transition to college life.

Students at CSCC can choose to live in on-campus housing. The college offers two residence halls, both of which feature suite-style living. On-campus residents have access to a variety of amenities, including a fitness center, laundry facilities, and a computer lab.
