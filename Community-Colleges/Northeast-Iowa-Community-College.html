Northeast Iowa Community College (NICC) is a two-year, public community college with campuses in Calmar, Peosta and Manchester, Iowa. The college offers over 50 academic programs in a variety of fields, as well as a number of non-credit courses and programs.

NICC offers a variety of financial aid options for students, including grants, loans and scholarships. The college also participates in a number of federal and state financial aid programs.

NICC is committed to providing a high-quality education to its students.
