Admission Requirements

Laney College is a two-year community college located in Oakland, California. The college is part of the Peralta Community College District and offers Associate's degrees and certificate programs.

Admission to Laney College is open to all students who meet the following requirements:

1. Complete an online application or submit a paper application.

2. Pay the non-refundable application fee.

3. Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities previously attended.

4. Complete placement testing, if required.

5. Attend an orientation session.

6. Register for classes.
