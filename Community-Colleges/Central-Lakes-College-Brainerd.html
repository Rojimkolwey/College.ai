Brainerd, MN
501 West College Drive, Brainerd, MN 56401-3900

Central Lakes College is a two-year community and technical college in central Minnesota. It is one of the largest community colleges in the state, serving over 11,000 students in credit and non-credit programs each year. The college has campuses in Brainerd and Staples, as well as a number of satellite locations throughout the region. Central Lakes College offers a wide variety of programs and courses, from liberal arts and sciences to business and industry-specific training. The college also provides a number of services to its students, including career counseling, tutoring, and financial aid. Central Lakes College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the Minnesota State Colleges and Universities system.
