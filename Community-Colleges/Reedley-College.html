Reedley College

Reedley College is a public community college in Reedley, California. It is part of the State Center Community College District and offers Associate's degrees and certificates in a variety of programs. The college was founded in 1926 and is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Community and Junior Colleges. It is the only college in the district that offers on-campus housing.

Reedley College has an enrollment of over 10,000 students and offers over 100 academic programs. The college is known for its agriculture program, which is one of the largest in the state. The college also offers a variety of other programs, including business, nursing, and liberal arts.

Reedley College is located in the San Joaquin Valley, which is one of the most agriculturally productive regions in the world. The college has a strong commitment to serving the local community and offers many programs and services that are designed to meet the needs of the region. The college is an active participant in the economic development of the region and works to create opportunities for its students and graduates.
