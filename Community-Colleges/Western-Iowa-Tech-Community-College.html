Western Iowa Tech Community College (WITCC) is a two-year public college in Sioux City, Iowa. It is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college offers over 50 programs in business, health, industrial, and liberal arts and sciences.

WITCC was founded in 1967 as Western Iowa Technical Institute. It became a community college in 1974. The college has grown significantly since then, adding new programs and campus facilities.

Today, WITCC serves over 5,000 students each year. The college is known for its affordable tuition, small class sizes, and personal attention from faculty and staff. WITCC graduates are prepared for successful careers in a variety of fields.

The college is located on a 120-acre campus in Sioux City, Iowa. The campus includes several buildings for classrooms, laboratories, offices, and student services. WITCC also has a library, bookstore, and fitness center.

WITCC is committed to providing an excellent education at an affordable price. The college offers a variety of financial aid options to help students pay for college. WITCC also offers a number of scholarships to help students cover the cost of tuition.
