Shawnee Community College

Ullin, IL
8364 Shawnee College Rd, Ullin, IL 62992
618-634-3200

Shawnee Community College is a public community college in Ullin, Illinois. It is part of the Illinois Community College System. The college was established in 1967 and serves Alexander, Massac, Pulaski, and Union counties. The college has an enrollment of over 3,000 students.

Shawnee Community College offers over 50 associate degree and certificate programs in a variety of disciplines, including business, healthcare, liberal arts, and trades and industry. The college also offers a number of online and distance learning opportunities.

Shawnee Community College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college is also accredited by the National League for Nursing and the National Accrediting Agency for Clinical Laboratory Sciences.

Admission Requirements

Shawnee Community College has an open admissions policy, meaning that any student who has a high school diploma or equivalent is eligible to enroll. However, there are a few steps that must be completed before students can register for classes.

First, students must complete an application for admission. This can be done online or in person at the college's Admissions Office. Once the application has been submitted, students will be contacted by an admissions counselor to schedule an appointment for an interview.

During the interview, students will be asked about their educational goals and their previous academic experience. The admissions counselor will also review the student's transcripts to ensure that they meet the minimum GPA requirements for admission.

Once the interview is complete, students will be given a placement test to determine their course level placement. Based on the results of the placement test, students will be advised on which classes they need to take in order to complete their degree.

Once all of the required steps have been completed, students will be able to register for classes and begin their studies at Shawnee Community College.
