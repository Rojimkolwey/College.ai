Wayland Baptist University Scholarships


Challenger And Acteen Scholarship - Wayland Baptist Univ.
The Challenger and Acteen Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Wayland Baptist University. You must have been involved in the Acteen or Challenger program to qualify for this award. more...

Christian Leadership/Academic Scholarship - Wayland Baptist Univ.
The Christian Leadership/Academic Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Wayland Baptist University. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and be involved in Christian leadership activities to qualify for this renewable award. In addition, you must be an active member of a Southern Baptist church. more...

Honors Program Scholarship - Wayland Baptist University
The Honors Program Scholarship is available to students admitted into the honors program at Wayland Baptist University. more...

Local Church Grant - Wayland Baptist University
The Local Church Grant is available to incoming freshmen at Wayland Baptist University. You must enroll full time and be a member of a local church that contributes to WBU to be eligible for this award. more...

Minister And Spouse Of Other Denominations Scholarship - Wayland
The Minister and Spouse of Other Denominations Scholarship is available to full-time students at Wayland Baptist University. You must be a full-time senior pastoral minister (or spouse) and belong to a normative Christian denomination other than Southern Baptist to qualify for this award. more...

Pioneer Scholarship - Wayland Baptist University
The Pioneer Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen, transfer, re-entering, valedictorian or salutatorian students at Wayland Baptist University. You must have a minimum 25 ACT or 1100 SAT score to qualify for this award. Transfer students must also have a minimum 3.25 GPA on all previous college work. Entering freshmen who are the salutatorian or valedictorian of their high school class are also eligible. The scholarship is renewable if you maintain a minimum 2.75 GPA. Award amounts vary according to ACT/SAT scores. more...

Residence Hall Grant - Wayland Baptist University
The Residence Hall Grant is available to incoming freshmen at Wayland Baptist University. You must be a resident of Hawaii, Alaska, Arizona, Kansas, Nebraska, New Mexico, Colorado, Oklahoma or San Antonio or Wichita Falls, Texas to qualify for this renewable award. You must also be a full-time student and a resident of a Wayland dormitory. more...

Southern Baptist Minister's Dependent Scholarship - Wayland
The Southern Baptist Minister's Dependent Scholarship is available to full-time students at Wayland Baptist University. You must be the dependent spouse or child of a full-time Southern Baptist minister, missionary, denominational minister or staff minister serving a Southern Baptist congregation to qualify. You must provide a letter from an official of the minister's church verifying active service to be eligible for this renewable award. more...
