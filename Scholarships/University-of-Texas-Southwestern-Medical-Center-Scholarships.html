University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center Scholarships


Art Department Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students attending Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas, interested in the field of Art. Portfolio review is required. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Beneficiary Scholarships
Scholarship for dependents of United Methodist clergy who are undergraduate students attending Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas. Awards are given regardless of financial need. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable. more...

Bishop Ernest T. Dixon, Jr. Scholarships
Scholarship for high achieving African-American, Hispanic and Native-American undergraduate students attending Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas. Awards are given regardless of financial need and are renewable up to eight semesters. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Brown Scholar Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted at Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas. Applicants must have at least a 3.8 GPA (on 4.0 scale) and rank in the top 5% of their class. Candidates should score at least a 1400 on the SAT or 32 on the ACT. Selection also based on quality and rigor of the high school academic record, performance on SAT or ACT, evidence of leadership, quality of written essay, personal interview with a member of admission staff, content of recommendation letters, and involvement in extracurricular activities. Finalists will be invited to campus to interview and compete for the award in March. Award is highly competitive. Award amount equal to full tuition, room and board. more...

General Academic Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Southwestern University. A very competitive freshman award. To be eligible for this highly competitive award, applicant must have a minimum composite ACT of 28 or a minimum combined SAT of 1200 and a high school class ranking in the top 5%. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Religious Education Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas, majoring in Religious Studies. Awards are given regardless of financial need. Submit letter from a church leader supporting students interest with application packet. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable. more...

Self-Defined Scholarships
Scholarship for first-year students accepted to attend Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas. Applicants must meet the required coursework for admittance. Students may take the highest combination of their SAT scores, combined with their high school rank and GPA to determine scholarship award. Ruter awards will be given to students with an award of $2000-$5000/year; Mood Scholar given to students with award of $51001-$10,000/year; and Cody awards given to students with an award of $10,001-$15,000/year. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable. more...

Theatre Department Scholarships
Annual award for undergraduate students attending Southwestern University in Georgetown, Texas, participating in Theatre. Audition is required. Audition dates are in February; contact Southwesterns Sarofim School of Fine Arts for exact dates. Due dates, number of awards and award amount varies. more...
