Maryland Institute College of Art Scholarships


C.V. Starr Scholarship For International Students
Scholarship for international students who are entering freshmen accepted at Maryland Institute, College of Art. Applicants must be a citizen of a foreign country, but may be studying either in the United States or abroad. This merit-based award recognizes outstanding artistic ability and talent. Renewable. more...

Fanny B. Thalheimer Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Maryland Institute, College of Art. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and SAT score of 1000 or ACT score of 21. Selection is based on outstanding studio abilities and academic achievement. Submit official transcripts, letters of recommendation, ACT/SAT scores, and a slide portfolio of 12-20 pieces of original artwork with application packet. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

International Scholarship Of Excellence
Annual award for entering freshman or transfer student accepted at Maryland Institute, College of Art (MICA). Applicant must be a citizen of a foreign country and be living outside the United States. This merit-based award recognizes outstanding artistic achievement and a commitment to pursuing a career in the visual arts. more...

Irving & Lois Blum Foundation Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate student attending Maryland Institute, College of Art, from the Baltimore School for the Arts. more...

Marie Walsh Sharpe Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Maryland Institute, College of Art. Applicants must have participated in the MWSF Summer Program. Renewable. more...

Marion I. & Henry J. Knott Scholarship
Annual award for Maryland resident who is a Catholic, graduating high school senior from a school within the Archdiocese of Baltimore that has been accepted at Maryland Institute, College of Art. Selection is based on students outstanding academic merit. more...

Maryland General Awards Program
Scholarship for undergraduate students planning to attend, or currently attending, Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). Applicants must be matching the Maryland Distinguished Scholar Awards for Finalists in the Visual Arts. Recipients will receive the award from MICA to match the Distinguished Scholar Award. more...

Mathias J. Devito Scholarships
Annuals award for a graduating high school senior admitted to Maryland Institute, College of Art. Applicant must have demonstrated talent and promise, based on a portfolio of artwork. Selection includes personal character; an inquisitive, open and discerning mind; and an interest in the social and cultural context for making art. Submit high school transcripts, letters of recommendation, and a slide portfolio of 12-20 pieces of original work with application packet. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Mica Graduate Fellowships
Scholarship for graduate students attending Maryland Institute, College of Art. Selection is based solely on merit, exceptional excellence in portfolio as well as academic accomplishments. Recipients are selected by Graduate Committee. more...

Mica Graduate Scholarships
Scholarship for graduate students planning to attend Maryland Institute, College of Art. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need. Students talent is recognized through portfolio review. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Mica International Graduate Fellowship
Annual award for international graduate student planning to attend Maryland Institute, College of Art, as an Intern Graduate Fellowship. Selection based on excellence in portfolio. Recipients selected by the Graduate Committee. more...

Scholastic Art Awards Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors admitted to the Maryland Institute, College of Art who have received national ranking in the Scholastic Art Portfolio Competition. Applicants must have financial need. Submit FAFSA, academic transcripts, ACT/SAT scores, recommendation letters, and a slide portfolio of 12-20 pieces with application packet. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...
