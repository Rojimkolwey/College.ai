Hood College Scholarships


Alumnae Referral Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen and undergraduate transfer students enrolling at Hood College. Applicants must be have been referred to admissions by an alumni. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Boa Leadership Scholarship
The BOA Leadership Scholarship is available to incoming freshman students at Hood College. This scholarship is awarded to high-achieving students who have taken active leadership roles in their high school, community, and/or region. more...

Chair Of The Board Scholarship - Hood College
The Chair of the Board Scholarship is available to incoming freshman students at Hood College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be eligible for this award. Additionally, you must have demonstrated the talent, ability, academic achievement, and motivation to compete for national and international recognition by the end of your undergraduate career. more...

Founders' Scholarship - Hood College
The Founders' Scholarship is open to incoming freshman students at Hood College. You must demonstrate strong records of academic performance and extracurricular involvement, as well as show potential to contribute to classroom activities and the college community. more...

Frederick County National Honor Society Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors of a Frederick County High School who are National Honor Society members enrolling at Hood College. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Hodson Trust Academic Scholarship

Hodson-Gilliam Scholarship
