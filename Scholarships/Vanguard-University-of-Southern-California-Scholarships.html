Vanguard University of Southern California Scholarships


Assemblies Of God Fine Arts Scholarship
Annual award for full-time undergraduates planning to attend, or currently attending, Vanguard University of Southern California. Applicants must have a superior rating in the A/G National Fine Arts Festival. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Campus Service Grant
Annual award for full-time undergraduates planning to attend, or currently attending, Vanguard University of Southern California. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need. Recipients may be asked to volunteer time to various campus activities to establish renewal of this award. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Graduate Of Vu Grant
Annual award for undergraduate students that are graduating at Vanguard University in Costa Mesa, California. Applicant must plan to enroll in additional undergraduate courses. Award amount is up to 25% of undergraduate tuition. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Lion Leadership Scholarship
Annual award for full-time undergraduate transfer students accepted to Vanguard University of Southern California. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need and a minimum 3.5 GPA. Student must have at least 24 college transfer units completed by the end of the fall semester. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Ministry Dependent Grant
Annual award for full-time undergraduate dependent students whose parents are involved in full-time Christian service as ordained and licensed ministers or missionaries. Applicant must have financial need. Award is limited to students whose primary family income is derived from parents ministry involvement. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Ministry Grant
Annual award for full-time undergraduate students planning to attend, or currently attending, Vanguard University of Southern California. Applicants must have financial need and be involved in full-time Christian service as ordained and licensed ministers or missionaries. Students primary family income must be derived from ministry to qualify for this scholarship. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Ministry Grant - Vanguard University Of Southern California
The Ministry Grant is available to full-time undergraduate students at Vanguard University of Southern California. You must be involved in full-time Christian service as an ordained and licensed minister or missionary with your primary family income derived from ministry involvement to qualify. You must also demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Vanguard Need Grant
Annual award for student attending, or planning to attend, Vanguard University in Costa Mesa, California. Applicant must have demonstrated financial need. Due date, number of awards, and award amount varies. more...
