Seattle Pacific University Scholarships


Ames Minority Leadership Scholarships
Scholarship for minority high school graduates who are incoming freshmen enrolling at Seattle Pacific University. Eligible applicants will automatically be considered for awards through the admissions process, no additional applications are necessary. Applicant must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident, filed a FAFSA, and have plans to return to local community after graduation. Consideration also given to leadership potential. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Athletic Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Seattle Pacific University who participate in the schools athletic program. Applicant must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident. Student must show outstanding ability in chosen sport. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. more...

Faculty-Staff Scholarships
Scholarship for continuing undergraduate students attending Seattle Pacific University. Applicants must be U.S. citizens or permanent resident. Selection based on academic achievement and contribution to SPU. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. more...

Fine & Performing Arts Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Seattle Pacific University who are interested in Music, Theater, or Art. Applicant must be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident. Selection is based on students audition or portfolio. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Free Methodist Scholar Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Seattle Pacific University freshmen who are affiliated with the Free Methodist Church (Pacific Northwest, Columbia River, Oregon, California or Pacific Coast Japaneses conferences). Eligible applicants will automatically be considered for awards through the admissions process, no additional applications are necessary. Selection based on academic achievment and involvement in church, school, and community. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
