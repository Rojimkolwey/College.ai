Carroll College Scholarships


Campus Visit Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Carroll College in Montana. This award offers $500 award for students who visit campus for a visit program and a scheduled individual visit and enroll. The award also includes a $125 voucher for the campus bookstore for four years. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Carroll College All Saints Schlarship
Scholarship for outstanding undergraduate students attending Carroll College in Montana full-time. All applicants are considered based on recalculated (non-weighted and academic courses only) GPA and test scores. Renewable as long as recipient remains in good academic standing. more...

Carroll College All Saints Transfer Student Schlarships
Scholarship for outstanding transfer students enrolling at Carroll College in Montana. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA. Candidates must be working towards their first bachelors degree. Submit complete admission file by the priority admission deadline. Number of awards varies. more...

Carroll College Bishop Carroll Scholarship
Scholarship for outstanding undergraduate students attending Carroll College in Montana full-time. All applicants are considered based on recalculated (non-weighted and academic courses only) GPA and test scores. Renewable as long as recipient remains in good academic standing. more...

Carroll College Presidential Scholarship For Transfer Students

Elsie Corette Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for Montana residents who are undergraduate sophomores or juniors attending Carroll College in Montana, majoring in Pre-Med. Applicants must write a 250 words or less essay stating why student needs this award. Candidate must also write an essay, in 500 words or less, on a current event topic (not in their field of study). Bonus $1,000 will be awarded to student with best-written essay. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Fr. John Sladich Memorial Scholarship For The Native American
Scholarship for Native Americans who are undergraduate or graduate students attending Carroll College in Montana. Preference will be given to Montana Native American Tribes. Applicant must apply for admission (must complete application form, including essay; application fee; official copy of school transcripts; secondary school report; ACT or SAT scores; completed FAFSA and SAR) and be accepted to Carroll College by March 1. Number of awards vary. Renewable. more...

Honors Scholars Program Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshman planning to attend Carroll College in Montana, enrolling in the Honors Scholars Program (HSP). Applicant for the HSP Scholarship must complete a HSP Application; a resume which includes: past/present employment, accomplishments, community and social service, awards won, and extra-curricular activities; a two page essay, high school and/or college transcripts, ACT or SAT results. Interview may be required. Selection is based on academic scholarship, personal character, and civic and social commitment. Number of awards varies. more...

Missoula Electric Cooperative Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Missoula, Montana who are members, or their children and dependents, of the Missoula Electric Cooperative that are undergraduate students attending at Carroll College in Montana. Members must have an active account; include account number when submitting application packet. more...

Parish Scholarship
Scholarship for outstanding Catholic undergraduate students attending Carroll College in Montana. Applicants must be active in their church community. Number of awards varies. Nonrenewable. more...

Presidential Transfer Scholarship - Carroll College
The Presidential Transfer Scholarship is available to transfer students at Carroll College. You must have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Thomas Francis Meagher Hibernian Scholarship
Scholarship for Helena, Montana, Catholic residents of Irish descent who are outstanding undergraduate sophomores, juniors or seniors attending Carroll College in Montana full-time. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and financial need. Two Hibernian awards are for the Spring semester. more...
