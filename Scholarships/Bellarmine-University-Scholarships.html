Bellarmine University Scholarships


Art/Music Scholarship - Bellarmine University
The Art/Music Scholarship is available to students attending Bellarmine University. You must be majoring in art or music. Art students must submit a portfolio and music majors must audition to be eligible for this award. Music award recipients must participate in two ensembles each year to renew the award. more...

Athletic Grant - Bellarmine University
The Athletic Grant is available to students at Bellarmine University who plan to participate in the intercollegiate athletics program. Applicable sports include basketball, cross country, track, golf, soccer, tennis, baseball, field hockey, softball, lacrosse and volleyball. more...

Bellarmine Scholars Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Bellarmine University. Students must demonstrate academic achievement, talent, community service, and leadership skills. Award amount is for full tuition. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Kentucky Governor Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at Bellarmine University. Students must have completed the GSP and GSA program and demonstrate academic achievement, leadership skills, talent, and community service. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Kentucky Governor Scholarships
The Kentucky Governor Scholarships is available to full-time entering freshmen who completed the GSP or GSA program at Bellarmine University. more...

Monsignor Horrigan Scholarships

The ROTC Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Bellarmine University who plan to participate in the campus ROTC program. You must be at least 17 years of age and a U.S. citizen. Your selection is also based on leadership, extracurricular and athletic activities. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Bellarmine University
