Presbyterian College Scholarships


Belk Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Presbyterian College. Selection based on minimum ACT score of 27 or SAT score of 1200, 3.5 GPA, and leadership skills. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Fuqua Artist Awards
Scholarship for incoming undergraduates accepted to Presbyterian College who are Music majors with exceptional musical talent. Candidates for this award are chosen from the regular music auditions and are invited to try out. Award covers full tuition and music fees. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. Contact the Music Department for more information and/or application. more...

Fuqua Performer Awards
Scholarship for incoming undergraduates accepted to Presbyterian College who are Music majors with exceptional musical talent. Candidates for this award are chosen from the regular music auditions and are invited to try out. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. Contact the Music Department for more information and/or application. more...

John I. Smith Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Presbyterian College. Selection based on minimum ACT score of 29 or SAT score of 1300, 3.7 GPA, and leadership skills. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Multicultural Leadership Awards
Scholarship for minority undergraduate students enrolled in Presbyterian College. Preference is given to students from diverse cultural/ethnic backgrounds. Award is based on demonstrated leadership in high school, church and/or community. Applicant should have a minimum ACT score of 20 or SAT score of 950, 2.25 GPA and rank in the top 75% of class. Contact the Admissions Office for more information and/or application. more...

National Presbyterian College Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Presbyterian College. Applicants must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents, be members of a Presbyterian Church, have a minimum 3.0 GPA, have financial need, take the SAT and a have a recommendation from the church pastor. Number of awards may vary. more...

Pc Ga Scholarship
Scholarship for Georgia residents who are incoming freshmen or transfer students accepted to Presbyterian College. Applicants must qualify for the Georgia Hope Scholarship to be eligible. Award amount is equivalent to a Hope Scholarship. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Presbyterian Church Usa Academic Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Presbyterian College who are members of the Presbyterian Church. Selection based on minimum ACT score of 29 or SAT score of 1300, 3.7 GPA, and an interview. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Quattlebaum Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Presbyterian College. Selection based on minimum ACT score of 31 or SAT score of 1350, 3.75 GPA, leadership skills, and an interview. Interviews will be held in February. Award amount covers full tuition, fees, room, and board. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Southeastern Or Dillard Elliott Scholarship
