Midway University Scholarships


Athletic Scholarship - Midway University
The Athletic Scholarship is available to students at Midway University. You must participate in equestrian (riding teams), track and cross country, tennis, volleyball, basketball, softball or soccer to be eligible for this award. Additionally, to qualify for this award, you must live on campus. more...

Bowling Academic Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Midway College full-time. Applicants must have exceptional academic credentials. Recipients are required to live on campus. Number of awards varies. Award amount is equal to full tuition. more...

Country Scholarships
Scholarship for residents of Woodford, Fayette, Scott, Anderson, Franklin, Mercer and Jessamine counties in Kentucky who are incoming freshmen accepted to Midway College full-time. Applicants must rank in the top 25% of their class, have a minimum 2.2 GPA and achieve an ACT score of at least 18 or an SAT score of 860. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain 2.5 GPA. more...

Disciples Of Christ Scholarship
The Disciples of Christ Scholarship is available to students at Midway University. You must be a member of the Disciples of Christ church to be eligible for this award. more...

Disciples Of Christ Scholarship
Scholarship for members of the Disciples of Christ church who are undergraduate students attending Midway College full-time. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Legacy Scholarship
The Legacy Scholarship is available to students at Midway University. You must have relatives who attended Midway to be eligible for this award. more...

Lewis & Ann Piper Scholarship
Scholarship for non-traditional undergraduate students over age 60 attending Midway College full-time. Women may take classes for credit or audit; men may take School for Career Development offerings for credit or audit. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Ruth Slack Roach Scholarship
Scholarship for female undergraduate students attending Midway College full-time. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and have earned 65 credit hours. Students must be interested in holding a leadership position and have a commitment to community service. Interview is required. Recipient is required to live on campus. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...
