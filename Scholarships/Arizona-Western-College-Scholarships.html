Arizona Western College Scholarships


Awc Foundation Scholarship
The AWC Foundation Scholarship is open to full-time students at Arizona Western College. You must be a U.S. citizen and a legal resident of Yuma or La Paz County to qualify for this award. more...

Backus Honor Scholarship
The Backus Honor Scholarship is open to third-semester students at Arizona Western College. You must meet all requirements of the AWC Honors program. You also cannot be a physical education major. more...

Charles Ennis Rice Agricultural Scholarship
The Charles Ennis Rice Agricultural Scholarship is open to students at Arizona Western College. You must be majoring in agriculture and have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible for this award. more...

David L. Baughman Memorial Scholarship
The David L. Baughman Memorial Scholarship is open to full-time students at Arizona Western College. You must be majoring in mathematics and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Donald Clay Memorial Scholarship
The Donald Clay Memorial Scholarship is open to part-time environmental sciences students at Arizona Western College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA to qualify for this award. more...

Dorothy A. Taber Endowment Scholarship
The Dorothy A. Taber Endowment Scholarship is open to second-year students at Arizona Western College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA to qualify for this award. more...

Erick Inman Scholarship
The Erick Inman Scholarship is open to full-time students at Arizona Western College who are Yuma-area graduates. You must be involved in extracurricular activities other than sports. An essay is required. more...

Ernest Lopez, Sr. And Betty Lopez Memorial Scholarship
The Ernest Lopez, Sr. and Betty Lopez Memorial Scholarship is open to full-time students at Arizona Western College. You must be majoring in welding technology and have a minimum GPA of 3.0. Also, you must have graduated from a district area high school within the past two years to be eligible for this award. more...

Ernest Lopez, Sr. And Betty Lopez Memorial Scholarship
The Ernest Lopez, Sr. and Betty Lopez Memorial Scholarship is open to full-time welding majors at Arizona Western College. You must be a graduate from a college-district high school within the previous two years to qualify for this award. more...

George H. And Leila M. Payne Scholarship
The George H. and Leila M. Payne Scholarship is open to students with physical disabilities at Arizona Western College. You must be taking at least nine credit hours and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to qualify for this award. more...

Gonzalo D. Lucila Huerta Scholarship
The Gonzalo D. Lucila Huerta Scholarship is open to full-time welding technology students at Arizona Western College. You must be a graduate from a Yuma or La Paz County school within the past two years to qualify for this award. more...

Sunbelt Communications Scholarship
The Sunbelt Communications Scholarship is open to full-time students at Arizona Western College. You must be majoring in broadcasting or a related major, be a U.S. citizen, have a minimum 2.5 GPA, and have graduated from a high school in Arizona, California, Idaho, Montana, Nevada or Wyoming to qualify for this award. more...

Sylvia Plotts Memorial Scholarship
The Sylvia Plotts Memorial Scholarship is open to Native American students at Arizona Western College. You must maintain a minimum 2.0 GPA and be a resident of Yuma or La Paz county to qualify for this award. more...
