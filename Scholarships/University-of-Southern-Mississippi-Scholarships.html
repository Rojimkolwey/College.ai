University of Southern Mississippi Scholarships


2012 Southeast Region Scholarship
XAP Corporation, the leading provider of online student and career software and services, is sponsoring a $1,000 scholarship competition in honor of a recently retired employee who acted as the Regional Director of Sales for our Southeast Region. Our colleague, Barbara McDaniel, a sales professional dedicated to providing XAP products to students in the Southeast region, is a graduate of the University of Southern Mississippi. What better way to honor her tenure at XAP than by offering a scholarship to a student in her region who will be attending her alma mater. Applicant must be a resident of Mississippi who will be attending the University of Southern Mississippi as a freshman for the Fall 2012 Semester. Must also be a user of Choices Planner. more...

Mississippi Power Company Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for Mississippi residents who are undergraduate students attending the University of Southern Mississippi who are majoring in Marketing, Accounting, Computer Science, Business Administration, Advertising, Communcations, or Journalism. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. more...

Usm Community College Transfer Academic Scholarship
The USM Community College Transfer Academic Scholarship is available to entering transfer students at the University of Southern Mississippi. You must be transferring from a community/junior college and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...
