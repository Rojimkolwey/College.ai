Richmond Community College Scholarships


Annie Brooks Beam Memorial Scholarship
The Annie Brooks Beam Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Richmond Community College. You must be majoring in nursing and be a resident of Richmond County to be eligible for this award. more...

Bennett Family Memorial Scholarship
The Bennett Family Memorial Scholarship is available to full - time, first - year students at Richmond Community College. You must be a customer, employee, or an immediate family member of a customer or employee of the Ellerbe Telephone Company to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students majoring in electronics engineering technology or business administration. more...

Csx Transportation Scholarship
The CSX Transportation Scholarship is available to students at Richmond Community College. You must be an employee of the CSX System, child of an employee, or a dislocated employee from CSX to be eligible for this award. You must also be majoring in electronics engineering or mechanical engineering technology and be a resident of Richmond County. more...

Gene And Alice Burrell Scholarship
The Gene and Alice Burrell Scholarship is available to full - time, second - year students at Richmond Community College. You must be planning to transfer to a four - year college / university to be eligible for this award. more...

J. Robert Harrison Scholarship
The J. Robert Harrison Scholarship is available to full - time, first - year students at Richmond Community College. You must be enrolled in the electronics engineering, mechanical engineering, or microcomputer systems technology curriculum to be eligible for this award. more...

Joseph D. Beam Scholarship
The Joseph D. Beam Scholarship is available to full - time, first - year students at Richmond Community College. You must be a resident of Richmond County and be majoring in electronics engineering technology or mechanical engineering technology to be eligible for this award. more...

Joseph W. Grimsley Memorial Scholarship
The Joseph W. Grimsley Memorial Scholarship is available to full - time, first - year students at Richmond Community College. You must demonstrate academic merit and leadership potential to be eligible for this award. more...

Robert L. Memorial Cole Scholarship
The Robert L. Cole Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Richmond Community College. You must be a resident of Richmond County. You must also be enrolled full time in nursing, criminal justice, or human services technology to qualify for this award. more...
