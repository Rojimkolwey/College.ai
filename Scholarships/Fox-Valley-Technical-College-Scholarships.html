Fox Valley Technical College Scholarships


Carlton E. Kuck Scholarship
Scholarship for college sophomores enrolled at Fox Valley Technical College in the Associate Degree Technical Nursing program. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

E. Peggy Bergomi Nursing Education Scholarship
Scholarship for college sophomore students enrolled in the Associate Degree Technical Nursing Program at Fox Valley Technical College. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Ione Berg Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Fox Valley Technical College pursuing studies in Culinary Arts or Hotel and Restaurant Management. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and demonstrate financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Ruth & Sarah Mielke Educational Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Fox Valley Technical College, the University of Wisconsin-Fox Valley, or Northeast Wisconsin Technical College. Students must have successfully completed 25% of the credits for their program of study. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Ruth Kossel Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Fox Valley Technical College studying to become a Licensed Practical Nurse. Number of awards may vary. Contact the college for more information and/or application. more...

Sloan & Gerard Fine Art (Studio) Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at the University of Wisconsin-Fox Valley in the schools Art program. Students must demonstrate desire to pursue the Arts as a lifelong endeavor. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Stanley H. Ostrand Fvtc Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Fox Valley Technical College in a Nursing program. Students must have completed a minimum of one semester and have a minimum 2.675 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...
