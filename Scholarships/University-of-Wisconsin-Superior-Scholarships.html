University of Wisconsin-Superior Scholarships


Chancellor's Scholarship - Uw, Superior
The Chancellor's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at the University of Wisconsin, Superior. You must graduate in the top quarter of your class and have a minimum 21 ACT score to be considered for this award. more...

Ed And Amy Greve Library Science Scholarship
The Ed and Amy Greve Library Science Scholarship is available to students majoring or minoring in library science at the University of Wisconsin, Superior. A minimum 3.0 GPA is required. Students who have completed their baccalaureate degree with a teaching certificate and are seeking to enhance their credentials by working toward library media certification in either Wisconsin or Minnesota will also be considered. more...

Gulland Scholarship
The Gulland Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of Wisconsin, Superior. You must rank in the top half of your class, be under the age of 25, and have graduated from a high school in one of the following Minnesota or Wisconsin counties: Aikin, Carlton, Cook, Lake, St. Louis, Crow Wing, Ashland, Bayfield, Douglas or Iron. This award may be renewed with a maintained minimum 3.0 GPA. more...

Hermanson Scholarship - Uw, Superior
