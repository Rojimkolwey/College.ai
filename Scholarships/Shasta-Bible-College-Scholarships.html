Awana Citation Award

Children's Steadfast Award
The Children's Steadfast Award is available to students at Shasta Bible College. more...

Christian Leadership Scholarship - Shasta Bible College
The Christian Leadership Scholarship is available to students at Shasta Bible College. The scholarship will be awarded to one student from each local church. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and must also have recommendations from your Youth Pastor and Senior Pastor to be eligible for this award. To apply, you must complete the financial aid application and submit it along with a letter stating what you have done to demonstrate Christian Leadership. more...

Gerald K. Duckett Memorial Award
The Gerald K. Duckett Memorial Award is available at the end of the year to students at Shasta Bible College who shows potential excellence in Bible teaching and/or Jewish evangelism. more...

Multiple Sibling Discount - Shasta Bible College
The Multiple Sibling Discount is available to multiple siblings at Shasta Bible College who are attending school concurrently. The first student receives no discount. The Second student receives a 10% tuition discount. The third and subsequent students will receive a 20% tuition discount. Married students are noteligible for the multiple sibling discount. more...

Music Scholarship - Shasta Bible College
The Music Scholarship is available to students at Shasta Bible College who qualify through audition with the music department for membership in Sounds of Shasta or other college musical teams. You must attend rehearsals regularly and be available for music ministry on Sundays, at special events and for summer tours to be eligible for this award. more...

Striving For Excellence Award
The Striving for Excellence Award is available to students at Shasta Bible College who demonstrate significant academic improvement. more...

Student Ministry's Creative Discipleship Award
The Student Ministry's Creative Discipleship Award is available to students at Shasta Bible College. more...

Warthan Family Garbc Scholarship
The Warthan Family GARBC Scholarship is available to students at Shasta Bible College who are committed to pasturing within the General Association of Regular Baptist Churches (GARBC). This award is renewable each semester up to four years. more...

Word Of Life Bible Institute Certificate Award
The Bible Institute Certificate Award is available to full-time students at Shasta Bible College who have graduated from the Word of Life Bible Institute in Schroon Lake, New York. The award will be applied to your first year's tuition after graduation. more...
