Elmhurst University Scholarships


Dean's Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Dean's Scholarship is available to entering freshman students at Elmhurst College. You must have performed at a high level in advanced placement and honors courses in a strong high school program to qualify for this award. more...

Enrichment Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Enrichment Scholarship is available to full - time freshmen and transfer students attending Elmhurst College. You must be a member of a traditionally underrepresented group to be considered for this award. more...

Founders Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Founders Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Elmhurst College who have strong academic credentials. This award is renewable if you maintain full - time enrollment and a minimum 2.5 GPA. more...

Music Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Music Scholarship is available to music students at Elmhurst College. You must audition to qualify for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to students transferring to Elmhurst College. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa and have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Presidential Scholarship is available to incoming freshman students at Elmhurst College. You must have excelled in a challenging high school curriculum, including a number of advanced placement and honors courses to qualify for this award. more...

Transfer Achievement Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Transfer Achievement Scholarship is available to students transferring to Elmhurst College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Recognition Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The Transfer Recognition Scholarship is available to entering transfer students at Elmhurst College who have strong academic credentials. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

United Church Of Christ Scholarship - Elmhurst College
The United Church of Christ Scholarship is available to freshmen and transfer students at Elmhurst College who are members of a United Church of Christ congregation. Your pastor must submit a letter of nomination to the Director of Financial Aid for you to be considered for this award. more...
