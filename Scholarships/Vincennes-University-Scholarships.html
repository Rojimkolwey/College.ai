Allomatic Products Machine Trades Scholarship
Scholarship for Sullivan County, Indian residents who are undergraduate students attending Vincennes University pursuing studies in Machine Trades Technology. Selection is based on activities, work experience, essays, recommendations, academic achievement, and financial need. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Archer Daniels Midland Company (Adm) Scholarships (College)
Scholarship for current/former FFA members who are undergraduate students at select colleges pursuing studies related to Agricultural Business, Management, Finance, Marketing, Merchandising, Sales, Engineering or Food Science. Student must be a U.S. citizen. Select schools are: Vincennes University, Blackhawk Community College, Spoon River College, Rend Lake College, Purdue University, University of Illinois, Western Illinois University, Illinois State University, Iowa State University, University of Minnesota, University of Missouri, Oklahoma State University, North Carolina A&T; University, New Mexico State University, University of Nebraska, Florida A&M; University, Kansas State University, Texas Tech University, Texas A&M; University, or Ohio State University. Criteria used for selection includes students GPA (minimum 3.0), leadership in FFA activities, leadership in community activities, and involvement in the Supervised Agricultural Experience Program (SAE). Nonrenewable. more...

Eaa Chapter 1354 Aviation Scholarship (Membership)
Scholarship for Experimental Aircraft Association (EAA) Chapter 1354 members who are graduating high school seniors or college freshmen enrolled full-time at Purdue University-School of Aviation, Vincennes University, Indiana State University, Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, or University of Illinois pursuing a career as a commercial pilot, air transport pilot, airport administration, or in the field of Aeronautical Engineering. Students must exhibit good character and commitment to school and community. Selection is based on standardized test scores, GPA, and/or class rank which demonstrates academic preparation and performance. Number of awards may vary. A personal statement and an essay must be included with application packet. more...

Indiana National Guard Supplemental Grant
Scholarship for active Indiana Air and Army National Guard members who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Ball State University (all campuses), Indiana State University, Indiana University, Ivy Tech State College, Purdue University, University of Southern Indiana, or Vincennes University. Students must be high school graduates, home-schooled, or GED recipients, and be residents of Indiana since December 31st of the previous year. A FAFSA must be submitted by March 10th. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
