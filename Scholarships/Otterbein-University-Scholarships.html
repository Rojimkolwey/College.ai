Battelle Half Tuition Scholarship
The Battelle Half Tuition Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Otterbein University. You must be a resident of Franklin or a contiguous county to be eligible for this award. more...

International Baccalaureate Scholarship - Otterbein College
The International Baccalaureate Scholarship is available to incoming first- year students at Otterbein College who have participated in an international baccalaureate program. Selection for this award will be based on your level of achievement. more...

Otterbein Advantage Partnership Scholarship
The Otterbein Advantage Partnership Scholarship is available to transfer students at Otterbein College. You must be under the age of 23 and the child of an Otterbein Advantage Partner employee to be considered for this award. more...

Otterbein Full Tuition Scholarship Competition

Presidential Scholars Award - Otterbein University
The Presidential Scholars Award is available to entering freshmen at Otterbein University. You must demonstrate significant academic achievement to be considered for this award. more...

Sibling/Legacy Award - Otterbein College
