Arizona State University-Downtown Phoenix Scholarships


Amalio M. Suarez Memorial Assistantship
Scholarship for entering students at Thunderbird, The Garvin School of International Management pursuing studies in International Business Management. Students must have majored in Spanish. Awards stipulate a work requirement assisting either faculty or adminstrators in assigned projects. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Arizona Universities Assistantship
Scholarship for graduates of the University of Arizona, Arizona State University or Northern Arizona University who are incoming students at Thunderbird, The Garvin School of International Management pursuing studies in International Business Management. Awards stipulate a work requirement assisting either faculty or adminstrators in assigned projects. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Continuing Student Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate (freshmen ineligible) or graduate students at Thunderbird, The Garvin School of International Management who excel in full-time MIM or MBA programs. Students must have a minimum 3.6 CGPA, demonstrate visible leadership and initiative in improving the Thunderbird communitys quality of life, and demostrate a high level of on-campus participation and leadership. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Domestic Diversity Grant
Scholarship for entering students at Thunderbird, The Garvin School of International Management pursuing studies in International Business Management. Students must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents and belong to a U.S. ethnic group that is underrepresented in the Thunderbird student body. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
