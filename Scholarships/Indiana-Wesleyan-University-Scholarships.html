Indiana Wesleyan University-Marion Scholarships


Freshman Academic Scholarship - Indiana Wesleyan University
The Freshman Academic Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must have a minimum 3.2 GPA and at least a 24 ACT/1100 SAT score to be eligible for this award. more...

International Student Grant - Indiana Wesleyan University
The International Student Grant is open to international students at Indiana Wesleyan University. Canadian students are not eligible. more...

International Wesleyan Grant - Indiana Wesleyan University
The International Wesleyan Grant is open to full-time international students at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must have attended a Wesleyan church in your home country to be eligible for this award. more...

National Merit Finalist/Semi-Finalist Award - Indiana Wesleyan Univ.
The National Merit Finalist/Semi-Finalist Award is open to entering freshmen at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must be a National Merit Finalist or Semi-Finalist and have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be considered for this award. more...

Out-Of-State Wesleyan Grant - Indiana Wesleyan University
The Out-of-State Wesleyan Grant is open to students at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must be a U.S. citizen who is not an Indiana resident, attend a Wesleyan church in your community and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Pastor Dependent Grant - Indiana Wesleyan University
The Pastor Dependent Grant is open to students at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must be the dependent of a full-time minister serving at a local church to be considered for this award. more...

President's Scholarship
The President's Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must be a graduate of a Grant County high school to be considered for this renewable award. more...

Transfer Academic Scholarship - Indiana Wesleyan University
The Transfer Academic Scholarship is open to entering transfer students at Indiana Wesleyan University. You must have completed at least 24 transferable credit hours with a minimum 3.0 GPA to be considered for this renewable award. more...
