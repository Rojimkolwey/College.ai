Humboldt State University Scholarships


California Faculty Association, Humboldt Chapter Scholarship
Annual award for dependent children or relatives of active faculty/lecturers/coach of Humboldt State University who are undergraduate students enrolled at an accredited postsecondary institution. Students must demonstrate academic achievement, involvement in social and civic issues, responsibility, reliability, and integrity. Consideration may be given to financial need. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

First Presbyterian Church Of Eureka International Student Scholarship
Annual award for international undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the College of Redwoods or Humboldt State University. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and financial need. A personal statement and a copy of a U.S. Student Visa or I-20 must be included with application packet. more...

Follow Your Heart Scholarship
Annual award for female undergraduate students enrolled at Humboldt State University. Students must be 35 years of age or older and have been away from school for a minimum of five years. Preference given to full-time students. Consideration may be given to financial need. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Greenwood Family Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Humboldt State University majoring in Business. Students must demonstrate academic achievement, community service, and be graduates of Eureka High School. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Jane Irene Mishica Memorial Scholarship-M.S.W. Program
Annual award for graduate students enrolled full-time at Humboldt State University majoring in Social Work. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, be Humboldt County, California high school graduates, and have been out of school for a minimum of two years after obtaining a B.A. degree and work with children and families. Consideration may be given to financial need. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

John H. Collier Memorial Scholarship (B)
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled at Humboldt State University. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA, financial need, and be graduates of Eureka or Fortuna High Schools. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Joyce Lewis Nelson Memorial Scholarship (B)
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Humboldt State University. Students must be South Fork High School graduates and demonstrate academic achievement. Consideration may be given to financial need. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Rael Family Scholarship
Scholarship for Chicano/Mexican American Humboldt or Del Norte County, California residents who are undergraduate students enrolled at Humboldt State University. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA, financial need, demonstrate academic progress, and community service. A personal statement must be included with application packet. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...

Theresa Mary Mcniel Memorial Scholarship For The Study Of Mandarin
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled at Humboldt State University. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, financial need, and be enrolled in a Chinese language course. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...
