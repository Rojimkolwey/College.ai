Maine Maritime Academy Scholarships


Bath Iron Works Scholarship
The Bath Iron Works Scholarship is open to students at Maine Maritime Academy who are dependents of an employee of Bath Iron Works in Maine. more...

Boston Port And Seamen's Aid Society Scholarship
The Boston Port and Seamen's Aid Society Scholarship is open to students at Maine Maritime Academy who are coast guard license majors. You must be a Massachusetts resident, have a parent involved in the Merchant Marine Service, and intend to make a career in the maritime industry to be eligible for this award. more...

Maine Maritime Academy Grant
The Maine Maritime Academy Grant is open to incoming first-year students at Maine Maritime Academy who demonstrate financial need through the FAFSA. more...

Myron D. Rust Scholarship
The Myron D. Rust Scholarship is open to students at Maine Maritime Academy. You must have graduated from Marshwood, Traip, Wells or York High School and be a resident of Eliot, Kittery, Wells or York. more...

Scheel Scholarship - Maine Maritime Academy
The Scheel Scholarship is open to upperclass students at Maine Maritime Academy. You must demonstrate academic excellence and intellectual curiosity to be eligible for this award. more...
