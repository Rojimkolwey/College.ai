Saint Norbert College Scholarships


Diversity Leadership Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen (non-international) admitted to St. Norbert College. Consideration will be given to applicants racial, geographic, and ethnic background, commitment to the community through volunteer or community service efforts, outstanding personal talents, leadership potential, co-curricular involvement in high school, and if the applicant is a first-generation college student with financial need. Student must submit a Diversity Leadership Award application to be eligible for this award. Semi-finalists being considered will be contacted for an interview. Renewable. more...

Transfer Student Scholarship - St. Norbert College

Transfer-International Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming transfer/international students accepted at St. Norbert College. Applicants high school and postsecondary education records, standardized test scores, and extracurricular activities are used for selection. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Trustee Distinguished Scholarship
The Trustee Distinguished Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at St. Norbert College. You must maintain good social standing and a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Trustees Distinguished Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to St. Norbert College. This is a prestigious scholarship awarded to exceptionally talented students based on high school courses and involvement in and out of high school. Student must rank in the top 10% of their class, have a minimum 3.75 GPA, a minimum ACT composite score of 27 or a combined SAT score above the 90th percentile. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Van Dyke Award
