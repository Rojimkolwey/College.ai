Madonna University Scholarships


Felician Presidential Scholarship
The Felician Presidential Scholarship is available to entering full - time freshmen at Madonna University. You must have a minimum 3.6 GPA and a minimum 27 ACT score to be eligible for this award. more...

The Knights of Columbus-Michigan State Council Scholarship is available to full-time graduate and undergraduate students at Madonna University.
