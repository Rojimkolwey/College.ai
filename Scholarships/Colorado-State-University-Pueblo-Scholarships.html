Colorado State University Pueblo Scholarships


Csu Pueblo Distinguished Scholarship
The CSU Pueblo Distinguished Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo. You must have either a minimum GPA of 3.75, a minimum ACT/SAT score of 26/1260, or rank in the top 15 percent of your high school to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Csu Pueblo Emerging Scholarship
The CSU Pueblo Emerging Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo. You must have either a minimum GPA of 3.35, a minimum ACT/SAT score of 23/1140, or rank in the top 40 percent of your high school to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Csu Pueblo Graduate Education Scholarship - Athletic Training
The Graduate Education Scholarship - Athletic Training is available to graduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo who are majoring in Athletic Training. You must be enrolled on a full-time basis and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Csu Pueblo Presidential Scholarship
The CSU Pueblo Presidential Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo. You must have either a minimum GPA of 3.85, a minimum ACT/SAT score of 29/1360, or rank in the top five percent of your high school to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Csu Pueblo Promising Scholarship
The CSU Pueblo Promising Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo. You must have either a minimum GPA of 3.55, a minimum ACT/SAT score of 24/1180, or rank in the top 25 percent of your high school to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Csu Pueblo Welcome To The Pack Scholarship
The CSU Pueblo Welcome to the Pack Scholarship is available to incoming all freshman undergraduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo. This award is renewable with a minimum CSU Pueblo GPA of 3.0. more...

David B. Shaw Family Scholarship
The David B. Shaw Family Scholarship is available to students at Colorado State University-Pueblo who are members of the Golf Team. To be eligible for this award, you must permanently reside in one of the following counties in Colorado: Pueblo, Mineral, Chaffee, Saguache, Rio Grande, Conejos, Fremont, Custer, Alamosa, Costilla, Huerfano, Las Animas, Crowley, Otero, Kiowa, Bent, Prowers, or Baca. more...

Deb And Eric Beaudry Athletic Scholarship
The Deb and Eric Beaudry Athletic Scholarship is available to undergraduate students at Colorado State University-Pueblo who are pursuing studies in exercise science. To be eligible for this renewable award, you must have participated in athletics at your high school, have a minimum GPA of 3.0, and be a resident of Pueblo County, CO. more...

Doc Seilheimer Family Scholarship

Harry H. And Mary Elizabeth "Bodie" Simmons Memorial Scholarship
