University of Charleston Scholarships


Athletic & Cheerleading Scholarships - University Of Charleston
TheAthletic & Cheerleading Scholarships is available to students at the University of Charleston. You must meet UC admissions requirements to be eligible for this award. more...

Chorus Or Pep Band Scholarship - University Of Charleston
The Chorus or Pep Band Scholarship is available to students at the University of Charleston. You must be be enrolled in concert choir or pep band to be eligible for this award. Vocal students must take private voice study while at UC. Music major is not required. more...

Elizabeth S. Johnston Memorial Fund
Scholarship for West Virginia residents who are full-time undergraduates attending the University of Charleston. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA, financial need, a minimum ACT score of 20, and good moral character. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Freshman And Transfer Student On-Campus And Off-Campus Scholarships - University Of Charleston
The Freshman and Transfer Student On-Campus and Off-Campus Scholarship is available to incoming first-year and transfer students at the University of Charleston. Selection is based on high school GPA and ACT/SAT scores for freshmen or based on college GPA for transfer students. more...

Presidential Scholars Program - University Of Charleston
The Presidential Scholars Program is available to incoming first-year students at the University of Charleston. You must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and a minimum 28 ACT/1250 SAT score. You must also live on campus to be eligible for this award. more...

Shepherd Family Fund
Scholarship for residents of Boone, Clay, Fayette, Jackson, Kanawha, Lincoln, or Putnam counties, West Virginia who are enrolled at the University of Charleston pursuing a degree in Nursing. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA, minimium ACT score of 20, and good moral character. Award amount and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Welch Colleague Program Scholarship
The Welch Colleague Program Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Charleston. You must have a minimum 3.25 GPA and demonstrate community involvement. You must also reside on campus to be eligible for this award. Selection will also be based on your involvement during Experience UC Day. more...
