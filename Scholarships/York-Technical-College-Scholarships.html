Project Hope Scholarship
Project HOPE is part of the Health Profession Opportunity Grant (HPOG) program, a demonstration project funded by the Administration for Children and Families (ACF) in the U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS).
Scholarships are available to members of FI (TANF) families, and qualifying low income individuals who are
at least 200% of the poverty level or below, including SNAP recipients, US Veterans, and Catawba Indians.

T.E.A.C.H. Early Childhood® Scholarship
T.E.A.C.H. (Teacher Education and Compensation Helps) offers an effective strategy for addressing the education, compensation and retention of the early childhood workforce across the country. The T.E.A.C.H. Early Childhood® Project is also building the capacity of educational institutions. As a purchaser of college education credits for thousands of early childhood professionals across the county, T.E.A.C.H. has played a significant role in increasing the demand for early childhood college level coursework.
To apply for a T.E.A.C.H. Early Childhood® Scholarship, download the attached application.
To request an application and brochure to be sent to you, please email T.E.A.C.H. or call us at 1-866-845-1555.
Completed applications can be mailed or faxed to the following:
T.E.A.C.H.
PO Box 5616
Greenville, SC 29606-5616
Fax: 864-250-8680
