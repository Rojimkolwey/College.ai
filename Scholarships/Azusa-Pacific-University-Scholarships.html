Azusa Pacific University Scholarships


Canadian Scholarships
Scholarship for Canadian incoming freshmen enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Renewable. more...

Celebrate Azusa Citizens Scholarship-Nancy Moore Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of the Azusa Unified School District who are graduating high school seniors from Azusa or Gladstone High Schools planning to attend Azusa Pacific University. Students must have a Christian commitment, have a minimum 2.7 GPA, financial need, work a minimum of 10 hours per week doing community service, and demonstrate leadership. A FAFSA must be submitted by June 1st. Renewable. more...

Christian Workers Discount For Missionaries, Pastors & Christian Organization Workers Dependents
Scholarship for dependents of full-time foreign missionaries, church pastors, or other church or parachurch workers who are undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Number of awards may vary. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Apu
The Dean's Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Azusa Pacific University. You must have at least a 3.9 GPA and have a minimum 1200 SAT / 26 ACT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Deans Scholarships I & Ii
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Students must have a CGPA between 3.45 and 3.749, a minimum 24 ACT or a minimum 1100 SAT score (or ACT equivalent). Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Directors Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Students must have a CGPA between 3.0 and 3.449 GPA, and a minimum 950 SAT score, or minimum 21 ACT score. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Earl E. Grant Scholarship
The Earl E. Grant Scholarship is available to graduate students at Azusa Pacific University's School of Theology. You must be attending classes at the San Diego Regional Center to qualify for this award. more...

Ernest L. Boyer Teacher Scholarship
The Ernest L. Boyer Teacher Scholarship is available to teaching credential students at Azusa Pacific University. You must be planning a career in education to qualify for this award. more...

Financial Need Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Students must demonstrate financial need. Number of awards may vary. A FAFSA must be included with application packet. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...

James L. Stamps Foundation Scholarship
The James L. Stamps Foundation Scholarship is available to graduate students at Azusa Pacific University pursing their master's degree. You must be part of the pastoral staff of a small church to qualify for this award. more...

Multi-Ethnic Leadership Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Selection is based on GPA, leadership experience, passion for diversity advocacy, and financial need. Applicants must participate in the Multi-Ethnic Leadership program. Number of awards may vary. more...

Presidents Scholarships I & Ii
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Students must have a minimum 3.75 GPA, a minimum 27 ACT score or a minimum 1200 SAT score. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Science & Math Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled at Azusa Pacific University majoring in Biology, Chemistry, Biochemistry, Natural Science, Applied Health, Mathematics, Physics, or Engineering. Due date and award amount may vary. more...

Sibling Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Azusa Pacific University. Students must be dependent undergraduate siblings attending Azusa Pacific University in the same year. Number of awards may vary. more...

Stauffer Fellowships
Annual Fellowships for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University majoring in Chemistry or Biochemistry. Due date and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

The Joshua Kim Scholarship
The Joshua Kim Scholarship is available to Korean students at Azusa Pacific University's School of Theology. You must be from the Los Angeles Regional Center doing master’s theology work to be eligible for this award. more...

The Watson-Wong Scholarship Fund
The Watson-Wong Scholarship Fund is available to entering or continuing graduate students at Azusa Pacific University's School of Theology. You must be enrolled in music and/or theology program and a single parent to be eligible for this award. more...

The Youn Sung Lee Scholarship
The Greenwald Scholarship is available to Korean students at Azusa Pacific University. You must be preparing for Korean ministry to be eligible for this award. more...

Theatre Arts-Media Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Azusa Pacific University. Students must show involvement in the theater or media programs and/or in student film projects. An audition is required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Walter And Mary Hartley Scholarship
The Walter and Mary Hartley Scholarship is available to graduate students at Azusa Pacific University. You must be interested in biblical studies and have a desire to minister in overseas or inner city missions to qualify for this award. more...
