Dakota Wesleyan University Scholarships


Activity Awards (Fine Arts)
Scholarship for undergraduate students participating in performances in the fine arts who are attending Dakota Wesleyan University. Selection is based on high school and college performance and expected collegiate participation. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Activity Awards (Sports)
Scholarship for undergraduate students participating in intercollegiate athletics attending Dakota Wesleyan University. Selection is based on high school and college performance and expected collegiate participation. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Athletic Award - Dwu
The Athletic Award is available to students at Dakota Wesleyan University. You must be involved in athletics to be eligible for this award. more...

Dwu Family Grant
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Dakota Wesleyan University. Students must be immediate family members of a DWU graduate or current student. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Dwu United Methodist Recognition Award
The DWU United Methodist Recognition Award is available to students at Dakota Wesleyan University. You must be a member of the United Methodist Church to be eligible for this award. more...

Methodist Awards
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are confirmed members of the United Methodist Church attending Dakota Wesleyan University. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Randall Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors accepted to Dakota Wesleyan University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.8 GPA and minimum 27 ACT or 1240 SAT. Selection is based on SAT, ACT, GPA, recommendations, essays, and leadership ability. Students must attend Scholarship Day. Renewable. more...

Recognition Award
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Dakota Wesleyan University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 CGPA and minimum 16 ACT. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Tiger Award
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Dakota Wesleyan University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and minimum 18 ACT or 850 SAT. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Transfer Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolling at Dakota Wesleyan University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 college GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
