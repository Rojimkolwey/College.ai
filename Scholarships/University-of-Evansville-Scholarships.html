University of Evansville Scholarships


Academic Scholarship (Transfer)
Scholarship for full-time transfer students accepted to the University of Evansville. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Academic Scholarships (First-Time)
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen accepted to the University of Evansville. Selection based on GPA, difficulty of curriculum, class rank and SAT/ACT scores. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

American Corporate Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen accepted to the University of Evansville who are enrolled in the School of Business. Selection based on a minimum 3.6 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1730 or ACT scores of 25. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Art, Music, & Theatre Scholarships
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen or transfer students accepted to the University of Evansville who are majoring in Art, Music or Theatre. Selection based on audition or portfolio. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. more...

Cultural Diversity Award
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen or transfer students accepted to the University of Evansville. Selection based on academic achievement, student leadership and community involvement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Ensemble Participation Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Evansville who participate in the schools music program (non-music majors). Selection based on audition and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Fine And Performing Arts Scholarship - University Of Evansville
The Fine and Performing Arts Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen who plan on majoring in art, music or theatre at the University of Evansville. Your selection for this renewable award will be based on your high school record. Please contact the department to schedule an audition or portfolio presentation. more...

First Choice-First Choice Achiever Awards For Indiana Twenty-First Century Scholars
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen accepted to the University of Evansville who are Indiana Twenty-first Scholars. Selection based on GPA, difficulty of curriculum, class rank and SAT/ACT scores. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. more...

Indiana Honors Diploma Scholarships
Scholarship for Indiana residents who are full-time, first-time freshmen accepted to the University of Evansville that graduated with an Academic Honors Diploma. Selection based on GPA, difficulty of curriculum, class rank and SAT/ACT scores. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Leadership Activity Award
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen accepted to the University of Evansville. Selection based on GPA, difficulty of curriculum, class rank, SAT/ACT scores and activities in school/church/community. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Physics Scholarships
Scholarship for current undergraduate students attending the University of Evansville who are majoring in Physics. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Southhold Historic Preservation Scholarship
Annual award for Indiana residents who are full-time undergraduate or graduate students in their junior or senior year that are pursuing studies at the University of Notre Dame, University of Evansville, or Ball State University leading toward a degree in or related to Historic Preservation. Preference will be given to residents of St. Joseph County, Indiana. Selection is based on academic performance and merit. more...

Steinkamp Family Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors of Southridge High School planning to attend the University of Evansville. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact students high school guidance counselor or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Toyota Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, first-time freshmen accepted to the University of Evansville who are enrolled in the College of Engineering and Computer Science. Selection based on a minimum 3.6 GPA and minimum SAT score of 1730 or ACT score of 25. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

University Of Evansville-John R. Tooley-Sae Engineering Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend the University of Evansville majoring in Engineering. Students must be U.S. citizens, have a minimum 3.5 GPA, a minimum 27 ACT score or a minimum 180 SAT Reasoning Test score, and participation in extracurricular activities. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...
