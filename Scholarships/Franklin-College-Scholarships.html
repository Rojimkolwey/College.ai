Franklin College Scholarships


Admissions Award
Scholarship for entering undergraduate students at Franklin College. Applicants must have academic potential or financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Ben Franklin Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at Franklin College. Applicants must rank in the top 5% of high school class, and have a minimum 1920 SAT or 29 ACT score. Number of awards may vary. Award amount is full-tuition. Renewable. more...

Distinguished Dean's Scholarship - Franklin College
The Distinguished Dean's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Franklin College. You must be in the top 30% of your high school graduating class, have a minimum ACT score of 22 and a minimum SAT score of 1530 to be eligible for this award. more...

Distinguished Deans Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at Franklin College. Applicants must rank in the top 10% of high school class and have a minimum 1760 SAT or 26 ACT score. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Key Scholarship
Scholarship for entering undergraduate students at Franklin College. Awarded to students under-represented in the Franklin College population. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount varies up to full-tuition. Renewable. more...

Pulliam School Of Journalism Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at Franklin College majoring in Journalism or a related field. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
