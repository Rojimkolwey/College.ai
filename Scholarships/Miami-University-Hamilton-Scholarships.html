Miami University Hamilton Freshman Merit Award
The Miami University Hamilton Freshman Merit Award is available to incoming freshman students at Miami University Hamilton. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.25 and minimum ACT score of 22 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...
