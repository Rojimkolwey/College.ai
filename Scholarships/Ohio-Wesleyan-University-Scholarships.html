Ohio Wesleyan University Scholarships


Child-Spouse Of Clergy Award
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University whose spouse or parent is an ordained Methodist Minister. Applicant must be pursuing their first undergraduate degree. Renewable. Number of awards may vary. more...

Deans Awards
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University. Applicants must have outstanding academic skills, leadership potential, a minimum SAT score of 1100 or ACT score of 24 and rank in the top 20% of graduating class. Award amount varies from $5,000 to one-half tuition. Renewable. more...

Diversity Awards
Scholarship for African-American, Asian/Pacific Islander, American-Indian, Alaskan Native, or Latino students who are entering first-year freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University. Applicants must have a minimum SAT score of 1100 or ACT score of 24, 3.5 GPA, and graduate in the top 10% of class. Renewable. more...

Godman Award
Scholarship for Ohio residents who are incoming freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University. Number of awards may vary. more...

Helen Crider Smith Women As Leaders Scholarship
Scholarship for female incoming freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University. Applicant must have outstanding academic achievement and demonstrated leadership. Award amount covers full-tuition. Renewable. more...

Legacy Grants
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University who are children, step-children, grandchildren, or step-grandchildren of OWU alumni. Number of awards may vary. more...

Meek Community Service & Leadership Awards
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University who have a strong background in community service. Renewable. more...

Regional Awards
Scholarship for freshmen students admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University. Applicants must be American students residng outside Ohio. Number of awards may vary. more...

The Schimmel Scholarship
Scholarship for female incoming freshmen admitted to Ohio Wesleyan University. Applicants must have outstanding academic achievement and demonstrated leadership. Award amount covers tuition, room, and board. Renewable. more...
