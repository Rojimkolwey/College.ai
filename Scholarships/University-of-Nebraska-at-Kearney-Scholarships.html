University of Nebraska at Kearney Scholarships


Board Of Regents' Scholarship - Unk
The Board of Regents' Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. You must be a graduate from a Nebraska high school to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Chancellor's Scholarship - Unk
The Chancellor's Scholarship is available to incoming, full-time freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. more...

Cooperating School Scholarship - Unk
The Cooperating School Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. You must graduate from a school participating in UNK's teacher preparation program through the College of Education, have a minimum 23 ACT / 1050 SAT score, and rank in the top 30% of your graduating class to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Davis-Chambers Scholarship
The Davis Scholarship is available to incoming minority freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. You must be a resident of Nebraska and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Unk
The Dean's Scholarship is available to incoming, full-time freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. more...

Nebraska Legacy Scholarship - Unk
The Nebraska Legacy Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. You must be the natural born or legally adopted child of a University of Nebraska graduate to be eligible for this renewable award. Additionally, you must have a minimum 24 ACT (1100 SAT) score and rank in the top 33% of your class. more...

Non-Resident Scholarship - Unk
The Non-Resident Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. You must be a resident of a state other than Nebraska to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Unk Multicultural Community Service Scholarship
The UNK Multicultural Community Service Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Nebraska, Kearney. You must help create multicultural student groups and participate in multicultural and campus-wide activities and organizations to be eligible for this renewable award. more...
