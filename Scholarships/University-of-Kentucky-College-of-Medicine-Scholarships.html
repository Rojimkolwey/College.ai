University of Kentucky Scholarships


Academic Scholarships (A)
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at the University of Kentucky. Applicants must have a minimum ACT score of 28 or a minimum SAT score of 1240 and a minimum 3.3 GPA. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. more...

Academic Scholarships (B)
Scholarship for current undergraduate or transfer students at the University of Kentucky. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and completed a minimum of 12 credit hours. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. more...

Blackhurst Student Research Fund
The Blackhurst Student Research Fund is open to graduate students at the University of Kentucky, School of Education. You must be enrolled in the Department of Special Education and Rehabilitation Counseling to qualify for this award. You must also be conducting research as part of a master's thesis, educational specialist degree research project or doctoral dissertation to qualify for this award. more...

C. Michael Nelson Endowed Graduate Fellowship
The C. Michael Nelson Endowed Graduate Fellowship is open to graduate students at the University of Kentucky, College of Education. You must be majoring in one of the emotional and behavioral disorder programs and be conducting research to qualify for this award. more...

College Of Agriculture-First Year Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to the University of Kentucky who are enrolling in the College of Agriculture. Selection criteria varies. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Commonwealth Scholars Program
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at the University of Kentucky who are involved in the schools debate program. Selection criteria varies. Due date, number of awards and awards amount may vary. Renewable. Contact the director of the Debate Program for more information. more...

Csems Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time juniors, seniors, or graduate students attending the University of Kentucky who are majoring in Computer Science, Comupter Technology, Engineering, Engineering Technology or Mathematics. Must have a minimum 3.25 GPA and financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Uk College Of Dentistry
The Dean's Scholarship is available to students entering the University of Kentucky, College of Dentistry. You must demonstrate prior academic achievement and achievement on the Dental Admission Test to be eligible for this award. A personal interview is required. more...

Debate Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at the University of Kentucky who are involved in the schools debate program. Selection criteria varies. Due date, number of awards and awards amount may vary. Contact the director of the Debate Program for more information. more...

Donovan Scholarship In Gerontology
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate and graduate students attending the University of Kentucky who are majoring in Gerontology. Selection based on academic achievements and career goals. Nonrenewable. more...

Legacy Tuition Program-Alumni Club Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Kentucky. Awards are administered by the individual alumni clubs and have varying requirements. Contact the local club for more information and/or application. more...

Legacy Tuition Program-Association Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Kentucky who are student members of the Alumni Association and are dependendts of UK alumni and live out of state. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and a minimum ACT score of 22. Due date varies. more...

Medical Alumni Association Scholarship - Ukmc
The Medical Alumni Association Scholarship is available to first - year students at the University of Kentucky College of Medicine. You must demonstrate academic excellence and be a resident of Kentucky to be eligible for this award. more...

Need-Based (B)

Need-Based Scholarships (A)
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at the University of Kentucky. Applicants must have a minimum ACT score of 28 or a minimum SAT score of 1240 and have financial need. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. more...

Otis A. Singletary Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at the University of Kentucky who are involved in the schools debate program. Applicants must have a minimum 3.3 GPA, and minimum ACT score of 28 or SAT score of 1240. Due date, number of awards and awards amount may vary. Contact the director of the Debate Program for more information. more...

Otis A. Singletary Scholarship
The Otis A. Singletary Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at the University of Kentucky. You must have a minimum 3.8 GPA and at least a 33 ACT or 1450 SAT score to be considered for this award. Selection is based on academic performance, leadership abilities, and the potential for being a leader in an academic field. more...

Presidential Scholarship - University Of Kentucky
The Presidential Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at the University of Kentucky. You must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and at least a 31 ACT or 1390 SAT score. Selection is based on academic performance, leadership abilities, and the potential of being a leader in an academic field. more...

Provost Scholarship - University Of Kentucky
The Provost Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at the University of Kentucky. You must have a minimum ACT score of 26 or SAT of 1230 and achieve a 3.30 unweighted high school GPA on a 4.0 scale to be eligible for this award. This award may be renewed for four years. more...

Susan Mcevoy Scholarship
The Susan McEvoy Scholarship is available to second-, third- and fourth-year students at the University of Kentucky, College of Dentistry. You must demonstrate outstanding clinical skills with a focus on general restorative dentistry to be eligible for this award. An essay and interview are required. more...

University Of Kentucky Bluegrass Spirit Scholarship
The University of Kentucky Bluegrass Spirit Scholarship is open to entering non-resident freshmen at the University of Kentucky. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and either minimum ACT score of 25 or SAT score of 1200 to be eligible for this award. more...

Women's Medical Scholarship
The Women's Medical Scholarship is available to third - year students at the University of Kentucky College of Medicine. Selection based on academic performance and financial need. more...
