Scholarship for undergraduate (freshmen ineligible) students enrolled full-time at Dalton State College. Students must have a minimum 2.75 GPA, have completed a minimum of 24 credit hours. Preference given to non-traditional students majoring in Math, Physics, Biology, or Chemistry, or Middle/Secondary Education majors who wish to teach in those fields. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...
