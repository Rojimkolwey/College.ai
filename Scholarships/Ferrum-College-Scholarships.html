Ferrum College Scholarships


Academic Achievement Scholarships
Scholarship for entering freshmen or transfer students enrolling at Ferrum College. Applicants must have a 3.0-3.39 CGPA and a minimum 1000 SAT. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Academic Scholarships For Returning Students
Scholarship for returning full-time undergraduate students attending Ferrum College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Anna Fowler & Robert Harless Moore Scholar Program
Scholarship for residents of the cities of Altavista, Lynchburg, or Bedford, Virginia or the counties of Campbell, Franklin, Pittsylvania, or Pulaski in Virginia or the counties of Mason, McDowell or Mercer in West Virginia who are entering freshmen at Ferrum College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.3 GPA and a minimum 1000 SAT score. Selection is based on academic ability and demonstrate leadership potential. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Corella & Bertram F. Bonner Foundation Scholars Program
Scholarship for full-time incoming freshmen admitted to Ferrum College. Selection is based on need, class rank, and history of volunteer service. Recipients must stay involved with community service activities. Due date may vary. more...

Ferrum College Tuition Assistance Grant (Fctag)
Scholarship for out-of-state undergraduate students attending Ferrum College. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA, and be a U.S. citizen. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Franklin County Grants
Scholarship for Franklin County, Virginia residents who are full-time undergraduate students attending Ferrum College. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Top Scholar Awards
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Ferrum College. Students must have the highest CGPA in the class. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Virginia Conference Scholarship Program (Employment)
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are United Methodist ministers or diaconal ministers serving an appointment in the Virginia Conference or their dependents attending Ferrum College. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Virginia Conference Scholarship Program (Members)
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are members of the United Methodist Church in the Virginia Conference attending Ferrum College. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...
