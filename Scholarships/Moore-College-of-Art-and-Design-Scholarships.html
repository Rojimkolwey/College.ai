Moore College of Art and Design Scholarships


Early Action Scholarship - Moore College Of Art And Design
The Early Action Scholarship is available to new first - year students at Moore College of Art and Design. You must have completed your admissions application by December 1st and have paid a tuition deposit by May 1st to be eligible for this award. Recipients of this award will also receive priority housing. more...

Early Decision Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time first-year students enrolling at Moore College of Art & Design who choose the early decision option. Selection based on artistic talent and academic achievement. Award gives students priority housing and $500. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Emerging Leader In The Arts Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time first-year students enrolling at Moore College of Art & Design and are selected to participate in the ELA program. Selection based on artistic talent, academic achievement and leadership potential in the Arts. Renewable. more...
