Milligan University Scholarships


Academic Scholarship - Milligan College
The Academic Scholarship is open to incoming freshman and transfer students at Milligan College. Freshmen are selected based on their GPA and ACT/ SAT scores, while transfers are selected based on their previous college GPA. more...

John Willis Drama Scholarship
The John Willis Drama Scholarship is open to students at Milligan College. You must be minoring in theatre arts and/or be active in campus dramatic productions to qualify for this award. more...

Melvin And Rosemary Farmer Scholarship For Asian Students
The Melvin and Rosemary Farmer Scholarship for Asian Students is available to traditional undergraduate students at Milligan College. You must be unmarried and a native of Asia to be eligible for this award. You must also have a minimum 3.0 GPA and a minimum 21 ACT score. more...

Milligan College Grant
The Milligan College Grant is open to students at Milligan College who demonstrate financial need. more...

Nacc Bible Bowl Scholarship - Milligan College
The NACC Bible Bowl Scholarship is open to students at Milligan College. You must be a member of the first or second place Bible Bowl team at the North American Christian Convention. The top 20 individual scorers are also eligible to apply. Recipients must maintain a minimum 2.5 GPA. more...

Patricia Huffine Scholarship
The Patricia Huffine Scholarship is open to students at Milligan College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Preference is given to students who are members of West Union Christian Church in West, Union, IL. more...

Ronald T. And Sylvia M. Roberts Endowed Scholarship
The Ronald T. and Sylvia M. Roberts Endowed Scholarship is open to students at Milligan College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and be a member of a Christian Church or Church of Christ to qualify for this award. more...

Ruth Buchanan Scholarship
The Ruth Buchanan Scholarship is open to students at Milligan College. You must be a Bible studies major and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to qualify for this award. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Milligan College
The Transfer Scholarship is open to students transferring to Milligan College who demonstrate financial need. Selection is based on previous college GPA. more...

W. Edward And Billye Joyce Fine Education Scholarship
The W. Edward and Billye Joyce Fine Education Scholarship is open to students at Milligan College. You must be majoring in education and be a resident of Carter, Washington, Johnson, Sullivan or Unicoi County to qualify for this award. more...
