The University of West Florida Scholarships


C.L. Fountain Family Business Ethics Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at University of West Florida College of Business. Students must demonstrate academic achievement, financial need, community service, and have taken a minimum of one hour in an approved Business Ethics course. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Charles & Fran Switzer Business Ethics Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at University of West Florida College of Business. Students must demonstrate financial need, community service, and have completed a Business Ethics course. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

College Of Business Scholastic Excellence Awards
Scholarship for college juniors or seniors enrolled full-time at University of West Florida College of Business. Students must demonstrate academic achievement and have completed a minimum of 30 semester hours at UWF. Due date and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Colonial Properties Trust Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at University of West Florida College of Business. Students must demonstrate academic achievement. Preference given to students majoring in real estate (finance, marketing, etc.) or a related field. Award is for the cost of an apartment. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Criminal Justice Practitioner Scholarship
Employee-Related awards for criminal justice practitioners who are undergraduate students attending the University of West Florida and are pursuing course related to the profession. Applicants must have at least 12 credit hours completed in CJ coursework and have financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Criminal Justice-Academic Execellence Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending the University of West Florida who are majoring in Criminal Justice. Applicants must have a minimum 3.8 GPA and at least 12 credit hours completed in CJ coursework. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Criminal Justice-Military Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of West Florida who are majoring in Criminal Justice. Students must be active duty military, retired military, or military dependents. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Criminal Justice-Need Based
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending the University of West Florida who are majoring in Criminal Justice. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable. more...
