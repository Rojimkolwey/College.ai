Arizona Podiatric Medicine Program (Apma) Educational Foundation General Scholarship
Scholarship for third- or fourth-year graduate students enrolled full-time at the Midwestern University majoring in Podiatric Medicine. Preference given to students who demonstrate financial need, academic achievement, leadership skills, and podiatric community service. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

George E. Clark Scholarship
Scholarship for Alabama, Mississippi, Tennessee, North Carolina, South Carolina, Louisiana, Kentucky, or Georgia residents who are third- or fourth-year graduate students enrolled full-time at Midwestern University majoring in Podiatric Medicine. Due date may vary. more...

Greater Texas Education Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship for second-, third-, or fourth-year graduate students enrolled full-time at Midwestern University majoring in Podiatric Medicine. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, financial need, demonstrate community service, and be American College of Foot and Ankle Surgeons (ACFAS) student members. Preference given to Texas residents or graduated from a Texas undergraduate school. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Indian Health Service Health Professions Scholarship
Scholarship for Native American or Alaskan Native graduate students enrolled full-time at Midwestern University majoring in Osteopathic Medicine, Podiatric Medicine, Pharmacy, or Physician Assistants. This is a forgiveable loan, students must practice on year for the Indian Health Service for each year of award with a minimum obligation of two years. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...
