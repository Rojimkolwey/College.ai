Webber International University Scholarships


Bright Future Match Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students accepted to Webber International University who are recipients of one of the Bright Future scholarships awarded by the state of Florida. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Business Scholarship
Scholarship for new, incoming students accepted to Webber International University who are not on athletic scholarship. Selection based on academic achievement, recommendations and an essay. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Dockery Presidential Award
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending Webber International University. Selection based on community/school service and financial need. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information. more...

Early Commitment Incentive
Annual incentive for undergraduate students attending Webber International University. Students must get deposits and paperwork finished by early acceptance deadlines. Incentives vary depending on residency. Contact the Admissions Office for more information. more...

Ellison Work Study
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Webber International University who are in an on-campus work-study program. Selection based on need. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. more...

Entrance Exam Award
Scholarship for incoming, full-time, undergraduate students accepted to Webber International University. Applicants must have a minimum SAT score of 1090 or ACT score of 23. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Florida Community College Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time transfer students enrolling at Webber International University who hold an AA or AS degree from a Florida Community College. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

International Student Incentive
Scholarship for international undergraduate students attending Webber International University who refer other international students to the school. Referred student must enroll for the current student to receive the incentive. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Kelly A. Wilson Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Webber International University. Applicants must have overcome personal challenges in their life to be eligible and have financial need. Student must submit an essay on these events. Due date may vary. Renewable. more...

Prostart Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Webber International University who participated in a high school culinary program. Applicants must have a minimum 2.75 GPA and be seeking a degree in Hospitality Business Management. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Samir Sabbagh Scholarship
Scholarship for new, incoming students accepted to Webber International University who will be living on-campus. Selection based on community/school involvement and activities. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact the Admissions Office for more information and/or application. more...

Souhail Sabbagh Grant
Scholarship for non-Florida residents who are full-time, incoming freshmen accepted to Webber International University. Selection based on community and high school service. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Talent & Leadership Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Webber International University. Various scholarships related to talent or leadership abilities. Criteria varies with each award. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...
