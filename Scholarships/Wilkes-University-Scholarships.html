Wilkes University Scholarships


Wilkes Blue And Gold Opportunity Grant
The Wilkes Blue and Gold Opportunity Grant is available to entering transfer students at Wilkes University who are not recipients of the Transfer Merit Scholarship. Selection is based on academic merit. more...

Wilkes Provost Scholarship
The Wilkes Provost Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Wilkes University. Selection is based on academic merit. more...

Wilkes Trustee Scholarship
The Wilkes Trustee Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Wilkes University. Selection is based on academic merit. more...

Wu Commitment Awards
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Wilkes University. Applicants must have a minimum SAT score of 900 or ACT equivalent and graduate in the top 50% of graduating high school class. Students admissions application is reviewed for this award. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Deans Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Wilkes University. Applicants must have a SAT score between 1000 to 1099 or ACT equivalent or rank in the top 20% of graduating high school class. Students admissions application is reviewed for this award. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Leadership Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Wilkes University who hold a postition in one of the schools student publications or office in select organizations on campus. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Multicultural Service Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Wilkes University who can add to the diversity of the campus. Applicants must be participating in at least one campus or community service activity and have financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Named Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Wilkes University. These are endowed scholarships with varying criteria. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Performance Awards
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Wilkes University who are majoring in Theatre Arts, Music Performance, Music Education, or Musical Theatre. Selection based on audition. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Presidential Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Wilkes University. Applicants must have a SAT score between 1100 to 1199 or ACT equivalent. Students admissions application is reviewed for this award. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Scholars Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Wilkes University. Applicants must have a minimum SAT score of 1200 or ACT equivalent. Students admissions application is reviewed for this award. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...

Wu Transfer Student Scholarships
Scholarship for transfer students enrolling at Wilkes University. Applicants must have a minimum of 24 transferable credit hours and 3.0 GPA. Students admissions application is reviewed for this award. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and/or application. more...
