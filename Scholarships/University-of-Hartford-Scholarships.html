University of Hartford Scholarships


Alumni Grant Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Hartford in Connecticut. Applicants must complete the FAFSA and have demonstrated financial need. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Artistic Merit Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Hartfords Hartford Art School in Connecticut. Selection is based on the quality of applicants portfolio and academic achievement. Due date varies. more...

Family Scholarship Program
Scholarship for sisters, brothers, parents or spouse of current undergraduate students, who wish to attend the University of Hartford in Connecticut full-time. Applicants are eligible to receive a 50% tuition award. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Performing Arts Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Hartford in Connecticut full-time majoring in Fine Arts. Applicants must complete excellent academic achievement. Candidates must attend an audition. Award amount is equal to full tuition. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

University Of Hartford-Hartford Art School Scholarships
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Hartford-Hartford Art School. Students must have a minimum 1000 SAT score, a B average, a minimum of 16 Carnegie units of academics, and be an on-campus resident. Due date may vary. A FAFSA and a statement of purpose must be included with application packet. Renewable. Contact the Art Academys admissions office or Scholastic for more information and/or application. more...
