Maryville University of Saint Louis Scholarships


Academic Promise Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Maryville University full-time. Selection criteria based on a minimum ACT score of 24 or SAT score of 1010, and minimum 3.3 GPA. Number of awards may vary. Renewable; maintain 3.2 GPA. more...

Art & Design Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen or transfer students accepted to Maryville University majoring in Fine Arts or Interior Design. Selection criteria based on minimum 3.0 GPA, minimum SAT score of 880 or ACT score of 21, letter of recommendation and portfolio review. Number of awards may vary. more...

Art And Design Scholarship Program - Maryville University Of St. Louis
The Art and Design Scholarship Program is open to incoming freshmen and transfer students who will study art and design at Maryville University of St. Louis. Selection is based on your GPA, test scores and a portfolio review. more...

Leadership Activity Award-Freshmen
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Maryville University full-time. Selection criteria based on 3.0 GPA, ACT score of 21 or SAT score of 880, two letters of recommendation, and demonstrated leadership in at least two high school or community activities. Award amount for commuter students is $1,200 and $2,400 for full-time. Number of awards may vary. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA and demonstrated leadership in at least two college activities. more...

Leadership Activity Award-Transfer
Scholarship for undergraduate transfer students accepted to Maryville University full-time. Selection criteria based on 3.0 GPA, two letters of recommendation, and demonstrated leadership in at least two high school or community activities. Award amount for commuter students is $1,200 and $2,400 for full-time. Number of awards may vary. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA and demonstrated leadership in at least two college activities. more...

M-Pact Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen students accepted to Maryville University full-time. Selection is based on students potential to make an M-PACT at Maryville University. Students will assist in the enhancement of a vibrant campus life. Each M-PACT scholar is expected to participate in this program for 4 years. Submit activities resume with application packet. Due date varies. Renewable; maintain 2.5 GPA and fulfull service requirements. more...
