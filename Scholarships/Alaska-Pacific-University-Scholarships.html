Alaska Pacific University Scholarships


Alaska Pacific University Alaska Native-Native American Scholarship
Scholarship for Alaskan Native and Native American undergraduate students enrolled at Alaska Pacific University. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date is rolling. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Alaska Resident Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Alaska who are undergraduate students enrolling at Alaska Pacific University in the traditional Campus Undergraduate Program. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount is up to $2,000 per semester. Renewable for up to a total of four years. more...

Amu-Apu Alumni Association Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate or graduate students at Alaska Pacific University. Undergraduate applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and graduate students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Selection is also based on financial need, extracurricular activities, and honors/awards received. Preference given to family members of alumni. An essay must be included with application packet. Due date is rolling. Number of awards and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Anchorage Amateur Radio Club Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Alaska Pacific University pursuing studies in Science, Mathematics, Communications, Radio, Electronics or Astronomy. Selection based on academic achievements, financial need and vocational interest (club participation in related field). Due date is rolling. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable; but recipients may reapply. more...

Anchorage Daily News Scholarship
Annual award for an undergraduate or graduate student enrolled at Alaska Pacific University who will be pursuing a career in Business. Due date is rolling. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Apu Foreign Graduate Student Scholarship
Scholarship for international students who are graduates of Alaska Pacific University and are pursuing graduate studies at APU. Due date is rolling. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Selection based on academic achievement and career goals. Due date is rolling. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable. more...
