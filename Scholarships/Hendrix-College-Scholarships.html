Hendrix College Scholarships


Academic Scholarship - Hendrix College
The Academic Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Hendrix College. This award is based on your GPA, standardized test scores, leadership, recommendations, and extracurricular activities. more...

Fine Arts Performance Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen who are enrolling at Hendrix College freshmen and are majoring in or participate in the schools music, theatre or art programs. Selection based on audition or portfolio, interview, recommendations, academic performance, test scores, and extracurricular activities. Number of awards may vary. Contact the Office of Admissions or Fine Arts Department for more information and/or applications. more...

Hays Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Hendrix College. Applicants must have a 3.6 GPA in college preparatory classes, a minimum ACT score of 32 or SAT of 1410. Selection is based on academic performance, test scores, leadership, extracurricular activities, recommendations. Finalists will be invited to interview. Award amount up to full fees. Renewable. more...

Hendrix Scholarship For Arkansas Governors Distinguished Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Hendrix College who are Arkansas Governors Distinguished Scholars. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA in college preparatory classes, a minimum ACT score of 32 or SAT of 1410 or are National Merit Finalists or National Achievement Scholars. Selection is based on academic performance, test scores, leadership, extracurricular activities, recommendations, and an interview. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Hendrix-Lilly Service Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Hendrix College. Selection is based on participation/involvement in volunteer services. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Ib Diploma Recognition Award - Hendrix College
The IB Diploma Recognition Award is available to entering freshmen at Hendrix College. You must be an IB diploma candidate to be eligible for this award. more...

National Merit Finalists, National Achievement Finalists, & National Hispanics Finalists Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen entering Hendrix College who are National Merit Finalists, National Achievement Finalists or National Hispanic Finalists. Students must list Hendrix College as their first college choice. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Odyssey Honors & Distinction Awards
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Hendrix College. Selection is based on achievements outside the classroom. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Odyssey Honors And Distinction Award
The Odyssey Honors and Distinction Award is open to incoming freshmen at Hendrix College. This award is based on your outside class achievements. All students will be automatically considered. more...

Robert & Ruby Priddy Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming undergraduates enrolling at Hendrix College who come from middle income families. Selection based on academic achievement. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Robert And Ruby Priddy Scholarship
The Robert and Ruby Priddy Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Hendrix College. You must be from a middle-income family to be eligible for this award. more...

United Methodist-Umyf Leadership Program
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Hendrix College who are members of the United Methodist Church. Applicants must have leadership experience in local and regional United Methodist Youth ministries, and demonstrate potential for Christian leadership on the Hendrix campus. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
