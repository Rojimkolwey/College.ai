Mount Mercy University Scholarships


Busse Grant
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Mount Mercy College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Distinguished Honor Scholarship
Scholarship for transfer students enrolling at Mount Mercy College. Applicants must have between 3.0 - 3.49 transfer GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Distinguished Honor Scholarship (Freshman)
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Mount Mercy College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 930 or ACT score of 20. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Presidential Scholarship (Freshman)
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Mount Mercy College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1160 or ACT score of 26. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Presidential Scholarship (Transfer)
Scholarship for transfer students enrolling at Mount Mercy College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 transfer GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Mt. Mercy University
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Mount Mercy University. You must have a minimum GPA of 4.0, have at least a 30 ACT / 1360 SAT score, OR rank number one in your graduating class to be eligible for this award. more...

Sister Mary Ildephonse Holland Scholarship
The Sister Mary Ildephonse Holland Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Mount Mercy University. You must have a minimum 3.7 GPA and at least a 26 ACT / 1230 SAT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Social Work Awards
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors or transfer students enrolling at Mount Mercy College who are majoring in Social Work. Selection based on minimum 2.5 GPA, ability, talent and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Speech-Drama Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors or transfer students enrolling at Mount Mercy College who will be participating in the schools speech or drama program. Selection based on minimum 2.5 GPA, talent and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Vocal Music Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors or transfer students enrolling at Mount Mercy College who have vocal music talent. Selection based on minimum 2.5 GPA, audition, ability, talent and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
