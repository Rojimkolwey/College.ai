University of Maryland, Baltimore Scholarships


Mantech Scholarship
The ManTech Scholarship is available to graduate students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and be active military personnel to be considered for this award. more...

Mark Gerencser Endowed Scholarship Fund
The Mark Gerencser Endowed Scholarship Fund is available to students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must have completed 15 undergraduate credits at UMUC, have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. Preference given to students who have incurred financial difficulty due to the death, or disability of a parent or financial supporter. more...

Marriott Foundation Scholarship
The Marriott Foundation Scholarship is available to undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Orkand Scholars Program
The Orkand Scholars Program is available to students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must have completed 90 credits, have a minimum 3.75 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Umuc
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Ralph Hiteshew Memorial Scholarship
The Ralph Hiteshew Memorial Scholarship is available to graduate students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must be enrolled in the management information systems track of the master of science in management program and have completed at least nine graduate credits. Also, you must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Roscoe E. Dann Sr. Memorial Scholarship

The Joseph And Mary Ellen Hrutka Scholarship
The Joseph and Mary Ellen Hrutka Scholarship is available to students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must have completed 15 undergraduate credits at UMUC, have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

The Working Family Scholarship Fund
The Working Family Scholarship Fund is available to students at the University of Maryland, University College. You must be working full-time, have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...
