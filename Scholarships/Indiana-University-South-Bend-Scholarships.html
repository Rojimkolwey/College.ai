Indiana University-South Bend Scholarships


Articulation Grant For Graduate Students - Iusb
The Articulation Grant for Graduate Students is open to graduate students at Indiana University, South Bend. You must be a resident of southwest Michigan to be considered for this award. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Charles W. Cole Scholarship
The Charles W. Cole Scholarship is open to students at Indiana University, South Bend. You must be a resident of St. Joseph County or one of the adjacent counties, be pursuing a baccalaureate degree, demonstrate financial need and rank in the top 10 percent of your high school class to be considered for this award. more...

Civil Rights Heritage Center Community Service Award
The Civil Rights Heritage Center Community Service Award is open to students at Indiana University, South Bend. You must demonstrate a understanding of the values of personal responsibility and commitment to community serviceto be considered for this award. more...

Crowe Chizek And Company Accounting Scholarship
The Crowe Chizek and Company Accounting Scholarship is open to accounting majors at IU South Bend. You must enroll at IU South Bend for the following fall semester to be eligible for this award. more...

Iu South Bend Chancellor Merit Award
The IU South Bend Chancellor Merit Award is open to students at Indiana University, South Bend. You must be a resident of a state other than Indiana to be eligible for this award. more...

Iu South Bend Elkhart County Scholarship
The IU South Bend Elkhart County Scholarship is open to students at Indiana University, South Bend. You must be a resident of Elkhart County, be pursuing a degree, and be in good academic standing to be eligible for this award. more...

Walt Risler Scholarship
The Walt Risler Scholarship is open to criminal justice majors at IU South Bend. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA in criminal justice core courses, have completed at least 40 credit hours, and submit an essay to be considered for this award. more...
