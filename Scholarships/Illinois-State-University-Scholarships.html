Illinois State University Scholarships


Anna Ropp Scholarship Fund
The Anna Ropp Scholarship Fund is available to students attending Illinois State University - Mennonite College of Nursing. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to a non-traditional nursing student in their junior or senior year, or participating in the RN/BSN or equivalent program or graduate students who demonstrate concern for body, mind and spiritual aspects of nursing. more...

Clara Gerhart Endowed Scholarship
The Clara Gerhart Endowed Scholarship is available to undergraduate and graduate students attending Illinois State University - Mennonite College of Nursing. more...

Colwell Outstanding Pre-Medical Student Award
The Colwell Outstanding Pre- medical Student Award is available to seniors at Illinois State University- College of Arts and Sciences. You must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and have completed the MCAT to be eligible for this award. In addition, you must demonstrate outstanding achievement and personal qualities needed to become a competent, dedicated, and compassionate physician. more...

Edwin Niemi Metals Scholarship
The Edwin Niemi Metals Scholarship is available to undergraduate and graduate students attending Illinois State University- College of Fine Arts. You must have a concentration in metals and demonstrate strong artistic ability through the submission of a portfolio to be eligible for this award. more...

Nims Associates Award
The Nims Associates Award is available to full- time juniors and seniors attending Illinois State University- College of Business. You must be enrolled in the business information systems sequence in accountancy and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...
