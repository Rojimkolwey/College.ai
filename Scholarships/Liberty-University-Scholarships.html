Liberty University Scholarships


Academic Achievement Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors and transfer students attending Liberty University. Eligibility based on SAT/ACT scores and high school cumulative GPA or college GPA if more than 24 hours. Renewable. more...

Academic Honors Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Liberty University. Student must have a minimum 1270 SAT or 29 ACT, and a minimum 3.5 GPA. Students must also apply to Honors Program before April 16. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Athletic Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors and transfer students who have been accepted at Liberty University. Applicant must participate in specific university sports. Award amount varies. Contact the Athletic Department for more information and/or application. more...

Athletic Training Education Scholarship
Scholarship for juniors or seniors attending Liberty University who are enrolled in the Athletic Training Education Program. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and maintain current certification in CPR/AED training. Award amount and number of awards may vary. more...

Awana Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Liberty University who are recipients of the Awana Citation Plaque. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Early Deposit Award
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors and transfer students accepted to Liberty University. Students who submit a $250 deposit on or before specific due dates set by the university receive an additional credit applied to the first years tuition. Earliest due date is December 15 with largest tuition credit amount of $1,000. Tuition credit amounts decrease with subsequent due dates. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information. more...

Faith Christian Academy Scholarship
Scholarship for graudates of Faith Christian Academy accepted Liberty University. Applicants must have attended FCA for four years. Award is for 75% tuition discount. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

International Missions Board (Imb) Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Liberty University. Based on financial need and matching funds from students parents. Recipients must serve as a Prayer Warrior, Prayer Leader or a Spiritual Life Director on campus. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Liberty Baptist Fellowship
Annual fellowship for undergraduate students attending Liberty University. Applicants must be members of a church. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Liberty Champion Award
The LIberty Champion Award is available to entering freshmen at Liberty University. This award will be renewed for all four years of study, with the award amount increasing each year. more...

Liberty Champion Scholarship
Scholarship for undergradaute students attending Liberty University. Student must be in good academic standing. Award graduates over each year. Transfer and re-admitted students will get first year scholarship. 5+ year seniors are not eligible. more...

Liberty Christian Academy Scholarship
Scholarship for graduates from Liberty Christian Academy and are enrolling at Liberty University. Selection based on academic achievement and character. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Middle America Scholarship
The Middle America Scholarship is available to full time resident undergraduate students at Liberty University. You must have verified FAFSA with an EFC between 1 and 18,000 and U.S. citizen to be eligible for this ward. more...

National Merit Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Liberty University. Students must have been named finalists or semi-finalists by the National Merit Foundation and be accepted to the Honors Program. Full tuition, room and board to finalist. Full tuition to semi-finalst. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to transferring students to Liberty University. You must be coming directly from a two-year college as a resident undergraduate student from the school in which they were inducted into PTK to be eligible for this award. more...

Southern Baptist Conservatives Of Virginia Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors and transfer students accepted to Liberty University. Applicants must be a member of a Southern Baptist Conservative of Virginia (SBCV) church. Church pastor must submit letter of selection to the university. Renewable. more...

Teen Mania Scholarship
Scholarship for transfer students from Teen Manias Honor Academy enrolling at Liberty University. Selection based on academic achievement and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Word Of Life Bible Institute 2
Scholarship for transfer students from the Word of Life Bible Institute enrolling at Liberty University. Applicants have attended WOLBI for two years and have a minimum 2.5 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Word Of Life Bible Institute I
Scholarship for transfer students from the Word of Life Bible Institute enrolling at Liberty University. Applicants have attended WOLBI for one year and have a minimum 2.5 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Word Of Life Church Ministries Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Liberty University. Applicants must be recipients of either the Childrens Steadfast Award or the Cre8tive Discipleship Completion. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Word Of Life Scholarship
