SUNY Morrisville Scholarships


Camera Ready Award
The Camera Ready Award is available to students at SUNY Morrisville State College. You must be a journalism student and demonstrate achievement in layout, design and photography to be eligible for this award. more...

Caterpillar / Syracuse Supply Scholarship
The Caterpillar / Syracuse Supply Scholarship is available to students at SUNY Morrisville State College. You must be a first- or second-year student majoring in agricultural engineering or diesel technology to be eligible for this award. more...

Dairy Club Award - Suny At Morrisville
The Dairy Club Award is open to incoming freshman students at the State University of New York, Morrisville State College. You must be in involved in dairy activities on local and state levels to be eligible for this award. Selection is based on involvement in activities, leadership and career potential in the dairy industry. more...

Doug L. Youngs Memorial Award
The Doug L. Youngs Memorial Award is open to journalism students at the State University of New York, Morrisville State College. You must be a senior to qualify for this award. more...

New York State Flower Industries Association Award
The New York State Flower Industries Association Award is available to students at SUNY Morrisville State College. You must be a senior horticulture student planning to enter the flower industry to be eligible for this award. more...

R. Bruce Wise Memorial Scholarship
The R. Bruce Wise Memorial Scholarship is open to seniors at the State University of New York, Morrisville State College in the accounting program. You must have completed your freshman year at Morrisville and be registered for the senior year to qualify for this award. more...

Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Award - Suny Morrisville
The Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute Award is open to seniors at the State University of New York, Morrisville State College. You must be studying engineering science to qualify for this award. Selection is based on academic excellence. more...

Taze Huntley Memorial Scholarship
The Taze Huntley Memorial Scholarship is open to first-year and second-year students at the State University of New York, Morrisville State College. You must demonstrate good citizenship, academic merit, campus involvement and good character to qualify for this award. more...

Thomas V. Ciarrocchi Scholarship
The Thomas V. Ciarrocchi Scholarship is open to students at the State University of New York, Morrisville State College. You must demonstrate academic achievement, good moral character, leadership and interest in a food service vocation to qualify for this award. more...
