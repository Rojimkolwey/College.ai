Keuka College Scholarships


American Baptist Scholarship - Keuka College
The American Baptist Scholarship is available students at Keuka College. You must be a member of an American Baptist Church and submit a letter of recommendation from your church pastor to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable for up to four years with a maintained 2.5 GPA and maintained residence in on-campus housing. more...

Board Of Trustees Scholarship
The Board of Trustees Scholarship is available to freshmen students at Keuka College. You must have a GPA between 3.5 and 4.0 to qualify for this award. more...

Keuka 911 Service Scholarship
The Keuka 911 Service Scholarship is available to full-time students at Keuka College who are recipients of the World Trade Center Memorial Scholarship. more...

Keuka Grant
The Keuka Grant is available to students at Keuka College. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Keuka Transfer Scholarship
The Keuka Transfer Scholarship is available to transfer students at Keuka College. You must have a GPA between 3.25 and 3.49 to qualify for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Award
The Phi Theta Kappa Award is available to full-time, transfer students at Keuka College. You must be a current member of Phi Theta Kappa to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Achievement Award
The Transfer Achievement Award is available to full-time, transfer students at Keuka College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be eligible for this award. more...
