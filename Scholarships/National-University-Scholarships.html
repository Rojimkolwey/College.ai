National University Scholarships


Collegiate Honor Award - National University
The Collegiate Honor Award is open to students at National University. You must have completed at least 54 semester units at a US regionally accredited community college or four - year college with a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Tuition Scholarship - National University
The Presidential Tuition Scholarship is open to students at National University who demonstrate high financial need. You must have completed at least 56 credits with a minimum 2.3 GPA, be a U.S. citizen or eligible non - citizen, be working towards your first undergraduate degree, and have an annual income of no more than $22,000 for married and $15,000 for single applicants. You must also be either African-American, Hispanic or Native American, or be a single parent or student with a certified disability. more...

Promising Scholar Award
The Promising Scholar Award is available to transfer students at National University. You must be enrolling directly from a California Community College to be eligible for this award. more...
