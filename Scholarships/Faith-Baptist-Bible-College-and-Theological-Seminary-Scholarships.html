Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary Scholarships


Christian S. Thomsen Grant
The Christian S. Thomsen Grant is available to new students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must demonstrate Christian character and conduct and submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid to be eligible for this award. Special consideration is given to students who are interested in missions. more...

Christian Workers Grant - Faith Bbc&Ts
The Christian Workers Grant is available to new students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must be the dependent student of a full- time Christian worker, submit a Free Application for Federal Student Aid and live on campus to be eligible for this award. more...

Earl And Shirley Dannenberg Mk Student Grant
The Earl and Shirley Dannenberg MK Student Grant is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must demonstrate potential for missions to be eligible for this award. more...

Far Eastern Missionary Grant
The Far Eastern Missionary Grant is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must be seeking to enter a missionary ministry in Asia, preferably going out under Baptist Mid-Missions. You must also have completed at least one semester at FBBC&TS to be eligible for this award. more...

Graham Student Missionary Grant
The Graham Student Missionary Grant is available to new students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must be enrolled in the missions program and be seeking to enter a missionary ministry. You must also demonstrate potential for missions work to be eligible for this award. more...

Great Scripture Memorization
The Great Scripture Memorization Grant is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must be able to perfectly quote a designated Scripture passage to be eligible for this award. more...

Herbert Brudtkuhl Scholarship
The Herbert Brudtkuhl Scholarship is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must be currently working full or part time in the FBBC&TS maintenance department and have worked at least 200 hours during the previous 12 months. You must also be a sophomore or above and demonstrate Christian character and conduct to be eligible for this award. more...

Husband And Father's Legacy Scholarship
The Husband and Fathers Legacy Scholarship is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must be a married male student with young children and be returning as a sophomore or above. You must also plan to go into full-time Christian Service, have completed at least two years of study at FBBC&TS and demonstrate Christian character and conduct to be eligible for this award. more...

Irma Schwarck Grant
The Irma Schwarck Grant is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must plan to enter full-time specialized Christian ministry such as pastor, youth worker, missionary or Christian school. You must also demonstrate Christian character and conduct to be eligible for this award. more...

Robert E. Moritz Scholarship
The Robert E. Moritz Scholarship is available to students at Faith Baptist Bible College and Theological Seminary. You must demonstrate Christian character and conduct, financial need and potential for missions work to be eligible for this award. more...
