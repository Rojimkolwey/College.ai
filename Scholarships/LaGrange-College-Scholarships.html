LaGrange College Scholarships


Clark And Ruby Baker Scholarship
The Clark and Ruby Baker Scholarship is available to students at LaGrange College. You must be a Georgia resident and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students from rural or small towns. more...

Margaret Adger Pitts Scholarship
The Margaret Adger Pitts Scholarship is available to students at LaGrange College. You must be a dependent of an ordained United Methodist minister, appointed by the Bishops of the North and South Georgia Conferences to be eligible for this award. more...

Rosa Wright Boyd Scholarship
The Rosa Wright Boyd Scholarship is available to students at LaGrange College. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Traylor Scholarship
The Traylor Scholarship is available to students at LaGrange College. You must be a graduate of the Troup County School System to be eligible for this award. more...

United Methodist Foundation Scholarship - Lagrange College
The United Methodist Foundation Scholarship is available to full-time students at LaGrange College. You must demonstrate financial need, be a full and active member of the United Methodist Church for at least one year and be a U.S citizen to be eligible for this award. more...

United Methodist General Scholarship - Lagrange College
The United Methodist General Scholarship is available to full-time students at LaGrange College. You must demonstrate financial need, be a full and active member of the United Methodist Church for at least one year and have a minimum "B" average during the year prior to be eligible for this award. more...

Virginia Speegle Kyle Scholarship
The Virginia Speegle Kyle Scholarship is open to students at LaGrange College. You must demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

William And Estelle Jones Culpepper Scholarship
The William and Estelle Jones Culpepper Scholarship is available to seniors at LaGrange College. You must demonstrate academic excellence and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

William Henry Belk Scholarship
The William Henry Belk Scholarship is open to students at LaGrange College. You must be a member of the Presbyterian Church or a graduate of Rabun-Gap Nacoochee School, and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

The William Luther McMinn Jr. Scholarship is open to students at LaGrange College. This award is based on academic promise and financial need. more...
