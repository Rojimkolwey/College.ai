Doctor Of Ministry Grants

Dr. Floyd Shacklock Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students enrolled at Pacific School of Religion. Students must demonstrate academic achievement, Christian service, and a commitment to parish or educational ministry. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Harland E. Hogue Fellowship For Excellence In Ministry
Annual Fellowships for graduate students enrolled at Pacific School of Religion. Students must be Master of Divinity candidates, demonstrate a commitment to ministry in the church, promise of intellectual and spiritual growth. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Loan Forgiveness Fund Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students enrolled at Pacific School of Religion. Students must be completeing the Master of Divinity degree, have financial need, and demonstrate promise for ministry. This is a forgiveable loan; award is forgiven at 20% per year after graduation as long as the student remains in full-time parish ministry. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
