Cu Boulder Arts & Humanities Achievement Scholarship
The CU Boulder Arts & Humanities Achievement Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at the University of Colorado-Boulder who are non-residents. To be considered for this renewable award, you must have a minimum weighted GPA of 3.85. Additionally, you must major in one of the following areas: Art History, Asian Studies, Chinese, Classics, Dance, English, Cinema Studies and Moving Image Arts, French, German Studies, History, Humanities, Italian, Japanese, Jewish Studies, Philosophy, Religious Studies, Russian Studies, Spanish, Art Practices, or Theatre. Selection is based on academic merit, personal background, service/employment activities, and responsibilities. more...
