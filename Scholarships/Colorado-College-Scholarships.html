Colorado College Scholars Scholarships
Scholarship for residents of Arizona, Colorado, New Mexico, Oklahoma and Texas who are first-year undergraduate students attending Colorado College full-time. Applicants must have families with an adjusted gross income less than $150,000 annually. Selection is based on admissions application. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Crown-Goodman Presidential Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate senior students attending Colorado College full-time. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need, significant student-loan debt, and have demonstrated an impressive record of achievement and academic success. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Otis A. & Margaret T. Barnes Chemistry Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Colorado College, majoring in Chemistry. Applicants must be a U.S. citizen. Submit a letter describing background in science and reasons for choosing Chemistry major; photocopy of Colorado College application, transcripts and test scores; and letters of recommendation from three high school teachers (at least one must be from a chemistry teacher) to the Chairman of the Department of Chemistry. Award is for full-tuition (approximately $90,000). Renewable; must meet specific requirements. more...
