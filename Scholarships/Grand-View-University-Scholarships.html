Grand View University Scholarships


Dean's Scholarship- Grand View University
The Dean's Scholarship is open to full-time entering freshmen at Grand View University. You must meet two of the following criteria to be eligible: have a minimum 24 ACT; rank in the top 20 percent of your high school class; or have a minimum 3.5 GPA. more...

Director's Award- Grand View University
The Director's Award is open to full-time entering freshmen at Grand View University. You must meet two of the following criteria to be eligible: have a minimum 21 ACT; rank in the top 50 percent of your high school class; or have a minimum 3.0 GPA with a minimum ACT score of 17. more...

Grand View Grant
The Grand View Grant is open to full-time entering freshmen at Grand View University. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Honors Scholarship - Grand View College
The Honors Scholarship is open to full-time entering freshmen at Grand View College. You must meet two of the following criteria to be eligible: have a minimum 24 ACT; rank in the top 20 percent of your high school class; or have a minimum 3.5 GPA. This scholarship is renewable provided you maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Grand View University
The Presidential Scholarship is open to full-time entering freshmen at Grand View University. You must meet two of the following criteria to be eligible: have a minimum 28 ACT; rank in the top 10 percent of your high school class; or have a minimum 3.75 GPA. more...

Transfer Director's Scholarship
The Transfer Director's Scholarship is open to full-time transfer students at Grand View University. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Grant - Grand View University
The Transfer Grant is open to full-time transfer students at Grand View University. You must have a GPA that is less than 2.0 to be considered for this award. more...

Transfer Honors Scholarship - Grand View University
The Transfer Honors Scholarship is open to full-time transfer students at Grand View University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Presidential Scholarship - Grand View University
The Transfer Presidential Scholarship is open to full-time transfer students at Grand View University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be eligible for this award. more...

Viking Incentive Program
The Viking Incentive Program is open to full-time entering freshmen at Grand View College. You must have a minimum 21 ACT and rank in the top fifty percent of your high school class or have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be considered for this award. This scholarship is renewable provided you maintain a minimum 2.5 GPA. more...
