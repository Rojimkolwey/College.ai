University of Wisconsin-Stout Scholarships


Sentry Insurance Foundation Student Leadership
The Sentry Insurance Foundation Student Leadership is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must be in the top 15 percent of your graduating class, be involved in extracurricular and community service activities, and be majoring in Applied Math (Actuarial Science)/Computer Science (MIS) to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Shane M. Hopfensperger Scholarship
The Shane M. Hopfensperger Scholarship is available to undergraduate students majoring in construction at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must be a member of the Student Construction Association and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Sigma Lambda Chi Scholarship - Univ. Of Wisconsin, Stout
The Sigma Lambda Chi Scholarship is available to junior and senior students majoring in construction at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Society Of Manufacturing Engineers Scholarship
The Society of Manufacturing Engineers Scholarship is available to students majoring in manufacturing engineering at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Sonja Marie Bengtson Scholarship
The Sonja Marie Bengtson Scholarship is available to full-time students majoring in apparel design/manufacturing at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must be studying abroad during the 2015 - 2016 academic year and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Student Construction Association Scholarship
The Student Construction Association Scholarship is available to students at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must be majoring in construction and be a member of the UW-Stout Student Construction Association to qualify for this award. more...

Terry And Margy Ingram Scholarship
The Terry and Margy Ingram Scholarship is available to students majoring in photography studies at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. more...

William Heller Sr. Scholarship
The William Heller Sr. Scholarship is available to junior and senior packaging majors at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must have a proven interest in flexible packaging to qualify for this award. more...

William R. Lindsay Graphic Arts Scholarship
The William R. Lindsay Graphic Arts Scholarship is available to full-time juniors and seniors majoring in graphic communications management at the University of Wisconsin-Stout. You must have a minimum 2.8 GPA to qualify for this award. more...
