University of Kansas Scholarships


C.L. Clark Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate (college seniors ineligible) students enrolled full-time at the University of Kansas School of Law or Washburn University School of Law. Students must have a minimum 2.5 CGPA, financial need, and be graduates from a Saline County, Kansas high school. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An resume must be included with application packet. A FAFSA must be submitted. Renewable. more...

Elizabeth Miller Watkins Memorial Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for female undergraduate students pursuing studies at the University of Kansas. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. Number of awards may vary. more...

James L. Ogden Memorial Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Kansas State University or the University of Kansas majoring in Civil Engineering. Students must have completed a minimum of 12 hours in the major and have a minimum 2.5 GPA. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Karlin Law Scholarship
The Karlin Law Scholarship is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. You must have graduated from a high school in Kansas and intend to practice law in Kansas to be eligible for this award. more...

Shook, Hardy And Bacon Scholarship
The Shook, Hardy and Bacon Scholarship is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. You must be an article editor or note and comment editor of the Kansas Law Review or an entering law student to be eligible for this award. more...

Thomas H. Harkness Ku Law School Scholarship
The Thomas H. Harkness KU Law School Scholarship is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. Preference will be given to students who are military service veterans. more...

Voss Kansas Law Scholarship Fund
The Voss Kansas Law Scholarship Fund is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. Preference shall be given to students who are Kansas residents and who have demonstrated financial need. more...

W.A. Huxman Scholarship
The W.A. Huxman Scholarship is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. more...

Wal-Mart Legal Diversity Scholarship
The Wal-Mart Legal Diversity Scholarship is open to minority students at the University of Kansas School of Law. more...

Warren D. Andreas Scholarship In Law
The Warren D. Andreas Scholarship in Law is open to second- and third-year students at the University of Kansas School of Law. You must have graduated from a high school in a Kansas community with a population of 15,000 or less to be eligible for this award. more...

Willard G. Widder Scholarship
The Willard G. Widder Scholarship is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. You must demonstrate merit or financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

William And Judy Docking Law Scholarship
The William and Judy Docking Law Scholarship is open to students at the University of Kansas School of Law. more...
