Athletic Grant - Anderson University

The Baptist Scholarship is available to entering freshman students at Anderson University. You must have and maintain a minimum 2.5 GPA to qualify for this award. more...

The Church of God Minister's Scholarship is open to full-time students at Anderson University. You must be the child of a Church of God Minister to be considered for this award. more...

The Founder's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Anderson University. You must have a minimum 1150 SAT / 25 ACT score and a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Honors Program Scholarship
The Honors Program Scholarship is available to students attending Anderson University who are enrolled in the honors program. more...

Out-Of-State Grant - Anderson University
The Out-of-State Grant is available to incoming students at Anderson University who are residents of states other than South Carolina. You must intend to live on campus to qualify for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to transfer students at Anderson University. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa to be eligible for this award. more...

President's Scholarship - Anderson University
The President's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Anderson University. You must have a minimum 1300 SAT / 29 ACT score and a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Provost's Scholarship - Anderson University
