Southern New Hampshire University Scholarships


Alumni Family Scholarship - Shnu
The Alumni Family Scholarship is available to students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must be the dependent child of a SNHU alumnus. more...

Deca Scholarship - Southern New Hampshire University
The DECA Scholarship is available to students at Southern New Hampshire University. One award is given to a freshman who has the highest point total in the participatory, competency-based competition at the New Hampshire DECA Career Development Conference. The recipients of the other awards are chosen by the DECA advisor of each DECA chapter in the state of New Hampshire. more...

Fisher Family Scholarship
The The Fisher Family Scholarship is available to students at Southern New Hampshire University who who have shown a commitment to the academic support services of the university. Selection is based on your commitment to the improvement of the skills, knowledge and competencies needed to successfully complete your collegiate education as demonstrated by the continuous improvement of their academic performance over several semesters. Priority is given to tutors and mentors who have made contributions to the academic support services at the University. more...

Future Business Leaders Of America Scholarship - Snhu
The Future Business Leaders of America Scholarship is available to freshman students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must be selected by the State FBLA Group as the recipient of this award. more...

Henry W. Bloch Scholarship
The Henry W. Bloch Scholarship is available to juniors and seniors at Southern New Hampshire University who are majoring in business. You must demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Priority is given to students who are highly involved in college life and activities. more...

Honors Program Scholarship - Southern New Hampshire University
The Honors Program Scholarship is available to students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must be accepted into the Honors Program to be considered for this award. This scholarship is renewable based on your continued participation in the program. more...

Kappa Chi Scholarship - Southern New Hampshire University
The Kappa Chi Scholarship is available to students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must be a member of the Kappa Chi sorority to qualify for this award. more...

Kappa Delta Phi Scholarship - Snhu
The Kappa Delta Phi Scholarship is open to students at Southern New Hampshire University. Consideration is given to students who are members of the Kappa Delta Phi National Fraternity. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Transfer Scholarship - Snhu
The Phi Theta Kappa Transfer Scholarship is available to full - time transfer students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa and have received an associate's degree to be considered for this award. This scholarship is renewable provided you maintain a minimum 3.0 GPA. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Southern New Hampshire University
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering full - time undergraduate day students at Southern New Hampshire University. Selection for this award is based on your academic record, activities, and leadership. more...

Resident Assistant Scholarship Program
The Resident Assistant Scholarship Program is open to full - time upper-class students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible for this award. more...

Scott Caswell Memorial Scholarship
The Scott Caswell Memorial Scholarship is available to juniors and seniors at Southern New Hampshire University who are enrolled in computer-related majors. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and be a resident of New Hampshire to qualify for this award. more...

Tony Lambert Memorial Fund
The Tony Lambert Memorial Fund is open to juniors at Southern New Hampshire University. You must be majoring in retailing or management to qualify for this award. more...

William S. Green Scholarship
The William S. Green Scholarship is available to junior and senior students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and have conducted yourself in a manner that has both served and brought credit to the university. Financial need may also be considered. more...

Women's Faculty Scholarship - Snhu
The Women's Faculty Scholarship is available to returning undergraduate and graduate students at Southern New Hampshire University. You must demonstrate a commitment to human and environmental rights, economic justice, gender equity and community service. more...
