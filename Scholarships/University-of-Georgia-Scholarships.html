Bernard Ramsey Honors Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Georgia. Students must have a minimum 3.7 GPA a minimum 1400 SAT score or a minimum 31 ACT score and a history of accomplishments and leadership in extracurricular activities. Consideration given only to students who apply to the Foundation Fellowship. An interview is required. Award amount may vary. more...
