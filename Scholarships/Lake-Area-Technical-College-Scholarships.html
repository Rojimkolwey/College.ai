Lake Area Technical College Workforce Scholarship
The Lake Area Technical College Workforce Scholarship is available to students at Lake Area Technical College. With a Workforce Scholarship, a student is eligible to ‘partner’ with a sponsoring company. The partnering company will pay all or part of the student’s tuition and fees, to be determined in advance. In exchange, the student will commit to work for the company after graduation, for a predetermined length of time. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible for this program. more...
