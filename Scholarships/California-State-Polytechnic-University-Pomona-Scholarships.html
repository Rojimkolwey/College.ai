Completing The Dream Scholarship Program
The Sallie Mae Fund’s Completing the Dream Scholarship Program helps diverse students from historically underserved communities complete their post-secondary education.
Students on-track to complete their degree or continuous education program who are facing financial challenges may apply to receive a one-time scholarship of up to $2,500 to help complete their post-secondary education program.
Scholarship applications for AY 2022-23 are being accepted now through 5/31/23 or until all scholarship dollars have been awarded.
By 2024, we’ll award nearly 800 Completing the Dream Scholarships totaling $1.95 million, to help students from underserved communities complete their dream of higher education.

Students must be enrolled at an accredited post-secondary institution or in a continuous education program

Dr Leonard Hochstein Scholarship 2022
Scholarship Overview
Dr. Hochstein has spent many years practicing as a plastic surgeon and welcomes the opportunity to give back to our next generation of physicians. Dr. Leonard Hochstein announces his college scholarship which is geared to university and high school students who are on the route to one day become medical doctors.
Criteria
To apply, you should be a current student at a university in the United States or high school student that will attend university and who is studying to become a medical doctor.
We will award the scholarship based on an essay competition. One winner will be selected based on a creative essay of under 1000 words answering the following:
“Describe an area of medicine that needs drastic improvement and how you would use your medical degree to improve upon that area.”
Amount
One time award of $1,000
Deadline
The deadline to apply for the scholarship is December 15, 2022.
Winner Announced
The winner for the scholarship will be announced January 15, 2023.
How to Apply
Applicant should email their response to the essay question “Describe an area of medicine that needs drastic improvement and how you would use your medical degree to improve upon that area.” in Word Doc format to apply@drleonardhochsteinscholarship.com
Along with the essay, please provide the following information:
Full Name
Phone Number
Address
Email Address
Name of High School and Graduation Date
The University that you are currently enrolled in.
Personal Bio
GPA
