Tennessee Technological University Scholarships


All-Tennessee Academic Team Scholarship - Texas Tech
The All-Tennessee Academic Team Scholarship is available to transfer students at Tennessee Technological University. You must be a U.S. citizen/permanent resident and be a Phi Theta Kappa member chosen by your Tennessee Board of Regents Community College to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Golden Eagle Excellence Scholarship
The Golden Eagle Excellence Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Tennessee Technological University who are members of an underrepresented groups. You must be graduating from a Tennessee high school and be a resident of Tennessee to qualify for this award. more...

Honors Academic Scholarship
The Honors Academic Scholarship is available to out - of - state students enrolled in the Honors Program at Tennessee Technological University. You must have a minimum 26 ACT score and a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. Each semester, recipients of this scholarship must take at least one honors course and agree to contribute at least 60 hours of service to the University. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Texas Tech
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to transfer students at Tennessee Technological University. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and have completed at 60 credit hours at a community college to be eligible for this award. Full membership in Phi Theta Kappa is also required. more...

Tech Transfer Pride Scholarship - Texas Tech
The Tech Transfer Pride Scholarship is available to transfer students at Tennessee Technological University. You must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and have completed at 60 credit hours at a Tennessee community college to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Tennessee Tech Presidential Scholarship
The Tennessee Tech Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Tennessee Technological University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.4 and minimum ACT score of 23 or a minimum SAT score of 1130 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...
