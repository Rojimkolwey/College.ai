Renewable. more...

Annis I. Fowler-Kaden Scholarship
Scholarship for first-time, incoming freshmen enrolled at Black Hills State University, Dakota State University, Northern State University, or the University of South Dakota who are majoring in Elementary Education. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0GPA to be eligible. In addition to admissions application student must submit a Fowler/Kaden application form, an essay (1,000-1,500 words), two letters of recommendation, official high school transcript, and ACT score. Award amount is payable over 2 semesters. Contact the SD Board of Regents for more information and/or application. more...
