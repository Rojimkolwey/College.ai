Central College Scholarships


Central-Transfer Student Scholarship
Scholarship for transfer students enrolling at Central College in Iowa. Award amount is based on combination of students high school and college achievement. Number of awards varies. more...

Communications Studies Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Central College in Iowa, interested in continuing their study (majors and nonmajors) of Communication and/or participation in Communication activities (speech team, yearbook, newspaper, radio). Interview may be required. Number of awards varies. more...

Computer Science & Math Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Central College in Iowa, majoring in Mathematics, Computer Science or Comuter Information Systems. Applicants must have a minimum 3.2 GPA, ACT score of 20, and have financial need. Candidates must demonstrate academic potential and ability. Number of awards varies. more...

Cornerstone Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Central College in Iowa. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, ACT score of 21 or SAT score of 970. Number of awards varies. more...

Cornerstone Scholarship - Central College
The Cornerstone Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Central College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and at least a 20 ACT / 940 SAT score to be considered for this award. more...

Heritage Award - Central College
The Heritage Award is available to entering freshmen at Central College. You must be a member of the Reformed Church in America, be the child of alumni, or have a sibling who is attending or has graduated from Central College to be considered for this award. more...

Iowa Tuition Scholarship
Scholarship for Iowa residents who are undergraduate students attending Central College in Iowa. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need. Candidates must file the FAFSA application prior to July 1st. Number of awards varies. more...

Kuyper Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Central College in Iowa. Applicants must have a minimum 3.75 GPA, ACT score of 28 or SAT score of 1230, or rank #1 in their class. Candidates must attend Scholar Day. Award amount is equal to full tuition. Number of awards varies. more...

Modern Language Scholarship (French, German, Spanish)
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Central College in Iowa, interested in continuing their sudy of language (major or nonmajor) in French, German and/or Spanish. A proficiency exam, oral interview and letter of recommendation are required. Number of awards varies. more...

Out-Of-State Scholatship
Scholarship for undergraduate or transfer students attending Central College in Iowa. Applicants must be out-of-state residents. Contiguous states residents will receive an award of $500; non-contiguous states will receive an award of $1000. Number of awards varies. more...

Pella Rolscreen Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Central College in Iowa. Applicants must have a minimum 3.75 GPA, ACT score of 28 or SAT score of 1230, -or- rank #1 in their class. Candidates must attend Scholar Day. Number of awards varies. more...

Pre-Ministerial Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Central College in Iowa, who have an interest in pursuing a career in the ministry or other church-related vocation. Applicants must demonstrate outstanding academic ability, a record of involvement in school, church and community activities. Submit a personal essay and clergy/minister recommendation with application packet. Number of awards varies. more...

Presidential Community College Scholarship
Scholarship for transfer students enrolling at Central College in Iowa. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and need to have completed 45 semester hours from an accredited two-year institution. Number of awards varies. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Central College
The Presidential Scholarship is open to entering freshmen and transfer students at Central College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and at least a 24 ACT / 1090 SAT score. Transfer students must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and at least 26 credit hours. more...
