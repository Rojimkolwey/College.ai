Alumni Association Undergraduate Fellowship
The Alumni Association Undergraduate Fellowship, valued at more than $11,000, is funded by the Alumni Association to assist and reward a bright and promising undergraduate student at William Paterson University. The Alumni Association Undergraduate Fellow will receive a $7,500 scholarship, textbook reimbursement up to $300 per semester, a stipend of approximately $3,000+ for 15-20 hours per week of employment with the Office of Alumni Relations, and valuable experience in computer applications, research, writing, fundraising management, office administration, and public relations. The successful applicant must be a full-time undergraduate student who will complete their sophomore year by of the end of the current spring semester with at least 24 credits taken at William Paterson University, has a minimum cumulative grade point average of 3.0, strong organizational, written, and verbal communication skills, and satisfactory Alumni Relations Office performance standards. Financial need is not an eligibility criterion; however, this Fellowship may affect eligibility for financial aid from other sources (i.e., through the William Paterson University Office of Financial Aid). Students must be available to work 15-20 hours per week in the Office of Alumni Relations; students who are student teaching, engaged in an internship, or not available during regular business hours are not eligible.
Finalists will be contacted by the Alumni Association selection committee to schedule an in-person interview in August; students wishing to be considered must be available during that period.* more...

Arab American Endowed Scholarship
The Arab American Endowed Scholarship provides support for undergraduate students with financial need who seek to become leaders in their disciplines and contribute to their community. The scholarships are open to full-time incoming freshman, transfer, and matriculated students. Citizenship is not a requirement; students with international or refugee status are eligible.
