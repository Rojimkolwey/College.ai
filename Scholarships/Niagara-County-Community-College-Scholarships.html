Niagara County Community College Scholarships


Alliance Niagara Federal Credit Union Award
The Alliance Niagara Federal Credit Union Award is available to full - time sophomore students at Niagara County Community College and Niagara University. You must be a member of the Alliance Niagara Federal Credit Union and have a minimum GPA of 2.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Dolce-Valvo Scholarship
The Dolce-Valvo Scholarship is available to sophomore students at Niagara County Community College. You must be majoring in nursing, physical education, liberal arts and sciences, humanities, or social sciences and have a minimum GPA of 2.75 to be eligible for this award. more...

E. Doris Chalstrey Smith Memorial Scholarship
The E. Doris Chalstrey Smith Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Niagara County Community College. You must be a Tuscarora resident or a Native American territory resident of the Tuscarora community to be eligible for this award. more...

Eugene And Maria Munk Memorial Scholarship
The Eugene and Maria Munk Memorial Scholarship is available to non - traditional students at Niagara County Community College. You must maintain a minimum 2.25 GPA and reside in the Western New York area to be eligible for this award. Also, you must be involved in community service and demonstrate qualities of good citizenship. more...

Nick Antonacci Memorial Scholarship
The Nick Antonacci Memorial Scholarship is available to current NCCC or incoming students(part-time or full-time) enrolled in a culinary program at Niagara Falls Culinary Institute (NFCI). You must residents of Niagara County, minimum GPA of 2.25, and preferred that the you have experience working in a restaurant to be eligible for this award. more...

Nicole M. Argo Memorial Scholarship
The Nicole M. Argo Memorial Scholarship is available to full - time female students at Niagara County Community College. You must be a resident of Niagara County and currently employed to be eligible for this award. Also, you must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and submit an essay to be eligible for this award. Priority is given to students with a GED diploma. more...

Owls Scholarship
The Owl's Scholarship is available to female non-traditional students at Niagara County Community College. You must be in your second year, have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and plan to re-enter the job market to be eligible for this award. more...

Paul D. Balling Memorial Scholarship
The Paul D. Balling Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Niagara County Community College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible for this award. more...

Ruth J. Mcdonald Memorial Art Scholarship
The Ruth J. McDonald Memorial Art Scholarship is available to students at Niagara County Community College. You must be majoring in fine arts and have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Ruth J. Mcdonald Memorial Music Scholarship
The Ruth J. McDonald Memorial Music Scholarship is available to students at Niagara County Community College. You must be majoring in music and have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Statler Foundation Scholarship
The Statler Foundation Scholarship is available to full - time, second - year students at Niagara County Community College. You must be enrolled in the Niagara Falls Culinary Institute and have a minimum 2.25 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Unifrax Corporation Scholarship - Nccc
The Unifrax Corporation Scholarship is available to students entering the Niagara County Community College. You must be a June graduate of Niagara Falls High School or Niagara Catholic High School and plan to attend NCCC in the fall. Also, you must enroll as a full-time student and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...
