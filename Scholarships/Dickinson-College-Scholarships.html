Dickinson College Scholarships


Benjamin Rush Scholarship
The Benjamin Rush Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Dickinson College. You must rank in the top 10 percent of your high school class, have a minimum SAT score of 1350 (31 ACT) and demonstrate leadership in school or community activities to be eligible for this award. This award may be renewed with a maintained minimum 3.0 GPA. more...

John Dickinson Scholarship
The John Dickinson Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Dickinson College. You must rank in the top 10 percent of your high school class, have a minimum SAT score of 1350 (31 ACT), and demonstrate leadership in school or community activities to be eligible for this award. more...

John Montgomery Scholarship
The John Montgomery Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Dickinson College. You must have excellent grades in a challenging high school curriculum. You must also have exceptional talent in the performing or studio arts, superior proficiency in two or more foreign languages, high achievement in independent research or writing, or significant leadership on a regional or national level to qualify for this award. more...

National Merit Finalist Scholarship - Dickinson College
The National Merit Finalist Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Dickinson College. You must be a John Dickinson or Benjamin Rush Scholar and be a National Merit Finalist who designates Dickinson College as your first choice school to be eligible for this award. more...

Stafford Scholarship - Dickinson College
The Stafford Scholarship is available to entering students at Dickinson College who are John Dickinson scholarship winners. You must be interested in participating in undergraduate research and be planning on pursuing a career in research or medicine. more...
