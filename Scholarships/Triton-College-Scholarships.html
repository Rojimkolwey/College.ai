Triton College Scholarships


Board Of Trustees Adult Education Scholarship
The Board of Trustees Adult Education Scholarship is available to students at Triton College who reside within the Triton district. You must have been in Triton's G.E.D. program, demonstrate financial need and submit a written recommendation from a G.E.D. faculty member to be eligible for this award. more...

Classified Association Kathy Roman Memorial Scholarship
The Classified Association Kathy Roman Memorial Scholarship is available to new or continuing students at Triton College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.75 GPA, U.S. Citizen and Illinois resident to be eligible for this award. more...

Classified Association Ken Hoehamer And Earl Smith Memorial Scholarship
The Classified Association Ken Hoehamer and Earl Smith Memorial Scholarship is available to continuing students at Triton College. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Trustee Honors Scholarship
The Trustee Honors Scholarship is available to high school seniors who are enrolled in at least 12 credit hours per semester at Triton College. You must rank in the top 10 percent of your graduation class or have a minimum GPA of 3.35 at the end of the seventh semester, or an ACT score of 25 or higher to be eligible for this award. more...

Ups Scholarship
The UPS Scholarship is available to new or continuing students at Triton College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 to be eligible for this award. Essay question to be answered: What do you believe to be the greatest barrier(s) to completing a college degree, or reaching one's educational goals, and suggest a solution(s) for addressing these barriers? Describe your plan to ensure you accomplish your educational and career goals. Be sure to include your long- and short-term goals and why you are pursuing higher education. more...
