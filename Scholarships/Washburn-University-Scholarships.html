Washburn University Scholarships


Chemistry Scholarship - Washburn University
The Chemistry Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must be majoring in chemistry, have a minimum ACT score of 25 and a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Finnup Scholarship
The Finnup Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must have graduated from a high school in one of the following counties: Greeley, Wichita, Scott, Lane, Ness, Hamilton, Finney, Kearny, Ford, Gray, Grant, Stanton, Morton, Stevens, Seward, Meade, Clark, Haskell, Comanche or Hodgeman counties. You must also have a minimum 2.0 GPA. This award is renewable for four years with a maintained 2.0 GPA and completion of at least 12 credit hours per semester. more...

Garvey Competitive Scholarship
The Garvey Competitive Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. Your selection is based on the results of a comprehensive examination administered in January. In order to take this exam you must apply for admission, have a minimum 3.25 GPA and receive a recommendation from your high school guidance counselor. This award is renewable for four years with a maintained 3.25 GPA and completion of at least 12 credit hours per semester. more...

Hindman Scholarship
The Hindman Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must have graduated from a Rooks County high school with a minimum 2.0 GPA. This award is renewable for four years with a maintained 2.0 GPA and completion of at least 12 credit hours per semester. more...

Marcoux Business Scholarship
The Marcoux Business Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must be majoring in business, have a minimum ACT score of 26 and a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Nursing Scholarship - Washburn University
The Nursing Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must be majoring in nursing to be eligible for this award. more...

Physics Scholarship - Washburn University
The Physics Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University majoring in physics. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Washburn University
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must be a graduate of a Shawnee County high school, have a minimum 3.5 GPA and receive a recommendation from your high school counselor. This award is renewable for four years with a maintained 3.0 GPA and completion of at least 12 credit hours per semester. more...

Valedictorian Scholarship - Washburn University
The Valedictorian Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must be the valedictorian of your high school graduating class to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable for four years with a maintained 3.0 GPA and completion of at least 12 credit hours per semester. more...

Wiseman Scholarship
The Wiseman Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Washburn University. You must be from one of the following high schools in Topeka: Topeka West, Topeka High or Highland Park. This award is renewable for four years with a maintained 2.0 GPA and completion of at least 12 credit hours per semester. more...
