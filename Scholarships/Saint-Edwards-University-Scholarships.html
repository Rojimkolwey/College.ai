Saint Edward's University Scholarships


Admission Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen students accepted at St. Edwards University in Austin, Texas. Applicants must rank in the top 25% of their class with SAT I combined reading and math scores starting at 1100 or ACT score of 24 or above. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Athletic Department Scholarships
Scholarship for students interested in participating in intercollegiate athletics at St. Edwards University. Scholarships, based on athletic ability, are available for mens baseball & golf, womens volleyball & softball, and women & mens basketball, soccer and tennis, and are offered by the coach of each team. To arrange a tryout or to get more specific information, contact the coach of the sport that the student is interested in. Award amounts and number of awards vary. more...

College Assistance Migrant Program (Camp)
Scholarship for incoming Freshmen who are children of migrant farm workers accepted to St. Edwards University. Due date, award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Fifth-Year Accounting Student Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate senior or graduate student attending St. Edwards University, majoring in Accounting. Applicant must have completed at least 120 credit hours with at least 15 of these hours in the area of accounting, but must not have taken the uniform Certified Public Accountant examination. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Holy Cross Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen students accepted at St. Edwards University in Austin, Texas. Applicant must be in the top 10% of their senior class with standardized test results starting at 1250 on the SAT or 28 on the ACT. A superior application essay and extra-curricular involvement are also required. Award amount is equal to full tuition. more...

Moreau Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from Holy Cross high schools who are incoming freshmen accepted at St. Edwards University in Austin, Texas. Applicants must be in the top 10% of their class with standardized test results starting at 1250 on the SAT or 28 on the ACT. Number of awards varies. Award amount is equal to a full-tuition scholarship. more...

Theater Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending St. Edwards University in Austin, Texas, who participate in Theater. Selection based on demonstrated talent in acting, directing, theater management or technical direction. Auditions will be held in March; contact the Office of Undergraduate Admission to confirm exact date. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Saint Edward's University
The Transfer Scholarships are available to transfer students at St. Edward's University. You must have at least a 3.0 GPA to be considered for this award. Members of Phi Theta Kappa are eligible to receive and extra $2500. more...

Transfer Student Scholarships
Annual award for transfer students accepted at St. Edwards University in Austin, Texas. Applicants must have a cumulative GPA of 3.5 or higher. Members of Phi Theta Kappa will receive an additional $2500 in Scholarship awards. Number of awards varies. Renewable; must maintain 3.0 GPA. more...
