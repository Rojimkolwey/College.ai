St. Thomas Aquinas College Scholarships


Athletic Grant Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York. Applicants must have demonstrated athletic ability, together with academic achievement and financial need. Athletic Director makes recommendations to the Admissions Department for selection. Award amount ranges from $200 to full-tuition. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable; Athletic Director must make recommendation. more...

Commemorative Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen and transfer students accepted to St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York. Each award has different qualifying criteria and award amounts. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Contact the Office of Admissions and Financial Aid for additional information and/or application. more...

Mba Assistantships
Scholarship for graduate students attending St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York and have been accepted as fully matriculated in the M.B.A. program. Applicants must plan to take at least 24 credits during the year. Selection is based on students academic performance, recommendations, experience and ability. The available sites are the business office, human resources office and the Academic Skills Center. Summer assistantship program is not available in business program. Candidates should apply to the Director. Due date, number of awards varies and award amount varies. more...

Military Scholarship Assistance (Rotc)
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York, enrolled in the ROTC program. Applicants must participate in the Air Force ROTC program. The award is equal to full tuition, books, lab fees, plus $100 per month for up to 10 months of each school year. Contact the Office of Admissions and Financial Aid for additional information. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Mst/Msed Assistantships
Scholarship for graduate students attending St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York. Applicant will work an average of 15 hours per week for the college. Summer assistants will work a total of 60 hours during summer semester and take one 3 credit course tuition free. Tutorial assistantships are available for students who can assist others in the writing lab. Candidates must meet entrance requirements for the Master of Science in Education plan to study full time and apply to the Director. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Presidential Leadership Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate students attending St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York. Selection based on high academic achievement, and participation in school or community service activities. Candidate must have demonstrated leadership skills. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

St. Thomas Aquinas Grants
Scholarship for incoming freshmen and transfer students accepted to St. Thomas Aquinas College in New York. Applicants must have strong academic achievement, financial need and participate in extracurricular activities and/or community service. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...
