Bethel College-North Newton Scholarships


Bethel Scholar Scholarships
Scholarship for first-time incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Bethel College. Selection based on academic achievement and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

College Transfer Students Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students transferring to Bethel College. Applicants must be transferring a minimum of 24 credit hours Selection based on academic achievement and potential. Award amount is determined by students GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Founders Academic Merit Scholarships
Scholarship for first-time undergraduate students enrolling at Bethel College. Applicants must be National Merit Semi-Finalists or Finalists OR students with outstanding academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Legacy Awards
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolling at Bethel College whose parents or granparents attended Bethel. Selection based on academic achievement and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Mennonite Racial/Ethnic Grant
The Mennonite Racial/Ethnic Grant is open to minority students at Bethel College, Kansas. You must be affiliated with the Mennonite Church USA, be recommended by your admissions counselor, and have visited the campus in order to be considered. A recommendation from a youth sponsor or pastor is also required. more...

Millennium Iii Scholars
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolling at Bethel College who can add to the cultural diversity of the campus community. Selection based on academic achievement and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Performance Scholarships (Athletics)
Scholarship for first-time incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Bethel College who will be participating in the schools athletic program. Selection based on academic achievement, talent and potential. Scholarships are awarded in: Basketball, Football, Golf, Soccer, Tennis, Track, and Volleyball. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Performance Scholarships (Fine Arts)
Scholarship for first-time undergraduate students enrolling at Bethel College who are majoring/participating in Art, Forensics, Music, or Theater. Selection based on academic achievement, talent, and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Yellow Ribbon Program
The Yellow Ribbon GI Education Enhancement Program is available to students at Bethel College, Kansas. To be considered, you must have served an aggregate period of active duty after September 10, 2001, of at least 36 months; or have been honorably discharged from active duty for a service connected disability and served 30 continuous days after September 10, 2001; or be a dependent eligible for Transfer of Entitlement under the Post-9/11 GI Bill based on a veterans service. more...
