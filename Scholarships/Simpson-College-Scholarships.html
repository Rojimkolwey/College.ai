Simpson College Scholarships


Afs Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, Simpson College enrolling freshmen who have been a part of the AFS Program. Selection is based on the students academic performance and potential. Financial need is not a factor. Award amount varies. more...

All-Iowa Academic Team Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, incoming Simpson College transfer students from a junior or community college that are named to the All-Iowa Academic team (first, second or third team). Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. Number of awards varies. more...

Carver Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, Simpson College minority undergraduates, with high educational goals, pursuing a four year degree. Selection criteria is based on outstanding academic achievements and demonstrated leadership qualities in high school, community, or church. Award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Community-Junior College Achievement Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, incoming Simpson College transfer students from a junior or community college with outstanding academic achievements. Applicant must have earned an Associate of Arts Degree or 61 transferable credit hours and extracurricular activities. Award amounts and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Community-Junior College Honor Shcolarship
Scholarship for full-time, incoming Simpson College transfer students from a junior or community college. Applicant must have an Associate of Arts Degree or 61 transferable credit hours, a minimum 3.5 cumulative GPA, and demonstrated leadership skills. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

George Washington Carver Fellowship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen or transfer students enrolling at Simpson College who are from distinctive and diverse backgrounds. Selection criteria is based on outstanding academic achievement, leadership abilities and contributions to the community. Award amount and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

George Washington Carver Fellowship
The George Washington Carver Fellowship is open to entering freshmen and transfer students at Simpson College who have distinctive and broadly diverse backgrounds. You must exhibit potential for leadership, evidence of service and contribution to community and superior academic achievement in secondary school to be considered for this award. more...

Honor Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, Simpson College undergraduates pursuing a four year degree. Selection criteria is based on outstanding academic achievements and ACT or SAT scores. Award amount and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Matthew Simpson Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming, full-time, Simpson College undergraduates who are members of a United Methodist Church pursuing a four year degree. Selection criteria is based on outstanding academic achievements, ACT or SAT scores, leadership skills, community activities, and involvement in the church. Award amount and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Methodist Junior College Achievement Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, incoming Simpson College transfer students from a Methodist two-year institution with high academic achievement and significant extracurricular records. Student must have earned an Associate of Arts Degree or 61 transferable credit hours and extracurricular activities. Award amounts and number of awards varies. more...

Methodist Junior College Honor Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time, incoming Simpson College transfer students from a Methodist two-year institution that demonstrate high academic achievement. Applicants must have a minimum 3.3 GPA on a 4.0 scale, have earned an Associate of Arts Degree or 61 transferable credit hours and have demonstrated leadership experience. Number of awards varies. more...

Mutiple Dependency Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time students from the same family (two or more) that are pursuing a postsecondary education at Simpson College during the same term. Number of awards may vary. more...

Simpson Recognition Grant
Scholarship for full-time students who are graduating high school students that are pursuing postsecondary education at Simpson College. Applicants must have above-average academic records from high scool and strong leadership qualities and cannot qualify for other merit aid. Financial need is not a factor. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Simpson Recognition Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time students entering Simpson College for the first time. Applicant must have strong academic ability with an ACT composite score of 23 or 24 or a combined SAT score from 1050 to 1130. Candidate must rank in the top one-third of his/her high school class. This award is for students with high academic potential who qualifies for no other Simpson College award. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Transfer Student Achievement Scholarship

Transfer Student Honor Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming transfer students enrolling at Simpson College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.3 GPA and earned an associate in arts degree or 61 transferable credit hours. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Trustee Scholarship - Simpson College
The Trustee Scholarship is open to incoming freshman students at Simpson College. You must be ranked first in your graduating class at the time of the application to be considered for this award. Selection is based on the candidate's leadership skills and high school performance, including test scores. more...

Youth For Understanding Scholarship
Scholarship for students who are graduating high school seniors that plan to pursue postsecondary education at Simpson College and have been part of the Youth for Understanding Program. Selection is based on the students academic performance and potential. Financial need is not a factor. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...
