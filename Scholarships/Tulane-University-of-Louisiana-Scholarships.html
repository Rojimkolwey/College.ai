Tulane University of Louisiana Scholarships


Architecture Portfolio Award - Tulane University
The Architecture Portfolio Award is open to students at Tulane University who have been accepted into the School of Architecture. Selection is based in-part on a portfolio submission. more...

Brandt Dixon - Tulane University
The Brandt Dixon is available to entering Latin-American female freshmen at Tulane University. You must pursue a degree in Liberal Arts, demonstrate strong academic credentials, and be involved in extracurricular activities to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

City (Mayoral) Scholarships
Scholarship for residents of Olean Parish, Louisiana who are graduating high school seniors from a New Orleans high school enrolling at Tulane University upon graduation. Applicants must have a strong academic record, financial need, leadership abilities, and participated in extracurricular activities or community service. Submit letters of recommendation and the Financial Aid Profile form with application packet. Award is for full-tuition. Renewable; maintain 2.3 GPA. more...

Collegiate Achievement Award
Annual award for transfer undergraduate students from a four-year accredited college or university, admitted to Tulane University full-time. Applicant must have a minimum 3.25 GPA. Selection is based on merit and financial need will be considered. Due date is November 15 for spring term or May 15 for the fall term. Number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Community Service Fellowship
The Community Service Fellowship is available to entering freshmen at Tulane University. You must demonstrate high academic achievement as well as outstanding dedication to serving the community with leadership, passion, peer engagement, and resourcefulness to be eligible for this renewable award. If selected as a fellow, you will join a cohort of active and engaged Fellows on campus, attend monthly group meetings, complete approximately 100 service/leadership hours a year, and learn to be a dynamic leader and community advocate. more...

Dean's Honor Scholarship - Tulane University
The Dean's Honor Scholarship is open to full-time entering freshmen at Tulane University. You must rank in the top five percent of your class to be considered for this award. Selection is based on your class rank, academic program, honors and extracurricular activities. more...

Deans Honor Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Tulane University. Students who have a SAT score of 1450 or above or an ACT score of 33 or above, rank in the top 5% of their class, have taken honors and advanced placement courses, and participated in extracurricular activities are eligible to be considered. Submit teachers recommendation and a project with application packet. Award amount covers full tuition. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Global Scholarship - Tulane University
The Global Scholarship is available to entering full-time freshmen at Tulane University. You must be in the top 20% academically (based on testing and GPA) of the applicant pool to be considered for this renewable award. more...

John Hainkel Louisiana Scholars Award
Annual award for Louisiana residents that are entering freshmen admitted to Tulane University full-time. Selection is based on superior merit. The award covers the full cost of tuition, fees, room, board and an $800 stipend for books and supplies. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Louisiana Excellence Award - Tulane University
The Louisiana Excellence Award is open to entering freshmen at Tulane University. You must be a graduate of a Louisiana high school to be eligible for this award. more...

Mayoral Scholarships
The Mayoral Scholarships is open to full-time entering freshmen at Tulane University. You must be a graduate of a New Orleans high school and resident of Orleans Parish to be eligible for this award. You must also have a strong academic record, demonstrate financial need, and participate in extracurricular activities or community service. more...

Musicianship Scholarship
The Musicianship Scholarship is available to full-time incoming freshmen students at Tulane University. You must demonstrate a history of talent and dedication to music to be eligible for this award. You must apply to one of the following concentrations: Jazz Studies, Music Composition, Music Performance (Instrumental), Music Performance (Vocal), or Musical Theatre. Selection is based in-part on a portfolio submission. more...

Paul Tulane Award - Tulane University
The Paul Tulane Award is available to incoming freshman at Tulane University. Selection is based on superior high school performance, extremely competitive standardized test scores, exceptional community, secondary school involvement, and extraordinary intellectual promise. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Tulane University
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to junior and senior-level undergraduate students at Tulane University-School Of Professional Advancement who are Phi Theta Kappa members. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be eligible for this award. more...

Rotc Scholarship
Annual award for students entering the ROTC program who have been admitted to Tulane University. Students completing this program are commissioned as officers in their respective branch of the armed forces upon graduation. Award is equal to full or partial tuition, fees, book expenses and a monthly $150 expense allowance. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. Contact the commanding officer at Tulane for the particular service in which student is interested (i.e. Air Force, Army, Navy). more...

Sotomayor Full-Tuition Public Service Scholarship
The Sotomayor Full-Tuition Public Service Scholarship is available to incoming freshman at Tulane University. You must demonstrate exceptionally high academic achievement and a commitment to public service to be eligible for this award. more...

Stamps Scholarship - Tulane University
The Stamps Scholarship is available to entering full-time freshmen at Tulane University. Selection is based on exemplary leadership, perseverance, scholarship, service, and innovation. more...

Sub-Saharan Africa Leadership Award - Tulane University
The Sub-Saharan Africa Leadership Award is available to entering full-time freshmen at Tulane University. You must be from or studying in Sub-Saharan Africa to be considered for this renewable award. Selection is based on strong academic and extracurricular credentials. more...

Tulane University Marching Band (Tumb) Scholarship
The Tulane University Marching Band (TUMB) Scholarship is available to full-time incoming freshmen students at Tulane University. You must participate in the Tulane Marching Band to be eligible for this award. Selection is based on performance and good academic standing. more...

Valedictorians Scholarship
Annual award for Louisiana residents who are graduating high school seniors awarded Valedictorian by their secondary school and admitted to Tulane University. Due date varies. If secondary school designates more than one valedictorian, the award amount will be divided equally among the students. more...
