Mount Saint Joseph University Scholarships


Band Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the College of Mount St. Joseph. Students must be planning to participate in the Mount Band. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Challenge Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at College of Mount St. Joseph. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, and an 18-19 ACT score or 860 SAT. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

College Of Mount St. Joseph Art Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at the College of Mount St. Joseph. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount is for full tuition. A FAFSA and a statement of purpose must be included with application packet. Renewable. Contact the colleges admissions office or Scholastic for more information and/or application. more...

Moore-Eckel Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending College of Mount St. Joseph majoring in Education, Business or Art. Applicants must show academic talent. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Mount St. Joseph Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the College of Mount St. Joseph. Applicants must show financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Schmidlapp Scholarship
Scholarship for female undergraduate students attending College of Mount St. Joseph majoring in Biology, Chemistry, Math or Computer Science. Students must have financial need and academic merit. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Sister Mary Lea Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending College of Mount St. Joseph. Applicants must show academic talent. Number of awards may vary. more...

Sisters Of Charity Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the College of Mount St. Joseph. Awards are based on academics and need. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...
