The Bay Side Lodge 232 Ancient, Free and Accepted Masons Scholarship is open to students enrolled at least part time at Anne Arundel Community College. To be considered for this award, you must be a U.S. citizen and be a resident of southern Anne Arundel County (MD). You must also demonstrate financial need. more...

Bpa Faculty Transfer Scholarship
The BPA Faculty Transfer Scholarship is open to graduating high school seniors entering the business and public administration (BPA) transfer program at Anne Arundel Community College. To be considered for this award, you must submit an essay, your recent high school transcript with a minimum 3.0 GPA and two recommendations. more...

Crosby Marketing Scholars Program
The Crosby Marketing Scholars Program is available to students at AACC who have completed at least 9 credits in the areas of marketing, public relations, creative writing, graphic design, video or web design/development, or transfer studies. You must be a U.S. citizen, reside in Maryland, have a minimum GPA of 2.5, and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. This scholarship funds two years of study and offers a paid internship opportunity as well. more...

The Edwin J. Lasner Memorial Endowed Scholarship is open to students at Anne Arundel Community College. Additionally, you must demonstrate financial need. Preference is given to students who have completed twelve credits of paralegal studies core courses. more...
