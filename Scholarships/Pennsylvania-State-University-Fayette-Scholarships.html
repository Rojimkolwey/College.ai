Judge Samuel D. Braemer Memorial Scholarship Award
The Judge Samuel D. Braemer Memorial Scholarship is open to full-time juniors and seniors attending Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. You must be enrolled in the administration of justice program and have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Mary Ellen And F. Todd Burhans Memorial Award
The Mary Ellen and F. Todd Burhans Memorial Award is open to students at Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. You must be a resident of Dunbar Borough and/ or the immediate vicinity, including Mt. Braddock. more...

Port Family Service Award
The Port Family Service Award is open to full- time students at Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. You must have completed at least 24 credit hours, have a minimum 3.25 GPA, and demonstrate substantial involvement in secular public service projects in your community or on campus to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Ces Scholarship - Fayette
The Presidential CES Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen accepted into Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. This scholarship is based on high school achievement and potential for college achievement. more...

Rita P. Peters Scholarship
The Rita P. Peters Scholarship is open to full- time students at Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. You must be a resident of Fayette County, have completed at least 30 credit hours, and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Sergeant Dennis William Hippo Scholarship
The Sergeant Dennis William Hippo Scholarship is open to full- time students attending Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. You must intend to enroll or be currently enrolled in an associate degree program to be eligible for this award. You must also demonstrate satisfactory academic achievement, good citizenship and financial need. more...

Swimmer Scholarship - Pennsylvania State University, Fayette
The Swimmer Scholarship is open to full- time nursing students at Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. You must have been born in Fayette County and/ or have resided there for the last 10 years and be in your second year of nursing to be eligible for this award. You must also have demonstrated the most academic improvement (not best GPA) from the beginning of your first year until the end of your first year. Financial need will also be considered. more...

Uniontown Hospital Auxiliary Scholarship
The Uniontown Hospital Auxiliary Scholarship is open to nursing students attending Pennsylvania State University, Fayette. This award is normally given to freshmen and is renewable for a second year provided you maintain academic progress and demonstrate evidence of professional and personal development. more...
