University of Hawaii at Manoa Scholarships


Colonel Harold E. Jensen And Mrs. Lill Jenson Scholarship
The Colonel Harold E. Jensen and Mrs. Lill Jenson Scholarship is open to full-time undergraduate students at the University of Hawaii. You must be from Makakilo or other communities on the Waianae Coast and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Edward Hastings Scholarship
Annual award for a student enrolled full-time at the School of Travel Industry Management, University of Hawaii at Manoa, and majoring in travel industry management. Contact the Director of Internship & Career Development at 808-956-4887 for more information and/or application. Due date varies. more...

Jean Erdman Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Hawaii at Manoa majoring in Dance. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay and a personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Kaiser Permanente Health Care Scholarship - U. Of Hawaii
The Kaiser Permanente Health Care Scholarship is open to entering first-year students at the University of Hawaii. You must be a resident of Hawaii and a graduate of a Hawaii public high school to be eligible to apply. You must also be majoring in any field of health care, have a minimum 3.0 GPA, and demonstrate financial need and a commitment to a career in health care as shown by volunteering or working at the Farrington Health Academy. more...

Manoa Excellence Scholarship
The Manoa Excellence Scholarship is open to incoming freshman students who are not residents of Hawaii. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.8 in order to be considered for this award. more...

Manoa International Excellence Scholarship
The Manoa International Excellence Scholarship is open to International incoming freshman students at the University of Hawaii at Manoa. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.8 in order to be considered for this award. more...

Ruth Small Memorial Scholarship
The Ruth Small Memorial Scholarship is open to undergraduate music majors at the University of Hawaii. more...

Student Opportunity Fund In Music
The Student Opportunity Fund in Music is open to entering undergraduate music majors at the University of Hawaii. You must have a high school diploma, a minimum GPA of 3.0 and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

University Of Hawaii Manoa Provost Achievement Scholarship
The University of Hawaii Manoa Provost Achievement Scholarship is open to incoming freshman undergraduate students at the University of Hawaii. You must be a resident of Hawaii and have a minimum GPA of 3.8 in order to be considered for this renewable award. more...

University Of Hawaii Manoa Regents Scholarship
The University of Hawaii Manoa Regents Scholarship is open to incoming freshman undergraduate students at the University of Hawaii. You must be a resident of Hawaii and have a minimum GPA of 3.8 in order to be considered for this renewable award. more...

Wallace Farrington Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend full-time at the University of Hawaii-Manoa. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay and a personal statement must be included with application packet. Contact the students guidance counselor for additional information and/or application. more...

Walter A. And Diane N. Dods Endowed Scholarship
The Walter A. and Diane N. Dods Endowed Scholarship is open to full-time undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Hawaii. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, have graduated from a Hawaii public high school, and be a U.S. citizen or resident alien to be eligible for this award. more...

William Pfeiffer Memorial Scholarship
The William Pfeiffer Memorial Scholarship is open to undergraduate and graduate music majors at the University of Hawaii. Preference is given to voice and ethnomusicology majors. more...
