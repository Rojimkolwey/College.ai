New Jersey Institute of Technology Scholarships


Architecture Design Competition Scholarship - Njit
The Architecture Design Competition Scholarship is open to freshmen at the New Jersey Institute of Technology, School of Architecture. more...

Athletic Scholarship - Njit
The Athletic Scholarship is open to students at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. You must participate in intercollegiate sports to be eligible for this award. more...

Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Scholarship
The Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Scholarship is open to entering freshman at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. You must be a resident of New Jersey with a strong academic record to be eligible for this award. more...

Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Urban Scholarship
The Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Urban Scholarship is open to entering freshman at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. You must be a high-achieving graduate of a New Jersey public high school in an economically distressed urban district to be eligible for this award. more...

Engineering Technology Scholarship - Njit
The Engineering Technology Scholarship is open to new transfer students at New Jersey Institute of Technology who will be studying engineering technology. You must be a non-New Jersey resident, demonstrate academic talent, and be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident to be eligible for this award. more...

Faculty Scholarship - Njit
The Faculty Scholarship is open to new freshman students at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. You must have a minimum 1200 SAT score and rank in the top 30 percent of your graduating class to be eligible for this award. more...

Honors Merit Award - Njit
The Honors Merit Award is open to students at New Jersey Institute of Technology who have been accepted into the honors program. more...

Honors Residential Scholarship - Njit
The Honors Residential Scholarship is open to students at the New Jersey Institute of Technology who are in the honors program. You must reside on-campus to be eligible for this award. more...

National Merit Scholarship - Njit
The National Merit Scholarship is open to full-time students at the New Jersey Institute of Technology. You must maintain a minimum GPA of 3.0 to qualify for this award. more...
