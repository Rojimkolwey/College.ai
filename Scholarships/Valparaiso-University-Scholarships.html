Valparaiso University Scholarships


Allen Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are preparing for a career in a church profession. Selection based on academic achievement, commitment to leadership and service in church and society, and financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Alumni Heritage Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are sons or daughters of a Valpo alumna or alumnus. Selection based on academic achievement. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Alumni Heritage Scholarship - Valpo
The Alumni Heritage Scholarship is open to students at Valparaiso University. You must be the child of Valparaiso University alumni to be considered for this award. more...

Ament Brenner Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are members of a Lutheran congregation in the greater St. Louis, MO area and are preparing for a career the Healthcare field. Selection based on academic achievement and financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Award For Excellence Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Valparaiso University. Applicants must have been nominated by principal during junior year of high school for academic success and leadership skills. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Board Of Directors Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Valparaiso University. Applicants must be an early action candidate. Selection based on academic achievement, commitment to leadership and service in church and society, and an interview. Students must file the FAFSA. All students applying for admission will automatically be reviewed for this award. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Board Of Directors Scholarship - Valpo
The Board of Directors Scholarship is available to students entering Valparaiso University. You must have the highest combined subscore of the ACT / SAT to be eligible for this award. more...

Charles W. Wolf Award
Scholarship for college juniors attending Valparaiso University who are majoring in Chemistry. Selection based on academic achievement. Students must file the FAFSA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Dow Chemical Company Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship for first-year students accepted to Valparaiso University who will be majoring in chemistry with the potential to receive a Ph.D. in chemistry or biochemistry. Selection based on academic achievement. Students must file the FAFSA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

East Asian Studies Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen or transfer students accepted to Valparaiso University who are majoring in Chinese or Japanese Studies. Selection based on academic achievement. Students must file the FAFSA. Due date may vary. Renewable. more...

Ed Voelz Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are graduates of St. Peters Lutheran School (IN). Selection based on academic achievement. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Edith S. Lessor Memorial Scholarships
Scholarship for female, undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who majoring in Chemistry. Selection based on academic achievement. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Glenn D. Peters Law School Scholarship
The Glenn D. Peters Law School Scholarship is available to incoming first-year or returning students attending Valparaiso University School of Law. You must be from Northern Indiana to be eligible for this award. more...

International Achievement Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming, international freshmen accepted to Valparaiso University. Selection based on GPA and SAT/ACT scores. Applicants must file the FAFSA. All students applying for admission will automatically be reviewed for this award. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Lake County Bar Association's Law School Scholarship
The Lake County Bar Association's Law School Scholarship is available to incoming first-year students attending Valparaiso University School of Law. You must be a permanent resident of Lake County, Indiana, to be eligible for this award. more...

Lutheran Leadership Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are preparing for a career to be a lay leader within the Lutheran church/synod. Applicants must be nominated. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Martin Luther Awards
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are dependents of full-time professional, rostered Lutheran church workers. Applicants must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

National Merit Scholarship - Valparaiso University
The National Merit Scholarship is available to freshmen students at Valparaiso University. You must be a National Merit Finalist and designate Valparaiso University as your first choice school to be considered for this award. more...

South Suburban Bar Association Scholarship
The South Suburban Bar Association Scholarship is available to incoming first-year students attending Valparaiso University School of Law. You must reside or intend to practice in the South Suburbs of Cook County, Illinois, to be eligible for this award. more...

Summer Research Assistant In Astronomy
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are majoring in Astronomy. Summer research opportunity for a 10-12 week period on the Valparaiso University campus. Applicants must be familiar with the use of a computer and be willing to learn and work hard. Nonrenewable. more...

Summer Research Assistant In Atmospheric Physics
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are majoring in Physics with an emphasis on Atmospheric Physics. Summer research opportunity in the analysis of satellite data and modeling of trace constituents in the earths atmosphere. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Summer Research Assistant In Condensed Matter Physics
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are majoring in Physics. Summer research opportunity for students interested in condensed matter physics. Research will take place at the Argonne National Laboratory in Illinois. Number of awards may vary. A stipend of $400 per week is given. Nonrenewable. more...

Summer Research Assistant In Nuclear Physics
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Valparaiso University who are majoring in Physics. Research opportunity for students interested in Nuclear Physics. It is a 10-12 week program with most of the work being done on the campus. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...
