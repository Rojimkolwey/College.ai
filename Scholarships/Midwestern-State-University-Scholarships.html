Dflc Continuing Students Scholarship
Scholarship for continuing undergraduate students who are attending Washington State University and are majoring or minoring in Foreign Language. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Department of Foreign Languages & Cultures for more information. more...

Dflc Film Studies
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Washington State University who are minoring in Film Studies. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Department of Foreign Languages & Cultures for more information. more...

Dflc New Students Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen, current freshmen and sophomores, and transfer students who are attending Washington State University and are majoring or minoring in Foreign Language. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Department of Foreign Languages & Cultures for more information. more...

Dflc Study Abroad Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Washington State University who are studying abroad and majoring or minoring in Foreign Language (Russian, Spanish, German, French or Chinese). Selection based on academic achievement. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Department of Foreign Languages & Cultures for more information. more...
