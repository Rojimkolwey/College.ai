Gannon University Scholarships


Country Fair Scholarship
The Country Fair Scholarship is available to students at Gannon University. more...

Delaney Medical School Scholarship Fund
The Delaney Medical School Scholarship is available to students who have been accepted to allopathic medical school. You must be a graduate of an Erie County high school as well as Gannon University to be eligible for this award. more...

Ellen Elizabeth Mcnamara Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for female undergraduate students enrolled at Gannon University, Erie, Pennsylvania. Students must demonstrate financial need, academic achievement, outstanding character, strong faith, and personality. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the universitys financial aid office for additional information and/or application. more...

Gannon University-Sae Engineering Scholarship
Annual award for graduating high school seniors planning to attend Gannon University majoring in Engineering. Students must be U.S. citizens and demonstrate academic achievement. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Hugh M. Nelson Engineering Scholarship
Scholarship for Erie County, Pennsylvania residents who are college sophomores or juniors enrolled at Pennsylvania State University, Erie-The Behrend College or Gannon University majoring in Engineering. Students must have financial need, demonstrate academic achievement, and participation in college activities. Number of awards may vary. A personal letter must be included with application packet. Contact the colleges financial aid office for additional information and/or application. more...

Louis W. Balmer Erie Engineering Societies Council Scholarship
Scholarship for Northestern Pennsylvania residents who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Allegheny College, Edinboro University, Gannon University, Grove City College, or Pennsylvania State, Erie-The Behrend College majoring in Engineering, Science, Technology or a related field. Students must demonstrate academic achievement and participation in extracurricular activities. Number of awards may vary. Contact the financial aid office for additional information and/or application. more...

Sister M. Lawreace Antoun, Ssj Scholarship
Scholarship for female students currently enrolled or planning to attend a four-year postsecondary institution. Students must be attending Villa Maria Elementary, Villa Maria Academy, Gannon University, or Mervyhurst College in Erie, Pennsylvania, demonstrate financial need and academic achievement. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the students guidance counselor for additional information and/or application. more...

Walter & Florence Guelcher Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Gannon University majoring in Engineering. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, demonstrate a high moral character, be active in their faith, and be Erie County, Pennsylvania high school graduates. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the universitys financial aid office for additional information and/or application. more...

Wilburn J. Johnson And Edward M. Gabriel Scholarship
The Wilburn J. Johnson and Edward M. Gabriel Scholarship is available to students at Gannon University. You must be majoring in international studies to qualify for this award. more...
