Lewis & Clark College Scholarships


Animal Law Scholarship - Lewis & Clark Law School
The Animal Law Scholarship is available to continuing students at the Lewis and Clark Law School. You must demonstrate a commitment to the study and practice of animal law to be eligible for this award. more...

Davis, Wright, Tremaine International Law Writing Competition
The Davis, Wright, Tremaine International Law Writing Competition is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. To qualify for this award, you must submit an original paper written in the past year on any topic in private or public international law, but not comparative law or the law of a foreign nation. more...

Gantenbein Fellows Scholarship
The Gantenbein Fellows Scholarship is available to second - and third - year students at the Lewis & Clark Law School who have a record of outstanding academic performance. more...

Jane Wiener Memorial Scholarship
The Jane Wiener Memorial Scholarship is available to students with physical disabilities at the Lewis & Clark Law School. more...

John E. And Susan S. Bates Scholarship
The John E. and Susan S. Bates Scholarship is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. You must be planning to pursue a career in business law to qualify for this award. more...

Milt Smith And James Richardson Business Law Scholarship
The Milt Smith and James Richardson Business Law Scholarship is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School who are in their final year of law school. You must demonstrate that you have studied business or tax law in your coursework and an interest for practicing business law upon graduation to qualify for this award. more...

Neva Elliott Scholarship
The Neva Elliott Scholarship is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. You must be an evening student to qualify for this award. more...

Patricia Kraske Scholarship
The Patricia Kraske Scholarship is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. You must be a female, non-traditional student to qualify for this award. more...

Paul H. Casey Scholarship
The Paul H. Casey Scholarship is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. You must be majoring in business or public interest law and demonstrate attention to values, integrity and character to qualify for this award. more...

Research Fellowship Program
The Research Fellowship Program is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. more...

Terry Wilson Scholarship
The Terry Wilson Scholarship is available to students at the Lewis & Clark Law School. You must show a willingness to use your legal skills to help others in trouble and in need of a champion of their cause to qualify for this award. Preference is given to students from Washington who have an intention to practice there. more...
