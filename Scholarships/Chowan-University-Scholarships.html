Chowan University Scholarships


Bsc Of Nc Grant
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students who are dependents of full-time employees of the Baptist Southern Convention of North Carolina or institutions and agencies affiliated with state conventions of the southern Bapitis tradition. Due date, number of awards, and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Children & Spouses Of Ordained Ministers & Missionaries Grant
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students attending Chowan College who are dependent children or spouses of ordained ministers or missionaries related to churches or conventions of the Southern Baptist Tradition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Chowan Christian Service Association Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolling for the first-time at Chowan College who feel the call into Chrisitan service or the ministry. Due date, number of awards, and award amount may vary. more...

Chowan Christian Service Association Scholarship
The Chowan Christian Service Association Scholarship is available to students at Chowan University. You must be seeking a vocation in the ministry to be eligible for this award. more...

Chowan Leadership Scholarship
The Chowan Leadership Scholarship is available to students at Chowan University. You must be currently involved in a leadership position in your church youth group, athletics, student government, or another student organization. You must have at least a 2.7 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Chowan Merit Scholarships
The Chowan Merit Scholarships are available to students entering Chowan University. You must have at least a 2.5 GPA and an SAT score of at least 1350 to be eligible for this award. more...

Coca Cola First Generation Scholarship
Annual award for full-time undergraduate students attending Chowan College who are the first in their immediate family to attend college. Applicant must have financial need. Recipient must participate in campus and community activities. Due date and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Dependents Of Chowan College Faculty-Staff Grant
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students attending Chowan College who are dependent children or spouses of full-time staff or faculty at Chowan College. Dependent children must be under 25 years of age. Renewable. more...

Employee Of Chowan College Grant
Employee-Related award for undergraduate students or auditing students attending Chowan College who are employees of the college. Applicants must meet admissions requirements. Renewable. more...

Graphic Communications Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen to Chowan College and will be majoring in Graphic Communications. Applicants must have either: won first, second, or third place in the Regional, State, or National VICA contest; won first, second, or third place in the South Eastern Region Flexo High School Contest; or graduated in the top 10% of class. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Leadership Grant Program
Scholarship for full-time incoming freshmen or transfer students enrolling at Chowan College. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and held an officer position in Student Government, or been a class officer, or captain of a varsity squad, or hold a leadership position in a civic/church organization. Due date may vary. Award amount is for full tuition. Recipients will be required to attend/participate in additional functions. Renewable. more...

Merit Based Grants & Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming Chowan College freshmen and transfer students. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA, ACT score of 17 or SAT of 830, and rank in the top 50% of their class. Students admissions application is reviewed for these awards. Awards are renewable for a total of four years with maintained 2.0 GPA for grants and 3.0 GPA for scholarships received. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Ministerial Students Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Chowan College who are Ministerial students related to the Southern Baptist tradition. Recipients must participate in the Campus Ministerial Alliance and or the Baptist Student Union. Renewable. more...

North Carolina Baptist Grant
Annual award for undergraduate students attending Chowan College who are members of a church affiliated with the Baptist State Convention of North Carolina. Applicants must a have been a member for a minimum of one year. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Pastors Scholarship
Scholarship for first-time undergraduate students enrolling at Chowan College who are affiliated with a church in the Southern Baptist traditions. Applicant must attend full-time and their church must also contribute a minimum of $500 worth of scholarship funds. Number of awards may vary. more...

Regional Grants
Scholarship for residents of select counties in northeastern North Carolina and southeastern Virginia who are Chowan College undergraduate students. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA, ACT score of 17 or SAT of 830, and rank in the top 50% of their class. Students admissions application is reviewed for these awards. Awards are renewable for a total of four years. more...

Science Olympiad Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Chowan College who won a Science Olympiad medal during their junior or senior year in high school and will be pursuing studies in Science. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Senior Citizens Grant
Scholarship for mature undergraduate students attending Chowan College. Applicant must meet admissions requirements and be 55 years of age or older. Due date, number of awards, and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Whitehead Scholarship
Scholarship for Christian, female, undergraduate students attending Chowan College. Applicants must be residents from one of the nine southern states and have financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
