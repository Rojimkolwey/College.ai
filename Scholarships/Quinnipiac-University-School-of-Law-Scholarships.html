Morris And Molly Shaiman Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Morris and Molly Shaiman Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to students at Quinnipiac University, School of Law. You must demonstrate good scholastic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Multiple Sibling Scholarship - Quinnipiac University
The Multiple Sibling Scholarship is available to full - time students at Quinnipiac University. You and one or more siblings must be concurrently enrolled at Quinnipiac to be eligible for this award. more...

Myron B. Sokolowski Endowed Scholarship
The Myron B. Sokolowski Endowed Scholarship is available to students at Quinnipiac University School of Law. You must have a background in the physical sciences and wish to draw on that knowledge in your legal career to be eligible for this award. more...

Oaklawn Foundation Scholarship
The Oaklawn Foundation Scholarship is available to students at Quinnipiac University School of Law. You must demonstrate good scholastic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Pollina Family Endowed Scholarship
The Pollina Family Endowed Scholarship is available to second- and third-year students at Quinnipiac University School of Law. You must demonstrate good academic achievement and have a strong interest in family law, particularly protecting children's rights and issues of child abuse to be eligible for this award. more...

R. Loricco Family Endowed Scholarship
The R. LoRicco Family Endowed Scholarship is available to students at Quinnipiac University, School of Law. You must be in good academic standing and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Samuel A. Gilliland Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Samuel A. Gilliland Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to second- and third-year students at Quinnipiac University School of Law. You must have successfully completed your first year of law school to be eligible for this award. more...

Silver, Golub And Teitell Endowed Scholarship
The Silver, Golub and Teitell Endowed Scholarship is available to first-year students at Quinnipiac University School of Law. You must demonstrate intellectual promise and qualities that would be a credit to the legal profession to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students who are interested in family law. more...

Terrence H. Benbow Endowed Scholarship
