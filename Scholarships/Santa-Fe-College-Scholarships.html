Colonel R. James Glikes Memorial Scholarship
The Colonel R. James Glikes Memorial Scholarship is available to graduating seniors from Alachua or Bradford County high schools who plan to pursue a degree in one of the health related programs at Santa Fe College. You must be from Alachua or Bradford County and have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Joyce Duke Riherd/Bradford County Public Health Nursing Scholarship
The Joyce Duke Riherd/Bradford County Public Health Nursing Scholarship is available to graduating seniors from Bradford County High School who plan to pursue a career in nursing or health related programs at Santa Fe Community College. You must be a resident of Bradford County and have a minimum 2.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
