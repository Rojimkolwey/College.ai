Keystone College Scholarships


Academic Excellence Scholarship - Keystone College
The Academic Excellence Scholarship is open to full-time entering freshmen at Keystone College. You must rank in the top five percent of your graduating class and have a minimum SAT score of 1100 or an ACT score of 24. This award provides half to full tuition and is renewable with a maintained minimum GPA of 3.0. more...

Drinko Criminal Justice Scholarship
The Drinko Criminal Justice Award is open to full-time students enrolled in the criminal justice administration program at Keystone College. To be eligible for this award, you must demonstrate financial need and submit the FAFSA. The award provides up to $4,000 per year for four years. more...

International Student Freshman Scholarship - Keystone College
The International Student Freshman Scholarship is available to full-time, first-year international students at Keystone College. You must demonstrate satisfactory academic progress, and non-English speaking students must have a minimum 500 TOEFL (173 computer-based) score to be eligible for this award. This scholarship may be renewed with a maintained minimum 2.5 GPA after two semesters of study. more...

International Student Scholarship For Currently Enrolled Students - Kc
The International Student Scholarship for Currently Enrolled Students is available to full-time continuing international students at Keystone College. You must have completed a minimum of 30 credit hours with at least a 2.75 GPA to be eligible for this award. Students who have previously received an international student scholarship will not be considered. more...

Leadership Award - Keystone College
The Leadership Award is open to full-time first-year students at Keystone College. You must demonstrate participation in extracurricular activities and show potential for academic success to be considered for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Keystone College
The Presidential Scholarship is open to academically talented, full-time entering freshmen at Keystone College. Financial need is not a consideration. Selection will be based on high school academic record/rank and SAT/ACT scores. This award may be renewed with a maintained minimum 2.75 GPA. more...

Professional Accounting Scholarship - Keystone College
The Professional Accounting Scholarship is open to full-time associate or bachelor degree students enrolled in the accounting program at Keystone College. To be eligible for this award, you must demonstrate financial need and submit the FAFSA. The award provides up to $4,000 per year for four years. more...

Shoney's Culinary Arts Award
The Shoney's Culinary Arts Award is open to full-time students enrolled in the culinary arts or hospitality program at Keystone College. To be eligible for this award, you must demonstrate financial need and submit the FAFSA. The award provides up to $4,000 per year for two years. more...

Transfer Presidential Scholarship - Keystone College
The Transfer Presidential Scholarship is available to full-time, degree-seeking transfer students at Keystone College. You must have earned an associate degree and/or completed a minimum of 60 transferable credit hours with at least a 3.0 GPA to be considered for this renewable award. more...

Transfer Trustee Scholarship - Keystone College
The Transfer Trustee Scholarship is available to full-time, degree-seeking transfer students at Keystone College. You must have earned an associate degree and/or completed a minimum of 60 transferable credit hours with at least a 2.5 GPA to be considered for this renewable award. more...

Trustee Scholarship - Keystone College
The Trustee Scholarship is open to academically talented, full-time entering freshmen at Keystone College. Selection is based on a combination of high school academic record/rank and SAT/ACT scores. This award may be renewed with a maintained minimum 2.5 GPA. more...
