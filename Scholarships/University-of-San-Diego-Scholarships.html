University of San Diego Scholarships


Athletic Scholarship - University Of San Diego
The Athletic Scholarship is open to student athletes at the University of San Diego. All NCAA Division 1 sports have scholarships except football and men's rowing. more...

Bishop Maher Catholic Leadership Scholarship
The Bishop Maher Catholic Leadership Scholarship is open to students at the University of San Diego. You must demonstrate leadership capabilities in your parish or within your Catholic community and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Incoming freshmen must have a high school GPA of 3.0 and acceptable SAT scores. Transfer students from other four-year universities and community colleges must have a minimum GPA of 3.0. more...

Deans Graduate Merit Scholarship - University Of San Diego
The Deans Graduate Merit Scholarship is open to graduate students at the University of San Diego. more...

John Ronchetto Scholarship
The John Ronchetto Scholarship is available to undergraduate students at the University of San Diego. You must be a student who exemplifies the qualities of service, ingenuity and dedication to the Knauss School of Business and USD as a whole in order to be considered for this award. more...

Undergraduate Merit Scholarship (Alcala, Trustee, Presidential And Torero Pride) - Usd
The Undergraduate Merit Scholarship (Alcala, Trustee, Presidential and Torero Pride) is open to incoming freshmen at the University of San Diego. Selection is based on superior academic achievement, test scores, leadership, service, talent and other personal qualities irrespective of financial circumstances. more...

University Of San Diego Student Veteran Endowed Scholarship
The Student Veteran Endowed Scholarship is available to undergraduate and graduate students at the University of San Diego. You must be a US Military Veteran and be fully funded through VA funding, the Yellow Ribbon program and other scholarships in order to be considered for this award. more...
