Unity College Scholarships


Challenge Scholarship - Unity College
The Challenge Scholarship is available to incoming students at Unity College. You must have overcome adversity and the disadvantage of significant obstacles such as serious illness, or a physical or learning disability to be eligible for this award. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Unity College
The Dean's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Unity College. Selection is based on superior scholastic achievement along with involvement in school activities and the local community. more...

Environmental Action Scholarship - Unity College
The Environmental Action Scholarship is available to incoming students at Unity College. You must demonstrate active, action-oriented and hands-on involvement with the environment to be eligible for this award. more...

Leadership Scholarship - Unity College
The Leadership Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Unity College. You must demonstrate dedication to an activity such as community service, athletics, school spirit, academic leadership, clubs, organizations and other co-curricular activities to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable for three additional years. more...

Legacy Scholarship - Unity College
The Legacy Scholarship is available to incoming students at Unity College. You must be the child or grandchild of Unity College alumni to be eligible for this award. more...

Native American Scholarship - Unity College
The Native American Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Unity College. You must be Native American to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable for three additional years. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Unity College
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen students at Unity College. You must demonstrate academic achievement, and involvement in school activities and the local community to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Unity College
The Transfer Scholarship is available to incoming transfer students at Unity College. You must have at least 12 college credits and a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
