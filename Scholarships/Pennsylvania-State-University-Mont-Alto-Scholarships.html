Pennsylvania State University-Penn State Mont Alto Scholarships


Dorothy Scruggs Plummer Memorial Scholarship
The Dorothy Scruggs Plummer Memorial scholarship is available female students at Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. You must be a Franklin County resident, have a positive academic record and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to students majoring in journalism, liberal arts or education. more...

John R. And Marion G. Campbell Endowed Scholarship
The John R. and Marion G. Campbell Endowed Scholarship is available to students at Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. You must demonstrate a superior academic record and financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to Franklin County students enrolled in an engineering program. more...

Patrick D. Dill Memorial Scholarship
The Patrick D. Dill Memorial Scholarship is open to Waynesboro Area High School seniors planning on attending Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. more...

Penn National Golf Course Community Scholarship
The Penn National Golf Course Community Scholarship is available to nursing students at Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. You must demonstrate superior academic merit to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to students from Franklin, Fulton or Adams County in Pennsylvania or Washington County in Maryland. more...

Penn State Club Of Franklin County Endowed Scholarship
The Penn State Club of Franklin County Endowed Scholarship is open to full-time students attending Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. You must be a resident of Franklin County, PA with demonstrated financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Richard E. Rose Memorial Award
The Richard E. Rose Memorial Award is open to students attending Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. You must be majoring in forestry or game management to be eligible for this award. more...

Sprint Next Step Program Endowed Scholarship - Psuma
The Sprint Next Step Program Endowed Scholarship is available to students at Pennsylvania State University, Mont Alto. You must demonstrate academic promise and financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to female students enrolled in the Next Step Program. more...

Steven L. Kolumban Memorial Award
