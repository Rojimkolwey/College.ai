Church Matching Grant - Wsc
The Church Matching Grant is open to first-year students at Westminster Seminary California. The seminary will match up to $1,000 provided your congregation awards you a grant of no more than $1,000 for your first full year. more...

Presidential Merit Scholarship - Wsc
The Presidential Merit Scholarship is available to full-time students at Westminster Seminary California. Selection is based on potential for future leadership in both ordained and non-ordained vocations. more...

Reformed Scholarship Fund-Wsc
The Reformed Scholarship Fund is available to full-time, first-year students at Westminster Seminary California. You must be a member of the Reformed or Presbyterian Church in good standing, demonstrate financial need, and show Christian leadership to be eligible for this award. more...
