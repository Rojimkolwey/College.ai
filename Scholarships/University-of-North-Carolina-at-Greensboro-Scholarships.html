University of North Carolina at Greensboro Scholarships


Alice Mcarver Ratchford Scholarship
The Alice McArver Ratchford Scholarship is open to female students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must live on campus and have never been married. You must also not have a car and have received no other scholarships to be eligible for this award. more...

Arc Greensboro Special Education Scholarship
The Arc Greensboro Special Education Scholarship is available to graduate and undergraduate students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must be a full-time student enrolled in the licensure program for special education teachers to be eligible for this award. more...

Non-Traditional Adult Scholarship - Uncg
The Non-Traditional Adult Scholarship is open to female adult undergraduate students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. There must have been a five-year break in your education for you to be considered for this award. more...

Robert Andrew Fleming And Mary Cottrell Fleming Athletic Scholarship
The Robert Andrew and Mary Cottrell Fleming Athletic Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must participate in the men's basketball, baseball, or soccer programs to be considered for this award. more...

Taylor George Steele Memorial Scholarship
The Taylor George Steele Memorial Scholarship is open to incoming male students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must have attended Western Guilford High School and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Thomasville Furniture Scholarship
The Thomasville Furniture Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. Preference will be given to students from Thomasville and Davidson County. more...

Todd Owen Carter Music Fund Scholarship
The Todd Owen Carter Music Fund Scholarship is open to graduate students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must be enrolled in the School of Music to be considered for this award. Preference is given to students who are majoring in choral or instrumental conducting. more...

Tom Zirker Memorial Flute Scholarship
The Tom Zirker Memorial Flute Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must be enrolled in the School of Music and have your principle instrument be the flute to qualify for this award. more...

Uncg Double Reed Endowed Scholarship
The UNCG Double Reed Endowed Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must be enrolled in the School of Music and your principle instrument must be oboe or bassoon to be considered for this award. more...

Vera Mae Hedrick Scholarship
The Vera Mae Hedrick Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must be a resident of North Carolina or Piedmont Virginia (which includes the following counties: Loudoun, Clarke, Fauquier, Culpeper, Rappahannock, Madison, Orange, Greene, and Albemarle). more...

Virginia Mayberry Scholarship
The Virginia Mayberry Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must demonstrate financial need and academic excellence to be eligible for this award. more...

William F. Black Scholarship
The William F. Black Scholarship is open to students at the University of North Carolina, Greensboro. You must be majoring in jazz studies to be considered for this award. more...
