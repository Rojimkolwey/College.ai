Community Service Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Lycoming College. Applicants must have participated in community service projects and continue to do so. Due date may vary. more...

Creative Writing Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Creative Writing Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Lycoming College. You must plan on majoring in creative writing to be eligible for this renewable award. You must submit writing samples. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Dean's Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Lycoming College. You must have a minimum 3.00 GPA and have a minimum score of 1000 on the SAT (22 on ACT) to be considered for this award. more...

Departmental Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Departmental Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Lycoming College. You must have a minimum 3.3 GPA and have a minimum 1200 SAT / 27 ACT score to be eligible for this award. An interview is required. more...

Faculty Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Faculty Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Lycoming College. You must have a minimum 3.65 GPA and have a minimum 1300 SAT / 29 ACT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Faculty Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Lycoming College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.65 GPA and have a minimum SAT score of 1950 or ACT score of 29. Students must interview with the Dean of the College and a faculty department chairperson. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Ministerial Grant
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students attending Lycoming College who are dependent children of ordained, practicing ministers. Selection based on academic achievement. Award amount covers up to 33% of tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Music Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Music Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Lycoming College. You must have an interest in vocal or instrumental music and plan to perform at Lycoming to be eligible for this award. An audition is required. more...

Pre-Ministerial Student Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Lycoming College who are pursuing pre-ministerial studies. Selection based on academic achievement. Award amount covers up to 25% of tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

The Lycoming Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Lycoming College Applicants must have graduated in the top 30% of their class and have a minimum SAT score of 1500 or ACT score of 22. Students admissions application is automatically reviewed for these awards. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Theater Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Lycoming College who have talent in Acting, Directing, or the Technical aspects of Theater. Applicants must audition with readings from two contrasting scripts of the students choice. Auditions must be completed by April 1; appointments can be made through the Office of Admissions. more...

Theatre Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Theatre Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Lycoming College. You must plan on majoring or minoring in theatre to be eligible for this award. An audition and/or portfolio is required. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Transfer Scholarship is open to incoming transfer students at Lycoming College. You must have a minimum 3.00 GPA with at least 24 credits completed to be eligible for this award. more...

Trustee Scholarship - Lycoming College
The Trustee Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Lycoming College. You must rank first or second in your class at the end of your junior year in high school to be considered for this award. more...
