Daisy & Frederick Stone Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for Seneca County, Ohio residents who are juniors and seniors attending Tiffin University and are majoring in Criminal Justice. Selection is based on academic achievement, minimum 3.0 GPA and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Zahn Scholarship Competition
Annual competition in which Tiffin University conducts the Zahn Scholar examination consisting of vocabulary aptitude and critical thinking skills. The high school senior scoring the highest on the exam is awarded a full tuition scholarship for 4 years. Second through fifth place winners each receive a half tuition scholarship for 4 years. Applications for the examination and additional information are available from the Office of Admissions. more...
