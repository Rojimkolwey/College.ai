Texas A & M University-College Station Scholarships


Baccalaureate Scholarship Program
Scholarship for Texas residents who are undergraduate students enrolled at a Texas postsecondary institution. Students must be actively participating in 4-H youth development programs, have passed all sections of the TAAS, TASP, and/or TAKS tests, have a minimum 910 SAT score, or 19 ACT score. Selection is based on academic achievement and 4-H experience. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. An essay and a personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Century Scholars Program-Texas A & M University
The Century Scholars Program is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Texas A & M University. You must be graduating from a targeted Texas high school and show academic achievement under difficult circumstances to be eligible for this award. Selection is based on academic merit, campus/community activities, leadership roles, and employment. This award is renewable and includes a one-time $1,000 scholarship to be used for a Texas A & M approved education abroad experience. more...
