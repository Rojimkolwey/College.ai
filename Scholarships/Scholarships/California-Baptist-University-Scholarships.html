California Baptist University Scholarships


Athletic Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Athletic Scholarship is available to athletes at California Baptist University. To qualify for this award you must represent the university on one of the intercollegiate athletic teams. more...

California Baptist University Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending California Baptist University. Selection is based on merit and financial need. Applicants must file the FAFSA. Award amount is determined by students GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Cbu Grant
The CBU Grant is available to students at California Baptist University who demonstrate merit and financial need. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Church Related Vocation (Crv) Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate, Southern Baptist students, attending California Baptist University who are preparing for a Southern Baptist vocational Ministry. Applicant must be pursuing a vocation as a minister of education, music, children or youth, pastor, missionary, or a denominational minister through the Southern Baptist Convention and be interviewed and recommended by a faculty member of the Christian Studies Division. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Drama Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Drama Scholarship is awarded to students at California Baptist University. To qualify for this award, you must participate in drama department productions and the drama ministry team or on the forensics team. more...

Engineering Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Engineering Scholarship is available to students at California Baptist University who are majoring in engineering. more...

Journalism And Speech/Debate Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Journalism and Speech/Debate Scholarship is available to students at California Baptist University who are majoring in journalism and speech/debating more...

Minister's Kin Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Minister's Kin Scholarship is awarded to students at California Baptist University. To qualify for this award, you must be the spouse or dependent of a minister engaged in full-time service within the Southern Baptist Convention as a pastor; minister of education, music, children, or youth. They may also be a chaplain or a missionary appointed by the Southern Baptist Home Mission Board or the Foreign Mission Board or a denominational employee engaged in a full-time vocational ministry. more...

Ministers Kin Scholarships
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students accepted to California Baptist University who are spouses or dependent children of Southern Baptist Ministers. The Southern Baptist minister (sponsor) must be in full-time service with the SBC as a pastor, minister of education, music, children or youth, chaplain or a missionary appointed by the Southern Baptist North American Mission Board or International Mission Board; or denominational employee engageed in a full-time vocational ministry. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Multi-Sibling
The Multi-Sibling Scholarship is available to students at California Baptist University who are concurrently enrolled full-time with at least one other family member. more...

Music Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Music Scholarship is awarded to students at California Baptist University who are majoring in vocal, instrumental or piano performance. more...

Varsity Athletic Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending California Baptist University who are participating in one of the schools varsity programs. Applicants must be eligible under the rules of the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics and the Golden State Athletic Conference. Award amounts may be up to full tuition, fees, room, and board. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Visual Art Scholarship - California Baptist University
The Visual Art Scholarship is available to students at California Baptist University who are majoring in visual art. more...
