Centenary College of Louisiana Scholarships


1825 Scholars Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Centenary College of Louisiana full-time. Applicants are selected from their essay submitted by February 15th. Notification of awards is made during the week of March 21st. In addition to essay, consideration will be given for high school curriculum, GPA, SAT/ACT scores, extracurricular activities and leadership/service roles. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Academic Achievement Scholarship - Centenary College
The Academic Achievement Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Centenary College. You must have a minimum 2.75 GPA and a minimum 900 SAT / 19 ACT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Academic-Top 10% Transfer Student Scholarships
Scholarship for transfer students admitted to attend Centenary College of Louisiana full-time. Applicants must rank in the top 10% of their high school class or have had a 3.7 GPA at the time of graduation. Candidates must have completed at least 17 high school core units and obtained an ACT score of 25 or SAT score of 1140. Students must have a minimum 3.0 or greater from all colleges previously attended. Number of awards varies. more...

Academic-Top 20% Transfer Student Scholarships

Candidates must have completed at least 17 high school core units and obtained an ACT score of 26 or SAT score of 1180. Students must have a minimum 3.0 or greater from all colleges previously attended. Number of awards varies. more...

Centenary T.O.P.S. Honors Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Centenary College of Louisiana full-time. Applicants must meet all State requirements for TOPS, as well as the following: ACT score of at least 22; GPA of 2.5 or higher; must have completed 16.5 core units; and file the FAFSA application. Due date and number of awards varies. Reneweable; must maintain 2.5 GPA. more...

Centenary T.O.P.S. Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen students attending Centenary College of Louisiana full-time. This award supplements the TOPS funds of forty selected students with institutional funding to raise a students total financial package to the level of full tuition. Student must meet all basic State requirements for TOPS, as well as: ACT score of 27; GPA of 3.5; file the FAFSA; have completed 16.5 core units. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...
