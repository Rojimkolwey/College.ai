Duke University Scholarships


Angier B. Duke Memorial Scholarship
The Angier B. Duke Memorial Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Duke University. Recipients of this award will also spend a summer abroad in New College at Oxford University and will receive an additional $5000 for research or other educational enrichment programs. more...

Benjamin N. Duke Memorial Scholarship Program
The Benjamin N. Duke Memorial Scholarship Program is available to entering freshmen at Duke University. You must be a resident of North or South Carolina to be eligible for this award. Selection is based on academic achievement, leadership potential, initiative, and significant community involvement. more...

Black Church Studies And Latinx Studies Fellowship - Duke University
The Black Church Studies and Latinx Studies Fellowship is open to students entering Duke Divinity School. You must intend to pursue a certificate in Black Church Studies or a certificate in Latinx Studies to be eligible for this award. Additionally, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and a demonstrated engagement in the practice of ministry or service in other areas indicating compatible work or transferrable skills in African-American and/or Latinx communities OR a record of academic work that draws from the intellectual, theological, or practical projects of these contexts. more...

Board Of Governors Medical Scholarship Program (Bgms)
Scholarship for North Carolina residents who are graduate students enrolled at Wake Forest University School of Medicine, Duke University School of Medicine, East Carolina University Brody School of Medicine, or the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill School of Medicine. Students must have financial need, intend to practice medicine in North Carolina, and be nominated by one of the medical schools. Award amounts is for full tuition. This is a forgiveable loan, students must work in the field of Medicine in the state of North Carolina upon graduation from medical school. Renewable. more...

Gertrude B. Elion Mentored Medical Student Research Awards
Scholarship for female graduate students enrolled full-time at Duke University Medical Center, East Carolina University School of Medicine, the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill School of Medicine, or Wake Forest University Baptist Medical Center who are interested in a health-related research. Applicant must be have the support of a faculty mentor and have completed a minimum of one year of medical school. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Hitchings Fund For Health Research & Science Education
Annual award for Chatham, Durham, Orange, or Wake County, North Carolina residents who are pre-doctoral students enrolled at Duke University, North Carolina Central University, North Carolina State University, or the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill pursuing research work in the Health Sciences. Students must be entering their second year of graduate school. Applicants must have worked to help others with alcoholism, drug abuse, and addictive disorder treatment or with preventive education onf the subject of addition. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

M.L.King, Jr. Scholarship (M.Div., M.C.M.) - Duke Divinity School
The M.L.King, Jr. Scholarship (M.Div., M.C.M.) is open to students at Duke Divinity School. You must be a first-year ethnic minority student who demonstrates financial need, an outstanding promise for ministry and strong academic performance to qualify for this award. more...

Ray C. Petry Scholarship (M.T.S.)- Duke Divinity School
The Ray C. Petry Scholarship (M.T.S.) is available to entering students at Duke Divinity School. You must demonstrate outstanding promise for ministry and strong academic performance to qualify for this award. more...

Reginaldo Howard Scholarship
The Reginaldo Howard Scholarship is available to first - year students of African descent at Duke University. To be considered, you must demonstrate outstanding leadership ability, scholastic achievement, community involvement, and evidence of serious commitment to a life of service to others. more...

Trinity Scholarship - Duke University
The Trinity Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Duke University who are residents of specific geographic areas of North Carolina. This is four- year award covers tuition, room and board and summer stipends for study, work or travel. You are considered eligible for this scholarship on the basis of outstanding academic achievement, extracurricular activities and personal qualities. more...

University Scholars Program - Duke University
The University Scholars Program is available to students at Duke University. You must demonstrate financial need, the ability to explore new academic horizons, an excitement for original research, collaborative thinking, and innovative scholarship to be eligible for this award. more...

Warren Thornhill Law School Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Duke University School of Law. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...
