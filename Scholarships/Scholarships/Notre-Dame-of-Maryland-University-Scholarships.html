Notre Dame of Maryland University Scholarships


Art Competition
The Art Competition is open to incoming first year and transfer students at the College of Notre Dame of Maryland. Students with artistic talents may submit a portfolio to be eligible for this award. more...

Endowed Scholarships - College Of Notre Dame Of Maryland
The Endowed Scholarships is open to incoming freshmen and transfer students at the College of Notre Dame of Maryland. Eligibility varies by each scholarship, excluding athletic participation to be eligible for this award. more...

Knott Scholarships
The Knott Scholarships is open to incoming first year catholic students at the College of Notre Dame of Maryland. You must be residing within the Archdiocese of Baltimore who have the highest levels academic excellence and consistent display of leadership and service to be eligible for this award. more...

Notre Dame Awards
The Notre Dame Awards is open to incoming first year and transfer students at the College of Notre Dame of Maryland. You must have strong academic records, co-curricular activities and community service to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarships
The Presidential Scholarships is open to incoming freshmen students at the College of Notre Dame of Maryland. You must have a minimum 3.7 GPA and SAT score of 1350 or higher to qualify for this full-tuition award. more...

Transfer Scholarship - College Of Notre Dame Of Maryland
The Transfer Scholarship is open to transfer students at the College of Notre Dame of Maryland. You must have completed an associate's degree and have a minimum GPA of 3.25 to qualify for this award. Students should have completed all courses for the degree at one institution and followed an educational track from high school to community college to Notre Dame. more...
