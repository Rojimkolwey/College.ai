Manchester University Scholarships


Church Matching Scholarship Program
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Manchester College in Indiana. Applicants must be Christian students participating in Church. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Connections Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students planning to attend Manchester College in Indiana. Applicants must be a member of the Church of the Brethren -or- have a parent who attended Manchester College. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Manchester Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Manchester College in Indiana. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Multicultural Student Leadership Scholarship
Scholarship for minority undergraduate students attending Manchester College in Indiana. Applicants must have academic potential, leadership, character and motivation. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Non-Profit Leadership Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend Manchester College in Indiana, with an interest in non-profit management. Applicants must rank in the top 49% of their class, and have a minimum SAT score of 1050 or ACT score of 23. Candidate should have taken college prep curriculum in high school. Due date and number of awards varies. more...
