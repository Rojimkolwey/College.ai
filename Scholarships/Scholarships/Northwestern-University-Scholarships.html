Northwestern University Scholarships


Citigroup Fellows Program (C)
Scholarship for Hispanic juniors or seniors enrolled full-time at select colleges and are majoring in Business, Business Administration, Economics, or Finance. Students must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents, have a minimum 3. CGPA and file the FAFSA. Selection is based on academic achievement, personal strengths, leadership, and financial need. Select colleges are: Columbia University, Cornell University, Dartmouth College, Duke University, Georgetown University, Harvard University, New York University, Northwestern University, Princeton University, Rutgers University, Stanford University, University of California-Los Angeles, University of Chicago, University of Pennsylvania, University of Virginia, University of Texas-Austin, or Yale University Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Harris Family Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for graduate students attending Northwestern University accepted into the Master of Science in Marital and Family Therapy Program. Applicants must be committed to working in communities of underserved populations upon degree completion. Consideration may be given to financial need. Renewable. more...

Vedder Price Diversity Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students enrolled in Chicago-Kent College of Law, DePaul University, Loyola University Chicago, Northwestern University, University of Chicago or University of Illinois pursuing a Law degree.
