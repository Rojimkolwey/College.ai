D'Youville College Scholarships


Academic Initiative Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen attending DYouville College in Buffalo, New York. Applicants must be a U.S. citizen, have a minimum SAT score of 1000 or ACT score of 21-23. Award amount is equal to 25% off tuition and 50% off room and board, or 25% off tuition for commuters. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

The Achievement Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at D'Youville College. You must have an ACT score between 19-23 or a SAT score between 900-1090 and a grade average between 80-84 to be considered for this award. You must also demonstrate leadership and community service. more...

Honors Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen attending DYouville College in Buffalo, New York. Applicants must be a U.S. citizen, have a minimum 3.5 GPA, SAT score of 1100 or ACT score of 24, and rank in the top 15% of their graduating class. Award amount is equal to 50% off tuition and 25% off room and board, or for commuters, 50% off tuition. Renewable. more...
