SUNY Westchester Community College Scholarships


Alpha Group Scholarship - Wcc
The Alpha Group Scholarship is available to continuing students attending Westchester Community College. You must be enrolled in a business-related curriculum to be eligible for this award. more...

Arthur James Hackett Scholarship - Wcc
The Arthur James Hackett Scholarship is available to graduating students attending Westchester Community College. You must be majoring in engineering science or engineering technologies and planning to continue your education in engineering science or engineering technologies to be eligible for this award. more...

Harold L. Drimmer Scholarship - Wcc
The Harold L. Drimmer Scholarship is available to graduating students attending Westchester Community College. You must be planning to continue your education at a four-year university and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Lawrence Nugent Memorial Award
The Lawrence Nugent Memorial Award is available to graduating students attending Westchester Community College. You must be actively involved in student government, demonstrate academic achievement, and be planning to continue your education at a four-year institution to be eligible for this award. more...

Lim Ebon And Tan Le Eng Scholarship - Wcc
The Lim Ebon and Tan Le Eng Scholarship is available to continuing students attending Westchester Community College. You must be enrolled in a mathematics, engineering, or physical sciences curriculum; have completed at least 24 credits with a minimum GPA of 3.0; and be enrolled for a minimum of six credits during the term of the award to be eligible for this scholarship. In addition, recipients are encouraged to assist other students as a math tutor. more...

Lisa Mancher Memorial Scholarship
The Lisa Mancher Memorial Scholarship is available to students attending Westchester Community College. You must be a student with a disability and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

N. Anne Mckiernan Memorial Scholarship - Wcc
The N. Anne Mckiernan Memorial Scholarship is available to second-year female students attending Westchester Community College. You must be enrolled in the associate degree nursing program to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to students who have demonstrated an interest in behavioral psychology. more...

Nancy Jo Abeles Honors Scholarship - Wcc
The Nancy Jo Abeles Honors Scholarship is available to continuing students attending Westchester Community College. You must be enrolled in the WCC Honors Program, have successfully completed two honors courses and plan to take a minimum of two honors courses during the year of the award to be eligible for this scholarship. more...

Nancy Jo Abeles Scholarship - Wcc
The Nancy Jo Abeles Scholarship is available to entering full-time freshmen attending Westchester Community College. You must demonstrate academic achievement to be eligible for this award. more...

Olivia J. Moshier Memorial Scholarship - Wcc
The Olivia J. Moshier Memorial Scholarship is available to entering freshmen attending Westchester Community College. You must be planning to pursue a business or fine arts degree and demonstrate academic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...
