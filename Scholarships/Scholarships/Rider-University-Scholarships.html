Rider University Scholarships


Acting Scholarship
Scholarship for first-time freshmen accepted at Rider University who have exceptional theatrical talent. Applicants must have an interest in performing in Riders theater productions. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount is full-tuition. Contact the Fine Arts Department for more information and/or application. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Rider University
The Dean's Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Rider University. You must have a minimum 1050 SAT / 23 ACT score and a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen accepted at Rider University who are designated as New Jersey Distinguished Scholars (Non-Urban Category). Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Founder's Scholarship - Rider University
The Founder's Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Rider University. You must have a minimum 1000 SAT / 21 ACT score and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Rider University

Provost Scholarship - Rider University
