Bethel University Scholarships


Alumni Legacy Award
The Alumni Legacy Award is available to new students at Bethel College. You must have parent(s) or grandparent(s) that are Bethel alumni to be eligible for this award. more...

Art, Music And Theatre Arts Awards
The Art, Music and Theatre Arts Awards is available to students at Bethel College. You must have outstanding performance or achievement through an audition or portfolio review to be eligible for this award. more...

Athletic Grant - Bethel College
The Athletic Grant is available to students at Bethel College. You must participate in varsity athletics at Bethel to be eligible for this award. more...

Bethel Grant - Bethel College
The Bethel Grant is available to students at Bethel College. You must demonstrate leadership, talent, academic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Christian Workers Grant
The Christian Workers Grant is available to students at Bethel College who demonstrate financial need. You must be the dependent of a full time employee of other church denominations or para-church organizations to be eligible for this award. more...

Family Grant - Bethel College
The Family Grant is available to students at Bethel College. You must have two or more members of your family attending Bethel on a full-time basis to be eligible for this award. more...

International Student Scholarship - Bethel College
The International Student Scholarship is available to international students at Bethel College. Selection is based on academic ability, financial need and the mission of the college to prepare students for service in their own county. more...

Lilly Endowment Recognition
The Lilly Endowment Recognition is available to students at Bethel College. You must be a Indiana Lilly Endowment Scholars who designated Bethel College to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Missionary Pastor Grant - Bethel College
The Missionary Pastor Grant is available to students at Bethel College. You must be a dependent of a full-time Missionary Church pastor or missionary and you must live on campus to be eligible for this award. more...

National Merit Recognition Scholarship - Bethel College
The National Merit Recognition Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen students at Bethel College. You must be a National Merit, National Achievement, National Hispanic Scholar Semi-Finalist or Finalist who designated Bethel College as your first choice to be eligible for this renewable award. more...
