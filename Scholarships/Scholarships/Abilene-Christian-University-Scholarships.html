National Merit Semi-Finalist Scholarship - Abilene Christian University
The National Merit Semi-Finalist Scholarship is open to undergraduate students entering Abilene Christian University who are National Semi-Merit Finalists. To be considered for this award, you must have fewer than 12 credit hours and list ACU as your first choice with the National Merit Corporation. The award provides full tuition and may be renewed for up to eight semesters. more...
