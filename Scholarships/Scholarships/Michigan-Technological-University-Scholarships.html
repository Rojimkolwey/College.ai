Michigan Technological University Scholarships


Athletic Grant - Michigan Technological University
The Athletic Grant is available to students at Michigan Technological University who are chosen to participate in MTU intercollegiate athletics. You must demonstrate athletic excellence and be nominated by the MTU coaches to be eligible for this award. This award provides up to full- time tuition, room and board, and book allowance. more...

Michigan Tech Ets-Impress Scholarship
The Michigan Tech ETS-IMPRESS Scholarship is available to incoming freshman students and transfer students who plan to major in electrical engineering technology, mechanical engineering technology, or computer network and system administration. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Michigan Tech Leading Scholar Award-Non-Resident
The Michigan Tech Leading Scholar Award-Non-Resident is available to incoming freshman students at Michigan Technological University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.3, and show exemplary leadership attributes and outstanding scholarly achievement to be eligible for this renewable award. Selection is based on academic merit and essay submission. more...

Michigan Tech Leading Scholar Award-Resident
The Michigan Tech Leading Scholar Award-Resident is available to incoming freshman students at Michigan Technological University. You must be a Michigan resident, have a minimum GPA of 3.3, and show exemplary leadership attributes and outstanding scholarly achievement to be eligible for this renewable award. Selection is based on academic merit and essay submission. more...

National Merit Scholarship - Michigan Technological University
The National Merit Scholarship is available to entering freshmen students at Michigan Technological University. You must be a U.S. citizen, member of the current year's high school graduating class, and a National Merit Scholarship Finalist to be eligible for this award. You must also have listed MTU as your first choice university with the National Merit Corporation. more...

Wade H. Mccree, Jr. Incentive Scholarship
The Wade H. McCree, Jr. Incentive Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen students at Michigan Technological University who are Michigan residents. You must have been enrolled in a Detroit public high school to be eligible for this award. more...
