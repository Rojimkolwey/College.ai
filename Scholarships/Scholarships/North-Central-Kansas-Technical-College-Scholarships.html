North Central Kansas Technical College Scholarships


Nck Tech Departmental Scholarship
The NCK Tech Departmental Scholarship is available to first-year students at North Central Kansas Technical College who are pursing an Associate of Applied Science degree. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Nck Tech Kansas Promise Scholarship
The NCK Tech Kansas Promise Scholarship is available to students at North Central Kansas Technical College. You must study nursing, automotive technology/collision technology, building construction, carpentry/cabinetmaking, diesel technology, electrical technology, heavy equipment operation, IT, pharmacy technician, plumbing, HVAC, telecommunications, or welding to be eligible for this award. Additionally, you must be a U.S. citizen, a resident of Kansas, and demonstrate financial need. more...

Nck Tech Technical Honor Scholarship
The NCK Tech Technical Honor Scholarship is available to incoming freshman students at North Central Kansas Technical College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.75 to be eligible for this award. more...
