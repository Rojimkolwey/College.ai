Cayuga County Community College Scholarships


Anne Drancsak Gallager Memorial Scholarship
The Anne Drancsak Gallager Memorial Scholarship is available to returning sophomore students at Cayuga Community College. You must be majoring in nursing and demonstrate academic achievement to qualify for this award. more...

Claire E. And Frank A. Fama Sophomore Scholarship
The Claire E. and Frank A. Fama Sophomore Scholarship is available to returning sophomore students at Cayuga Community College. You must have demonstrated academic excellence during your freshman year to qualify for this award. Preference is given to students majoring in the following fields in order: engineering, mathematics, computer science, computer information science, computer hardware/software design, mechanical technology or electrical technology. more...

Daniel C. Labeille Grant
The Daniel C. Labeille Grant is available to students at Cayuga Community College. You must display an aptitude for a career in the arts to qualify for this award. more...

Dellaphene Winters Memorial Scholarship
The Dellaphene Winters Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Cayuga Community College. You must be a resident of Cayuga County and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Fulton Savings Bank High School Scholarship
The Fulton Savings Bank High School Scholarship is available to entering full-time students at Cayuga Community College. You must have been the highest ranking graduate at one of the following high schools to qualify for this award: Fulton, Phoenix, Central Square, C. W. Baker, Hannibal, Mexico, Pulaski, Sandy Creek, Altmar-Parish-Williamstown or Oswego. more...

Honey And Dr. Joseph F. Karpinski, Sr. Memorial Scholarship
The Honey and Dr. Joseph F. Karpinski, Sr. Memorial Scholarship is available to non-traditional students at Cayuga Community College. You must be majoring in nursing and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Jack M. Hayden, Jr. Memorial Scholarship
The Jack M. Hayden, Jr. Memorial Scholarship is available to returning full-time sophomore students at Cayuga Community College. You must be majoring in a technology field and demonstrate academic progress and financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Kenneth Jahn And Margaret Lesch Memorial Scholarship
The Kenneth Jahn and Margaret Lesch Memorial Scholarship is available to entering students at Cayuga Community College. You must be a graduate of Auburn High School and demonstrate academic success to qualify for this award. more...

Patricia M. Callahan Scholarship
The Patricia M. Callahan Scholarship is available to full-time returning sophomore students at Cayuga Community College. You must be majoring in early childhood education and demonstrate academic excellence and financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Preston H. Thomas Memorial Scholarship
The Preston H. Thomas Memorial Scholarship is available to returning sophomore students at Cayuga Community College. You must be majoring in mechanical technology design and drafting to qualify for this award. more...

Vincent M. Klein Scholarship
The Vincent M. Klein Scholarship is available to full-time entering students at Cayuga Community College. You must be majoring in business and be a graduate of a Cayuga County high school to qualify for this award. more...
