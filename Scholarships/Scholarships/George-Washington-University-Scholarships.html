George Washington University Scholarships


Academic Achievement Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must have a minimum 3.5 CGPA. Special consideration given to graduating high school seniors. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Arthur J. Ellis Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design majoring in Photojournalism. Students must demonstrate academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. A portfolio must be included with application packet. more...

Bender Scholarship To The University Of Cambridge
Scholarship for graduating seniors and third year law students attending George Washington University pursuing studies at the University of Cambridge. Applicants must have participated in the Rhodes and/or British Marshall competitions, and have high academic achievement, leadership skills or potential and community service. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Corcoran College Of Art & Design Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must have financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Corcoran College Of Art & Design Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must demonstrate academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Corcoran College Of Art & Design Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must have financial need. Number of awards may vary. A FAFSA and a statement of purpose must be included with application packet. Renewable. Contact the colleges admissions office or Scholastic for more information and/or application. more...

Delaware College Of Art & Design Partnership Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must be transferring from the Delaware College of Art and Design. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

District Scholars Program
The District Scholars Program is open to full - time, first - year undergraduate students at George Washington University. You must be a resident of the District and qualify for the D.C. Tuition Assistance Grant to be eligible for this award. more...

Elliott Engineering Honor Scholarships
Scholarship for sophomores, juniors or seniors transferring to George Washington University pursuing a major in Engineering. Applicants must have a minimum of 30 transferable credit hours with a minimum of 3 credits in chemistry or physics and 6 credits in calculus or higher math. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Endowed Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

George Washington Guaranteed Grant
Scholarship for full-time incoming undergraduate students enrolling at George Washington University. Applicants must demonstrate financial need. Parents and students Federal Income Tax Return must be included with application packet. Students must file the FAFSA. Renewable. more...

Gw Family Tuition Grant
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending George Washington University. Applicants must have one or more siblings enrolled at George Washington University. Parents federal tax return must be included with application packet. Number of awards may vary. Award amount is half tuition. Renewable. more...

J.B. & Maurice C. Shapiro Scholarship To The University Of Oxford
Scholarship for graduating seniors attending George Washington University accepted to Oxford. Applicants must have applied for the Rhodes or British Marshall Scholarships. Due date and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

On-Campus Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must have financial need and reside in Corcoran College housing. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Pembroke-Gw Program
Scholarship for juniors attending George Washington University pursuing an opportunity to study at Pembroke College in Oxford. Due date and award amount may vary. more...

Presidential Academic Scholarships (Freshmen)
Scholarship for incoming freshmen who are National Merit, National Hispanic Scholars, or National Achievement finalists enrolling at George Washington University. Due date, number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Presidential Academic Scholarships (Transfers)
Scholarship for sophomores, juniors or seniors who are Phi Theta Kappa or Alpha Beta Gamma members transferring to George Washington University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.7 GPA and have a minimum of 56 transferable hours. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Presidential Arts Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at George Washington University. Applicants must show promise in the fine arts and in music, theatre or dance. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Presidential Recognition Award
Scholarship for juniors or seniors attending George Washington University. Applicants must be currently receiving a merit award, have completed a minimum of 60 credit hours ar GW and have a minimum 3.8 GPA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Presidents Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must demonstrate academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Randolph C. Shaw Graduate Fellowship
The Randolph C. Shaw Graduate Fellowship is available to students pursuing an advanced degree in the Environmental Law Program at George Washington University Law School. You must be willing to assist the the director of Environmental Law Program on a part-time basis throughout the academic year to be eligible for this full-tuition award. more...

Scholastic Art Award Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the Corcoran College of Art and Design. Students must have been named by the National Scholastic jury. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Thomas Buergenthal Scholarship
The Thomas Buergenthal is available to students pursuing an advanced degree at George Washington University Law School. You must have graduated from a law school outside of the U.S. and demonstrate both academic merit and financial to be eligible for this award. more...
