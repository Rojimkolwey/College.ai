The University of Texas Rio Grande Valley Scholarships


Excellence Scholarship - University Of Texas-Pan American
The Excellence Scholarship is available to full-time undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Inter National Bank Business Scholarship
The Inter National Bank Business Scholarship is available to full-time junior and senior students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must be majoring in accounting or finance, be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident, and have a minimum 2.8 GPA to be eligible for this award. This award also includes an optional summer internship with Inter National Bank. more...

Mario Medrano Endowed Scholarship
The Mario Medrano Endowed Scholarship is available to full-time juniors and seniors at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must be majoring in political science, be a U.S. citizen and have a minimum 2.8 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Pharmacy Scholars Program Scholarship - Utpa
The Pharmacy Scholars Program Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must be majoring in pharmacy, be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident, and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - University Of Texas-Pan America
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to full-time transfer students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa, have your associate's degree, and have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Utpa Housing Scholarship
The UTPA Housing Scholarship is available to full-time entering freshman students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must live on campus to be eligible for this award. more...

Wilcox Engineering Scholarship
The Wilcox Engineering Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must be majoring in engineering and have a minimum 2.75 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Wilcox Music Scholarship
The Wilcox Music Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must be majoring in music and have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Women's Athletic Fundraisers Scholarship - Utpa
The Women's Athletic Fundraisers Scholarship is available to students at the University of Texas-Pan American. You must participate in women's athletics and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
