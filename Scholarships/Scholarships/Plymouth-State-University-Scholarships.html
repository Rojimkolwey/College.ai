Plymouth State University Scholarships


Barbara Boyce Oakes Scholarship Fund
The Barbara Boyce Oakes Scholarship Fund is available to female elementary education students at Plymouth State University. You must be a New Hampshire resident and demonstrate financial need and merit to be considered for this award. more...

Regina E. Thornton Memorial Scholarship
The Regina E. Thornton Memorial Scholarship is available to business majors attending Plymouth State University. You must demonstrate financial need and be a resident of the Manchester, New Hampshire area to be considered for this award. more...

Roi M. White Memorial Fund
The Roi M. White Memorial Fund is available to theatre arts majors at Plymouth State University. You must demonstrate academic achievement as well as participation in theatre-related activities and contributions to PSU theatre to be considered for this award. more...

Scott Descoteaux Scholarship Fund
The Scott Descoteaux Scholarship Fund is available to students majoring in business at Plymouth State University. You must have graduated from Nashua Senior High School to be considered for this award. This award is based on merit and financial need. Preference is given to the child of a singe parent. more...

Van A. Hartman/Vernon Kelly Scholarship
The Van A. Hartman/Vernon Kelly Scholarship is available to physical education majors attending Plymouth State University. You must be enrolled in a four-year degree program and exemplify the finest in scholarship and citizenship to be considered for this award. more...

William L. Taylor Memorial Fund
The William L. Taylor Memorial Fund is available to students at Plymouth State University who are majoring in one of the social science disciplines. more...
