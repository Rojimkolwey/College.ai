Andrew Alexander Scholarship
The Andrew Alexander Scholarship is open to underrepresented students at Ohio University, Athens campus. You must be majoring in journalism with an emphasis on newswriting and editing to be considered for this award. A minimum 3.0 GPA is also required. more...

Athens County Education Award
The Athens County Education Award is open to full-time sophomores, juniors, seniors and graduate students at Ohio University. You must be majoring in accounting if you are an undergraduate and physical therapy if you are a graduate student. First preference is given to graduates of Athens High School. This award may be renewed. more...

Caribbean Student Scholarship - Ohio University

Charlotte E. Latourette Outdoor Recreation Scholarship
The Charlotte E. Latourette Outdoor Recreation Scholarship is available to students majoring in outdoor education and camping at Ohio University. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Classified Senate Academic Scholarship - Ou
The Classified Senate Academic Scholarship is open to full-time undergraduate students at Ohio University, Athens. Continuing students must meet the University-established scholarship criteria. Entering first-year students must have a minimum 28 ACT/1240 SAT score and rank in the top 20 percent of their high school class to be eligible for this award. more...

Kincade Latin Scholarship is available to students majoring in any of the four tracks (Latin, Greek, Latin and Greek or Classical Civilization) of the classics program at Ohio University, Athens. Ohio University Women's Club Of Cleveland Scholarship
The Ohio University Women's Club of Cleveland Scholarship is available to full-time incoming first-year students at Ohio University. You must be a female student from the Greater Cleveland area and have a minimum 25 ACT/1140 SAT score to qualify for this award. You must also have a minimum 3.5 GPA and rank in the upper 20 percent of your high school class. more...

Robert And Phyllis Butner Scholarship

Robert L. Henning School Of Theater Scholarship

Selection will also be based on exceptional talent and academic achievement in theater. more...
