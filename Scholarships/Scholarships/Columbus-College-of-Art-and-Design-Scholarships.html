Columbus College of Art and Design Scholarships


Arts Recognition & Talent Search Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Columbus College of Art & Design who have been recognized at the national level in the National Foundation for the Advancement of the Arts Competition. Selection based on artistic talent. Award is for two-year, full-tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact high school art teacher or guidance counselor for more information. more...

Batelle Scholars Program
Scholarship for residents of Franklin, Pickaway, Licking, Madison, Union, Delaware or Fairfield counties, Ohio who are graduating seniors enrolling at Columbus College of Art & Design. Selection based on academic achievement, leadership and portfolio. Finalists must attend an interview. Renewable. more...

Ccad Portfolio Competition Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors interested in attending Columbus College of Art & Design. Students must submit 10-15 pieces of art work, such as drawings and paintings (especially from observation), a recommendation letter and official high school transcripts. Renewable. more...

Columbus College Of Art & Design Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at the Columbus College of Art and Design. Due date and award amount may vary. A FAFSA and a statement of purpose must be included with application packet. Renewable. Contact the college or Scholastic for more information and/or application. more...

Dorothy Wirth Holden Scholarship
Scholarship for female residents of Central Ohio who are undergraduate students attending Capital University, Columbus College of Art and Design, Columbus State Community College, Franklin University, Ohio Dominican University, Ohio State University or Otterbein College. Awards will be based on financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Ohio Governors Youth Art Exhibition
Scholarship for Ohio residents who are graduating high school seniors enrolling at Columbus College of Art & Design that competed at the state level in the Ohio Governors Youth Art Exhibition. Selection based on artistic talent. Number of awards may vary. Contact high school art teacher or guidance counselor for more information. more...

Scholastic Nomination Art Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend the Columbus College of Art & Design. Student must have been selected by the Scholastic Art Awards jury as a Scholarship Nominee from their National Portfolio Competition. Scholarships are merit-based, & equal full-tuition for 2 years; second year renewal based on first year achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact the Admissions Office for more information & application. more...
