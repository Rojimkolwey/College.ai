William Woods University Scholarships


A+ Award
Scholarship for first time freshmen enrolling at William Woods University. Applicants must have graduated from an A+ certified high school and have completed the requirements for the A+ program. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Amy Shelton Mcnutt Scholarship
Annual award for incoming freshmen at William Woods University. Applicants must have a minimum 31 ACT or 1380 SAT score, a minimum 3.85 high school GPA and demonstrate leadership qualities and service. Award amount is 75% of tuition, room and board. Renewable. more...

Biological Sciences Award
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling full-time at William Woods University pursuing a major in the Biological Sciences including Pre-Med, Pre-Vet, Pre-Dentistry, Pre-Nursing, Pre-Physical Therapy, Conservation, Wildlife Management, Ecology, etc. Applicants must have a minimum 28 ACT or 1260 SAT, rank in the top 15% of the high school class, leadership skills and self-motivation. Number of awards may vary. more...

Board Of Associates-Fulton Colleges Fund Scholarship
Scholarship for Callaway County, Missouri residents who are full-time undergraduate students attending William Woods University. Applicants must demonstrate academic excellence and involvement in extracurricular activities. Renewable. more...

Callaway County Alumni Association Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending William Woods. Applicants who are entering freshmen must have a minimum 2.5 GPA; returning WWU and transfer students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA. Consideration will be given to applicants in the following order: Children of a WWU Alumni who lives in Callaway County; relative of any WWU Alumni; Callaway County resident for a minimum of two years. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Communications Award
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending William Woods University majoring in Communications. Selection is based on academic achievement, talent and potential. An essay must be included with application packet. An interview is required. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Disciples Of Christ Grant
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students who are children of ordained ministers in the Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) attending William Woods University. Number of awards may vary. Award amount is half-tuition. more...

Distinguished Scholar Award
Scholarship for full-time freshmen enrolling at William Woods University pursuing a first undergraduate degree. Applicants must have a minimum 30 ACT or 1340 SAT score and a 3.85 high school GPA and demonstrate leadership and service. An essay must be included with application packet. An interview is required. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Equestrian Science Award
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending William Woods University majoring in Equestrian Science. Applicants must show academic achievement and equestrian riding skills at at quality level. A videotape of students riding must be included with application packet. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Honors Scholar Award
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students accepted to the Honors Program attending William Woods University. Applicants must have a minimum 27 ACT or 1220 SAT score or a minimum 3.5 GPA, and exhibit leadership skills and self motivation. An essay must be included with application packet. An interview is required. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Human Performance Award
Scholarship for full-time freshmen attending William Woods University pursuing a major in the Department of Human Performance. Applicants must have a minimum 22 ACT or 1010 SAT score, rank in the top 50% of the high school class, and exhibit leadership skills and self-motivation. Number of awards may vary. more...

Lowry Aid Fund Scholarship
Scholarship for Missouri residents who are full-time undergraduate students attending William Woods University. Applicants must be members of the Christian Church (Disciples of Christ), and demonstrate financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Ovid Bell Freshman Scholarship
Scholarship for Callaway County, Missouri residents who are freshmen enrolling at William Woods University. Applicants must show academic excellence and involvement in extracurricular activities. Preference is given to children of Ovid Bell employees. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Presidential Scholar

An interview is required. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
