Simpson University Scholarships


A.B. Simpson Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Simpson University. Applicants must have participated in the Bible Quizzing or Operation Good News during the summer to be eligible. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

A.B. Simpson Leadership Grant
The A.B. Simpson Leadership Grant is available to students at Simpson University. This Simpson University foundational scholarship is awarded at varying amount to all full-time undergraduate students who apply and are eligible to receive financial aid. more...

Athletic Scholarship - Simpson University
The Athletic Scholarship is available to students at Simpson University. You must be an exceptional athlete and have the recommendation of the athletic department to be eligible for this award. more...

Christian Worker-Dependent Grant
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students attending Simpson University who are dependents of a full-time Missionary or Pastor. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Christian Workers Dependent Grant - Simpson University
The Christian Workers Dependent Grant is available to students at Simpson University. You must be a dependent of a full-time Christian service worker and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Church Matching Grant - Simpson University
The Church Matching Grant is available to students at Simpson University. You must be eligible for, and have received, financial aid as an official act of your church to be eligible for this award. more...

Early Bird Bonus Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduates accepted to Simpson University during early admissions. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Immediate Family Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Simpson University who also has an immediate family member attending SU. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Immediate Family Grant - Simpson University
The Family Grant is open to full-time students at Simpson University. You must be one of two immediate family members attending Simpson simultaneously in the traditional undergraduate program to be eligible for this award. more...

International Missionary Dependent Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Simpson University who are dependents of missionaries serving outside of the continental United States. Applicants must have financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Ministry Dependent Grant - Simpson University
The Ministry Dependent Grant is open to students at Simpson University. You must be the dependent of a full-time Christian Pastor or Missionary to be considered for this award. more...

Music Scholarship - Simpson University
The Music Scholarship is available to students entering Simpson University. You must be an exception musician and have the recommendation of the Music Chair/Department to be eligible for this award. more...

President's Scholarship - Simpson University
The President's Scholarship is available to students entering Simpson University. You must have at least a 3.7 GPA and a minimum 1300/30 SAT/ACT score to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Promising Musician Award - Simpson University
The Promising Musician Award is open to students at Simpson University. You must be an exceptional musician to be eligible for this award. more...

Provost Academic Merit Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Simpson University. Award is based on GPA (minimum 3.0) and ACT/SAT scores (minimum ACT 21/SAT 1000). Award amount depends on varying combinations of GPA/test scores. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Red Hawks Athletic Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Simpson University who participate in the schools athletic program. Selection is based on athletic ability and potential. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...
