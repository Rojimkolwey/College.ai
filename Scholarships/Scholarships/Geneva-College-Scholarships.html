Geneva College Scholarships


Alumni Scholarship
The Alumni Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Geneva College. You must have a GPA between 3.3 and 3.69 to be eligible for this award. more...

Athletic Grants
Scholarship for incoming full-time freshmen accepted to Geneva College who have demonstrated athletic ability. Selection is based on ability and financial need. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Beaver Falls High School Grant
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors of Beaver Falls High School enrolling at Geneva College. Selection is based on financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Renewable. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

C. M. Lee Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming full-time freshmen accepted to Geneva College. Applicants must have high academic standing. Renewable. more...

Christian Service Grant
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students who are dependent children of parents in full-time Christian service and are attending Geneva College. Applicants must be unmarried. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount is 15% of tuition. more...

Church Denomination Grant (Rpcna Students)
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students who are members of an RPCNA church and are attending Geneva College. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount is 20% of tuition. more...

Founders Scholarship
The Founders Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Geneva College. You must have a GPA between 3.7 and 3.99 to be eligible for this award. more...

Geneva Grants
Scholarship for incoming full-time freshmen accepted to Geneva College. Applicants must have financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Renewable. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Geneva Scholar Academic Awards
Scholarship for entering full-time freshmen at Geneva College. Selection is based on SAT/ACT scores, high school grades and excellence, honors, merit or recognition. Renewable. Number of awards may vary. more...

Geneva Scholar For Ptk Members
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate transfer students who are members of Phi Theta Kappa enrolling at Geneva College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 transfer GPA and a minimum of 34 credits earned. Number of awards may vary. more...

Graduate Of Christian School Or Home School
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending Geneva College. Applicants must be graduates of a Christian school or home school. Due date and number of awards may vary. Award amount is 15% of tuition. more...

Henry Mancini Musical Theatre Awards
Scholarship for high school juniors or seniors planning to attend Geneva College the fall after high school graduation and major in an Arts Program. Applicants must participate in a musical entered in the Mancini Awards including cast, sets, tech, orchestra or crew. Number of awards may vary. more...

Northwood Award
The Northwood Award is available to entering freshmen at Geneva College. You must have a GPA between 2.8 and 3.29 to be eligible for this award. more...

Pro Christo Scholarship
The Pro Christo Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Geneva College. You must have at least a 4.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
