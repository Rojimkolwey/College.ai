Austin Peay State University Scholarships


Adma Hammam Shibley Memorial Scholarship In Library Science
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Penn State University planning a career in Library Science. First consideration is given to students planning a career in the field of library science; second consideration is given to stuents enrolled in the College or the Liberal Arts. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Ava Faltz-Miller Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time college juniors attending Penn State University with a demonstrated interest in Library Studies, Information Services or Librarianship. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Balanced Man Scholarship
The Balanced Man Scholarship is available to male students at Austin Peay State University. You must demonstrate academic excellence, leadership skills, and a commitment to your health and well - being to be considered for this award. more...

Bobbi Coulter Rich Scholarship (Chronic)
Scholarship for full- or half-time undergraduate or graduate students with end stage renal disease or are awaiting or have received a kidney transplant who are attending Penn State University. If there are no qualified applicants consideration will be given to students with a chronic renal condition or disorder. Applicants must have been enrolled as a resident instruction student at any Penn State location for a minimum of two semesters. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Charles T. & Ella Fowler Douds Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for disable full-time undergraduate or graduate students attending Penn State University. Applicants must have demonstrated need, have been enrolled as a resident instruction student at any Penn State location for a minimum of two semesters, have superior academic records, and show promist of success in their chosen field. Preference is given to students with chronic health problems, sight or hearing impairments, or inability to walk or other physical disabilities. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

College Of Earth & Mineral Sciences-Deans Freshmen Scholarship
Scholarship for entering freshmen at Penn State Univeristy enrolled in the College of Earth and Mineral Sciences. Applicants must have a minimum 3.7 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1250. All students admitted to the College are automatically considered for the award. Nonrenewable. more...

David R. Martino Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for disabled full-time undergraduate or graduate students attending Penn State University. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Delta Chi Disabled Student Grant Fund

Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...
