Scholarship for juniors transferring to Knox College. Applicants must have completed an associate degree at Carl Sandburg College in Galesburg, have a minimum 3.5 GPA in a strong liberal arts curriculum and be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
