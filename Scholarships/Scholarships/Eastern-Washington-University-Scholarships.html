Ewu Special Honors Scholarship
The EWU Special Honors Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Eastern Washington University. You must have been named a Washington Scholar or be a National Merit Finalist or Semi-Finalist to be eligible for this award. more...

Gary Brown Memorial Scholarship - Eastern Washington University
The Gary Brown Memorial Scholarship is open to students at Eastern Washington University. You must be majoring in electronic media journalism or photo- journalism and have a minimum 2.75 GPA (3.0 GPA in major) to be eligible for this award. more...

Jeremy Wilkes Balanced Man Scholarship
The Jeremy Wilkes Balanced Man Scholarship is available to male students at Eastern Washington University. You must demonstrate academic excellence, leadership skills, and a commitment to your health and well - being to be considered for this award. more...

Keith Kelly Memorial Scholarship
The Keith Kelly Memorial Scholarship is open to juniors and seniors at Eastern Washington University. You must be majoring in film, have at least a 3.0 in major GPA and a 2.75 overall GPA and have graduated from a high school in the Pacific Northwest to be eligible for this award. more...

Spokane District Dental Society Scholarship
