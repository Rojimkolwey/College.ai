Rocky Mountain College Scholarships


Activity Grant - Rocky Mountain College
The Activity Grant is available to students at Rocky Mountain College who demonstrate ability and talent in art, music, theater, creative writing, forensics, cheerleading/dance, or athletics. An audition or a portfolio review is also required to be considered for this award. more...

American Indian Scholarship - Rocky Mountain College
The American Indian Scholarship is available to incoming students at Rocky Mountain College who are of American Indian descent. You must have at least a 3.4 high school GPA to be eligible for this renewable full-tuition award. more...

Bridge To Bachelors American Indian Scholarship - Rmc
The Bridge to Bachelors American Indian Scholarship is available to incoming transfers at Rocky Mountain College who are transferring from a Montana Tribal College. more...

Bridge To Bachelors American Indian Scholarship Program
Scholarship for American Indian undergraduate students transferring to Rocky Mountain College. Students must have be transferring from Montana Tribal Colleges or Community College. An essay and Tibal Enrollment Card must be included with application packet. Students must file the FAFSA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Church Matching Fund Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Rocky Mountain College. Students local church must be making a financial contribute to the students education. A letter from the church stating this commitment must be included with application packet. more...

Creative Writing Activity Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Rocky Mountain College majoring or minoring in Literary Studies or Writing. A writing portfolio must be included with application packet. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Rocky Mountain College
The Dean's Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen and transfer students at Rocky Mountain College who have at least a 2.5 GPA. more...

Family Grants - Rocky Mountain College
The Family Grants are available to students at Rocky Mountain College who have at least one other family member simultaneously enrolled at RMC during the same academic year. more...

Montana Valedictorian Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen who are Montana Valedictorians enrolling at Rocky Mountain College. An essay must be included with application packet. An interview is required. Renewable. more...

Music Department Activity Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students with musical talent attending Rocky Mountain College. An audition is required. Recipients must commit to participate in campus music programs. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Rocky Mountain College
The Presidential Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen and transfer students at Rocky Mountain College who have at least a 3.0 GPA and demonstrate high school and community leadership experience. more...

Rmc Daycare Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students with children attending Rocky Mountain College. Students must have need for child daycare and demonstrate need. Due date and award amount may vary. more...

Rmc Grant - Rocky Mountain College
The RMC Grants are available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Rocky Mountain College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Speech & Debate Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at Rocky Mountain College. Students must show talent in debate or competitive speech. Recipients must take COM 256 (Forensics) and participate in debate events. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Transfer Student Scholarship - Rocky Mountain College
The Transfer Student Scholarship is available to transfer students at Rocky Mountain College. You must have earned at least 27 semester hours with a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. The amount of this award will be determined by your GPA. more...

Trustee Scholarship - Rocky Mountain College
The Trustee Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen and transfer students at Rocky Mountain College who have at least a 3.5 GPA and demonstrate high school and community leadership experience to qualify for this award. more...

Winston Cox Memorial Scholarship
The Winston Cox Memorial Scholarship is available to students attending Rocky Mountain College. You must have and maintain a minimum 3.5 GPA and enroll in at least one course each semester in the field of business administration to be eligible for this award. more...
