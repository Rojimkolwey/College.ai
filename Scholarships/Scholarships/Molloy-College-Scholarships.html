Molloy College Scholarships


Catherine Umbach Giammarino Memorial Scholarship
Annual award for student who is a senior attending Molloy College full-time majoring in Music Therapy. Applicant must demonstrate a dedication to the field of Music Therapy, community service and involvement in campus organizations and clubs. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies (minimum of $500). more...

Dominican Academic Scholarships
Annual award for entering freshmen attending Molloy College full-time. Selection is based on academic achievement. Number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Dr. Kathleen Kilkelly Graduate Nursing Scholarship
Annual award for graduate students attending Molley College in New York full-time to major in Nursing. Applicant must be matriculated in the Master of Science Program in Nursing, completed 12 graduate level credits and have maintained a financial need as determined through the Office of Financial Aid. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Dual Degree For Registered Nurses Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students who are ineligible for the undergraduate Transfer Scholarship and will be attending Molley College in New York full-time to study Nursing. Applicant must have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Dual Degree For Second Degree Students Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate transfer students attending Molley College in New York full-time to major in Nursing. Applicant must have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Award is for the first two years of attendance or until completion of the sequence NUR230 and NUR231. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Fine Arts & Performing Arts Scholarships
Annual award for entering freshmen attending Molloy College full time to major in Fine Arts or Performing Arts. Applicant must have exceptional talent or have achieved proficiency in art, music or communication arts. Qualifications are demonstrated through audition, the submission of a portfolio or other documented experience. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Molloy Grants
Annual award for undergraduate students attending Molley College in New York full-time. Applicant must have a minimum 2.0 GPA, and demonstrated financial need (as determined through the financial aid forms). Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Nonrenewable; may reapply. more...

Molloy Scholars Program Award
Annual award for entering freshmen attending Molloy College full-time. Applicant must have a minimum of 95% high school average and a minimum SAT score of 1250 or a SAT score of 28. Award amount is equal to full tuition. Number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain 3.5 GPA. more...

Robert R. Curren Memorial Scholarship
Annual award for student who will be a senior attending Molloy College full-time majoring in Music/Music Therapy or an active Glee Club member. Applicant must be recommended by the director/Moderator/Glee Club Officers and Music Department Chairperson. Student must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and represent or do volunteer service for the Music Department. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Sister Anna Marie Hardenburgh Music Scholarship
Annual award for student who is a junior attending Molloy College full-time to major in Music. Applicant must demonstrate outstanding character, service and musicianship. Recipient is selected by the Music Department annually. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...
