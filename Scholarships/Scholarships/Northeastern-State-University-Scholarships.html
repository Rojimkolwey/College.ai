Northeastern State University Scholarships


Academic Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at Northeastern State University. Students must have a minimum 30 ACT or a minimum 3.8 GPA and rank in the top 4 percent of the class. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Advantage Transfer Scholars
Scholarship for sophomores, juniors or seniors transferring to Northeastern State University. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and have completed a minimum of 24 college hours. Preference is given to students transferring from a two-year institution. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Baccalaureate Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at Northeastern State University. Applicants must have a minimum 30 ACT and a minimum 3.5 GPA. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Collegiate Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at Northeastern State University. Students must have a minimum 27 ACT and a minimum 3.5 GPA. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Freshman Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at Northeastern State University. Students must have a 22-24 ACT and a minimum 3.25 GPA. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Redmen Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at Northeastern State University. Students must have a 25-26 ACT score and a minimum 3.25 GPA. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Regents Tuition Waiver
Scholarship for undergraduate students transferring to Northeastern State University. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Transfer Connections Scholars
Scholarship for sophomores, juniors or seniors transfering to Northeastern State University. Students must have a minimum 3.8 GPA and have completed a minimum of 48 college hours. Preference is given to transferring from a two-year institution. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Valedictorian Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at Northeastern State University. Students must have a minimum 23 ACT, a minimum 4.0 GPA and have a class rank of 1. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
