University of Missouri-Columbia Scholarships


Balanced Man Scholarship - University Of Missouri
The Balanced Man Scholarship is available to male students at the University of Missouri. You must demonstrate academic excellence, leadership skills, and a commitment to your health and well - being to be eligible for this award. more...

Black Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for Greater Kansas City, Missouri residents who are African-American graduates from a local Kansas City high school attending the University of Missouri, Kansas City. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Della Mabel Wright Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Bollinger or Stoddard counties, Missouri who are graduating high school seniors and plan to attend Southeast Missouri State University or the University of Missouri upon graduation. Applicant must be a U.S. citizen. Selection based on academic performance and financial need. Award amount and number of awards may vary. Renewable. Contact the universitys financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Dentistry Assistance
Scholarship for Kansas residents who are graduate students attending the University of Missouri in Kansas City pursuing studies in Dentistry. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Board of Regents or the University of Missouri for more information and/or application. more...

Dr. Thomas E. & Mary M. Ashley Scholarship Fund Medical Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students enrolled at the University of Missouri Medical School in Columbia. Students must have financial need. Preference given to students who obtained their pre-medical education in a Missouri college/university and are from Southwest Missouri. Number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...

George C. Scott Memorial Scholarship - Umc
The George C. Scott Memorial Scholarship is available to students at the University of Missouri at Columbia. You must be majoring in theater performance with an interest in acting or directing and have a minimum GPA of 2.6 to be eligible for this award. Selection is based on talent and commitment to the program. more...

H. Michael Coburn Legal Advocacy Fund
Scholarship for graduate students who are attending the University of Missouri, Kansas City Law School and secondary University of Missouri, Columbia, University of Kansas and Washburn University studying Law. Students must be finalists of Appellate advocacy competition and the top students in the UMKC trial advocacy program. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

James M. Miller Musical Theatre Scholarship
The James M. Miller Musical Theater Scholarship is available to theater majors at the University of Missouri at Columbia. You must be a student performer in musical theater to be eligible for this award. more...

John-Ross Berneche Scholarship
The John-Ross Berneche Scholarship is available to students at the University of Missouri at Columbia. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and be majoring in theater performance to be eligible for this award. more...

Kansas City Initiative Scholarship
Scholarship for minority residents of the Kansas City Metropolitan area who are undergraduate students attending a UNCF member college or university or the University of Missouri at Kansas City. Applicants who are entering freshmen must have a minimum 2.5 GPA; upperclassmen must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Students must submit the FAFSA. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Kansas City Securities Samuel M. Davidson Athletic Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from the Greater Kansas City area who are enrolled at the University of Missouri in Kansas City. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA. Number of awards may vary. more...

Optometry Scholarship
Scholarship for Kansas residents who are graduate students attending the Missouri School of Optometry, University of Missouri in St. Louis, Missouri or Southern College of Optometry in Memphis, Tennesse or School of Optometry at Northeastern State University, in Tahlequah, Oklahoma pursuing studies in Optometry. Students attending school in Tennessee or Oklahoma must provide service as an Optometrist in Kansas one year for each year of scholarship support. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Samuel C. Kraus, Jr. Memorial Scholarship

Sarah Beisner Memorial Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for Delta Gamma Sorority members who are undergraduate students currently enrolled at the University of Missouri in Columbia, Missouri. Students must have financial need and demonstrate leadership capabilities within the sorority and/or the campus. Number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

St. Louis Chapter 17 Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students enrolled in select Missouri and Illinois colleges who are student members of the SME St. Louis Chapter 17 and are majoring in Manufacturing Engineering, Industrial Technology or related fields. Undergraduate students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and graduating high school seniors must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. College juniors and seniors must be enrolled at the University of Missouri-Rolla, Southeast Missouri State University-Cape Girardeau, or Southern Illinois University-Carbondale. College freshmen or sophomores may attend Jefferson College, Mineral Area College or St. Louis Community College-Florissant Valley and then transfer to one of the above four year institutions. Nonrenewable; but recipients may reapply annually. more...
