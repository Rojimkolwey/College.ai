Pepperdine University Scholarships


Brenden Mann Foundation Scholarship
The Brenden Mann Foundation Scholarship is available to undergraduate students in their freshman or sophomore years at Pepperdine University. You must demonstrate academic and extracurricular interests in both Jewish Studies and interfaith programming to be eligible for this award. more...

Chiang Kai-Shek Scholarship
The Chiang Kai-Shek Scholarship is available to doctoral students at the Graduate School of Education and Psychology at Pepperdine University. You must be majoring in education and be from the Republic of China to be eligible for this award. more...

Church Of Christ Scholarships
The Church of Christ Scholarships are available to students at Pepperdine University. You must demonstrate active participation in your home Church of Christ congregation to be eligible for this award. more...

Karen L. Olson Psychology Scholarship
The Karen L. Olson Psychology Scholarship is available to students at the Graduate School of Education and Psychology at Pepperdine University. You must be pursuing study in psychology and be at the Irvine Graduate Campus to be eligible for this award. more...

Regents' Scholars Program - Pepperdine University
The Regents' Scholars Program is available to incoming freshman students at Pepperdine University. You must demonstrate outstanding academic achievements, course rigor, and overall university fit to be considered for this renewable award. more...

Scott H. Racine Award For Excellence In The Study Of Taxation Award
The Scott H. Racine Award for Excellence in the Study of Taxation Award is available to graduating students at Pepperdine University School of Law. You must demonstrate financial need, excel in the tax curriculum, and be pursuing a career in tax law to qualify for this award. more...

Shirley J. Brooke Endowed Scholarship
The Shirley J. Brooke Endowed Scholarship is available to female students at Pepperdine University School of Law. You must demonstrate above average academic achievement to be eligible for this award. more...

Sons Of Italy Foundation-George L. Graziadio Scholarship
Scholarship for students of Italian descent who are undergraduate students enrolled at George L. Graziadio School of Business and Management at Pepperdine University in California. Students must be U.S. citizens. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the college or the Organization for more information and/or application. more...

Special Achievement Scholarships
The Special Achievement Scholarships is available to incoming freshmen at Pepperdine University. You must demonstrate strong talent in the areas of athletics, music, art, theatre, debate, science to be eligible for this award. more...

Terry M. Giles Honor Scholars Program
The Terry M. Giles Honor Scholars Program is available to third-year students at Pepperdine University School of Law. You must demonstrate scholastic achievement, co-curricular and extracurricular involvement, and strong personality and character to be eligible for this award. An interview with the donor is required. more...

Thomas And Glenna Trimble Endowed Scholarship
The Thomas and Glenna Trimble Endowed Scholarship is available to students at Pepperdine University School of Law. You must be a member of the Church of Christ to be eligible for this award. more...

Warren And Rosalie Gummow Endowed Scholarship
The Warren and Rosalie Gummow Endowed Scholarship is open to students at Pepperdine University School of Law who demonstrate financial need and/or merit. more...

Woodrow Judkins Endowed Scholarship
The Woodrow Judkins Endowed Scholarship is open to students at Pepperdine University School of Law who are in good academic standing. more...
