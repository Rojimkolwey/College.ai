Golden Gate University-San Francisco Scholarships


Hugh Geoffrey Major Endowed Women's Law Scholarship
The Hugh Geoffrey Major Endowed Women's Law Scholarship is open to female students at the Golden Gate University School of Law. You must demonstrate financial need and academic standing to qualify for this award. more...

Merit Scholarship - Golden Gate University School Of Law
The Merit Scholarship is available to entering students at Golden Gate University School of Law. Selection is based on merit, including past academic achievement and LSAT scores. more...

Michael A. Zamperini/ W. Clay Burchell Endowed Scholarship
The Michael A. Zamperini/W. Clay Burchell Endowed Scholarship is available to gay and lesbian students in their final year at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must demonstrate overall academic achievement and academic performance in the Writing & Research courses to be eligible for this award. An essay is also required. more...

Minority Scholarship - Golden Gate University School Of Law
The Minority Scholarship is available to entering students of all races and ethnicities at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must demonstrate substantial and meaningful commitment to serve minority communities to be eligible for this award. more...

Paul S. Jordan Endowed Law Scholarship
The Paul S. Jordan Endowed Law Scholarship is available to continuing students at Golden Gate University School of Law. Preference is given to students who demonstrate scholastic excellence and financial need. more...

Phillip Burton Endowed Scholarship
The Phillip Burton Endowed Scholarship is available to continuing students at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Professor James B. Smith Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Professor James B. Smith Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to continuing students at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must be a female, part-time student with significant financial need or an older student of either gender to be eligible for this award. more...

Public Interest And Environmental Law Scholars Program
The Public Interest and Environmental Law Scholars Program is available to entering students at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must be pursuing a public interest or environmental career to be eligible for this award. This program provides a faculty mentor and a one-time employment stipend for a full-time summer position with a public interest agency. more...

Richard W. Johnson Memorial Scholarship
The Richard W. Johnson Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to third-year students at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must demonstrate great academic improvement since starting law school to be eligible for this award. more...

Roderick S. Polentz Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Roderick S. Polentz Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to continuing evening students at Golden Gate University School of Law. more...

Union Bank Of California Scholarship - Golden Gate U, School Of Law
The Union Bank of California Scholarship is available to continuing students at Golden Gate University School of Law. You must have a low-income or be a minority student to be eligible for this award. more...
