Pennsylvania College of Technology Scholarships


Susquehanna Health Systems Forgiveness Loan Program
The Susquehanna Health System Forgiveness Loan Program is available to students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be majoring in nursing, paramedics or radiography and have at least a 2.5 GPA with a majority of your pre-nursing course work completed to be eligible for this award. Two letters of reference and a statement of professional goals are required with your application. Students chosen for this program must commit to one year of employment in the Susquehanna Health Systems for each year of funding. more...

Transportation Technology Scholarship - Pct
The Transportation Technology Scholarship is available to students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be pursuing a major in the School of Transportation Technology and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Tulpehocken Garden Club Annual Scholarship
The Tulpehocken Garden Club Annual Scholarship is available to students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be majoring in floral design/interior plantscape or landscape/nursery technologies and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Victaulic Manufacturing Professional Scholarship
The Vitaulic Manufacturing Professional Scholarship is available to junior students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be pursuing a bachelor of science degree in manufacturing engineering technology and have at least a 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. Full-time students will be given preference. more...

West Branch Susquehanna Builders Association Scholarship
The West Branch Susquehann Builders Association Scholarship is available to students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be first-year student pursuing a major in the School of Construction and Design Technologies. You must be a resident of Lycoming or Clinton County and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Wheeland Lumber Company Scholarship
The Wheeland Lumber Company Scholarship is available to students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be a full-time student majoring in forestry technology. You must also be from Lycoming, Bradford, Sullivan, Potter, Tioga, McKean, Cameron or Clinton County to be eligible for this award. more...

Whiting-Turner Contracting Company Construction Management Scholarship
The Whiting-Turner Contracting Company Construction Management Scholarship is available to continuing students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be a construction management bachelor degree student and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

William D. Davis Scholarship
The William D. Davis Scholarship is available to full-time students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. First preference will be given to residents of Loyalsock Township, Williamsport, PA. more...

Williamsport Rotary Club Scholarship
The Williamsport Rotary Club Scholarship is available to first-year nursing students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must be a graduate of Bishop Neumann High School, Loyalstock Township High School, South Williamsport High School or Williamsport Area High School and have at least a 3.0 GPA. You must demonstrate good citizenship, dedication and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Women's Forum Scholarship - Pct
The Women's Forum Scholarship is available to students at the Pennsylvania College of Technology. You must have earned at least 12 credits and have a minimum 2.5 GPA. You must also demonstrate financial need and a record of community and college service to be eligible for this award. Two letters of recommendation are required with your application. more...
