Transylvania University Scholarships


Cane Ridge Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Transylvania University who are majoring in Religion or Church Vocation. Selection based on national leadership in the church. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Disciples Chalice Grants
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Transylvania University who are Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) members. Selection based on minister recommendation and financial need. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Governors Scholars-Governors School For The Arts Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Transylvania University who are designated Governors Scholars from Kentucky, Ohio or Tennessee OR participated in the Governors School for the Arts. Selection based on academic achievement and future potential. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Guy & Nell Waldrop Disciples Leadership Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Transylvania University who are majoring in Religion or Church Vocation. Selection based on national/regional leadership in the church. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

James Morrison Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Transylvania University. Selection based on academic achievement, leadership abilities, and future potential. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

John R. Bryden Appalachian Scholarships
Scholarship for residents of southern Appalachia who are incoming freshmen or transfer students enrolling at Transylvania University. Selection based on academic achievement, financial need, and community/school involvement. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Kentucky River Properties Appalachian Scholarships
Scholarship for residents of Knott, Leslie, Letcher, or Perry counties, Kentucky who are undergraduate students attending Transylvania University. Selection based on academic achievement and financial need. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Ministers Dependent Grants
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students enrolling at Transylvania University who are dependent children of ministers or missionaries of the Christian Chruch (Disciples of Christ) or the United Church of Christ. Selection based on financial need. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Transy & Team (Totally Excited About Ministry) Grants
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Transylvania University who are involved in the Transy and TEAM program. Selection based on involvement. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Transylvania Endowment Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Transylvania University. Selection based on merit and financial need. Number of awards and award amouts may vary. Renewable. more...

Transylvania National Merit Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Transylvania University who are National Merit Finalists that selected TU as their first choice. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Transylvania Scholarships
Scholarship for juniors or seniors attending Transylvania University. Selection based on academic achievement and involvement in campus life and activities. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable. more...
