Ball State University Scholarships


Ellison Scholarship
Scholarship for first-time freshmen enrolling at Ball State University. Students must have graduated from a Delaware County, Indiana high school, be nominated by their high school. Selection is based on outstanding leadership, creativity, and academic achievement. Number of awards may vary. Award amount is for full tuition, fees, and room and board. more...

Fine Art Scholarships (Art)
Scholarship for freshmen with talent in art enrolling at Ball State University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Selection is based on academic record and art portfolio. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Harry Watkins Scholarship
Scholarship for Indiana residents who are undergraduate students attending Ball State University. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and have financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Holmes-Mcfadden Minority Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending Ball State University majoring in Journalism. Preference is given to minority students. Renewable. more...

Indiana Realtors Education Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship for Indiana residents who are undergraduate students attending Ball State University majoring in Residential Property Management. Applicants must be enrolled in or have completed FCSMR 235 Introduction to Residential Property Management, and have a minimum 2.75 overall GPA and 3.0 GPA in the major. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

John R. & Aline B. Emens Scholars Program
Scholarship for first time freshmen enrolling at Ball State University. Students must have participated in academic and extracurricular activities in a leadership role. Students must be nominated by a high school counselor, or principal. Award amount is for full in-state tuition. Renewable. more...

Mildred Moore Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time freshmen enrolling at Ball State University majoring in Family and Consumer Sciences. Applicants must have a minimum 1120 (old) or 1690 (new) or a minimum 24 ACT score. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Miller College Of Business Scholarships & Awards
Scholarship for full-time sophomores, juniors or seniors students attending Ball State University enrolled in the College of Business. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Minority Teacher Scholarship
Scholarship for minority residents of Indiana who are undergraduate students enrolling at Ball State University and majoring in Education or Special Education. Number of awards may vary. more...

Outstanding Freshman Art Scholarships
Scholarship for first-time freshmen enrolling at Ball State University pursuing a major in the Department of Art. Number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Residential Property Management Graduate Student Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students attending Ball State University enrolled in Residential Property Management. Applicants must have completed 6 graduate hours of RPM required courses and have a minimum 3.0 overall GPA and a 3.5 GPA in the major. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Whitinger Scholars
Scholarship for first-time freshmen enrolling at Ball State University. Students must have a minimum 3.7 GPA, and a minimum 1950 SAT score (minimum 600 on each section) or a minimum 29 ACT (minimum 26 on both the ACT math and English). Selection is based on academic ability, character, creativity, and potential for leadership. Award amount covers full tuition, fees, and room and board. Renewable. more...
