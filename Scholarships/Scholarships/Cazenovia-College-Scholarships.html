Michael M. Muchisky, Ph.D. Scholarship
The Michael M. Muchisky, Ph.D. Scholarship is available to students at Cazenovia College who are majoring in psychology. You must have ranked in the top twenty- five percent of your prior- year academic program and must also be of Ukrainian or Polish descent to be eligible for this award. more...

Nancy N. Paton Scholarship
The Nancy N. Paton Scholarship is available to students of limited resources at Cazenovia College. more...

O'connor Foundation Scholarship
The O'Connor Foundation Scholarship is available to students at Cazenovia College who reside in Delaware county in New York and the surrounding counties of Broome, Chenango, Greene, Otsego, Schoharie, Sullivan, and Ulster. more...

Presidential Leadership Award - Cazenovia College
The Presidential Leadership Award is available to entering freshmen at Cazenovia College. You must demonstrate a record of outstanding contributions in school, religious, or community activities to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to active students in the first or second quintiles. more...

Presidential Transfer Scholarship - Cazenovia College
The Presidential Transfer Scholarship is available to transfer students at Cazenovia College. You must have earned your associate's degree and intend to live on campus to qualify for this award. more...

Richard And Margaret Clark Scholarship
The Richard and Margaret Clark Scholarship is available to students at Cazenovia College who demonstrates imagination and vision. more...

Richard H. Hawks Scholarship
The Richard H. Hawks Scholarship is available to students at Cazenovia College. You must be caring and exemplify strength of spirit and devotion to be eligible for this award. more...

Rochester Alumni Scholarship - Cazenovia College
The Rochester Alumni Scholarship is available to students at Cazenovia College who are from the greater Rochester, New York area. You must exemplify academic achievement and have financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to students who are descendants of Cazenovia alumni. more...

Russell E. Corser Award
The Russell E. Corser Award is available to students at Cazenovia College who demonstrate academic excellence. more...

Valedictorian Scholarship - Cazenovia College
The Valedictorian Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Cazenovia College. You must be the valedictorian of your high school graduating class to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable with a maintained 3.0 GPA. more...
