Herkimer County Community College Scholarships


Delta Kappa Gamma Scholarship
The Delta Kappa Gamma Scholarship is available to graduating female students at Herkimer County Community College. You must be transferring to a four - year program as an education major to be eligible for this award. more...

Margaret T. Quackenbush Memorial Scholarship
The Margaret T. Quackenbush Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Herkimer County Community College. You must be a graduating student planning to transfer to a baccalaureate program to be eligible for this award. more...

Officer Joseph D. Corr Scholarship
The Officer Joseph D. Corr Scholarship is available to students at Herkimer County Community College. You must be majoring in criminal justice and plan to pursue a career in law enforcement. Also, you must demonstrate community service and campus involvement to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students interested in baseball. more...

Polish Community Home Scholarship
The Polish Community Home Scholarship is available to students at Herkimer County Community College. You must be a resident of Herkimer County and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Herkimer Community College
The Presidential Scholarship is available to students at Herkimer County Community College. You must be an adult student, have a minimum 2.0 GPA, and be in your final semester of study to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students with special need. more...

Richard Karpen, Jr. Memorial Fund Scholarship
The Richard Karpen, Jr. Memorial Fund Scholarship is available to graduating students majoring in science at Herkimer County Community College. You must plan to transfer to a baccalaureate degree to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to Herkimer High School graduates. more...

Universalist Society Of Herkimer Scholarship
The Universalist Society of Herkimer Scholarship is available to freshmen at Herkimer County Community College. You must be planning to enter into the "helping professions," and demonstrate academic excellence and financial need. You must also be a resident of Herkimer County, with preference given to a graduate of Herkimer High School, to be eligible for this award. more...

Utica National Insurance Group Scholarship
The Utica National Insurance Group Scholarship is available to full - time students at Herkimer County Community College. You must have a minimum 3.25 GPA to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to children of Utica National employees. more...

Vincent J. Iocovozzi Scholarship
The Vincent J. Iocovozzi Scholarship is available to science students at Herkimer County Community College. You must plan to transfer to Simmons or another mortuary science program to be eligible for this award. more...

Wesley P. Small Scholarship
The Wesley P. Small Scholarship is available to students at Herkimer County Community College. You must be majoring in humanities to be eligible for this award/. more...

William Hennessey Memorial Scholarship
The William Hennessey Memorial Scholarship is available to freshmen at Herkimer County Community College. You must have graduated from Ilion, Mohawk, Herkimer, Frankfort or Little Falls high schools and demonstrate academic excellence to be eligible for this award. more...
