Emory University Scholarships


Benjamin E. Mays Scholarship
The Benjamin E. Mays Scholarship is available to students at Emory University. To be considered, you must have attended an Atlanta public school and demonstrate outstanding contributions to society. more...

Chris A. Yannopoulos Scholars Award
The Chris A. Yannopoulos Scholars Award is available to students at Emory University. You must have demonstrated an interest in classical studies or represent the strengths brought to the United States by immigrants to be eligible for this award. This scholarship is distributed through the Emory Scholars Program and provides full tuition. more...

Emory Scholars Program
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Emory University in Georgia full-time. Candidates must be a U.S. citizen. Applicants must be nominated by their high school counselor (see Emory College catalog for detailed information). Recipients of these academic awards are selected by a committee comprised of Emory faculty and staff. Selection is based on merit, not financial need. Award amount varies. more...

Goizueta Scholars Award
The Goizueta Scholars Award is available to incoming freshman students at Emory University-Goizueta School of Business. To be considered for this renewable award, you must have superior academic credentials and a demonstrated interest in business as evidenced by engagement in entrepreneurial activities, participation in internships, membership in business clubs, and/or involvement in business-related conferences, competitions, and similar offerings. This award covers 50-100 percent of tuition and includes a study abroad option. more...

Graduate & Professional Student Scholarships
Scholarship for graduate students attending Emory Universitys schools of Public Health, Graduate Arts & Sciences, Law, Business and Theology, Medicine, Nursing and Allied Health. Selection for these awards is typically based on financial need. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Martin Luther King, Jr. Scholarship - Emory University
The Martin Luther King, Jr. Scholarship is available to students at Emory University. You must have attended an Atlanta public school and demonstrate outstanding contributions to society. more...

National Merit Scholarship - Emory University
The National Merit Scholarship is available to undergraduate National Merit finalists who select Emory as their first choice college. more...

Student Courtesy Tuition Benefit Scholarship
Employee-Related awards for dependent children, spouses or domestic partners of eligible Emory University faculty and staff who are attending Emory University. Students eligible for Courtesy benefits, who also receive Emory merit awards, cannot receive awards that exceed their eligibility. Due date is at least 60 days prior to registration. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

United Methodist Ministerial Tuition Benefit Undergraduate Scholarship
Scholarship for dependents of active United Methodist ministers or missionaries, who are undergraduate students attending Emory University in Georgia. This award may be adjusted when combined with other tuition awards. Award is equal to 45 percent of tuition. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

United Methodist Verde Dickey Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for United Methodist Church members who are undergraduate students that are juniors or seniors attending Emory University in Georgia majoring in Education or Physical Education. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. more...
