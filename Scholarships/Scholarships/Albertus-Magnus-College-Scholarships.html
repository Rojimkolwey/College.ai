Honorable Richard C. Lee Scholarships For New Haven Area Students
Scholarship for first-time entering freshmen accepted for enrollment to Albertus Magnus College who attended high school in New Haven, North Haven, West Haven, East Haven, Hamden, or Orange/Woodbridge, Connecticut. Students must have a minimum combined 1,100 SAT and minimum 3.5 GPA for 75% tuition reduction; minimum 1000 SAT and minimum 3.0 GPA for 50% reduction; minimum 900 SAT and minimum 2.5 GPA for 25% reduction. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Longstreth-Rudnick Science Scholarship

Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, minimum 1000 SAT score with no lower than 450 on either verbal or math portion, and have a B or better average in Biology, Chemistry and Physics. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
