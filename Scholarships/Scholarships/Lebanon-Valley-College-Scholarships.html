Lebanon Valley College Scholarships


Academic Recognition Scholarship For Transfer Students
Scholarship for college juniors or seniors who are members of the Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society at Lebanon Valley College. Applicants must have completed a transfer program or received an Associates Degree. Renewable. Award amount is for one-half tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Alumni Tuition Awards
Scholarship for full time undergraduate students at Lebanon Valley College. Students must be a child of a Lebanon Valley College alumni. Renewable. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Edna & Clark Carmean Scholars In Music
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors accepted to Lebanon Valley College. Students must demonstrate proficient skills in Music. Carmean Scholars are chosen from recipients of the LVC Presidential Scholarship Program. An audition with members of the music faculty is included in the application process. Renewable. more...

Edna & Clark Carmean String Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors accepted to Lebanon Valley College. Stduents must demonstrate musical ability on a string instrument. Selection is based on an audition with members of the music faculty. Renewable. more...

Graham Biology Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Lebanon Valley College majoring in Biology. Students must rank in the top 10% of their class, and have a minimum combined SAT score of 1200. An interview with members of the biology faculty is included in the application process. In addition to admissions application students must also submit two letters of recommendation and an essay. Renewable. more...

Leadership Awards
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors accepted to Lebanon Valley College. Student must rank in the top 20% of their class. Award amount covers one-third of students tuition fees. Award is renewable up to a total of 8 semesters if continuing criteria is met. Number of awards may vary. more...

Lutz Scholarship
Scholarship for graduates of Cocalico High School who have been accepted to Lebanon Valley College. Students must demonstrate financial aid. Number of awards may vary. Contact the Cocalico High School Guidance Office for more information and/or application. more...

Multicultural Fellowships
Scholarship for African-American, Asian-American, Hispanic-American, and Native-American graduating high school seniors accepted to Lebanon Valley College. Student must demonstrate commitment to work, service and scholarly achievement. Awards are renewable for a total of four years if continuing criteria is met. Due date and number of awards vary. more...

Presidential Scholarship For Transfer Students
Scholarship for full-time college transfer students at Lebanon Valley College. Students must have 16 or more credit hours, good academic status, and a declared major. Due date and number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

Recognition Awards
Annual award for full time undergraduate students at Lebanon Valley College. Students must be a recipient of the PA Governors School Award, HOBY Seminar Award, Girl Scout Gold Award, or the Eagle Scout Award. Award is renewable up to a total of four years. more...

Vickroy Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming college freshman at Lebanon Valley College. Students must rank in the top 10% of their class. Award amount covers one-half of students tuition fees. Renewable. Number of awards may vary. more...
