California State University-East Bay Scholarships


Angelina Aliberti Ruggie & Lawrence L. Ruggie Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending California State University, Hayward full-time to major in Humanities. Applicant must have financial need. Award amount varies. more...

Bateman Family Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduates (freshmen ineligible) attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. Award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Charles & Marguerite Arolla Scholarship
Scholarship for California residents of Alameda or Contra Costa Counties, who are entering freshmen accepted to California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Selection based on minimum 3.6 GPA, class rank (top 5%), and minimum SAT score of 1100 or ACT score of 25. Candidate must have financial need. Award amount varies. more...

Csu Student Research Competition Award
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students attending California State University, Hayward full-time. Applicant must showcase a recently completed research project at the annual competition. There are first and second place winners in both undergraduate and graduate categories. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Early Pioneer Scholarship
Scholarship for California residents who are high school seniors accepted into the California State, Hayward Dual Matriculation Program (taking college-level courses during their senior year in high school). Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, and a PSAT/SAT score of at least 550 verbal and 550 math, or ACT score of 24 English and 23 Math. Award is equal to the registration fees for courses taken. Number of awards varies. more...

Ellis & Hilda Mccune Award
Annual award for a transfer student from a community college accepted to California State University, Hayward as a full-time student, who is planning to major in Teaching in a postsecondary level. Selection based on minimum 3.5 GPA. Submit 2 letters of recommendation (1 from junior college professor), Community College transcript and a personal statement. Award amount varies. more...

Erwin Bigger Scholarship (Alpha Phi Omega)
Annual award for undergraduate attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student, who has completed 30 credit hours. Selection based on minimum 2.0 GPA. Award amount may varies. more...

Fee Exemption For Dependents Of Deceased Or Disabled Military Veterans Scholarship
Annual award for dependents of deceased or disabled military veterans who are undergraduate or graduate students planning to attend the California State University, Hayward. Award is equal to full tuition and all fees, including application and State University Fees. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Fee Waiver For Senior Citizens Scholarship
Annual award for California residents, 60 years of age or older, who have applied for regular admission to California State University, Hayward. Award is equal to tuition fees, application fees and University fees. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Forgivable Loan-Doctoral Incentive Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students attending California State University (CSU), Hayward full-time. Applicants may borrow up to $10,000/year (up to a total of $30,000 in a five-year period). Students who complete their doctoral studies and teach in the California State University system as full-time faculty members will have their loan forgiven at the rate of 20% for each year of teaching. Those that do not teach at CSU are required to repay the loan with interest. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Georgia Babladelis Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduates (freshmen ineligible) who are re-entry students attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Graduate Equity Fellowships
Scholarship for California residents who are graduate students attending California State University, Hayward full-time. Applicant must have financial need of at least $1000. Candidate must demonstrate potential to succeed and complete a masters degree program in no more than two years. Number of awards varies. more...

Graduate Writing Associate Program
Scholarship for graduate students attending California State University, Hayward full-time that are interested in developing writing and teaching skills. Applicant must complete a four (4) unit seminar, and then be paired with faculty members to work as writing tutors and instructors within those faculty members classes. Recipients will receive $1200 for every class in which they serve as a Graduate Writing Assistant. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Onvia Ticer Tillinghast Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduates attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Applicant must have financial need. Award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Pioneer Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Applicant must have a minimum 3.3 GPA and SAT scores of at least 550 verbal and 550 math, or ACT scores of 24 English and 23 Math. Award amount is equal to full registration fees for four years of qualified California State Hayward attendance. Number of awards varies. more...

Robert M. Odell Scholarship In Public Administration
Scholarship for upper-level undergraduate or graduate attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Special consideration given to first-generation students majoring in Public Administration or specializing in Finance. Award amount varies. more...

Robert Stenger Memorial Scholarship
Annual award for International Student (not intending to become a citizen) accepted to California State University, Hayward. Selection based on a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. Award amount varies. more...

Sydney Noel Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for Undergraduates (freshmen ineligible) attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. Candidate must have already completed 30 California State University, Hayward units. Award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Y. Charles Soda Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate student attending California State University, Hayward as a full-time student. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and financial need. Candidate must have already completed at least 36 units. Award amount varies. more...
