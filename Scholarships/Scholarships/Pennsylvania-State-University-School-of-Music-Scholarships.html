Pennsylvania State University-World Campus Scholarships


Foreman Architects Engineers Study Abroad Scholarship
The Foreman Architects Engineers Study Abroad Scholarship is available to full- time third- year undergraduate students enrolled in the Department of Architecture at Penn State University. You must plan to study abroad during a semester in your your fourth year. You must demonstrate outstanding design capabilities, superior graphic talent, and a willingness and ability to help others to be eligible for this award. more...

Willa C. Taylor Scholarship
The Willa C. Taylor Scholarship is available to students at the Penn State School of Music. You must be majoring in voice or music education and demonstrate outstanding music ability and positive academic records to qualify for this award. more...
