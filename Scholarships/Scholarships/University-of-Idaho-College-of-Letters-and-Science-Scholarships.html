Alpha Gamma Rho-Beta Phi Chapter Of The University Of Idaho
Scholarship for male FFA members who are graduating high school seniors or first time, incoming freshmen accepted at the University of Idaho and will be enrolled in the College of Agriculture or the College of Natural Resources. Student must be a U.S. citizen. Criteria used for selection includes students GPA, ACT or SAT scores, class rank, leadership in FFA activities, leadership in community activities, and involvement in the Supervised Agricultural Experience Program (SAE). Nonrenewable. more...

Animal Sciences Scholarship - University Of Idaho
The Animal Sciences Scholarship is open to University of Idaho undergraduate students who are majoring in animal science or veterinary science. Your selection for this award is based on character, extracurricular activities, integrity and leadership. more...

Arthur Gittins Entomology Scholarship
The Arthur Gittins Entomology Scholarship is available to undergraduate students at the University of Idaho. You must be majoring in entomology and demonstrate academic achievement to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to freshmen, then to transfer students and to those changing their majors to entomology. more...

Cenex Harvest States Corporation (Harvest States) Scholarship
The Cenex Harvest States Corporation (Harvest States) Scholarship is available to undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Idaho. You must be enrolled in the college of agriculture and life sciences and be from a rural area to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to children of members of Cenex. more...

Chevron Society Of Women Engineers Scholarship
The Chevron Society of Women Engineers Scholarship is available to sophomores and juniors at the University of Idaho. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0, be a member of the UI Soceity of Women Engineers, and be majoring in one of the following fields to be eligible for this award: civil engineering, chemical engineering, electrical engineering, mechanical engineering and computer science. more...

Clark/Gordon/Puec Scholarship
The Clark/Gordon/PUEC Scholarship is open to juniors, seniors and graduate students majoring in an area covered by the College of Business and Economics at the University of Idaho. You must have a GPA of at least 2.9 and demonstrate an interest in the utilities industry to be considered for this award. Preference will be given to female and minority students. more...

Lola & Duane Hagadone Idaho Scholarship Program
Scholarship for Idaho residents who are graduating high school seniors, or equivalent, planning to attend the University of Idaho or Lewis-Clark State College, Coeur dAlene and Lewiston Campuses only.
