The College of Saint Rose Scholarships


Academic Talent Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming full-time freshmen and transfer at College of Saint Rose. Applicants must have demonstrated outstanding achievement or talent in English, Spanish, Mathematics, History, Science, Psychology or Business. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Art & Music Awards
Scholarship for incoming full-time freshmen and transfer students enrolling at the College of Saint Rose. Students must demonstrate extraordinary artistic or musical ability and high academic ability. An audition or portfolio review is required. Number of awards may vary. Award amount is $10,000 to full-tuition. more...

Athletic Scholarships (Athletic Service Awards)
Scholarship for undergraduate students with athletic talent attending College of Saint Rose. Students must participate on an intercollegiate team. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Carondelet Scholarships
Scholarship for full-time freshmen enrolling at the College of Saint Rose. Qualified students usually have a 98-100 high school GPA, and 1400-1500 SAT score, or minimum 32-36 ACT score. Special consideration is given to students with leadership potential and a commitment to service to school and/or community. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

George & Caroline Winkler Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time graduate students attending the College of Saint Rose majoring in Communication Disorders. Selection is based on undergraduate academic performance and recommendations. Due date and award amount may vary. more...

John J. Gargiulo Memorial Scholarship In Business
Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate students attending the College of Saint Rose majoring in Business. Applicants must demonstrate leadership and community service; incoming freshmen must be in the top 20% of their graduating class. more...

Mary H. Langan Memorial-Langan Group Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students who are employees or the Langhan Group or a family and are attending the College of Saint Rose. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Mary H. Langan Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate students who are employees or children of employees of the Cerebral Palsy Center for the Disabled and are attending the College of Saint Rose. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Michael R. Donohue Scholarship (In Honor Of Mother Mary Bernadette)
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students attending the College of Saint Rose entering the field of Special Education. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Multicultural Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending College of Saint Rose. Selection based on academic achievement, extra curricular activities and essay competition. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Peter M. Tully Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for new graduate student attending the College of Saint Rose pursuing a Master of Business Administration degree. Preference is given to students employed in a business based in the Capital Region. Selection is based on prior academic achievement. An essay must be included with application packet. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Rooney-Gibbons Scholarship
Scholarship for full-tim freshmen attending the College of Saint Rose. Applicants must have demonstrated superior academic achievement through high school GPA, SAT/ACT scores, and class rank. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Sacred Heart Scholarship (Donated By Professor Walter L. Hahn)
Scholarship for full-time graduate students attending College of Saint Rose. Selection is based on undergraduate performance and financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Award amount may vary. more...

Saint Rose Scholarships
Scholarship for freshmen students enrolling in the College of Saint Rose. Students must exhibit extraordinary academic ability and achievement measured by courses attempted in high school. Qualified students usually have a 95-100 high school GPA, and 1300-1600 SAT score or 29-36 ACT score. Special consideration is given to students with leadership potential and a commitment to service to school and/or community. Number of awards may vary. more...

Sister Anne L. Clark Graduate Scholarship-Multicultural Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time graduate students attending College of Saint Rose. Applicants must demonstrate superior academic achievement. Renewable. more...
