Suffolk University Scholarships


Alumni Discount Award - Suffolk University
The Alumni Discount Award is available to students attending Suffolk University. You must be the legal dependent of a Suffolk University graduate and be enrolled full time in an undergraduate program to be eligible for this award. more...

Community Service Scholarship - Suffolk University
The Community Service Scholarship is available to undergraduate and graduate students attending Suffolk University. You must demonstrate community service involvement and be in good academic standing to be eligible for this award. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Suffolk University
The Dean's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Suffolk University. Your selection is based on your academic standing and financial need. This award is renewable with a minimum 3.0 GPA. more...

Education And Human Service Part-Time Scholarship - Suffolk University
The Education and Human Service Part-Time Scholarship is available to entering part-time graduate students at Suffolk University. You must be enrolled in education and human services to be eligible for this award. more...

Family Discount Plan - Suffolk University
The Family Discount Plan is available to full-time undergraduate students at Suffolk University. You must be from a family with two undergraduate dependents enrolled at Suffolk University at the same time to be eligible for this award. more...

Graduate Management Part-Time Scholarship - Suffolk University
The Graduate Management Part-Time Scholarship is available to entering part-time graduate students at Suffolk University. You must be enrolled in the School of Management to be considered for this award. Students with no or partial employer tuition remission benefits are encouraged to apply. more...

Griffin Fellows Scholarship
The Griffin Fellows Scholarship is available to full-time entering freshmen at Suffolk University. You must be enrolled in the Frank Sawyer School of Management to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable with a minimum 3.3 GPA. more...

Maria Stewart Scholarship
The Maria Stewart Scholarship is available to full-time entering freshmen or transfer students at Suffolk University. You must be a graduate of a Boston public high school with demonstrated financial need and talent. This award is renewable with a minimum 2.5 GPA. more...

Stephen A. Baker & Co., Inc. Award
The Stephen A. Baker & Co., Inc. Award is available to students attending Suffolk University. You must be an accounting major completing your junior year to be eligible for this award. more...
