University of Pennsylvania Scholarships


Dean's Scholarship - University Of Pennsylvania Law School
The Dean's Scholarship is available to students at the University of Pennsylvania Law School. You must demonstrate academic achievement, leadership, service and life/work experiences to be eligible for this award. more...

Emerging Economy Fellowship
The Emerging Economy Fellowship is available to first - year MBA students at the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School. You must be from a country with an emerging economy and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Howard E. Mitchell Fellowship
The Howard E. Mitchell Fellowship is available to first - year MBA students from under-represented backgrounds at the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School. more...

Joseph Wharton Fellowship
The Joseph Wharton Fellowship is available to first - year MBA students at the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School. You must have an outstanding record of academic, personal, and professional achievements to be considered for this award. more...

Levy Scholars Program Scholarship
The Levy Scholars Program Scholarship is available to entering students at the University of Pennsylvania Law School. You must demonstrate outstanding scholarship to be eligible for the award. more...

Melissa Sengbusch Inspirational Scholarship
The Melissa Sengbusch Inspirational Scholarship is open to students in the School of Nursing at the University of Pennsylvania. You must stay in the School of Nursing for all four years of undergraduate study and maintain a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Scholars Educational Loan Forgiveness (Self) - U. Penn
The Scholars Educational Loan Forgiveness Scholarship is open to students in the School of Nursing at the University of Pennsylvania. You must stay in the School of Nursing for all four years of undergraduate study, maintain a minimum GPA of 2.75 and agree to a one-year work commitment in a hospital, home or healthcare agency in the U.S. after graduation to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Silverman-Rodin Scholars Scholarship
The Silverman-Rodin Scholars Scholarship is available to entering students at the University of Pennsylvania Law School. Selection is based on academic achievement and demonstration of leadership. more...
