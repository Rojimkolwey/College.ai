Illinois Institute of Technology Scholarships


Balanced Man Scholarship - Iit
The Balanced Man Scholarship is available to male undergraduate students at the Illinois Institute of Technology. You must demonstrate academic excellence, leadership skills, and a commitment to your health and well - being to be eligible for this award. more...

Camras Scholars Program
The Camras Scholars Program is open to full - time freshmen at the Illinois Institute of Technology. You must have an outstanding academic record and show exceptional academic promise and merit. more...

Camras Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students to attending Illinois Institute of Technology. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and 1280 SAT or 29 ACT. Applicants must submit admission application and Camras Supplemental Application. Qualified applicants will be invited to attend an interview weekend. Scholarship program recipients will participate in research, internships, projects and entrepreneur opportunities. Award amount varies up to full-tuition. Award is renewable for a total of four years. Contact the Office of Admission for more information and/or application. more...

Chicago Police And Fire Departments Scholarship - Iit
The Chicago Police and Fire Departments Scholarship is available to students at Illinois Institute of Technology. You must be the child or grandchild of a member of the Chicago Police Department or Chicago Fire Department to be considered for this award. more...

Crown Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to Illinois Institute of Technology and are majoring in Architecture. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA, 1280 SAT or 29 ACT, be in the top 10% of their high school graduating class, have participated in extracurricular activities and be a U.S. citizen or permanent resident. In addition to application, students must submit a one-page essay, freehand original drawing, and letter of recommendation. Admission application and Crown Scholarship Application must be submitted to the Office of Admission by January 12. Finalists will be invited to interview during Scholarship Weekend. Award amount covers full tuition. Awards are renewable for a total of five years. Contact the Office of Admission for more information and/or application. more...

Heald Scholarship
The Heald Scholarship is available to full - time freshmen students at Illinois Institute of Technology. more...

Heald Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to Illinois Institute of Technology. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA, 1280 SAT or 29 ACT and be in the top 25% of their high school graduating class. Admission application is automatically reviewed for eligibility. Award amount varies $6,000-$12,000. Awards are renewable for four years. Contact the Office of Admission for more information and/or application. more...

International Freshman Scholarships
Scholarship for International students who are incoming freshmen admitted to Illinois Institute of Technology. Applicants must have an F-1 visa, a minimum 3.5 GPA, 1280 SAT or 29 ACT and be in the top 25% of their high school graduating class. Contact the Office of Admission for more information and/or application. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Iit
The Presidential Scholarship is available to students transferring to Illinois Institute of Technology from one of the City Colleges of Chicago. You must be nominated by your institution's president to be considered for this award. more...

Thomas A. Roszak Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen admitted to Illinois Institute of Technology who are majoring in Architecture. Selection based on academic achievement and financial need. Nonrenewable. Contact the Office of Admission for more information and/or application. more...

Transfer Tuition Scholarships
Scholarship for full-time incoming transfer students admitted to Illinois Institute of Technology. Admission application is automatically reviewed for eligibility. Award amount varies $5,000-$10,000; determined by students transfer GPA. Awards are renewable. Contact the Office of Admission for more information and/or application. more...
