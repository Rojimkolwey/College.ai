Academic Accomplishment Scholarship For Minority Students
Scholarship for minority incoming freshmen students attending Marietta College in Ohio, with outstanding academic promise. Selection is based on high school records and curriculum, scores on national examinations and potential leadership for Marietta. Number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain 2.5 GPA and full-time enrollment. more...

Annual award for an undergraduate sophomore student attending Marietta College in Ohio, majoring in Chemistry. Applicant must show exceptional performance in sophomore chemistry courses and have potential of becoming a chemistry major. Award also includes a one-year subscription to The Journal of Chemical Education. Due date varies. more...

Scholarship for incoming and current undergraduate juniors attending Marietta College in Ohio. Applicants must have membership in the McDonough Leadership Program with a minor in Leadership Studies or a major in International Leadership Studies.
