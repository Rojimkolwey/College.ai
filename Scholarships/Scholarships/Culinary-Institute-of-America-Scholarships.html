The Alumni Referral Scholarship is available to entering first-year students at the Culinary Institute of America. Cream Of The Crop Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students pursuing studies in Culinary Arts at the Culinary Institute of America. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and outstanding academic and leadership skills. Due date may vary. Renewable. more...

First Generation Inner-City Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at The Culinary Institute of America who are first generation college students. Applicants must have been educated in a major metropolitan area in the U.S. Selection based on academic achievement and financial aid. Due date may vary. Award amount is for full-tuition. Renewable. more...

M.F.K. Fisher Scholarship

National Student Organization (Nso) Competition Scholarships
Students who compete and place first, second, or third in competitions sponsored by SkillsUSA, ProStart, or Family Career & Community Leaders of America (FCCLA) are eligible for scholarships sponsored by The Culinary Institute of America. These scholarships are renewable annually with a GPA of 3.0. more...

National Student Organization (Nso) Competition Scholarships
The National Student Organization (NSO) Competition Scholarships is available to students who plan to attend the Culinary Institute of America. You must compete and place first, second, or third in competitions sponsored by SkillsUSA, ProStart, or Family Career & Community Leaders of America (FCCLA) to be eligible for this award. Renewable annually with a minimum GPA of 2.75. more...

National Student Organization (Nso) Member Scholarship
The Culinary Institute of America offers an essay contest for NSO memebers (members of SkillsUSA, ProStart, or FCCLA). Members are eligible to apply for one of 45 non-renewable, $2,000 scholarships. Officers of SkillsUSA and FCCLA are also eligible for additional awards. more...

Sat/Act Achievement Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at The Culinary Institute of America. Applicants must have SAT scores of 550 or better in Math and 550 or better in Critical Reading or ACT scores of 20 or better in Math and 20 or better in English. Award is for the first semester. Due date and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. more...

Sat/Act Achievement Scholarship - Culinary Institute Of America
The SAT/ACT Achievement Scholarship is available to students at the Culinary Institute of America. You must have an SAT score of 550 or better in math or critical reading or have an SAT score of 20 or better in math or English to be eligible for this award. more...

The Cia Sat/Act Achievement Scholarship
Scholarships are available to students entering The Culinary Institute of America. If you have earned a combined SAT score (Math + Critical Reading) of 1,000 or an ACT score of 22, you are eligible for a $1,000 SAT/ACT Achievement Scholarship. If your scores are 1,200 on the SAT (Math + Critical Reading) or 27 on the ACT, you are eligible for a $2,000 scholarship. Official scores must be submitted with the admissions application. This scholarship is not renewable. more...

The Cias Alumni Referral Scholarship
The Culinary Institute of America offers this scholarship to prospective students who are encouraged by a CIA graduate to apply for admission. Qualifying students will receive a one-time, $1,000 scholarship for their first year in our degree programs. To be eligible for the CIA Alumni Referral Scholarship, students must include a letter of recommendation from the CIA graduate with their application for admission. more...

The Culinary Institute Of America Merit Scholarship
The CIAs merit scholarship system takes a good look at YOU-- your past academic performance, foodservice experience, extracurricular activities-- to determine if you qualify for a CIA Merit-Based Scholarship. Based on your CIA application for admission youll automatically be considered for awards ranging from $2,500 to $4,000. more...

Top Turkey Scholarship
