Fisher College Scholarships


Academic Improvement Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are sophomores, juniors and seniors attending Fisher College, Day Division in Boston, MA full-time. Applicants must show continuous academic improvement. Candidates must earn a GPA for the year that exceeds their previous years GPA. Number of awards varies. more...

Bachelors Degree Merit Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are juniors at Fisher College, Day Division in Boston, MA full-time. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5-2.99 GPA. Students must maintain a 2.5 GPA at Fisher College. Candidates who maintain a Fisher College GPA of 3.0 or higher for two years will receive a $4000 scholarship. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Business Administration Merit Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Fisher College, Day Division in Boston, MA full-time majoring in Business Administration. Applicants must have a minimum high school GPA of 2.5-2.99. Students with a GPA of 3.0 or higher will receive the highest award amount and must maintain at least a 3.0 GPA at Fisher College. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Computer Technology Merit Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Fisher College, Day Division in Boston, MA full-time majoring in Computer Technology. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5-2.99 high school GPA. Students with a minimum 3.0 GPA or higher will receive the highest award amount, but must maintain a 3.0 GPA at Fisher College. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Deans List Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are sophomores, juniors and seniors attending Fisher College, Day Division in Boston, MA full-time. Applicants must earn a place on the Deans List in either the fall or spring term of their previous year. Students receiving the Fisher Honor Scholarship are not eligible for this award. Number of awards varies. more...

Fisher Honor Scholarship
Scholarship for entering freshmen Day students attending Fisher College in Boston, MA full-time. Applicants must have academic and personal achievement in high school, and a minimum 3.0 GPA. Selection is based on academic achievement as well as community and high school service. Number of awards varies. Renewable; one year. more...

Fisher Honors Program Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Fisher College in Boston, MA full-time. Applicants must be accepted into the Fisher College Honor Program. The continuance of this scholarship is based on the students remaining in good standing within the Honor Program. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Fisher Trustee Scholarship
Scholarship for entering freshmen students attending Fisher College, Day Division in Boston, MA. Applicants must be full-time students and have demonstrated academic performance. Some awards are based on specific majors. Number of awards varies. more...
