Ripon College Scholarships


Advance College Experience (Ace) Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Ripon College in Wisconsin full-time. This award recognizes a students participation and achievement in two or more years of Ripon Colleges summer program for young people from grade 4-12. The award requires acceptance to the College followed by a review of the candidates academic credentials, community service and references. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; must remain full-time and have satisfactory progress. more...

Boys-Girls State Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Ripon College in Wisconsin full-time. Applicants must participate in their states annual Boy/Girl State Conference each summer. By involvement in this program, candidate has demonstrated leadership and commitment to our nations democratic ideals. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; must remain full-time and have satisfactory progress. more...

Forensics Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Ripon College who have an interest in Forensics. Candidates must have a minimum high school GPA of 3.5, ACT score of 27 or SAT of 1220. Applicants must have commitment to academic excellence and service to their school and community. On-campus interview is required. Student must remain active in a forensics group each year for renewal. Renewable; must remain full-time, participate in three invitationals, and have satisfactory academic progress. more...

Knop Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen attending Ripon College in Wisconsin full-time, majoring in Natural Science or Mathematics. Applicants must have a minimum 3.8 GPA, rank in the top 5% of their class and a recommendation from a high school teacher. Candidates must participate in community service activities. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; must maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Legacy Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen whose father, mother, brother, sister, grandmother, grandfather previously attended Ripon College, who will be majoring in Liberal Arts or Sciences. Applicants must have good academic credentials and participation in community service activities. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; must remain full-time and have satisfactory progress. more...

National Latin Exam Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Ripon College in Wisconsin. Student must have earned at least a Magna designation on the Levell II or higher National Latin Exam. Applicant must have been accepted to the college and have documentation of the exam results. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; must take one Latin, Greek or Classics course the first year, be enrolled full-time, and have satisfactory progress. more...

Pickard Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Ripon College. Student must have a minimum 3.8 GPA, ACT score of 30, SAT score of 1340, rank in the top 5% of their class, and be involved in extracurricular activities. Applicant must submit two recommendations, and will be required to attend interview. Renewable; must maintain 3.25 GPA. There is 1 award for $17,000, and 9 awards for $10,000. more...

Professor William J. Woolley History Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Ripon College in Wisconsin full-time, majoring in History. Number of awards varies. Renewable; must remain full-time and have satisfactory progress. more...

Theatre Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Ripon College, who are active in Theatre. Student must submit two letters of recommendation, and attend an interview and/or audition with the drama faculty. Candidate must have proven talent in performing arts, commitment to academic excellence and school/community service. Award is renewable as long as student remains active in theatre. more...

United Church Of Christ (Ucc) Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Ripon College in Wisconsin full-time. Applicants must be actively involved in the United Church of Christ. Candidates must have academic excellence, service to their school/community, and a special application and nomination from students minister. Number of awards varies. Renewable; must remain full-time and have satisfactory progress. more...

Valedictorian Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Ripon College who are ranked number one in their high school class. Student must submit two recommendations and attend an interview. Number of awards is unlimited. Award amounts up to $5,000. Renewable; must remain full-time and satisfactory progress. more...
