University of Minnesota-Crookston Scholarships


Achievement Scholarship - Umc
The Achievement Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at the University of Minnesota, Crookston. You must have an ACT score between 21 and 22 and have a GPA of 3.25 to be eligible for this award. Please locate your GPA and ACT scores on the chart listed on the sponsor's Web site to determine the amount of your award. more...
