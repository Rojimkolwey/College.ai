Hillsdale College Scholarships


Church Tuition Assistance Program
The Church Tuition Assistance Program is available to students at Hillsdale College. Hillsdale will match the amount given by your home church up to a maximum amount of $200 per semester. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Hillsdale College
The Dean's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hillsdale College. You must have an ACT score of between 23 and 25 in order to qualify for this award. more...

Distinct Honor Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at Hillsdale College. Students must have a minimum 3.85 high school GPA, a minimum 32 ACT score or a minimum 1420 SAT score, demonstrate leadership skills, and participation in extracurricular activities. An interview may be required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Faculty Scholarship - Hillsdale College
The Faculty Scholarship is available to entering freshman students at Hillsdale College. You must have an ACT score of between 20 and 22 to be eligible for this award. more...

Legacy Scholarship - Hillsdale College
The Legacy Scholarship is available to students who are enrolled for at least 12 hours at Hillsdale College. You must be the child or grandchild of a Hillsdale alumnus or the child of a parent who is employed as a minister and have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible. more...

Presidential Plus Scholarship - Hillsdale College
The Presidential Plus Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hillsdale College. You must have have an ACT score of 30 or higher to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Hillsdale College
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hillsdale College. You must have an ACT score of between 26 and 29 in order to qualify for this award. more...

Refer A Saint Scholarship
The Refer a Saint Scholarship is available to students at Hillsdale College. You must submit a letter of reference from a HIllsdale alumni in order to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Off-Campus Merit Scholarship
The Transfer Off-Campus Merit Scholarship is available to students transferring into Hillsdale College. You must have a cumulative GPA of at least 2.0 and be residing on-campus to be eligible for this award. more...
