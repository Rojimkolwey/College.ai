The Academic Merit Scholarship is available to second- and third-year students at California Western, School of Law, San Diego. You must be at the top of your class based on the first year's cumulative grade point average to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Annual award for incoming graduate students enrolled at California Western School of Law. Students must have a minimum of 10 years experience in a career or occupation and demonstrate financial need. Due dates are April 15 for fall semester and November 15 for spring semester. Award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Dean's Diversity Scholarship - Cwsl
The Dean's Diversity Scholarship is available to second - and third - year students at California Western School of Law. You must have at least an 80 cumulative grade point average after your first year to be eligible for this award. Selection is also based on academic achievement; financial need; demonstrated commitment to community activities; and ethnic, cultural or other diversity characteristics. more...

Deans Scholarship Program For Ethnic & Cultural Diversity
Scholarship for incoming graduate students enrolled at California Western School of Law. Students must be from under-represented groups, or African-American, Alaskan Native, Arabic/Asian-American, Hispanic/Mexican-American, Native American, Pacific Islander, Puerto Rican, or LGBT, or non-traditional, or from lower socio-economic backgrounds. Applicants must have financial need and demonstrate academic achievement. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...
