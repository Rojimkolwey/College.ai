Rockhurst University Scholarships


Achievement Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time freshmen enrolling at Rockhurst University. Students must have a minimum 3.4 GPA or rank in the top 35% of the class and have a minimum 22 ACT or 1020 SAT. Selection is based on academic credentials. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Community Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time freshmen enrolling at Rockhurst University. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA or rank in the top 40% of the class and have a minimum 22 ACT or 1020 SAT. Selection is based on academic credentials. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Distinguished Scholar
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students transferring to Rockhurst University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.6 CGPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Dowling Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time freshmen enrolling at Rockhurst University. Students must have a minimum 3.8 GPA or rank in the top 15% of the class and have a minimum 30 ACT or 1320 SAT score. Selection is based on academic credentials. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Family Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students transferring to Rockhurst University. Students must have dependents in the same family currently attending. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Fine & Performing Arts Award
Scholarship for entering freshmen or transfer students enrolling at Rockhurst University. Awards are based on fine or performing arts ability and participation. An audition, portfolio and/or interview are required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Finucane Service Award
Scholarship for full-time freshmen or transfer students enrolling in Rockhurst University. Applicants must have a sound academic record, and demonstrate outstanding service in the community. Renewable. more...

Greater Kansas City Community College Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students transferring to Rockhurst Univesity. Applicants must be transferring from the Greater Kansas City Community College systems, have completed a minimum of 12 credit hours and have a minimum 3.0 CGPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Jesuit Opportunity Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students transferring to Rockhurst University. Applicants must have a 3.0-3.29 CGPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Lakas Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Rockhurst University enrolled in Writing. A portfolio must be included with application packet. An interview is required. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Language Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Rockhurst University majoring in French or Spanish. An essay must be included with application packet. An interview is required. Renewable. more...

Tom D. Harmon Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Rockhurst University majoring in Nursing. Awards are based on competitive interview. Due date may vary. Award amount is full-tuition. Renewable. more...
