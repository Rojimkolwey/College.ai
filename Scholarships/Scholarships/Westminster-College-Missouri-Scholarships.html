Alumni/Sibling Award - Westminster College
The Alumni/Sibling Award is available to incoming freshmen at Westminster College who are a son, daughter, grandson, granddaughter, or sibling of a Westminster alumnus or current student. more...

Churchill Scholarship - Westminster College
The Churchill Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Westminster College. You must have a minimum 26 ACT/ 1800 SAT and a minimum 3.25 GPA to qualify for this award. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Westminster College
The Dean's Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Westminster College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and an ACT/ SAT score of 23/ 1590 to qualify for this award. more...

Leadership Award - Westminster College
The Leadership Award is available to incoming freshmen at Westminster College. You must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and at least a 21 ACT score (970 SAT) to be considered for this award. You must also have participated in extracurricular activities, volunteer work and church involvement. more...

President's Scholarship - Westminster College
The President's Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Westminster College. You must have a minimum 3.25 GPA and an ACT/ SAT score of 26/ 1800 to qualify for this award. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Westminster College
The Transfer Scholarship is available to transfer students at Westminster College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be considered for this award. more...

Trustee's Scholarship - Westminster College
