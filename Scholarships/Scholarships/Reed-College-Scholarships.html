Reed College Scholarships


Alta S. Corbett Collaborative Summer Research Fellowships
Annual award for undergraduate junior, senior or graduate student attending Reed College. Award is intended to support study by Reed students. Student must submit research proposal during the spring semester for consideration for summer research. Due date varies. more...

Beinecke Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate junior students attending Reed College full-time, majoring in the Arts, Humanities and Social Sciences, who plan to attend an accredited graduate school. Applicants must be a U.S. citizen, have demonstrated financial need and proven academic ability. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

British Marshall Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate senior or graduate students attending Reed College, who wish to study abroad at any university in the United Kingdom. Applicants must be a U.S. citizen; have a minimum 3.7 GPA and have demonstrated financial need. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

George J. Mitchell Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate senior or graduate students attending Reed College who wish to pursue one year of study at an institution of higher learning in Ireland or Northern Ireland. Applicants must be a U.S. citizen and under the age of 30 years old on October 1 in the year of application. Candidates must have strong academic record, leadership and community service. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Gerald M. Meier Scholarship For Distinction In Economics
Scholarship for undergraduate senior students attending Reed College, majoring in Economics. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Kaspar T. Locher Summer Creative Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Reed College, majoring in one of the Arts. Award can be used to support independent work in creative writing, visual arts, theater, dance or music. Submit 500-750 word proposal for your project and an additional 500 word statement on previous projects. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Sperling Studentship Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate senior or graduate students attending Reed College, who wish to pursue a three-year doctoral degree at Cambridge University in England. Applicants must have high academic achievement. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...
