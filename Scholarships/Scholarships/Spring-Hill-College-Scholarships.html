Spring Hill College Scholarships


Gulf Coast Scholars Scholarships
Scholarship for students who reside in counties bordering the Gulf of Mexico in Alabama, Florida (Escambia and Santa Rosa only) and Mississippi and are incoming freshmen accepted at Spring Hill College in Mobile, Alabama. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and an ACT composite score of 26. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Spring Hill Recognition Grant
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Spring Hill College, who served as an Azalea Trail Maid or a Dogwood Trail Maid. Submit list of extracurricular activities, high school transcript, AP scores (if applicable) and recommendation form from high school counselor with application packet. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable; maintain 2.0 GPA. more...

Spring Hill Scholars Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted at Spring Hill College in Mobile, Alabama. Recipients are chosen through a competitive interview process. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA, ACT score of 28 or SAT score of 1230, and class rank in to top 25%. Candidates must demonstrate student leadership, high school activities and church or community service. Due date varies, but must be admitted by January 13 to be included in the Spring Hill College Scholars pool. Renewable. more...

Trustee Honors Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen students accepted to attend Spring Hill College in Mobile, Alabama. Recipients are chosen through a competitive interview process. Selection is based on grades and scores of at least a 3.5 GPA and a ACT score of 28. Renewable. more...
