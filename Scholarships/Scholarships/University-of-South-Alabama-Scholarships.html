Abraham Mitchell Business Scholarship Program
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend full-time at the University of South Alabama Mitchell College of Business. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA, a 28-36 ACT score, and be U.S. citizens. Preference given to students from Mobile and Alabama. Number of awards may vary. more...

Adams Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students who are Alabama Young Bankers members attending Alabama Banking School at the University of South Alabama in Mobile. Applicants must be practicing bankers, deriving at least two-thirds of their total income from banking, have proved themselves good, sound bankers and business people, and contributed constructively to their own banks and to the banking profession and demonstrated sincere community interest. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...
