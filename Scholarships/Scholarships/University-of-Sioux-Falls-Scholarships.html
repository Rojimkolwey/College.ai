University of Sioux Falls Scholarships


Athletic Participation Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Sioux Falls participating in athletics. Freshmen must meet two of the following criteria: a minimum 18 ACT; rank in the top 50% of the high school class; a minimum 2.0 GPA. Selection is based on talent, participation and academic success. An audition is required. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Bailey Wyoming Scholarship
Scholarship for Wyoming residents who are undergraduate students attending the University of Sioux Falls. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Baptist Student Award
Scholarship for Baptist students who are full-time undergraduates attending the University of Sioux Falls. A pastors recommendation must be included with application packet. Students must file the FAFSA by March 1. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

The Board of Trustees Academic Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Sioux Falls. You must demonstrate high academic achievement to be considered for this award. more...
