Albion College Scholarships


Albion Alumni Grant
The Albion Alumni Grant is available to students at Albion College. Your family must include one or more Albion alumna/alumnus to be eligible for this award. more...

Distinguished Albion Scholars Award
The Distinguished Albion Scholars Award is available to incoming freshmen at Albion College. You must demonstrate outstanding academic achievements, skills, special talents, and abilities that define a superior Albion first-year student. Additionally, these students will serve as leaders and mentors for others in class, in co-curricular activities, and within the greater Albion community. Distinguished Albion Scholars are selected from among Trustee Scholarship awardees. more...

Fine Arts Scholarship - Albion College
The Fine Arts Scholarship is available to students demonstrating talent and planning to participate in art, music or theatre at Albion College. This award is renewable. more...

Ford Institute Scholarship - Albion College
The Ford Institute Scholarship is available to students at Albion College. You must express interest in law, government, or public service to be eligible for this award. more...

Gerstacker Institute Scholarship
The Gerstacker Institute Scholarship is available to students majoring in business management at Albion College. You must demonstrate academic achievement, financial need and personal merit to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

John Wesley Matching Scholarship - Albion College
The John Wesley Matching Scholarship is available to students at Albion College. You must have a scholarship from your local United Methodist Church to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Student Scholarship - Albion College
The Transfer Student Scholarship is available to students entering Albion College. You must be transferring from a two- or four-year college and have attained at least one full semester of acceptable academic course work to be eligible for this award. more...

United Methodist Clergy Award - Albion College
The United Methodist Clergy Award is available to students at Albion College. You must be a child of an ordained United Methodist clergy to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

United Methodist Student Scholarship - Albion College
The United Methodist Student Scholarship is available to students at Albion College. You must be an active member of your local United Methodist Church to be eligible for this award. more...

Verle Klungness Scholarship
The Verle Klungness Scholarship is available to students at Albion College who graduated from one of Michigan's Upper Peninsula high schools with an exceptional academic record. more...
