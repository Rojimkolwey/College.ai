Southern Methodist University Scholarships


Community College Honors Scholarships
Scholarship for transfer students from any community college enrolling at Southern Methodist University, enrolling in Honors courses. Applicants must have at least 50 transferable credit hours with a 3.50 transferable GPA. Award amount is half-tuition up to five semesters. Due date for fall entry-April 1; spring-November 1. Number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain full-time enrollment and 3.0 GPA. more...

Community College Scholarships
Scholarship for transfer students from any community college enrolling at Southern Methodist University. Applicant must have at least 50 transferable credit hours with a 3.50 transferable GPA. Award amount is $1,500 per semester up to five semesters. Due date for fall entry-April 1st; spring-November 1. Number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain full-time enrollment and 3.0 GPA. more...

Dallas-Area Community College Scholarships
Scholarship for Transfer students from Dallas, Tarrant, or Collin County community college districts enrolling at Southern Methodist University. Applicant must have at least 50 transferable credit hours with a 3.0 transferable GPA. Interview required. Submit an essay and 2-3 letters or recommendation with application packet. Award amount is full-tuition up to five semesters; renewable with full-time enrollment and 3.0 GPA. more...

Deans Scholars Award
Scholarship for entering freshmen accepted at Southern Methodist University who are finalists in SMUs Presidents Scholar Award. Awards based on minimum 17 high school credits, recentered SAT score of 1350, class rank (top 10%), demonstrated involvement, leadership abilities and interview. Completed admissions application is reviewed automatically for scholarship eligibility. Award amount is for half tuition; renewable up to four years with a 3.0 GPA and full-time enrollment. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Dedman College Of Humanities & Sciences
Scholarship for undergraduates enrolled at Southern Methodist University in the Dedman College of Humanities and Sciences pursuing a degree in the Humanities, Natural Sciences or Social Sciences. Numerous awards are offered with varying criteria. Submit a SMU Application for Scholarships with application packet to be considered. Award amount and number of awards varies. Renewable; maintain full-time enrollment in a Dedman College major and 3.0 GPA. more...

Edwin L. Cox School Of Business
Scholarship for juniors or seniors at Southern Methodist University enrolled in the Edwin L. Cox School of Business majoring in Business. Various awards based on academic excellence, outstanding leadership, and community service; some may have very specific donor stipulations. Applicant must file financial aid application in the spring semester for most awards. Award amount and number of awards varies. more...

Nancy Ann & Ray L. Hunt Leadership Scholars Program
Scholarship for entering Freshmen accepted at Southern Methodist University. Awards based on minimum 16 high school credits, recentered SAT score of 1200 or ACT score of 28, class rank (top 25%), demonstrated extraordinary leadership, essay, letter of recommendation addressing leadership abilities, and an interview. Completed admissions application is reviewed automatically for scholarship eligibility. Award amount is for full tuition and fees, less the amount of tuition/fees at the leading public university in the students state of residency. Award also includes tuition and transportation for a semester or full year abroad in one of SMUs International Programs. Renewable. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Transfer Scholarships
Scholarship for members of Phi Theta Kappa Honor Society who are transfer students enrolling at Southern Methodist University. Applicant must have at least 50 transferable credit hours with a minimum 3.5 CGPA. Award amount is $2,000 per year to be used as a supplemental award to the Honor Transfer award or to be used as the Community College Scholarship ($3000). Submit a PTK application and two letter of recommendations with application packet. Due date for fall entry-April 1st; spring-November 1st. Renewable up to five terms if full-time enrollment and a 3.0 GPA are maintained. more...

President's Scholar Award - Southern Methodist U.
The President's Scholar Award is open to entering freshmen at Southern Methodist University. You must have a minimum SAT score of 700 verbal, 650 math and 1350 composite (or the ACT equivalent) to be considered for this award. You must also rank in the top 10 percent of your graduating class, have a minimum 3.3 GPA, and demonstrate both involvement and leadership. This full-tuition award is renewable provided you maintain a minimum 3.3 GPA. more...
