Beech Street Foundation Scholarship
The Beech Street Foundation Scholarship is available to students at the University of the District of Columbia, David A. Clark School of Law. You must be a Washington, D.C. resident to be eligible for this full-tuition award. more...

Book Scholarship
Scholarship for international (non-US citizens) undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of the District of Columbia. Preference given to students who are not eligible for other funding. Awards given per semester. more...

Charles & Laverne Earley, Jr. Scholarship In Teacher Education
Scholarship for District of Columbia, Maryland, or Virginia residents who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of the District of Columbia majoring in Education. Students must have a minimum 2.5 CGPA, be graduates of Dunbar Senior High School in Washington, DC, and be pursuing a career in teaching. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Charles & Pamela Ogletree Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for District of Columbia residents who are graduating high school seniors from a District of Columbia Public High School planning to attend full-time at the University of the District of Columbia. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA, be first generation college students, and participate in one University leadership position. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...
