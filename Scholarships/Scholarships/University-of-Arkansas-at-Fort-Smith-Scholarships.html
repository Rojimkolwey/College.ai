University of Arkansas-Fort Smith Scholarships


Arkansas Firefighters Foundation Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. First priority is given to dependents of Arkansas firefighters who were killed or incapacitated in the line of duty; second priority to dependents of firefighters deceased or incapacitated other than in the line of duty, third priority to Arkansas firefighters or their dependents. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Arkansas Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at the University of Arkansas at Fort Worth. Applicants must have completed the Arkansas Scholars Program. Number of awards may vary. Award covers full tuition. Nonrenewable. more...

Boreham Engineering Scholarship
The Boreham Engineering Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at the University of Arkansas, Fort Smith. You must be majoring in mechanical or electrical engineering, have a minimum 27 ACT composite score, and a minimum 3.5 GPA to qualify for this award. The recipient of this award will also receive a new laptop computer and a $2,500 Sebastian Commons housing waiver. more...

C.A. & Carrie T. Lick Endowment (Club)
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. First consideration is given to family members of Weldon, Williams & Lick, Inc.; second consideration to employees of Weldon, Williams & Lick, Inc.; third consideration to participants in the Fort Smith Boys & Girls Club Program. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Chancellors Leadership Council Scholarship
Scholarship for first time entering freshmen at the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must have a minimum 27 ACT or equivalent SAT score, a minimum 3.5 GPA and excellent leadership performance. Students with outstanding leadership skills and lower ACT scores will be considered. Award amount is for full tuition, housing waiver and book allowance. Renewable. more...

Christ The King Church
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are active parishoners of the Christ the King Church attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Executive Network Of Women Fort Smith Chapter
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must be single mothers starting her education or re-entering the workforce. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Fort Smith Association Of Petroleum Landmen-Stuart Crews Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Sequoyah, Leflore, Haskell, Latimer, or Pittsburgh counties, Oklahoma or Crawford, Sebastian, Franklin, Logan, Johnson or Pope counties, Arkansas who are undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Preference is given to dependents of an individual who derives income from the oil and gas industry. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Fort Smith Commissary Kiwanis
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must have been members of the Key Club in high school. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Fort Smith Zonta International
Scholarship for sophomores attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Preference is given to an older female or single parent. Applicants must agree to attend a minimum of one Zonta meeting and give an outline of future plans. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Hembree Eagle Scout Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must have achieved the rank of Eagle Scout from a local area Council of Boy Scouts of America. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Jim Jenkins & Fred Kirkpatrick
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are dependents of a full-time employee of the Coca Cola Bottling Company in Fort Smith and are attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Kola Fortune Memorial
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must be single mothers. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Lion Pride (Out Of State) Scholarship
Scholarship for out of state residents who are first-time entering freshmen at the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must have a minimum 23 ACT or the equivalent SAT score, have a minimum 3.5 GPA and rank in the top one-third of the class. An interview is required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Lowes Employee Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are employees of the Lowes in Fort Smith and are attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Mollie Wilson Single Parent Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must be a single parent and have a strong academic record. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Robert M. Powell
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Preference is given to employees of USA truck. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Roland S. Boreham Jr. Engineering Scholarship
Scholarship for first-time entering freshmen at the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith majoring in Electrical or Mechanical Engineering. Applicants must have a minimum 27 ACT score or equivalent SAT score and have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Finalists will be contacted for an interview. Renewable. more...

University Honors Scholarship
Scholarship for first-time entering freshmen at the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must be talented and motivated to succeed, have a minimum 27 ACT or the SAT equivalent and have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Finalists will be invited to interview. Award amount covers full-tuition, housing waiver and book allowance. Renewable. more...

University Residential Scholarship
Scholarship for Arkansas or Oklahoma residents who are first-time entering freshmen at the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Applicants must have a minimum 26 ACT or the SAT equivalent and a minimum 3.25 GPA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

W.R. Walker Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are dependents of employees of Stephens, Inc. and are attending the University of Arkansas at Fort Smith. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
