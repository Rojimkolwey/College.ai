Paine College Scholarships


Alumni Dependent Grant
Scholarship for dependents of Paine College alumni who are undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Campus Wide Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Central Savannah River Area (Csra) Tuition Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend Paine College. Students must rank in the top 10% of the graduating high school class and have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

College Fund-Uncf Remission Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Paine College. Students must be dependents of a President of a College Fund/UNCF college. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Comparable Cost Grant
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from a Central Savannah River Area (CSRA) high school planning to attend Paine College. Students must rank in the top 25% of the graduating high school class Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Dependent Tuition Waiver Award
Scholarship for biological/adoptive dependents of regular, full-time Paine College employees who are undergraduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Spousal Tuition Waiver Award
Scholarship for spouses of regular, full-time Paine College employees who are undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Staff Tuition Waiver Award
Scholarship for full-time Paine College employees who are undergraduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Award is for one course per semester. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

United Methodist-Christian Methodist Episcopal Grant
Scholarship for United Methodist/Christian Methodist Episcopal undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards may vary. more...

United Methodist-Christian Methodist Episcopal Ministers Dependent Grant
Scholarship for dependents of ministers who are undergraduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

United Methodist-Christian Methodist Episcopal Ministers Grant
Scholarship for United Methodist/Christian Methodist Episcopal Ministers who are undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Paine College. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...
