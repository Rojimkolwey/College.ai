Columbia International University Scholarships


Abhe Grant - Ciu
The ABHE Grant is available to full - time seminary and graduate students at Columbia International University. You must have graduated from an ABHE - accredited university to be eligible for this award. more...

Ben Lippen Scholarship - Ciu
The Ben Lippen Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Columbia International University. You must be a graduating senior at Ben Lippen High School to be eligible for this award. more...

Board Scholarship - Ciu
The Board Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Columbia International University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.75 to be eligible for this award. Freshmen must also have a minimum 1200 SAT / 26 ACT score. more...

Dean Scholarship - Ciu
The Dean Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Columbia International University. Entering freshmen must have a minimum GPA of 3.25 and a minimum 1000 SAT / 21 ACT score to be eligible for this award. Transfer students must have a GPA between 3.25 and 3.49. more...

Impact 360 Grant - Ciu
The Impact 360 Grant is available to students at Columbia International University. You must have participated in the Impact 360 program for one year to be eligible for this award. more...

Preaching Award
Scholarship for male third-year graduate students enrolled at Columbia International University in the seminary program. Students must be pursuing a Master of Divinity degree and demonstrate exceptional knowledge of Gods Word. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Ciu
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Columbia International University. Entering freshmen must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and a minimum 1100 SAT / 24 ACT score to be eligible for this award. Transfer students must have a GPA between 3.5 and 3.74. more...

Ravencrest Grant - Ciu
The Ravencrest Grant is available to students at Columbia International University. You must have attended Ravencrest Bible School for one year to be eligible for this award. more...

Servant Leader Scholarship - Ciu
The Servant Leader Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Columbia International University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and a minimum 900 SAT / 19 ACT score to be eligible for this award. Transfer students must have a GPA between 3.0 and 3.24. more...

Timberline Grant - Ciu
The Timberline Grant is available to students at Columbia International University. You must have attended Timberline Grant Bible School for one year to be eligible for this award. more...

Valedictorian Grant - Ciu
The Valedictorian Grant is available to students at Columbia International University. You must be the valedictorian of a class of least five students to be eligible for this award. more...

Word Of Life Grant - Ciu
