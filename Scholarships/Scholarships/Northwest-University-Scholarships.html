Northwest University Scholarships


Northwest Athletic Scholarship
The Northwest Athletic Scholarship is available to students attending Northwest University. You must have recognized athletic talent to be eligible for this award. more...

Paul Fenton Memorial Scholarship
The Paul Fenton Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students attending Northwest University. You must demonstrate ministry potential in the ministerial or missions field, be a member or adherent of a Montana District of the Assemblies of God church, have a high school GPA of at least 3.5 (entering freshmen) or a college GPA of at least 3.0. You must also have an outstanding citizenship record and demonstrate a Christian lifestyle showing constructive leadership in a Christian college environment to be eligible for this award. more...

Tyler James Pecota Memorial Scholarship
The Tyler James Pecota Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students attending Northwest University. You must be majoring in elementary education or children and youth ministries with a minimum 3.0 GPA as a student in high school or college and demonstrate financial need. You must also have an outstanding citizenship record and demonstrate a Christian lifestyle showing constructive leadership in a Christian college environment. more...
