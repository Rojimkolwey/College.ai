Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi Scholarships


Achieve Scholarship-Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi
The Achieve Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi. You must have a converted minimum GPA of 4.00 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Impact Scholarship-Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi
The Impact Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi. You must have a converted minimum GPA of 2.5 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Islander Scholarship-Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi
The Islander Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi. You must have a converted minimum GPA of 3.3 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Morris L. Lichtenstein Jr. Endowed Scholarship
The Morris L. Lichtenstein Jr. Endowed Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi. You must rank in the top seven percent of your high school graduating class and have a minimum ACT score of 32 or SAT score of 1390 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Presidential Scholarship-Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi
The Presidential Scholarship is available to incoming freshman undergraduate students at Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi. You must have a converted minimum GPA of 4.01 to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Student Transfer Education Program (Step) Scholarship
The STEP Scholarship is available to students transferring to Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi who have completed at least 30 credit hours. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 to be eligible for this award. more...

Study Abroad Scholarship-Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi
The Study Abroad Scholarship is available to students at Texas A & M University-Corpus Christi who will participate in a credit-bearing study abroad program. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 for undergraduate or 3.0 for graduate students to be eligible for this award. Graduating seniors are not eligible for this award. more...
