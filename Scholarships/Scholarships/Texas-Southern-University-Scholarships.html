Texas Southern University Scholarships


Houston Louis Stokes Alliance For Minority Participation Scholarship
The Houston Louis Stokes Alliance for Minority Participation Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Texas Southern University. You must have a minimum 3.3 high school GPA, a minimum SAT score of 900 or a minimum ACT score of 19. You must also have completed three years of science courses in high school and declare a SMET major to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable. more...

School Of Business Bronze Scholarship - Tsu
The School of Business Bronze Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Texas Southern University. You must major in business, have a minimum 3.0 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1000 or a minimum ACT score of 18. This award is renewable with a maintained 3.0 GPA. more...

School Of Business Gold Scholarship - Tsu
The School of Business Gold Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Texas Southern University. You must major in business, have a minimum 3.5 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1200 or a minimum ACT score of 22 to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable with a maintained 3.25 GPA. more...

School Of Business Silver Scholarship - Tsu
The School of Business Silver Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Texas Southern University. You must major in business, have a minimum 3.25 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1100 or a minimum ACT score of 20. This award is renewable with a maintained 3.25 GPA. more...
