Greenville University Scholarships


Alumni Dependent Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Greenville College who are children of Greenville College Alumni. Selection based on academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Boyer Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Greenville College who are National Merit Finalists or Semi-finalists. Award amount is for 3/4 tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Christian Service Dependent Scholarship
Employee-Related awards for undergraduate students accepted to Greenville College who are dependant children of active/retired ministers or missionaries. Selection based academic achievement. Employees must be full-time and primary income is from the church. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Church Partnership Scholarship Program
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Greenville College. GC will match funds from applicants who have financial support from their local congregation. Matching funds will not exceed $1,000. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Concert Band-Chamber Orchestra Participation Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Greenville College who participate in the Chamber Orchestra or the Concert Band. Selection based on audition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Greenville University
The Dean's Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Greenville University. You must have a GPA between 2.95 - 3.29 and be enrolled on a full-time basis to qualify for this award. Selection is based on academic merit. more...

The Engineering Honors Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Greenville University who are majoring in engineering. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.75 and minimum ACT score of 29 to be eligible for this award. Additionally, you must live on-campus. more...

Scholarship for undergraduate students accepted to Greenville College who are members of active in Free Methodist church programs. Selection based on church activities and academic achievement. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
