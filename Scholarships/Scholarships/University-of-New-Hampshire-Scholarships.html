University of New Hampshire-Main Campus Scholarships


Christine Young Chadwick '29 Memorial Scholarship
The Christine Young Chadwick '29 Memorial Scholarship is open to undergraduate students at Keene State College, Granite State College, Plymouth State University and the University of New Hampshire in Durham and Manchester. You must be majoring in elementary education to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to residents of New Hampshire and made on the basis of merit and/or need. more...

Granite State Scholars Scholarship
The Granite State Scholars Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Keene State College, Granite State College, Plymouth State University and the University of New Hampshire at Durham and Manchester. You must be a New Hampshire resident, be in the upper 10 percent of your high school graduating class, and have a minimum SAT score of 1200. You must also demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Herbert C. "Dinty" Moore Memorial Fund Scholarship
The Herbert C. "Dinty" Moore Memorial Fund Scholarship is available to dairy science majors at the University of New Hampshire. You must be a New Hampshire resident and demonstrate sincere interest in the dairy industry and academic achievement to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

James J. Brunelli Memorial Fund Scholarship
The James J. Brunelli Memorial Fund Scholarship is available to students with documented learning disabilities at the University of New Hampshire. Preference is given to students enrolled in the Thompson School of Applied Science. Criteria for selection include demonstrated independence, motivation and determination toward the educational experience. more...

Jesse F. Davis '37 Scholarship
The Jesse F. Davis '37 Scholarship is open to music majors at Keene State College, Granite State College, Plymouth State University and the University of New Hampshire at both Durham and Manchester. You must be a junior or senior who has a minimum cumulative GPA of 3.0 to qualify for this award. more...

Leslie S. Hubbard Scholarship
The Leslie S. Hubbard Scholarship is open to adult students in the University System of New Hampshire. You must be a resident of Cheshire or Sullivan counties in New Hampshire or Windham County in Vermont. In addition, you must be a continuing student registered for as little as a single course, had your education interrupted or pursuing a college education while working or fulfilling family and other obligations. more...

Lucille Plante Davis '38 Scholarship
The Lucille Plante Davis '38 Scholarship is open to French majors at Keene State College. You must be a full-time junior or senior and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to qualify for this award. more...

Marjorie Paulsen Hilton '44 Scholarship
The Marjorie Paulsen Hilton '44 Scholarship is open to students in the University System of New Hampshire. You must be a junior or senior studying home economics or early childhood development. You must also be a resident of Connecticut or New Hampshire, and demonstrate strong promise in your field and financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Mary C.E. Hamilton '44 And Erik H. Hamilton Scholarship

Hamilton Scholarship is open to students at Keene State College, Granite State College, Plymouth State University and the University of New Hampshire at Durham and Manchester. National Association Of Women In Construction Scholarship - Unh
The National Association of Women in Construction Scholarship is available to female junior and senior students at the University of New Hampshire. You must be interested in a construction-related field to be eligible for this award. more...

New Hampshire Eastern States Exposition Trustees Scholarship - Unh
The New Hampshire Eastern States Exposition Trustees Scholarship is available to students majoring in restaurant management, dietetic technician, horticultural technology, animal science and forest technology at the University of New Hampshire. Selection is based on FFA participation, career goals and financial need. more...

New Hampshire Future Farmers Of America (Ffa) Scholarship - Unh
The New Hampshire Future Farmers of America (FFA) Scholarship is available to students majoring in restaurant management, dietetic technician, horticultural technology, animal science and forest technology at the University of New Hampshire. Selection is based on FFA participation, academic achievement and potential, career goals and financial need. more...

New Hampshire Rural Rehabilitation Corporation Scholarship - Unh
The New Hampshire Rural Rehabilitation Corporation Scholarship is available to first-year students enrolled in the Thompson School of Applied Science at the University of New Hampshire. You must have a farming background to be eligible for this award. Consideration is given to students New Hampshire farms, rural New Hampshire towns (population less than 5,000), or those with a vocational agriculture or 4-H background. more...

Paul J. Holloway Scholarship
The Paul J. Holloway Scholarship is open to full-time students in the University System of New Hampshire. You must be from New Hampshire and demonstrate academic excellence to qualify for this award. Preference will be given to students who have demonstrated outstanding promise as a teacher and been accepted into the teacher education program. more...

Peerless Insurance Company Excellence In Mathematics Award
