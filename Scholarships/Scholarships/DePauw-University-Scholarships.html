DePauw University Scholarships


Activity Grants
Activity grants for DePauw University undergraduates participating in Art, Communication (forensics, theater, or electronic media) or Journalism studies. Applicant must have extensive and meritorious experience in the area of the grant. Submit a portfolio, resume, and letters of recommendations with application packet. Due date, award amount and number of awards varies. Renewable; if continuing criteria is met. more...

Alumni Legacy Award - Depauw
The Alumni Legacy Award is available to students at DePauw University. You must be the child or grandchild of a DePauw University alumna/us to be eligible for this award. more...

Alumni Legacy Awards
Scholarship for DePauw University undergraduates whose mother, father, grandmother, or grandfather are alumni of the university. Financial need is not a consideration. Award amounts range $1,000-$5,000. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Edward Rector Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at DePauw University in Indiana. Applicants must demonstrate superior academic excellence and potential for contribution to the intellectual community at DePauw. Award amount covers full-tuition. Number of awards varies. Renewable; up to four years. more...

Honor Scholar Awards
Scholarship for students attending DePauw University in Indiana who are outstanding students in the Honor Scholar Program. Selection is based on an interview. Eligible candidates will be selected to attend the Honors & Fellows interview weekend by the students responses to questions on the application. Amount and number of awards varies. more...

L.E.A.D. Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending DePauw University in Indiana. Selection is based on students academic achievement, demonstrated leadership abilities and commitment to building a diverse community. Submit essay and detailed resume with application packet. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; recipients are required to attend monthly meetings and be involved in community service to complete the required monthly hours. more...

Malpas Trust Scholarship
Scholarship for Indiana residents (born in Indiana) who are graduating high school seniors from an Indiana high school planning to attend DePauw University. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need (per FAFSA application). Candidates must a minimum 3.5 GPA. Recipients of this award are not permitted to hold a job during the academic year. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable; must maintain 3.25 GPA. more...

National Merit Scholarship - Depauw University

Presidential Rector Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen at DePauw University in Indiana who are designated Rector Scholars. Applicants must demonstrate superior academic excellence and potential for contribution to the intellectual community at DePauw. All finalists will be invited to the campus for an interview. Award amount covers full-tuition, room, and board. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable; up to four years. more...

Rolla M. Malpas Trust Scholarship
Scholarship for Indiana residents who were born there, graduated from an Indiana high school, and are incoming freshman at DePauw University (IN). Applicant must have graduated high school with a minimum 3.0 GPA, be born of U.S. citizens, and have substantial financial need. Student must file the FAFSA. Award meets the recipients full need. Number of awards may vary. more...

Ruth Clark & Phillip Holton Memorial Scholarships
Scholarship for DePauw University students who have a strong academic record and demonstrate exceptional leadership and service to school, community, or family. Award is based on merit. Finalists will be contacted for an interview. Award amount ranges from $1,000 to full-tuition. Number of awards varies. Renewable; if continuing criteria is met. more...

Science-Mathematics Scholarships
Scholarship competition for high school seniors planning to attend DePauw University in Indiana, majoring in Science or Mathematics. A Science and Mathematics Scholarship Day is hosted for interested students to attend. Finalists will be contacted for an interview. Due date varies. Renewable; if continuing criteria is met. Contact the Office of Admissions for more information and the special application that is required. more...

United Methodist Foundation Awards
