Sinclair Community College Scholarships


Dayton Area Defense Contractors Association (Dadca) Scholarship In Science, Technology, Engineering, Or Mathematics (Stem)
Scholarship for Ohio residents who are Dayton Area Defense Contractors Association members, or their dependents, and are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Sinclair Community College majoring in Science, Technology, Engineering, or Mathematics. Students must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA and be U.S. citizens/permanent residents. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Displaced Industrial Workers Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Sinclair Community College. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA, work in the Dayton, Ohio area, and be involved in plant closings, forced manufacturing layoffs, or buyouts. A FAFSA must be submitted. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Finish Line Grant
Scholarship for Montgomery County, Ohio residents who are undergraduate students enrolled at Sinclair Community College. Students must have financial need and be within two quarters from graduation. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Hispanic-Latino Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students of Hispanic/Latino descent who are enrolled at Sinclair Community College. Students must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Mental Health Technology Book Grants
Scholarship for college sophomores enrolled at Sinclair Community College in the Mental Health Technology Program. Students must have a minimum 2.25 CGPA and be involved in the Mental Health Technology Club. Award is for summer quarter. Number of awards may vary. A personal statement and family income documentation must be included with application packet. more...

Mental Health Technology Tuition Grants
Scholarship for college sophomores enrolled at Sinclair Community College in the Mental Health Technology Program. Students must have a minimum 2.25 CGPA and be involved in the Mental Health Technology Club. Award is for the summer quarter. Number of awards may vary. A personal statement and family income documentation must be included with application packet. more...

Special Program Costs Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Sinclair Community College. Students must be enrolled in majors with higher than normal start-up costs and expenses. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Proof of expenses must be included with application packet. more...

Underrepresented Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Sinclair Community College. Students must be from underrepresented groups, ethnicity, disability, or national representation. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Working Student Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Sinclair Community College. Applicants must be under 24 years of age, ineligible for financial aid OR dependent students unable to provide parental income information and unable to submit a FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Proof of employment must be included with application packet. more...
