Lee Cares Basic Needs Application
Student success and support are at the heart of all we do at Lee College. If you are struggling, we want to help. Please fill out the application by clicking Apply below.
In order to apply you must have attended or currently be enrolled in the Summer 2022 term. You will also need your Lee College myLC email address and you will need to be able to access your myLC email to start the application.
You must first ‘sign up’ to access the scholarship application system. If you have previously applied in this system, you should ‘sign in’ using your email and previously created password.
If you aren’t sure about your myLC email account credentials, please read these helpful hints:
• Your myLC email should follow the format of first two letters of your first name, followed by first two letters of your last name, followed by your student ID.
For example: John Smith would be josm123456@mylc.lee.edu.
• Don’t worry: If you cannot remember your myLC email account credentials, we’ve got you! Just email the friendly folks at helpdesk@lee.edu and they will get you going.
If you have your myLC email account credentials, but are just having problems logging in, please contact Rigo @rruiz@lee.edu
ONE MORE THING BEFORE YOU GET STARTED! If you haven’t already, please choose a refund method in your MYLC account.
Here’s how you do that:
From your MYLC account navigate to the Financial Account Section and under My Account click on Manage Refund. After, click the blue button labeled “continue to refund setup” This will link you to the Bank Mobile site where there are step by step directions on how to choose a refund method.
*Applying does not guarantee an award. more...
