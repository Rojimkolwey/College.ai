Chase Dental Assistant Scholarship
The Chase Dental Assistant Scholarship is available to students enrolled in the dental assistant program at Northwestern Michigan College. You must be a resident of Antrim, Benzie, Grand Traverse, Kalkaska, Leelanau or Wexford County to be eligible for this award. You must also have a minimum 3.0 GPA, be enrolled in at least six credits, and demonstrate financial need. more...
