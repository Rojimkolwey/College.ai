Arcadia University Scholarships


Alumni Legacy Grant - Arcadia University
The Alumni Legacy Grant is available to full-time entering freshmen at Arcadia University who are the dependent children, grandchildren, or great - grandchildren of Arcadia alumni. You must rank in the top 25 percent of your graduating class and meet regular admissions criteria to be considered for this award. more...

Arcadia University Achievement Award
The Arcadia University Achievement Award is awarded to full-time Arcadia University undergraduate students who have demonstrated exceptional achievement in community service, school activities, leadership or special talent. Full-time fine arts majors will be considered based upon the quality of their portfolio, and acting majors may be considered based upon their audition. more...

Arcadia University Distinguished Scholarship
The Arcadia University Distinguished Scholarship is available to full - time freshmen and transfer students at Arcadia University. Selection for this award will be based on grades, SAT / ACT scores, high school rank, and school involvement. more...

Arcadia University Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship
The Arcadia University Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is awarded to entering full-time transfer students at Arcadia University. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa and have a minimum transfer GPA of 3.5 to be considered for this award. You must also have earned a minimum of 30 transferable credits and have completed coursework from at least two general academic disciplines as your prior institution. more...

Arcadia University Writing Achievement Award
The Arcadia University Writing Achievement Award is awarded to incoming students at Arcadia University. You must rank in the top tier of the accepted student pool on the ACT or SAT standardized writing section to be eligible for this award. more...

Charlotte W. Newcombe Foundation Scholarship For Mature Women
The Charlotte W. Newcombe Foundation Scholarship for Mature Women is awarded to female undergraduate students at Arcadia University who are 25 years of age or older. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and have completed at least half of the coursework necessary for a bachelor's degree to be eligible for this award. Selection will also be based on financial need and academic records. more...

Philadelphia Scholar Award
The Philadelphia Scholar Award is awarded to entering freshmen at Arcadia University who are graduates of one of the City of Philadelphia's 21 comprehensive high schools. You must be nominated by a high school official to qualify for this award. more...
