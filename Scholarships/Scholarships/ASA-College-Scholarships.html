ASA College Scholarships


Arthur J. Hidalgo Memorial Scholarship
The Arthur J. Hidalgo Memorial Scholarship is available to students at ASA College. You must be enrolled in a degree program to be eligible for this award. more...

Asa College Alumni Scholarship
The ASA College Alumni Scholarship is available to graduates of ASA College who would like to enroll into another AOS/AAS program at ASA. You must have completed an associate degree and be enrolled in a different AOS/AAS program to be eligible for this award. more...

Asa College Athletic Scholarship
The ASA College Athletic Scholarship is available to students - athletes at ASA College who are not residents of New York state. Scholarships are awarded based on athletic ability as determined by the head coach of the sport. more...

Asa College International Student Scholarship
The ASA College International Student Scholarship is available to full - time students at ASA College. You must have F-1 status to be eligible for this award. more...

Asa College Legislator's Scholarship
The ASA College Legislator's Scholarship is available to full - time students at ASA College. You must have a minimum 2.0 GPA and be recommended by a local, state, or federal legislator to be considered for this award. more...

Asa College Non-Resident Student Scholarship
The ASA College Non-Resident Student Scholarship is available to full - time students at ASA College who are not residents of New York state. more...

Donna Claytor Memorial Scholarship
The Donna Claytor Memorial Scholarship is available to full - time students at ASA College. You must be enrolled in a health discipline degree program and have a minimum 3.85 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
