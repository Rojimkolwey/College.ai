Waukesha County Technical College Scholarships


Delta Dental Of Wisconsin Scholarship
The Delta Dental of Wisconsin Scholarship is available to full - time, second - year students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be enrolled in the dental hygiene program, have a minimum GPA of 3.0, and be a resident of Wisconsin to be eligible for this award. more...

Fredricks/Hansen Scholarship
The Fredricks/Hansen Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be majoring in accounting to be eligible for this award. more...

Greater Milwaukee Foundation - John Ernst Memorial Scholarship
The Greater Milwaukee Foundation - John Ernst Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be majoring food service or restaurant management to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students who are employed by members of the Wisconsin Restaurant Association having their businesses in the greater Milwaukee area. more...

Greater Milwaukee Foundation - New Berlin Vfd Scholarship
The Greater Milwaukee Foundation - New Berlin Volunteer Fire Department Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be seeking a degree or certification in one of the following fields: fire science, police science, law enforcement, EMT, or a related field. Preference is given to residents of New Berlin, WI who are graduates of Eisenhower or New Berlin High School. more...

Jay Balchunas Memorial Scholarship
The Jay Balchunas Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be enrolled in either the fire protection technician associate degree program or paramedic technical diploma program. Also, you must be actively involved on or with the fire department to be eligible for this award. more...

Legacy Scholarship - Wctc
The Legacy Scholarship is available to non - traditional students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be over 25 years old and demonstrate scholastic experience to be eligible for this award. more...

Mel Parker Memorial Scholarship
The Mel Parker Memorial Scholarship is available to female students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be enrolled in an electronics program and be in at least your second semester with at least 12 hours earned from WCTC. Also, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Nicola Aversa Engineering Scholarship
The Nicola Aversa Engineering Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be enrolled in the electrical engineering technology program partnered with Marquette University to be eligible for this award. more...

Pamela Bach Schroeder Scholarship
The Pamela Bach Schroeder Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be enrolled in the culinary program and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Wisconsin Energy Corporation Foundation Scholarship
The Wisconsin Energy Corporation Foundation Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be enrolled in the IT programmer/analyst program to be eligible for this award. more...

Women's/Special Populations Endowment Scholarship
The Women's/Special Populations Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Waukesha County Technical College. You must be from one of the following categories to be eligible for this award: displaced homemaker, single parent, economically disadvantaged, academically disadvantaged, student with disabilities, or non - traditional student. more...
