Oklahoma Panhandle State University Scholarships


Educational Achievement Scholarship - Oklahoma Panhandle State Univ.
The Educational Achievement Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Oklahoma Panhandle State University. Freshmen must have a minimum ACT score of 22 or a minimum SAT score of 1010. Transfer students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and completed at least 30 semester credit hours to be eligible for this award. more...

Educational Incentive Scholarship - Oklahoma Panhandle State Univ.
The Educational Incentive Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Oklahoma Panhandle State University. You must have a minimum ACT score of 18 or a minimum SAT score of 860. Transfer students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and have completed at least 30 semester credit hours to be eligible for this award. more...

Guymon Pto Scholarship
The Guymon PTO Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Oklahoma Panhandle State University. You must be a graduate of Guymon High School to be considered for this award. more...

Hooker Lions Club Scholarship
The Hooker Lions Club Scholarship is available to students at Oklahoma Panhandle State University who are graduates of Hooker HIgh School. more...

Hooker Pto Scholarship
The Hooker PTO Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Oklahoma Panhandle State University. You must be a graduate of Hooker High School to qualify for this award. more...

Outstanding Student Scholarship - Oklahoma Panhandle State University
The Outstanding Student Scholarship is available to entering freshmen students at Oklahoma Panhandle State University. You must have a minimum ACT composite score of 24 or a minimum SAT score of 1080, or be the salutatorian of your high school graduating class to qualify for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Opsu
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Oklahoma Panhandle State University. You must have a minimum ACT score of 26 or a minimum SAT score of 1170, or be the valedictorian of your high school graduating class to qualify for this award. more...
