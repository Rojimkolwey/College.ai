Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University-Daytona Beach Scholarships


Colonel Louisa Spruance Morse Cap Scholarships
Scholarship to Civil Air Patrol members who are undergraduate students enrolled at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University. Students must have completed the first semester of AFROTC. Due date may vary. more...

Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University-Sae Engineering Scholarship (Daytona Beach)
Annual award for graduating high school seniors planning to attend the Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University majoring in Mechanical Engineering. Students must be U.S. citizens, have a minimum 3.5 GPA, and a minimum 1300 SAT score or a minimum 30 ACT score. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Nordam Dee Howard-Etienne Fage Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate (freshmen ineligible) students enrolled full-time at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University majoring in Aerospace Engineering or Aeronautical Systems Maintenance Engineering. Students must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA and be U.S. citizens. An essay and a resume must be included with application packet. more...

The Florida Harbor Pilots Scholarship Program
Scholarship for Florida residents who are African-American, full-time, college seniors or graduate students attending select institutions and are planning a career in the Harbor Pilots Industry or Maritime and Logistics. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrated financial need. Nonrenewable. Select intstitutions are: Barry University, Miami Shores; Bethune-Cookman College, Daytona Beach; Clearwater Christian College, Clearwater; Eckerd College, St. Petersburg; Edward Waters College, Jacksonville; Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Daytona Beach; Florida Institute of Technology, Melbourne; Florida Memorial College, Miami; Jacksonville University, Jacksonville; Lnn University, Boca Raton; Palm Beach Atlantic University, West Palm Beach; Saint Leo University, St. Leo; St. Thomas University, Miami; The University of Miami, Coral Gables; The University of Tampa, Tampa. Contact students financial aid office for more information and/or application. more...
