Rhodes College Scholarships


American Field Service Returnee Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are American Field Service returnees admitted to Rhodes College. An interview is encouraged. Renewable. more...

Bonner Scholars Program
Scholarship for first-year incoming students at Rhodes College. Applicants must have a minimum 1250 SAT or 27 ACT score, rank in the top 15% of their class, have an outstanding record of leadership, community service or social justice participation and have financial need. Recipients must make a commitment of ten hours per week of community service. more...

Cambridge Scholarship
Scholarship for entering undergraduate students at Rhodes College. Awards are based on merit. The average recipient has a 3.90 GPA, top 1% class rank, ACT score of 32 or SAT score of 1410. more...

Fine Arts Awards
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students at Rhodes College majoring or minoring in a Fine Arts discipline. Selection is based on outstanding achievement in art, music or theatre. An audition or portfolio is required. more...

Hoby Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are HOBY seminar participants entering Rhodes College. An interview is encouraged. Renewable. more...

Memphis Scholars Program
Scholarship for Memphis, Tennessee residents who are undergraduate students entering Rhodes College. Number of awards may vary. Award amount is full-tuition. Renewable. more...

Morse Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduates entering Rhodes College. Selection is based on merit. The average recipient has a 3.92 GPA, top 1% class rank, minimum ACT score of 33 or SAT score of 1570. An interviews is encouraged. more...

National Merit Awards
Scholarship for first-year students who are National Merit Finalists enrolling at Rhodes College. Students must have designated Rhodes as their first choice. Renewable. more...

National Presbyterian College Scholarships
Scholarship for first-year students entering Rhodes College. Applicants must indicate Rhodes is their first choice among participating Presbyterian Colleges. Award is based on need. An interview is encouraged. Renewable. Contact the Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.) or Rhodes Financial Aid Office for more information and/or application. more...

Presbyterian Partnership
Scholarship for full-time first-year and transfer students entering Rhodes College. Students must be nominated by the Session of a local Presbyterian Church (U.S.A.). Contact students pastor or Rhodes Financial Aid Office for more information and/or application. more...

Ralph C. Hon Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students entering Rhodes College majoring in Economics, Business, International Studies, Pre-Medicine, or Pre-Ministerial. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Rhodes Awards
Scholarship for undergraduates entering Rhodes College. Awards are based on merit. The average recipient has a 3.6 GPA, top 10% class rank, minimum ACT score of 27 or SAT score of 1210. An interview is encouraged. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Spencer Scholarships In Greek & Roman Studies
Scholarship for first-year students entering Rhodes College who have distinguished themselves in the study of Latin, Ancient Greek, or the civilizations of Ancient Greece and Rome. Renewable. more...

Walter D. Bellingrath Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted to Rhodes College. The average recipient has a 3.92 GPA, top 2% class rank, ACT score of 32 or SAT score of 1400. Students must be nominated by a high school counselor, teacher, principal, minister of the Presbyterian Church, or by alumni and friends of the College. Award amount is full tuition, fees, and room and board. Renewable. more...

Youth For Understanding Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students who are Youth for Understanding participants entering Rhodes College. Renewable. more...
