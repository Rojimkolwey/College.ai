Wesleyan University Scholarships


Bishop Joseph B. Bethea Scholarship
Scholarship for female, African-American, Southeastern Jurisdiction Black Methodist for Church Renewal active members who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Wesleyan College. Students must have a minimum B-(2.8 GPA), be U.S. citizens/permanent residents, haev financial need, and be United Methodist Church members for a minimum of one year. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Carole Ann Cushing Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for female undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Wesleyan College. Students must be from Skyland United Methodist Church or be residents of the Atlanta, Georgia area. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Edith M. Allen Scholarship
Scholarship for female, United Methodist Church, African-American undergraduate or graduate students enrolled full-time at Wesleyan College majoring in Education, Social Work, Medicine, or other health profession. Students must have a B+ average and have been active church members for a minimum of three years. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Era Monk Bryan Scholarship
Scholarship for female undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Wesleyan College. Preference given to Mulberry Street United Methodist Church members in Macon, Georgia or who are active mmembes of a United Methodist Church congregation and are planning to dedicate themselves to the clergy or lay service in the United Methodist Church. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
