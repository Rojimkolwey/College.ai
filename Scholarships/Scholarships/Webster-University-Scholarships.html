Webster University Scholarships


A+ Scholarship
Scholarship for Missouri residents that are first-time freshmen students who graduated from an A+ high school in Missouri and are planning to attend Webster University. Award is based on applicants cumulative GPAQ and class rank. Candidate must have a minimum score of 1000 on SAT or 21 on the ACT. Number of awards varies. more...

Afton High School Scholarship
Annual award for St. Louis, MO resident that is a graduating high school senior from Affton High School who plans to attend Webster University. Applicant must have a minimum 3.4 GPA, rank in the top 20% of their class and have an ACT score of 24 or above or an SAT score of 1100 or above. Submit a recommendation from candidates high school principal and/or guidance counselor with application packet. more...

Bishop Dubourg High School Scholarship
Scholarship for St. Louis, Missouri residents from DuBourg High School who plan to attend Webster University. Applicants must have maintained a minimum 4.0 GPA. Other students who have a 3.4 GPA, rank in the top 20% of their class and have an ACT score of 24 or above or an SAT score of 1100 or above will be considered as well. Submit a recommendation from candidates high school principal and/or guidance counselor with application packet. Number of awards varies. more...

Boys State Scholarship
Scholarship for male Missouri residents who are incoming freshmen planning to attend Webster University. Applicants must have participated in Boys State and have a minimum 3.0 GPA, rank in the top 33% of their class and have an ACT score of 22 or above or an SAT score of 1010 or above. Number of awards varies. more...

Daniel Webster Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Webster University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.8 GPA, rank in the top 10% of their class and have an ACT score of 29 or above or an SAT score of 1300 or above. Candidates may be requested to attend an interview. Award amount is equal to full tuition. more...
