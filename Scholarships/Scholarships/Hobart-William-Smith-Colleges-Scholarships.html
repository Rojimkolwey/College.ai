Hobart William Smith Colleges Scholarships


Faculty Scholarship - Hobart And William Smith Colleges
The Faculty Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hobart and William Smith Colleges. You must have some combination of the following criteria: a minimum 90 GPA or equivalent, minimum 1250 SAT/28 ACT, or rank in the top 15 percent of your class. more...

Hersh Scholarship
The Hersh Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hobart and William Smith Colleges who are Trustee Scholars winners. You must be involved in extracurricular activities and community service to be considered. more...

Hws Grant
The HWS Grant is available to students at Hobart and William Smith Colleges who demonstrate financial need. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Hobart And William Smith Colleges
The Phi Theta Kappa Trustee Scholarship for Academic Excellence for Graduates of Two-Year Colleges is available to transfer students at Hobart and William Smith Colleges. You must have a two-year degree from a community or junior college to be eligible for this award. You must also have a minimum cumulative 3.5 GPA for at least three semesters. more...

Presidential Leadership Scholarship - Hobart And William Smith Colleges
The Presidential Leadership Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hobart and William Smith Colleges. You must have strong academic records and demonstrate leadership and high motivation to be eligible for this award. more...

Rectory Grant - Hobart And William Smith Colleges
The Rectory Grant is available to students at Hobart and William Smith Colleges who are the children of a member of the clergy. more...

Wood Scholarship
The Wood Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Hobart and William Smith Colleges who are Trustee Scholars winners. You must demonstrate outstanding academic achievement to be considered for this award. more...
