Elon University Scholarships


Elon Engagement Scholarships
The Elon Engagement Scholarships are available to entering freshmen at Elon University. This award is based on high school academic record, standardized test scores, and your application essay. more...

Engineering Program Scholarship - Elon University
The Engineering Program Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Elon University. You must be admitted into the Dual Degree Engineering Program to be considered for this renewable award. more...

Music Scholarship - Elon University
The Music Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Elon University. You must major in music, music education, or music performance to be considered for this award. An audition / interview is also required. This scholarship is renewable for three additional years. more...

Performing Arts Scholarship - Elon University
The Performing Arts Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Elon University. You must major in theatre arts or music theatre to be considered for this award. This scholarship is renewable for three additional years. An audition/interview is required. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Elon University
The Presidential Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Elon University. This award is based on high school course selection, SAT/ACT scores and class rank and/or grades. This scholarship is renewable provided you maintain acceptable academic performance. Approximately the top one-fourth of entering freshmen are awarded this scholarship. more...

Rotc Scholarship - Elon University
The ROTC Scholarship is open to students who will be participating in the Army ROTC program at Elon University. Recipients will receive full tuition, textbook funds and $100 per month for personal expenses. Elon University also provides all ROTC scholarship recipients free room and board. more...
