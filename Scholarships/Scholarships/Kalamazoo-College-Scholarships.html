Kalamazoo College Scholarships


Heyl Graduate Fellowship To Yale Program
The Heyl Graduate Fellowship to Yale Program is available to graduate students at Kalamazoo College who have majored in chemistry, physics, biology, mathematics, computer science, or any cross-disciplinary combinations of any of the foregoing. You must be accepted to Yale University and eligible for a Ph.D. degree in chemistry, physics, engineering/applied science, molecular biophysics, or biochemistry to be eligible for this fellowship. Selection is based on academic merit, goals, and letters of recommendation. more...

Heyl Scholars Program
The Heyl Scholars Program is available to incoming freshman students at Kalamazoo College who are majoring in engineering, biology, chemistry, computer, mathematics, physics, or other natural science. You must be a graduate of Kalamazoo Central or Loy Norrix high schools (or other area highschool/homeschool) and will earn a certificate of completion from the Kalamazoo Area Math and Science Center to be eligible for this award. Additionally, you must have a minimum GPA of 3.5. Selection is based on academic merit and letters of recommendation. more...

K Tradition Scholarship
Scholarship for first-year freshmen and undergraduate students transferring to Kalamazoo College. Applicants must be the children of alumni of Kalamazoo College and have a minimum 3.0 high school GPA. more...
