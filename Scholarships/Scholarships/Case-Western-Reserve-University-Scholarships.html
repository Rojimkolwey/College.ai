Case Western Reserve University Scholarships


Adelbert Alumni Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in the Arts; Humanities; Natural, Social and Behavioral Sciences; Management or Accounting. Award amount is equal to full tuition. more...

Albert W. Smith Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Engineering or Science. Award amount is equal to full tuition. more...

Alden Undergraduate Fellowshps In Systems Engineering Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate juniors or seniors attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Systems Engineering. Applicants must be Systems Engineering majors at the time of application and must have a minimum 3.2 GPA for the three semesters preceding the application. Contact the Department of Systems Engineering for application procedures and additional information. more...

Alexander Treuhaft Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen planning to attend Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Science or Engineering. Award amount is equal to full tuition. more...

Andrew And Eleanor Squire Scholarship
This four-year full-tuition scholarship was originally established by Case Western Reseve University trustee Andrew Squire and his wife Eleanor to encourage students to learn about the outdoors. First-year applicants in the arts, humanities, natural, social and behavioral sciences, management, and accountancy are eligible to apply. more...

Bernadotte E. Schmitt Scholarship
Annual award(s) for graduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, pursuing an advanced degree in History. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Bioethics Department Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Bioethics. Selection is based on merit and financial need. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Case Alumni Association Junior & Senior Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate juniors and seniors attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. Selection is based on academic achievement, financial need, participation in extracurricular activities and service to the University. Student may be requested to attend interview. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Case Grant-In-Aid Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. Applicants must have financial need and academic success. Students may receive grants-in-aid for a maximum of ten semesters. Number of awards varies. more...

Cleveland Museum Of Art Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate and doctoral students attending Case Western Reserve University in Ohio. Selection is based on academic excellence and scholarly promise, but financial need is considered. Students must work 10 hours per week for the museum, usually in a curatorial department. Number of awards varies. more...

Creative Achievement Scholarships
Scholarship for entering freshmen attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. Scholarships are available to students who exhibit excellence in either music or studio art, but who may not major in those subject areas. Selection is based on an audition or portfolio review. more...

Curtis Lee Smith Scholarship
Award for incoming freshman planning to attend Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in the Arts; Humanities; Natural, Social and Behavioral Sciences; Management or Accounting. This award is offered once every four years. more...

Elizabeth Magee Scholarship
Annual award(s) for graduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, pursuing an advanced degree in Social History and Policy. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Frances Payne Bolton School Of Nursing Scholarship
Scholarship for entering freshmen attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Nursing. Number of awards varies. Renewable (maximum of four years). more...

Gerber Law-Medicine Fellowship - Case School Of Law
The Gerber Law-Medicine Fellowship is available to first-year students at Case Western Reserve University, School of Law. You must demonstrate merit and an interest in pursuing a career in health law to be eligible for this award. Fellows receive an additional stipend for work on a health law project during the academic year. more...

Henri Pell Junod Jr. Fellowship Scholarship
Annual award(s) for Ph.D. students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Industrial History. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

History Associates Scholarship
Annual award(s) for Ph.D. students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, pursuing an advanced degree in History. Applicants must be in the later stages of writing their dissertations. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

History Departmental Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in History. In most cases, the award is sufficient to cover tuition for the course work required. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

James Dysart Magee Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate seniors attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, in the Integrated Graduate Studies programs in Economics or the Social and Behavioral Sciences. Award amount varies. more...

Materials Science & Engineering Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen students accepted to attend Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Materials Science or Engineering. Selection is based on merit and capability. Renewable; if high achievement maintained. more...

Moca Cleveland Emily Hall Tremaine Curatorial Fellowship Scholarship
Annual award for a doctoral students attending Case Western Reserve University in Ohio, pursuing a Curatorial Career in the comtemporary art field and majoring in Art History. Selection is based on academic excellence and scholarly promise, but financial need is considered. Interview will be required. Students will participate in the curatorial activities and exhibitions. Award amount varies. Renewable; must maintain 3.5 GPA. more...

Mph (Master Of Public Health) Scholarships
Scholarship for current employees of a state or local Ohio state health department who are graduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, majoring in Public Health. Recipients must agree to work for two years at a public health agency in Ohio after completion of their degree. Award amount varies. more...

Ohio Leadership Scholarship
Scholarship for Ohio residents who are incoming freshmen planning to attend Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. A separate application, a nomination by a high school faculty member and an on-campus interview with a faculty member and an alumnus are required. Award amount varies. Renewable. more...

Pancoast Fellowship Scholarships
Scholarship for female graduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Ohio. Selection is based on academic excellence and scholarly promise, but financial need is considered. Students must plan to travel and study abroad. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Performing Arts Scholarship
A small number of full-tuition scholarships are available to students who exhibit excellence in the performing arts and will major in either music, theater, or dance. Scholarships are awarded based on an audition. Students should contact individual departments to make arrangements for an audition. more...

Peter Witt Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate juniors and seniors attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio, who are interested in Social Welfare, City Planning and Community responsibility. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Plain Dealer Charities Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Cuyahoga, Lake, Geauga, Portage, Summit, Medina, or Lorain counties, Ohio who are graduating high school seniors planning to attend Case Western Reserve University majoring in Business Management or Print Journalism. Applicants must be in the upper 20 percent of their high school class and must attain ACT or SAT scores in the upper 25th percentile. Candidates must have financial need. more...

Presidents Scholarships
Scholarship for entering freshmen attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. Selection is based on students academics, including high school performance (GPA, class rank) and strength of high school curriculum, involvement in school and community activities, evidence of leadership and standardized test results. more...

Provosts Special Scholarships
Scholarship for under-represented minorities who are entering freshmen at Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio that will add to the diversity of the student population. Members of underrepresented minority groups are encouraged to apply. Selection is based on academics, including high school performance (GPA, class rank), strength of high school curriculum, involvement in school and community activities, evidence of leadership and standardized test results. Renewable. more...

Ralph J. Besse Scholarship

Theater & Dance Department Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Case Western Reserve University in Ohio, majoring in Theater. Selection is based on merit. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Upperclassman President Scholarship
Scholarship for upperclassmen students attending Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. Selection is based on academics (minimum 3.75 GPA), involvement in school and community activities and evidence of leadership. Applicant must have earned at least 30 credit hours (sophomore), 60 credit hours (junior) and 90 credit hours (senior). Candidates who previously received the Trustee, President, or Provost award are not eligible. Award amount varies. more...
