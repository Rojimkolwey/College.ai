Caldwell University Scholarships


Alumni Scholarships & Grants
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending Caldwell College who are relatives of Caldwell College alumni. Selection based on academic achievement and potential. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the Office of Alumni Affairs for more information. more...

Caldwell Grant
The Caldwell Grant is available to full - time freshmen and transfer students at Caldwell University. You must submit a completed FAFSA and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Caldwell University/Nj Stars Scholarship
The Caldwell College/New Jersey Stars Scholarship is available to full - time sudents at Caldwell University. You must be transferring from a New Jersey community college and have completed your associates degree to be eligible for this award. more...

Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Scholars Program
Scholarship for New Jersey residents who are incoming freshmen enrolling at Caldwell College who have been selected for the Edward J. Bloustein Distinguished Scholars Program. Applicant must have graduate from a New Jersey high school and been selected by students guidance counselor. Students must be U.S. citizens/eligible non-citizen. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Friends Of Caldwell College Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending Caldwell College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, financial need, and participate in campus/community activities. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. more...

Law Enforcement And Fire Personnel Scholarship
The Law Enforcement and Fire Personnel Scholarship is available to continuing education students at Caldwell University. You must be a law enforcement or fire personnel officer to be eligible for this award. more...

Nursing Grants
The Nursing Grants are available to full - time freshmen at Caldwell University. You must have excellent academic high school achievement, minimum SAT scores of 1000 and majoring in nursing to be eligible for this award. more...

Outstanding Scholars Recuitment Program
Scholarship for New Jersey residents who are undergraduate students attending Caldwell College. Applicants must have been New Jersey residents for twelve consecutive months and a minimum SAT score of 1350. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Caldwell University
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to transfer students at Caldwell University. You must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Caldwell University
The Presidential Scholarship is available to full -time entering freshmen at Caldwell University. You must demonstrate high academic achievement and have a minimum 1200 SAT score (math and verbal combined) to be eligible for this award. more...

Recognition Award - Caldwell University
The Recognition Award is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Caldwell University. You must demonstrate exceptional leadership skills or a high level of commitment to extracurricular or community service activities to be eligible for this award. more...

Talent Scholarships
The Talent Scholarships to full - time students at Caldwell University. You must be studying art or music to be eligible for this award. A portfolio or audition is required. more...
