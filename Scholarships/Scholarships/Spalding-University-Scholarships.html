Spalding University Scholarships


Caritas Award
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Spalding University. Applicant must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and minimum ACT score of 22 or SAT score of 1010. Students must also submit an essay and attend an interview. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Cralle Scholarship
The Cralle Scholarship is available to female students at Spalding University. You must be at least 27 years old and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Governors Scholars
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Spalding University who are Kentucky Governors Scholars. Due date and number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

The Johnson Research Scholarship Fund is available to graduate students attending Spalding University. You must be majoring in social work and be pursuing research as it applies to social issues for policy-making that benefits or furthers human well-being to be eligible for this award. more...
