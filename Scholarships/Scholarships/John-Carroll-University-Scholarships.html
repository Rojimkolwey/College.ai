John Carroll University Scholarships


Alumni Board Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must be children of alumni of John Carroll University and have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Renewable. more...

American Values Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students attending John Carroll University. Applicants must have held leadership positions in extracurricular organizations and have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Andrew Mccarthy Scholarship
Scholarship for east coast residents who are freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA or rank in the top 25% of their class and be involved in extracurricular activities. Renewable. more...

Central Ohio (Columbus) Area Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of the Greater Columbus, Ohio area who are freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Renewable. more...

Charles S. & Murial A. Zodrow Akron Area Scholarship
Scholarship for Akron, Ohio area residents who are freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.8 GPA or rank in the top 25% of the class. Selection is based on academic merit, demonstrate leadership and financial need. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Chicago Club Leadership Scholarship
Scholarship for Greater Chicago area residents who are freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.75 GPA. Selection is based on demonstrated leadership, academic merit, essay and interview. Renewable. more...

Class Of 1964 Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must be relatives of a member of the Class of 1964 at John Carroll University. Selection is based on academic merit, other achievements and financial need. Renewable. more...

Dr. Thomas Picard Scholarship
Scholarship for Ashtabula or Mansfield, Ohio area who are freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University majoring in Science. Selection is based on academic merit and financial need. Nonrenewable. more...

Erie Cathedral Prep Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must be graduates of Erie Cathedral Preparatory High School. Selection is based on academic excellence and student involvement. Renewable. more...

Huntington Scholarships
Scholarship for Cuyahoga County, Ohio residents who are undergraduate students transferring to John Carroll University majoring in the Natural Sciences or Accounting. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 GPA. Students must file the FAFSA. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Ignatian Mission Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have graduated from a Jesuit high school. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

John Carroll Grant
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Mastin Scholarship (Act)
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University majoring in Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry or Physics. Applicants must have a minimum 27 ACT score and exceptional academic achievement. Renewable. more...

Mastin Scholarship (Honors)
Scholarship for freshmen who are National Merit Finalists or Semi-Finalists enrolling at John Carroll University majoring in Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry or Physics. Applicants must have exceptional academic achievement. Renewable. more...

Muer Detroit Club Scholarship
Scholarship for Detroit, Michigan area residents enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.75 GPA. Selection is based on academic merit and financial need. Renewable. more...

Presidents Honor Award (Pha)
Scholarship for freshmen enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and strong SAT or ACT scores. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Ralph Vince Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students transferring to John Carroll University. Applicants must be employed for a minimum of 12 hours per week and have a minimum 3.0 GPA. A letter from the students employer must be included with application packet. Due date may vary. Renewable. more...

Steve Burchyns Scholarship
Scholarship for freshmen who are children, grandchildren or stepchildren of Diplomatic Secret Service Special Agents (active, retired or deceased) enrolling at John Carroll University. Applicants must have a minimum 2.8 GPA. Selection is based on academic excellence, student involvement and financial need. Renewable. more...

Ted Ryan Scholarship
Scholarship for Chicago, Illinois area residents enrolling at John Carroll University. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable more...

Vietnam Veteran Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors who are children or grandchildren of a veteran who served during the Vietnam era enrolling at John Carroll University. If no candidate is found consideration will be given to children or grandchildren of any military veteran. Awards are based on academic merit and financial need. Renewable. more...
