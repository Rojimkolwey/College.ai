University of Pittsburgh-Pittsburgh Campus Scholarships


Alice Wroblewski Scholarship Fund
Annual award for an Allegheny County, Pennsylvania resident who is a college junior or senior attending the University of Pittsburgh School of General Studies. Applicants must demonstrate financial need and have a minimum B average. Contact the Universitys Financial Aid Office for more information and/or application. more...

Chaffee Undergraduate Nursing Award Fund
Annual award for a college junior attending the University of Pittsburgh School of Nursing who have not transferred from another nursing school. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 QPA, demonstrate potential for leadership, demonstrate potential to be an outstanding practitioner in the field of nursing and demonstrate personality characteristics acceptable to the nursing profession. Contact the School of Nursing for more information and/or application. more...

Chancellor's Scholarship - University Of Pittsburgh
The Chancellor's Scholarship is available to full-time incoming freshmen at the University of Pittsburgh. You must be admitted to Pitt Honors to be eligible for this award. more...

Chesko Computing Award
The Chesko Computing Award is available to students at the University of Pittsburgh, College of Business Administration. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0, completed no more than 60 credits toward your degree, and have unmet financial need to be eligible for this award. more...
