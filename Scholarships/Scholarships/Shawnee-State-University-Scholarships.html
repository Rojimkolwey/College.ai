Shawnee State University Scholarships


Ssu Upward Bound Math Science Scholarship
The SSU Upward Bound Math Science Scholarship is open to high school seniors entering Shawnee State University. To be eligible for this award, you must be an Upward Bound Math Science participant, have a minimum 3.0 GPA, a minimum 18 ACT score, and submit the FAFSA. Your extracurricular activities and community involvement will also be considered as part of the selection process. more...

Stan Callihan Memorial Scholarship
The Stan Callihan Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students at Shawnee State University. You must be an education major with a history or social science concentration, be of middle income and be in good academic standing to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to Kentucky residents. more...

Thomas B. Reynolds Memorial Scholarship
The Thomas B. Reynolds Memorial Scholarship is open to senior accounting students at Shawnee State University. Preference is given to the student with the highest GPA. Your community involvement will be considered as part of the selection process. more...

Victor W. Morgan, Jr. Scholarship
