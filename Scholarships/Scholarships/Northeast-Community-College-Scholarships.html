Northeast Community College Scholarships


Connie Day Memorial Scholarship
The Connie Day Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Northeast Community College. You must be a resident of the Northeast Community College service area, be a single parent, and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Harold Martindale Memorial/Cedar Knox Public Power District Scholarship
The Harold Martindale Memorial/Cedar Knox Public Power District Scholarship is available to freshmen students at Northeast Community College. You must be enrolled in the utility line program, have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and be a dependent of a resident rate payer of Cedar-Knox Public Power District to qualify for this award. more...

Jaque Houska Pta Memorial Scholarship
The Jaque Houska PTA Memorial Scholarship is available to sophomore students at Northeast Community College. You must be entering the second year of the physical therapy assisting program, have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Jim Duncan Memorial Scholarship
The Jim Duncan Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Northeast Community College. You must be majoring in information technology and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to qualify for this award. more...

Laurie Miller Memorial Scholarship
The Laurie Miller Memorial Scholarship is available to sophomore students at Northeast Community College. You must have good academic standing and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Liberty Centre Services/Mcintosh Scholarship
The Liberty Centre Services/McIntosh Scholarship is available to students at Northeast Community College. You must be participating in a program at Liberty Centre Services and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Master Gardener Scholarship
The Master Gardener Scholarship is available to sophomore students at Northeast Community College. You must be majoring in horticulture and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to qualify for this award. more...

Northeast Community College Staff Fund Scholarship
The Northeast Community College Staff Fund Scholarship is available to students at Northeast Community College. You must demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Northeast Nebraska Medical Association Alliance Scholarship
The Northeast Nebraska Medical Association Alliance Scholarship is available to students at Northeast Community College. You must be majoring in nursing and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Scholastic ability is also considered. more...

Tierone Scholarship
The TierOne Scholarship is available to sophomore students at Northeast Community College. You must be a resident of Nebraska and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Valmont Industries, Inc. Scholarships
Annual award for FFA members who are undergraduates enrolling/enrolled at Northeast Community College (NE) pursuing a two-year degree in Irrigation Technology. Students must be U.S. citizens. Criteria used for selection includes students GPA (minimum 2.0 GPA), ACT or SAT scores, class rank, leadership in FFA activities, leadership in community activities, and involvement in the Supervised Agricultural Experience Program (SAE). Renewable. more...

Vocational Scholarship
The Vocational Scholarship is available to full-time students at Northeast Community College. You must be enrolled in a vocational training program, be a Nebraska resident and U.S. citizen and have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to qualify for this award. You must also have a valid high school diploma or GED and financial need must also be demonstrated. more...
