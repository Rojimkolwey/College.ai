University of Maine Scholarships


Jack & Louise Saum Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for graduating seniors from Bucksport High School who will be pursuing postsecondary studies within the University of Maine system. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Nonrenewable. Contact the students guidance counselor or the Foundation for more information and/or application; please include the name of the scholarship fund with your request. more...

Margaret B. & Mary E. Franklin Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors of Ellsworth High School in Hancock County, Maine who are planning to attend the University of Maine (Farmington) seeking a degree in Education. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Ellsworth High School Guidance Office or the Foundation for more information and/or application; please include the name of the scholarship fund with your request. more...

Marshall Stern Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for residents of Penobscot County, Maine who have graduated from Bangor High School and are attending the University of Maine. Selection based on academic achievement, leadership, and financial need. Due date, award amount and number of awards may vary. Contact the Bangor High School Guidance Office or the Foundation for more information and/or application; please include the name of the scholarship fund with your request. more...

New Hampshire Alumni Scholarship
The New Hampshire Alumni Scholarship is available to second- or third-year students at the University of Maine, School of Law. You must be a resident of New Hampshire to be eligible for this award. more...

Pierce B. And Ariana K. Hasler Memorial Scholarship
The Pierce B. and Ariana K. Hasler Memorial Scholarship is available to second- or third-year students at the University of Maine, School of Law. You must demonstrate academic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Richard H. Armstrong Scholarship

Thompson Fund Scholarship
The Thompson Fund Scholarship is available to second- or third-year students at the University of Maine, School of Law. You must demonstrate interest and achievement in the area of marine resources law to be eligible for this award. more...

Tuition Waiver Program For Children & Spouses Of Emergency Medical Services Personnel
Scholarship for Maine residents who are children or spouses of emergency medical services personnel killed in the line of duty. Students must be undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at the University of Maine system, Community College system, or Maine Maritime Academy. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Tuition Waiver Program For Children & Spouses Of Firefighters & Law Enforcement Officers
Scholarship for children or spouses of firefighers or law enforcement officers killed in the line of duty and are Maine residents. Students must be undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at the University of Maine system, Community College system, or Maine Maritime Academy. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Tuition Waiver Program For Foster Or Adopted Children
Scholarship for adopted or foster children who are Maine residents and are undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at the University of Maine system, Community College system, or Maine Maritime Academy. Students must have been foster children under the custody of the Maine Department of Health and Human Services when graduated from high school OR be adopted persons or minor wards of a permanency guardian whose adoptive parents or guardians receive a subsidy from the Maine Department of Health and Human Services. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Violette/Lombard Scholarship
The Violette/Lombard Scholarship is available to second- or third-year students at the University of Maine, School of Law. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Wiley Family Scholarship
The Wiley Family Scholarship is available to second- or third-year students at the University of Maine, School of Law. You must be a single parent and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Women's Law Association Scholarship
The Women's Law Association Scholarship is available to second- or third-year students at the University of Maine, School of Law. You must demonstrate a commitment to the promotion of feminist values and display financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Wood Memorial Scholarship
