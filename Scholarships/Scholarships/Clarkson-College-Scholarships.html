Clarkson College Scholarships


Gateway To Success Scholarship For Minority Students
Scholarship for African American, Asian American, Hispanic or Native American undergraduate students attending Clarkson College majoring in Nursing. Applicants must be incoming freshmen or transfer students, be a U.S. citizen, have a minimum 2.5 GPA, and successfully complete a 12 week summer internship program at Nebraska Health Systems. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Presidents Bronze Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students at Clarkson College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 21, and rank in the top 50% of their graduating high school class. more...

Presidents Platinum Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students at Clarkson College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.4 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 23 and rank in the upper 30% of the graduating class. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Presidents Silver Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students at Clarkson College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.2 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 22, and rank in the upper 40% of their graduating high school class. more...

Service League Diamond Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students at Clarkson College. Applicants must have a minimum 3.8 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 26, and rank in the top 10% of their graduating high school class. more...

Service League Gold Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming undergraduate students enrolling at Clarkson College. Applicants must have a 3.6 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 24, and rank in the top 20% of their graduating high school class. more...
