University of Nevada-Las Vegas Scholarships


Barrick Graduate Fellowships
Scholarship for graduate students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas full-time (9 to 12 credits). Applicants must be outstanding doctoral students who have demonstrated excellent scholarship during their graduate study at UNLV, and must have completed at least 24 credits of doctoral study. Candidates must have a minimum graduate GPA of 3.5. Criteria for selection will also include demonstrated excellence in research. Due date varies. more...

Bluecoats, Inc. Scholarship
Scholarship for active or retired Clark County firefighters, police officers or highway patrol dependents who are undergraduate students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Ccsd Board Of School Trustees-Occupational Education Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Clark County, Nevada who are graduating high school seniors that completed a minimum of two years in one of the districts occupational education programs and will be pursing full-time studies at the Community College of Southern Nevada or the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 CGPA. Recipients must attend the Public Education Foundation Awards Reception. Nonrenewable. more...

Earl & Hazel Wilson Scholarship
Scholarship for first-generation college students who are entering freshmen attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Candidates must have financial need. Applicants must have achieved an unweighted overall GPA in the range of 2.80 to 3.70. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Edwin F. Wiegand Science & Technology Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Clark County, Nevada who are graduating high school seniors that will enrolling at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas and be majoring in Science, Technology, Computer Science or related fields. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA and financial need. Preference will be given to students who participated in the CyberCorps program or the districts InterAct Online Learning Community program. Recipients must attend the Public Education Foundation Awards Reception. Nonrenewable. more...

Gordy Fink Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Clark County, Nevada who are graduating seniors from Valley High School that will be enrolling full-time at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have financial need. Selection also based on leadership roles, community service activities, and career goals. Recipients must attend the Public Education Foundation Awards Reception. Nonrenewable. more...

Great (Graduate Research Training) Assistantships
Scholarship for doctoral students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas to pursue research over the summer; however, excellent Masters and Specialists students may be considered. Stipend is monthly for 3 months tuition support for 1-3 credits during one of the summer sessions and an unencumbered summer of scholarship. Selection is based on academic profile, project description, research orientation of the project and summer objectives. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Hughes Valedictorian Scholarship
Scholarship for Valedictorians who are entering freshmen attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have an ACT composite of 27 or an SAT score of 1210. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

James F. Adams-Gpsa Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have completed at least 12 credits of graduate study at UNLV and have a minimum undergraduate and graduate GPA of 3.5. Candidates must enroll for six or more credits in each semester of the scholarship year. Due date varies. more...

Judith Warner Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Clark County, Nevada who are graduating seniors from Rancho High School that will be enrolling full-time at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have a minimum 2.5 CGPA and financial need. Recipients must attend the Public Education Foundation Awards Reception. Nonrenewable. more...

Kenneth M. Devos Scholarship
Scholarship for minority undergraduate students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Number of awards varies. more...

Marie B. Woodrich Scholarship
Scholarship for entering freshmen students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have completed their coursework with an unweighted overall GPA of 3.0 or higher. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Mcnair Post-Baccalaureate Scholarships
Scholarship for first-year graduate students who participated in a McNair Scholars program. Applicants must have an undergraduate GPA of 3.0. Recipient must enroll in a minimum of 9 credit hours for two consecutive semesters. Due date, number of awards and award amount varies. more...

Phyllis Frias Science Scholarship
Scholarship for hispanic entering freshmen students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas planning to major in Environmental Science, Geoscience, Biology, Hydrology, Soil Science, Chemistry, Physics, or Ecology. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

President's Scholarship - Unlv
The President's Scholarship is available to entering students at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. You must be a National Merit Finalist and list UNLV as your first school choice to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Presidents Graduate Fellowships
Scholarship for graduate students already admitted into the graduate college at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must be doctoral students working primarily on the dissertation, have a minimum 3.5 GPA, and enroll as a full-time student (at least 9 graduate credits) in each semester of the fellowship year. Nominations must be submitted through the students department chair. Due date varies. more...

Presidents Scholar Award
Annual award for Valedictorians and National Merit Finalists who are entering freshmen planning to attend the University of Nevada. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Provost's Scholarship - Unlv
The Provost's Scholarship is available to entering students at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. You must be in the top five percent of your graduating class and have at least a 30 ACT / 1340 SAT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Rebel Achievement Scholarship

Sheila Tarr-Smith Memorial Scholarship

Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 CGPA and financial need. Condsideration is also given to community service activites. Recipients must attend the Public Education Foundation Awards Reception. Nonrenewable. more...

Station Casinos Scholarship
Scholarship for dependents of Station Casinos employees who are undergraduate students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Summer Session Scholarship
Scholarship for masters or doctoral students attending the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. Applicants must have completed at least 12 credits of graduate study at UNLV and have a minimum undergraduate and graduate GPA of 3.0. Candidates must enroll for six credits in any one or combination of summer sessions. Selection based on summer plans for conducting dissertation or thesis research. Due date varies. more...

Unlv Excellence Scholarship
