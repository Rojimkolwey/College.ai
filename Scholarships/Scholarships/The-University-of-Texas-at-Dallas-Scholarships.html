The University of Texas at Dallas Scholarships


Aage & Margareta Moller Scholarship For Veterans Of The War In Iraq & Afghanistan
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Texas-Dallas. Students must have served on active duty in the War in Iraq or Afghanistan. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Academic Distinction Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at the University of Texas-Dallas. Consideration may be given to extracurricular activities, community service, work experience, and leadership skills. Award amount is for full tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at the University of Texas-Dallas. Students must be National Merit Scholars and list the University of Texas-Dallas as their first choice of school. Consideration may be given to extracurricular activities, community service, work experience, and leadership skills. Award amount is for full tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Amelia Lundell Textbook Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Texas-Dallas. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA for undergraduate, a minimum 3.0 GPA for graduates, and financial need. A FAFSA must be submitted. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Association Of Ivorians In Texas (Ait) Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Texas-Dallas. Preference given to students who received part of their preparatory education in the Cote dIvoire or have an interest in the Cote dIvoire culture and are majoring in Management, Engineering, Computer Science, Natural Science and Mathematics, Economics, or Public Administration. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Eugene Mcdermott Scholars Program Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at the University of Texas-Dallas. Students must have an SAT/ACT score of 1350-1600, rank in the top 2% of the graduating high school class, are school leaders, community volunteers, athletes, artists, scholars, scientists, or musicians. Award amount is for full tuition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
