Central Penn College Scholarships


Academic Scholarship - Central Penn College
The Academic Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Central Penn College. You must have demonstrated academic excellence throughout high school and have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Donald B. Dorothy L. Stabler Foundation Scholarship
The Donald B. Dorothy L. Stabler Foundation Scholarship is available to students at Central Penn College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0, make a commitment to one day give back to your alma mater through scholarships, and exhibit strong leadership / motivation to be eligible for this award. more...

Early Start Scholarship
The Early Start Scholarship is available to students at Central Penn College. You must be enrolling in the summer academic term to be eligible for this award. more...

Eliminating Boundaries Scholarship
The Eliminating Boundaries Scholarship is available to full - time students enrolled at Central Penn College. To be considered, you must have a minimum GPA of 2.8 and submit a 500 - to 700 - word essay on the following topic: "A Central Penn student is described as resilient. Please describe to the scholarship committee a time where you experienced a hardship in your life and how you took steps to overcome it." more...

Fbla/Deca Scholarship - Central Penn College
The FBLA/DECA Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Central Penn College. You must be a member of FBLA / DECA and have a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Hacc Graduate Students Scholarship
The HACC Graduate Students Scholarship is available to new transfer students at Central Penn College. You must have graduated with an associate's degree from HACC to be eligible for this award. more...

Legacy Scholarship
The Legacy Scholarship is available to full - time students at Central Penn College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and be the child, grandchild, or sibling of a Central Penn graduate to be eligible for this award. more...

Opportunity Scholarship - Central Penn College
The Opportunity Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Central Penn College. To be considered, you must have a minimum 2.8 GPA and submit a 500 - word essay on the following topic: "Who has inspired you the most and why?" more...

Private Scholarship Matching Award
The Private Scholarship Matching Award is available to students at Central Penn College. You must have received a scholarship from a source outside of Central Penn College to be eligible for this award. more...
