School of the Art Institute of Chicago Scholarships


Hilgos Award
Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate students accepted to the School of the Art Institute of Chicago with the desire to create projects that support and assist the needs of elderly individuals pursuing artistic endeavors. Students must complete a minimum of 100 hours of direct service to the project. Due date may vary. Nonrenewable. more...
