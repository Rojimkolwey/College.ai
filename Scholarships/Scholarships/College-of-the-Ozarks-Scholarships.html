College of the Ozarks Scholarships


Athletic Scholarship - College Of The Ozarks
The Athletic Scholarship is available to students at The College of the Ozarks. You must participate in athletics to be eligible for this award. more...

C Of O Book Scholarship
The C of O Book Scholarship is available to full - time students at The College of the Ozarks. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Cost Of Education Scholarship
The Cost of Education Scholarship is available to full - time students at College of the Ozarks. Each student's cost of education is met 100 percent by participating in the work program and a combination of private, institutional, and federal / state student aid. more...

General Scholarship - College Of The Ozarks
The General Scholarship is available to students at The College of the Ozarks. Selection is based on financial need, GPA, and your major. more...

L.W. Hyer Book Scholarship
The L.W. Hyer Book Scholarship is available to students at The College of the Ozarks. You must have completed at least 24 credit hours, have a minimum 2.5 GPA, and be a dorm student. You must also demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Patriotic Scholarship - College Of The Ozarks
The Patriotic Scholarship is available to students who attend and satisfactorily complete the ROTC Leader's Training Course and / or the Leader Development and Assessment Course at the College of the Ozarks. more...

Summer Work Scholarship - College Of The Ozarks
The Summer Work Scholarship is available to students at The College of the Ozarks. Priority is given to current students. You must work either or both of the two six - week summer sessions for at least forty hours a week to be eligible for this award. more...
