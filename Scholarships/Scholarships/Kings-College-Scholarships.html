King's College Scholarships


Christi Regis Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming full-time college freshman at Kings College. Students must have graduated in the top 25% of their class, minimum 1050 SAT(23ACT), and a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

Deprizio Award
Scholarship for incoming full-time college freshman at Kings College. Students must have graduated in the top 50% of their class, minimum 2.0 GPA, demonstrated academic achievement and good character. Due date and number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

Diversity Award
Scholarship for multi-cultured incoming full-time college freshman at Kings College. Students must have demonstrated financial need. Due date, amount, and number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

Holy Cross Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming full-time college freshman at Kings College. Students must have minimum 1050 SAT(23 ACT), and a minimum 3.0 GPA, and graduate from a high school associated with the Holy Cross Brothers of the Eastern Province. Due date and number of awards may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

Presidential Scholarship - King's College
The Presidential Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at King's College. You must rank in the top five percent of your high school graduating class and have a minimum 28 ACT / 1260 SAT score and a minimum 3.5 GPA. A campus interview is also required. more...
