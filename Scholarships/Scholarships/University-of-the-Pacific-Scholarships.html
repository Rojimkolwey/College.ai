University of the Pacific Scholarships


Bay Area Minority Law Student Scholarship
Scholarship for underrepresented minority law students attending select colleges/universities in California. Selection based on potential and financial need. Select schools include: University of California-Berkley; University of California, Hastings College of Law; University of California-Davis; University of San Francisco; Golden Gate University; University of the Pacific, McGeorge School of Law; Santa Clara University; or Stanford University. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Hazel Combellack University Of The Pacific Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from El Dorado High School planning to attend the University of the Pacific. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact students guidance counselor or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Scholarships (B)
Scholarship for Latino graduate students enrolled at Santa Clara University, Lincoln Law School of Sacramento, University of California, Berkeley-Law School, University of San Francisco-Law School, University of California-Hastings College of the Law, Stanford University, Golden Gate University, University of California, Davis, or University of the Pacific-McGregor School of Law. Students must be La Raza Lawyers members. Consideration given to financial need, community involvement, peer counseling, and school activities. more...

Unity Scholarship Competition
Scholarship for ethnic minority graduate students enrolled at the University of California, Davis-School of Law, Lincoln Law School of Sacramento, University of Northern California-Lorenzo Patino School of Law, or University of the Pacific-McGeorge School of Law. Students must demonstrate academic achievement, leadership skills, and participation in extracurricular activities. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...
