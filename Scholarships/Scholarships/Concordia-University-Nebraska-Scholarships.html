Concordia University-Nebraska Scholarships


Alumni Grant - Concordia University Nebraska
The Alumni Grant is available to entering full-time freshmen at Concordia University Nebraska. You must have a parent who is an alumni of Concordia University Nebraska to be eligible for this award. more...

Athletic Scholarship - Concordia University Nebraska
The Athletic Scholarship is available to entering full-time freshmen at Concordia University Nebraska. You must demonstrate excellence in athletics to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Church Work Grant - Concordia University
The Church Work Grant is available to junior students at Concordia University. You must be enrolled in a professional church work program to be eligible for this award. more...

Lcms Church Work Grant - Concordia University
The LCMS Church Work Grant is available to incoming students at Concordia University. You must be a member of a Lutheran Church- Missouri Synod congregation to be eligible for this award. more...

Performance Award - Concordia University Nebraska
The Performance Award is available to entering full-time freshmen at Concordia University Nebraska. You must demonstrate excellence in music, theatre, art or forensics to be considered for this renewable award. more...

President's Scholarship - Concordia University
The President's Scholarship is available to incoming students at Concordia University. You must demonstrate highly competitive academic achievement to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Regent's Scholarship - Concordia University Nebraska
The Regent's Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Concordia University Nebraska. The award amount will be determined by your GPA and ACT/SAT scores. more...

Rotc Scholarship - Concordia University Nebraska
The ROTC Scholarship is available to entering full-time freshmen at Concordia University Nebraska. You must declare your intent to seek an active or reserved forces commission to be considered for this award. more...
