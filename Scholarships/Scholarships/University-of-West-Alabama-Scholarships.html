Community College Academic Awards

Trustee Excellence Award
Scholarship for first-time freshmen accepted to the University of West Alabama. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, ACT score of 30-36 or SAT score of 1340-1600, live on campus and be U.S. citizens/permanent residents. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...
