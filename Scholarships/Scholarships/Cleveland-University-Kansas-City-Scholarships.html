Cleveland University-Kansas City Scholarships


Academic Honor Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Cleveland Chiropractic College. Students must demonstrate academic achievement. Due dates are July 1st for the fall semester, November 1st for the spring semester, and March 1st for the summer session. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Academic Merit Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming0701 graduate students enrolled at Cleveland Chiropractic College. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due dates are July 1st for the fall semester, November 1st for the spring semester, and March 1st for the summer session. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Career Changers Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Cleveland Chiropractic College. Students must be starting a second career in Chiropractic or who have worked in a different field or were pursuing a major in a different subject. Due dates are July 1st for the fall semester, November 1st for the spring semester, and March 1st for the summer session. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Clevelands Merit Scholarship
Annual award for graduate students enrolled full-time at Cleveland Chiropractic College. Students must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA and have completed two trimesters in the Doctor of Chiropractic program. Due dates are July 1st for the fall semester, November 1st for the spring semester, and March 1st for the summer session. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

State Scholarship
Scholarship for Arizona, New Mexico, Michigan, Utah, Nevada, Oregon, and Washington residents who are undergraduate or graduate students enrolled at Cleveland Chiropractic College. Students must have a minimum 2.75 CGPA and financial need. Due dates are July 1st for the fall semester, November 1st for the spring semester, and March 1st for the summer session. An essay and proof of residency must be included with application packet. Nonrenewable. more...
