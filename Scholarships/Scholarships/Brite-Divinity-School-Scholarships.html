Academic Freshman Scholarship
Annual award for incoming freshmen attending Texas Christian University in Fort Worth, Texas. Applicant must have a minimum 3.25 GPA at the end of their freshman year. Number of awards varies. Renewable; must maintain 3.0 GPA. more...

Albertsons Tcu Spirit Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students attending the Texas Christian University (TCU) in Fort Worth, Texas. Preference will be given to students who are, or have been, employees of the Albertsons store located at 3120 South University Drive in Fort Worth, Texas. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA at TCU. Candidates must demonstrate leadership and community involvement through various student organizations. Due date is August 1 for fall award and December 1 for spring award. Number of awards varies. more...

Army Rotc Scholarship
Annual award for ROTC students who are undergraduates attending Texas Christian University (TCU) in Fort Worth, Texas. In addition to the award for all or part of tuition, students will receive between $250-$400 per month for up to ten months each year, plus a $600 allowance for books and fees. Recipients will receive a room and board grant worth $4800 if they score above an 1100 on the SAT or 24 on the ACT, or transfer to TCU with a 3.25 GPA. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Bermont Family Undergraduate Jewish Student Scholarship
Annual award for Jewish students who are undergraduates attending the Texas Christian University (TCU) in Fort Worth, Texas. Preference will be given to students who have demonstrated financial need as evidenced by the FAFSA. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Big Brothers Big Sisters Of Tarrant County Scholarship
Annual award for current or previous members of Big Brothers or Sister who are undergraduate students attending the Texas Christian University (TCU) in Fort Worth, Texas. Preference will be given to male minority applicants. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need (as established by their FAFSA application). Incoming freshmen must have ranked in the top 25% of their high school class. Transfer students and current TCU students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Cooperative Teacher Aide Scholarships

Harriet Tubman Scholarship
Annual award for African-American students who are graduates of Tarrant County High School and are planning to attend, or are currently attending, the Texas Christian University (TCU) in Fort Worth, Texas. Preference will be given to applicants who major in Banking, Business, Management, Accounting, Finance, Economics or related field. Applicants must have a minimum 2.8 GPA, and have demonstrated financial need (as established by FAFSA application). Freshmen applications are due by February 1st; transfer applications are due by April 1st. Number of awards and award amount varies. more...

M.D. Anderson Pre-Med Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students attending the Texas Christian University (TCU) in Fort Worth, Texas to study Pre-Med. Candidates must have a minimum 3.0 GPA overall, and have community service to TCU, fellow students and the profession.
