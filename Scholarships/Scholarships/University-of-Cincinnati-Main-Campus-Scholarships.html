University of Cincinnati-Main Campus Scholarships


American Institute Of Architecture (Aia) Dayton Architectural Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for residents of Champaign, Clark, Darke, Greene, Logan, Miami, Montgomery, Preble or Shelby counties, Ohio who are graduating high school seniors enrolling in select colleges in Ohio and will be majoring in Architecture. Due date and number of awards may vary. Participating colleges are: Miami University, Kent State University, Ohio State University, or the University of Cincinnati. Nonrenewable. more...

Brian James Cook Athletic Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors of Madison High School planning to pursue a Teaching career. Applicants must have participated in athletics. Preference is given to students who participated in the basketball program and to students attending the University of Cincinnati. Nonrenewable. more...

Darwin T. Turner Scholarship - University Of Cincinnati
The Darwin T. Turner Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at the University of Cincinnati. This award is merit-based and covers full tuition, fees, and book stipend. more...

Marian Spencer Scholarship
The Marian Spencer Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at the University of Cincinnati. To be eligible for this renewable award, you must rank within the top 10 percent of students within a Cincinnati Public High School and exemplify the "Spirit of Spencer". more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Uc
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to incoming community college transfer students entering the University of Cincinnati. You must be a Phi Theta Kappa member and have at least a 3.75 GPA to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Taft, Stettinius & Hollister Founders Scholarship
The Taft, Stettinius & Hollister Founders Scholarship is available to students at the University of Cincinnati, School of Law. You must demonstrate academic excellence to be eligible for this award. more...

Toepfer Family Scholarship
The Toepfer Family Scholarship is available to students at the University of Cincinnati, School of Law. You must demonstrate high scholastic achievement to be eligible for this award. more...

Uc Community College Transfer Scholarship
The UC Community College Transfer Scholarship is available to students entering the University of Cincinnati. You must be a transfer student with at least a 3.0 cumulative GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

University Of Cincinnati-College Of Applied Science Alumni-Sae Engineering Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend the University of Cincinnati College of Applied Science majoring in Architectural, Mechanical, or Manufacturing Engineering Technology. Students must be U.S. citizens. An essay must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Victor Ray Iii Memorial Fund
The Victor Ray III Memorial Fund is available to students at the University of Cincinnati, School of Law. You must demonstrate academic achievement to be eligible for this award. more...

Wil Ziegler Scholarship
The Wil Ziegler Scholarship is available to students at the University of Cincinnati, School of Law. You must be from Northern Kentucky to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to graduates of Thomas More College. Selection is based on academic record and financial need. more...

William J. Graham, Jr., Memorial Fund
The William J. Graham, Jr., Memorial Fund is available to students at the University of Cincinnati, School of Law. You must demonstrate superior academic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

William R. Collins Scholarship
The William R. Collins Scholarship is available to students at the University of Cincinnati, School of Law. Selection is based on scholastic achievement, legal aptitude and financial need. more...
