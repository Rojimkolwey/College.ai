Middle Tennessee State University Scholarships


Archer-Johnstone Scholarship
The Archer-Johnstone Scholarship is available to management students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must have completed at least 55 credit hours (excluding developmental studies courses), have a minimum 3.0 GPA, and demonstrate leadership abilities to be eligible for this award. Additionally, you must be a resident of Tennessee or Mississippi. more...

Archie Hartwell Nash Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate (freshmen ineligible) students enrolled at Middle Tennessee State University. Students must have a minimum 2.0 GPA and be working a minimum of 20 hours a week. Number of awards may vary. more...

Heritage Farms Scholarship
The Heritage Farms Scholarship is available to junior and senior students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be majoring in agriculture and demonstrate academic excellence in the area of milk processing to be eligible for this award. more...

Hugh H. Burrum Memorial Scholarship
The Hugh H. Burrum Memorial Scholarship is available to sophomore students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be majoring in agriculture and be a resident of Tennessee to be eligible for this award. more...

J. L. Fletcher Graduate Scholarship
The J. L. Fletcher Graduate Scholarship is available to entering graduate students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be majoring in the biological sciences to be eligible for this award. more...

Marion Wells Scholarship
The Marion Wells Scholarship is available to graduate students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be majoring in the biological sciences and be conducting summer research for your thesis to be eligible for this award. more...

Mary C. Dunn Graduate Scholarship
The Mary C. Dunn Graduate Scholarship is available to graduate students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be majoring in the biological sciences and be conducting research to be eligible for this award. The amount of this award is intended to help with the overall costs associated with research. more...

Patrick J. Doyle Freshman Scholarship
The Patrick J. Doyle Freshman Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be majoring in the biological sciences and be the third place scorer on the departmental exam to be eligible for this award. more...

Rotc Alumni Scholarship - Mtsu
The ROTC Alumni Scholarship is available to students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be enrolled in the ROTC Program and be in good standing with the university to be eligible for this award. Recipients are selected by faculty. more...

Tennessee Council Of Cooperatives Scholarship
The Tennessee Council of Cooperatives Scholarship is available to full-time third-year students at Middle Tennessee State University. You must be enrolled in the department of agribusiness/science and have a minimum GPA of 2.5. Also, your parent must be a member of the Tennessee Cooperative Council to be eligible for this award. more...

This award is intended to provide financial assistance to help defray the costs associated with research. more...
