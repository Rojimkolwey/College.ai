Chippewa Valley Technical College Scholarships


Arnold And Lois Domer Foundation Scholarship
The Arnold and Lois Domer Foundation Scholarship is available to continuing students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must have at least a 2.75 GPA with a minimum of 12 credits completed to be eligible for this award. First preference will be given to first-generation college student with financial need. more...

Cheryl A. Janke Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Cheryl A. Janke Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be majoring in accounting, administrative assistance, GIS technology, information technology, instructional assistance, marketing, office assistant, paralegal studies, supervisory management and have at least a 3.0 GPA with at least 12 credits earned to be eligible for this award. more...

Frank Sorrentino Memorial Scholarship
The Frank Sorrentino Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be majoring in electromechanical technology and have at least a 3.5 GPA with at least 12 credits completed. You must also be a US citizen residing in the CVTC District and demonstrate involvement in campus clubs or community service to be eligible for this award. more...

Johnson-Bakker Harmony Scholarship
The Johnson-Bakker Harmony Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be a student with nine credits completed with at least a 2.0 GPA. Preference will be given to minority, limited English proficiency and international students. more...

Lavern G. Soper Memorial Scholarship
The Lavern G. Soper Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be majoring in electromechanical technology and have at least a 3.5 GPA with at least 24 credits completed. You must also be a US citizen residing in the CVTC District and demonstrate involvement in campus clubs or community service to be eligible for this award. more...

Maybelle Brechlin Price Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Maybelle Brechlin Price Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA with at least 12 credits completed to be eligible for this award. more...

R.E. Carter Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The R.E. Carter Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be an automotive maintenance technician and have completed at least 12 credits to be eligible for this award. more...

Robert Straavaldson Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Robert Straavaldson Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be majoring in air conditioning, heating and refrigeration or plumbing. You must have at least a 12 credits completed to be eligible for this award. more...

Ruth R. Peterson Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Ruth R. Peterson Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be a single mother with custody of her minor children and financial need. You must also have at least a 3.0 GPA and a minimum of 12 credits completed to be eligible for this award. more...

Us Bank Endowment Scholarship
The US Bank Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be majoring in accounting, administrative assistance, computer information systems, marketing or office assistance. You must also have at least a 3.0 GPA and have completed at least 12 credits to be eligible for this award. more...

West Central Wisconsin Aib/Edna Brown Endowment Scholarship
The West Central Wisconsin AIB/EDNA Brown Endowment Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be a resident of Burnett, Washburn, Sawyer, Polk, Barron, Rush, St. Croix, Dunn, Chippewa, Eau Claire, Pepin, Pierce, Trempealeau or Buffalo County. You must be studying accounting, computer information, or marketing or to become an administrative assistant, office assistant or paralegal. You must have completed at least 12 credits with a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Wisconsin Hospital Association Foundation, Inc. Scholarship
The Wisconsin Hospital Association Foundation, Inc. Scholarship is available to students at Chippewa Valley Technical College. You must be in your final semester studying in a health-care field with at least a 3.0 GPA. You must be studying as an alcohol and other drug abuse associate, clinical laboratory technician, diagnostic medical sonographer, health information technician, nurse, radiographer or respiratory care practitioner to be eligible for this award. more...
