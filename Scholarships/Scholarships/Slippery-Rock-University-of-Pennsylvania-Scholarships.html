Slippery Rock University of Pennsylvania Scholarships


Biology Honors Scholarship - Slippery Rock University
The Biology Honors Scholarship is offered to a freshman biology majors at Slippery Rock University. You must rank in the top 20 percent of your high school graduating class, have a combined SAT score of at least 1000, or a composite ACT score of 23 or better to be eligible for this renewable award. You must include three letters of recommendation attesting to your character, potential for academic success, dependability and emotional maturity. more...

Boccio Family Scholarship
The Boccio Family Scholarship is available to second semester sophomore, junior and senior students at Slippery Rock University. You must be majoring in physical education, demonstrate financial need, and have a minimum 3.0 QPA to be eligible for this renewable award. First preference is given to women student athletes. more...

Sophomore Biology Honors Scholarship - Sru
The Sophomore Biology Honors Scholarship is open to biology majors with 25-40 semester hours completed at Slippery Rock University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and have completed Principles of Biology, and General Botany or General Zoology or Genetics and General Chemistry I and II along with their respective labs to be eligible for the award. The scholarship is automatically awarded to the top two students at the end of their freshman year. more...

Thomas W. Gaither Scholarship
