Binghamton University Scholarships


Bc Nysspe/Watson Scholarship
The BC NYSSPE/Watson Scholarship is open to transfer students at the Binghamton University. You must be a US citizen and be a two-year engineering science program transfer student from Broome, Delhi, Corning, or Tompkins-Cortland Community College to be considered for this award. more...

Niermeyer Foundation Scholarship
The Niermeyer Foundation Scholarship is open to African-American and Asian-American students at the State University of New York, Binghamton. You must be in good academic standing and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Samuel And Rae Eckman Scholarship
The Samuel and Rae Eckman Scholarship is open to students at the State University of New York, Binghamton. You must be enrolled in the School of Management to be considered for this award. You must also demonstrate academic promise and financial need. more...

Senior Challenge Scholarship
The Senior Challenge Scholarship is open to full-time students at the State University of New York, Binghamton. You must be in good academic standing and have achieved excellence in all areas of University life to be considered for this award. more...

Som 25Th Anniversary Scholarship
The SOM 25th Anniversary Scholarship is available to junior students at the School of Management at SUNY Binghamton University. You must be a full-time student in good academic standing and demonstrate leadership and contributions to the community to be eligible for this award. more...

Sophie Kradjian Memorial Scholarship
The Sophie Kradjian Memorial Scholarship is available to junior and senior French majors at the State University of New York, Binghamton. You must have a strong concentration in literature, especially as preparation for graduate study in French literature. Selection will be based on academic merit and financial need. more...

Terence "Tex" Haran Memorial Tau Scholarship
The Terrence "Tex" Haran Memorial TAU Scholarship is open to full-time male students who are juniors or seniors at the State University of New York, Binghamton. Preference is given to individuals who demonstrate a commitment to community service and involvement in one or more of the following: social activities, student life, clubs, sports teams, academic clubs. Preference is also given to well-rounded students who demonstrate a high level of motivation and perseverance as they work toward obtaining significant personal, community and educational goals. more...

Unger Family Scholarship
The Unger Family Scholarship is available to students in the School of Management at SUNY Binghamton University. You must demonstrate academic merit, leadership and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

William H. And Audree F. Rincker Scholarship
The William H. and Audree F. Rincker Scholarship is open to students at the State University of New York, Binghamton. You must be enrolled in the School of Management to be considered for this award. Preference is given to students from the greater southern tier of New York who are interested in finance. more...

William Semple Memorial Scholarship
The William Semple Memorial Scholarship is open to promising female minority students who are juniors at Binghamton University. You must be enrolled in the Watson School of Engineering to qualify for this award. more...
