Airfare Reimbursement Grant - Central Christian College
The Airfare Reimbursement Grant is open to students applying to Central Christian College. CCC will reimburse the cost of your airline ticket you used for a campus visit with a grant worth up to $500. To be eligible, you must fly from a location outside a 400 mile radius of McPherson, Kansas, and submit a receipt to verify ticket purchase. more...
