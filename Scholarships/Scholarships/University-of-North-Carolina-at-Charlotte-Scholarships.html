University of North Carolina at Charlotte Scholarships


Bonnie E. Cone Scholarship For Merit
The Bonnie E. Cone Scholarship for Merit is available to entering freshmen attending the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. You must rank in the top ten percent of your graduating class, have a minimum SAT score of 1200 or an equivalent ACT score and show evidence of leadership and service in school and/or community to be eligible for this award. In addition, you must be a U.S. citizen and demonstrate high moral character. more...

Robert G. Fowler, Jr. Esquire International Scholarship
The Robert G. Fowler, Jr. Esquire International Scholarship is available to students attending the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. You must be majoring in political science or international studies or minoring in international studies, have completed a minimum of 60 credit hours with at least 12 hours in international/crosscultural studies and have a minimum GPA of 3.2 to be eligible for this award. In addition, you must have participated in extracurricular and/or community activities which demonstrate an interest beyond classroom study in international or legal issues. Special consideration shall be given to students considering a career in law. more...
