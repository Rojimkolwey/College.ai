Truckee Meadows Community College Scholarships


Jacobs Presidential Scholarship
The Herb and Maxine S. Jacobs Presidential Scholarship is awarded to students who are under or unemployed and enrolled in six credits with a 2.0 GPA or higher. You must be admitted to the Jacobs Presidential Scholarship program to be eligible. For more information, please visit: http://www.tmcc.edu/aor/jacobs-presidential-scholars-program/. more...

Latimer Art Club Scholarship
Students must be a Fine Arts major with a 3.0 GPA or higher, enrolled in 12 or more credits. The students must also answer the question, How do you see art as part of your life? in a minimum of 250 words and submit a copy of a piece of art. The three finalist must submit the piece of art to the committee. more...

Mstr Sgt B.A. Tipps Scholarship
Students must answer the following question to be considered " How does music positively affect your life?" Students must have a 2.0 GPA, and enroll in at least 6 credits. Preference will be given to Veteran students. more...

Pennington Applied Tech Scholarship
The William N. Pennington Applied Tech Scholarship is awarded to students studying Advanced Manufacturing, Architecture and Design Technology, Auto, Construction Management, Diesel, HVAC, Machining or Welding. They must have a 2.0 GPA and be enrolled in 6 credits. Scholarships will be given to students who plan to remain in Northern Nevada upon completion of their program. more...

Pennington Nursing Scholarship
Pennington Nursing Scholarships are awarded each semester to the entering cohort of the Maxine S. Jacobs Nursing Program and continuing students. Students must have a GPA of 3.0 or higher, demonstrate financial need and be residents of Northern Nevada. more...

Pennington Vet Tech Scholarship
The William N. Pennington Veterinary Nursing Scholarship will be awarded to students who are enrolled in the Vet Nursing program with a 3.0 GPA. Scholarships will be given to students who plan to remain in Northern Nevada upon completion of their program. more...

Sala Family Dentistry
The Sala Family Dentistry scholarship will be awarded to 4 second-year Dental students who plan to live and work in Northern Nevada after graduation. Students are required to have at least a 3.0 GPA. Students must answer additional questions to be considered. more...
