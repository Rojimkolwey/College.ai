Church Work-Grant
Scholarship for Lutheran church-work undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Concordia University at Austin. Students must complete a Declaration of Professional Intent. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Concordia Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Concordia University at Austin. Students must have financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Distinguished Student Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Concordia University at Austin. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and a minimum 25 ACT score or a minimum 1150 SAT score for high school students and a minimum 3.5 GPA for transfer students. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Julia Krumm Aspiring Organist Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Concordia University at Austin Music Department majoring in Music Ministry. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and a minimum 3.0 CGPA in music courses, after twelve hours of courses, and must be studying organ and plan to seve as church organists. An audition is required. Number of awards may vary. more...

Leo & Thelma Gastler Distinguished Music Student Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Concordia University at Austin Music Department majoring in Music Ministry or Teacher Education. Students must have a minimum 3.0 CGPA and a minimum 3.25 CGPA in music courses, after nine hours of courses. An audition is required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...

Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...
