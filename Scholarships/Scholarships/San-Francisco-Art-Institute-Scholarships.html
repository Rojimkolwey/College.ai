San Francisco Art Institute Scholarships


California Community College Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the San Francisco Art Institute. Students must be transferring from a community college in California. Due date and number of awards may vary. A portfolio must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Deans Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the San Francisco Art Institute. Number of awards may vary. A portfolio must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Murphy & Cadogan Fellowships In The Fine Arts
Scholarship for Bay Area California residents who are graduate students enrolled at the Academy of Art College, California College of the Arts, John F. Kennedy University, Mills College, San Francisco Art Institute, San Francisco State University, Stanford University or the University of California at Berkeley. Students must be nominated and have completed at least one semester of gradute study. Number of awards may vary. more...

Osher Memorial Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the San Francisco Art Institute. Students must rank in the top of their class. Due date and number of awards may vary. A portfolio must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Peanuts On Parade Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for Sonoma County, California residents who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at a postsecondary institution majoring in Cartooning or Animation. Preference given to students with professional experience and who have been accepted to the San Francisco Art Institute, Academy of Art University, San Francisco, California, College of the Arts, or the Center for Cartoon Study, Vermont, or other approved art institutions. An interview is required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. A sample of work must be included with application packet. more...

San Francisco Art Institute Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled at the San Francisco Art Institute. Due date may vary. Renewable. A portfolio must be included with application packet. Contact the schools admissions office or Scholastic for more information and/or application. more...

Transfer Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the San Francisco Art Institute. Students must demonstrate exceptional merit and talent. Due date and number of awards may vary. A portfolio must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...
