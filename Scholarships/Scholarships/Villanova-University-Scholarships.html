Law School Moral Obligation Grant - Villanova University
The Law School Moral Obligation Grant is available to current students at Villanova University, School of Law. You demonstrate financial need to eligible for this award. Recipients undertake a moral obligation to not only repay the actual money awarded, but also to provide further support for other needy students after graduation. more...
