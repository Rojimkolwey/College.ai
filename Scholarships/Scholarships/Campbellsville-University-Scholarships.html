Campbellsville University Scholarships


Acteen Scholarships
Scholarship for female incoming freshmen admitted to Campbellsville University. Student must have earned one or more STUDIACT awards in Acteen programs in their church, and have financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Art, Drama, Journalism, Band, & Music Performance Grants & Competitive Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Campbellsville University who have talent in Art, Drama, Journalism, Band, or Music. Selection based on skills or auditions in applicable fields. Due date, number of awards and award amounts may vary. Awards may be renewable. more...

Athletic Performance Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students admitted to Campbellsville University who are involved in the schools intercollegiate sports program. Selection is based on ability, financial need, leadership, and potential. Mens sports include: basketball, tennis, golf, cross country, football, soccer, baseball, cheerleading, track, and wrestling. Womens sports include: basketball, tennis, golf, cross country, volleyball, softball, and track. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Baptist Leadership Awards
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Campbellsville University. Applicants must be nominated by a Baptist Pastor, have outstanding Christian commitment, achievements, and involvement in community, school and church. Due date may vary. Renewable. more...

Baptist Youth Speakers Tournament Sholarships
Scholarship for students admitted to Campbellsville University who are winners or first runners-up in the Baptist Youth Speakers Tournament in each participating Southern Baptist state convention. Student must show financial need. Renewable. more...

Christian Service Scholarships
Employee-Related awards for students admitted to Campbellsville University whose parent(s) serve in a full-time Christian vocation. Applicants must have financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Christian Vocation Grants
Scholarship for students admitted to Campbellsville University who have made a public commitment to a Christian vocation. Applicant must have financial need. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Church Matching Scholarships
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students admitted to Campbellsville University who are members of a church who will contribute matching funds. Applicants must have financial need. The College will match an amount up to $200 provided annually by the students church, fellowship or assembly. Awards are granted on a first-come, first served basis. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Governors Scholar Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Campbellsville University who participated in the Kentucky Governors Scholar program. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and a minimum ACT score of 25 or SAT score of 1140. Renewable. more...

High School Baptist Young Men Scholarships
Scholarship for young men admitted to Campbellsville University who have earned one or more Missions Challenge (or Service Aide) award(s) in the High School Baptist Young Men programs in their churches. Applicants must have financial need. Due date may vary. Renewable. more...

Kentucky Junior Miss Scholarships
Scholarship for female undergraduate students attending Campbellsville University who competed in the Junior Miss competition and were runner ups or winners of the competition. Applicants may be winners at the county level up to the state competition. Award amount ranges from $2,000 up to full-tuition, room and board. Renewable. more...

Missionary Kids Scholarships
Scholarship granted to the children of foreign missionaries admitted to Campbellsville University. Applicants must have financial need. Awards are for each foreign missionarys child eligible for a Margaret Fund scholarship from the Foreign Mission Board of the Southern Baptist Convention. Due date may vary. Award amount is for full-tuition. Renewable. more...

Presidential Excellence Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Campbellsville University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 GPA and ACT score of 33-36 or SAT score of 1450-1600. Due date, number of awards, and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Special Recognition Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolling at Campbellsville University. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, an ACT score of 21-22 or SAT score of 970-1040. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Womens Missionary Union Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming, female, freshmen admitted to Campbellsville University who have completed the Acteens Queen Regent award. Applicants must have financial need. Awards are sponsored by the Womens Missionary Union of Kentucky Baptist Convention and are renewable with a minimum 2.0 GPA. more...
