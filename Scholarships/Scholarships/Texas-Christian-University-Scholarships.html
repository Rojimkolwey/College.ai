Dean's Scholarship - Texas Christian University
The Dean's Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Texas Christian University. Your selection is based on class rank, recommendations, test scores, essay and co-curricular activities. This scholarship is renewable provided you complete at least 24 hours of "letter grade" course work per year with at least a 3.0 GPA following your freshman year and a 3.25 GPA every year after that. more...

Tcu/Tcc Cornerstone Scholarship
The TCU/TCC Cornerstone Scholarship is open to transfer students at Texas Christian University. You must have a minimum 3.25 GPA, and participated in Tarrant County College's Cornerstone Scholars program to be considered for this award. You must also have completed your course work at Tarrant County Junior College (two years full-time coursework or an Associate's Degree). This award is renewable provided you complete at least 27 hours of "letter grade" course work with a minimum 3.25 GPA. more...

Transfer Chancellor's Scholarship - Tcu
The Transfer Chancellors Scholarship is open to transfer students at Texas Christian University. You must have a minimum 3.25 GPA, and have either an associate's degree or at least 64 transferable hours of course work. You must also have outstanding personal qualities, and participate in outside activities or have work experience. This award covers full tuition for up to two years provided you completed at least 27 hours of "letter grade" course work with a minimum 3.25 GPA. more...
