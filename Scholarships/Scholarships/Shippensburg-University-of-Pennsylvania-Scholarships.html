Shippensburg University of Pennsylvania Scholarships


Abraham K. Smith Scholarship For Outstanding Students
The Abraham K. Smith Scholarship for Outstanding Students is available to students entering Shippensburg University. You must have at least a 1200 SAT score and rank in the upper 10 percent of your graduating class to be eligible for this award. more...

Carl A. Naugle Family Graduate Fellowship
The Carl A. Naugle Family Graduate Fellowship is available to graduate students at Shippensburg University. You must be pursuing a Master of Science in college student personnel work or college counseling and have at least a 3.2 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Catharine H. Ellenberger Scholarship
The Catharine H. Ellenberger Scholarship is available to graduate students pursuing a masters of education at Shippensburg University. You must be specializing in elementary school counseling or secondary school counseling to be eligible for this award. more...

Kendlehart Scholarship Fund-The Mary T. Kendlehart Award
Scholarship for full-time college sophomores, juniors, and seniors attending Shippensburg University majoring in Elementary Education. Applicants must be pursuing thier first baccalaureate degree and have aminimum 3.2 GPA. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. Contact the Universitys Financial Aid Office for more information and/or application. more...

Lawrence Tyson Wolfrom Memorial Scholarship
The Lawrence Tyson Wolfrom Memorial Scholarship is available to junior, senior and graduate students at Shippensburg University. You must be enrolled in geography, earth science or geo-environmental program and have at least a 2.8 GPA. You must also demonstrate personal character and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Mary Beasley Burch Gifted Minority Student Scholarship
The Mary Beasley Burch Gifted Minority Student Scholarship is open to entering full- time freshmen at Shippensburg University. You must be a minority student, have at least a 1050 SAT score, and rank in the top 10 percent of your class to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Rupe-Stuart College Student Personnel And Counseling Scholarship
The Rupe-Stuart College Student Personnel and Counseling Scholarship is available to students at Shippensburg University. You must be enrolled in the college student personnel or college student counseling masters program. You must also demonstrate academic achievement and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Samuel & Helen Gotwalt Fund
Scholarship for York County, Pennsylvania residents who are graduating high school seniors from William Penn Senior High School enrolled at Shippensburg University. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...
