Pennsylvania State University-Penn State Altoona Scholarships


Robert J. Prindle And Doris P. Prindle Memorial Scholarship
The Robert J. Prindle and Doris P. Prindle Memorial Scholarship is open to students majoring in the arts at Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

Robert L. Hite Scholarship
The Robert L. Hite Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen students attending Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must be a graduate of Altoona High School and demonstrate academic achievement, leadership ability and good citizenship to be considered for this award. more...

Robert W. St. Clair Scholarship
The Robert W. St. Clair Scholarship is open to students attending Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must be a graduate of a Blair County high school and demonstrate high academic achievement, leadership ability and good citizenship to be considered for this award. more...

Stephen G. Sheetz Endowed Scholarship
The Stephen G. Sheetz Endowed Scholarship is open to full-time undergraduate students majoring in business at Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must demonstrate a superior academic record and manifest promise of academic success to be eligible for this award. more...

Ted And Ester Holtzinger Memorial Scholarship
The Ted and Ester Holtzinger Memorial Scholarship is open to students attending Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must be enrolled on a part-time or less than part-time basis to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to students who have not previously received a Ted and Ester Holzinger Scholarship or a Harry E. Scholarship, and who have not recently attended high school or college. more...

Ted And Esther Holtzinger Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Ted and Esther Holtzinger Memorial Endowed Scholarship is open to students at Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must have been out of high school for at least four years to be eligible for this award. more...

Virginia L. Gates Endowed Scholarship
The Virginia L. Gates Endowed Scholarship is open to full-time juniors and seniors at Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must have a superior academic record and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. more...

William H. & M. Jean Coleman Endowed Scholarship
The William H. & M. Jean Coleman Endowed Scholarship is open to full-time sophomores majoring in the agriculture at Pennsylvania State University, Altoona. You must have a minimum 3.2 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
