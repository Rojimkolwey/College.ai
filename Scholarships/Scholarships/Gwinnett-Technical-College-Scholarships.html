Gwinnett Technical College Scholarships


Adam Allgood Automotive Leadership Award
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled at Gwinnett Technical College pursuing studies in Automotive Technology program. Students must demonstrate leadership and be of the highest honor and integrity. Due date may vary. more...

Agco Corporation Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Gwinnett Technical College pursuing studies in Heavy Equipment Technology Program. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA. more...

American Society Of Professional Estimators (Aspe)
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at Gwinnett Technical College pursuing studies in Commercial Construction Management. Students must have completed a minimum of 20 hours of Commercial Construction Management courses, have a minimum 3.0 GPA, and demonstrate a dedication to the commercial construction profession. more...

Bob Talley Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Gwinnett Technical College. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and have attended Maxwell High School. more...

General Veterinary Medical Association
Annual award for college sophomores enrolled at Gwinnett Technical College pursuing studies in Veterinary Technology program. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and have earned a total of 28 credit hours in the Veterinary Technology Program. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Jean Sheaffer Scholarship
Annual award for Gwinnett County, Georgia residents who are undergraduate students enrolled at Gwinnett Technical College pursuing studies in Automotive Technology program. Students must have been in the Adult Basic Education Program. A personal letter must be included with application packet. more...

Michael Pattillo Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Gwinnett Technical College pursuing studies in Management Marketing program. Students must demonstrate financial need and be employed in a full-time job. A copy of the previous years income tax return must be included with application packet. more...

Panoz Child Care Assistance Scholarship
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled at Gwinnett Technical College. Students must demonstrate financial need and have children younger than 13 years of age in licensed child care in order to attend classes at the college. Award is for child care cost. Due dates are January 20th, April 20th, July 20th, and October 20th. A copy of the previous years income tax return must be included with application packet. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...

Va Scholarship
Annual award for veterans or dependent children of vetarans who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Gwinnett Technical College. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA. Due dates are January 20th, April 20th, July 20th, and October 20th. An essay and proof of veteran affiliation must be included with application packet. Nonrenewable; recipients may reapply. more...
