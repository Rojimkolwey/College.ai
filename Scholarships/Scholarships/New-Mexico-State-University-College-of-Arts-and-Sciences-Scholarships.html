New Mexico State University-Main Campus Scholarships


Anayis Generalized Reciprocity Current Use Scholarship
The Anayis Generalized Reciprocity Current Use Scholarship is available to junior and senior students at New Mexico State University. You must be majoring in astronomy, chemistry, biochemistry, computer science, geology, math or physics and have a minimum 2.4 GPA to be eligible for this award. U.S. citizenship and financial need are also required. more...

Arts And Sciences Scholarship - New Mexico State University
The Arts and Sciences Scholarship is available to sophomore, junior and senior students at New Mexico State University. You must be majoring in the arts, humanities, sciences or social sciences and have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this renewable award. An essay describing ways in which your work in your major field will contribute to humanity's needs is also required. more...

Charles E. Stillwell Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Charles E. Stillwell Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to sophomore students at New Mexico State University. You must have a minimum 2.7 GPA and submit a short essay to be eligible for this award. more...

David And Reta Warren Endowed Scholarship
The David and Reta Warren Endowed Scholarship is available to students with at least 90 undergraduate credit hours at New Mexico State University. You must be entering dental school, have a minimum GPA of 3.5 and have been a member of the NMSU Pre-Dental society for at least one year in order to be considered for this award. Preference is given to graduates of New Mexico high schools. more...

John E. And Grace E. And John L. Gill Memorial Scholarship
The John E. and Grace E. and John L. Gill Memorial Scholarship is available to undergraduate students at New Mexico State University. You must be a resident of New Mexico and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference is give to graduates of Las Cruces High School and those pursing a degree related to a medical field. more...

Ralph W. Dressel Memorial Endowed Scholarship
The Ralph W. Dressel Memorial Endowed Scholarship is available to sophomore, junior and senior students at New Mexico State University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and complete three essay questions in order to be considered for this award. more...

S.J. Scheidegger Burke Endowed Scholarship
The S.J. Scheidegger Burke Endowed Scholarship is available to sophomore, junior and senior students at New Mexico State University. You must be majoring in biology, chemistry, biochemistry, computer science, math or physics and have a minimum 3.2 GPA to be eligible for this award. You must also be a single parent, a resident of New Mexico and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...
