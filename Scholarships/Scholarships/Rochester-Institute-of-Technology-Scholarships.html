Rochester Institute of Technology Scholarships


Achievement Scholarships For Art, Design & Crafts
Scholarship for freshmen entering Rochester Institute of Technology enrolling in Art, Design or Crafts. Applicants must have a minimum 1170 SAT or 26 ACT score and a B+ average. A portfolio must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

Achievement Scholarships For Business, Liberal Arts & Hospitality Management
Scholarship for freshmen entering Rochester Institute of Technology enrolling in Business, Liberal Arts, or Hospitality Management. Applicants must have a minimum 1200 SAT score or 26 ACT, a minimum B+ average and outstanding leadership, service, entrepreneurship or citizenship. Number of awards may vary. more...

Achievement Scholarships For Transfer Students
Scholarship for sophomores, juniors or seniors transferring to Rochester Institute of Technology. Applicants must have a minimum 3.5 transfer GPA and have completed a minimum of 30 semester hours or 45 quarter hours. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Achievement Scholarships-All Programs
Scholarship for freshmen entering Rochester Institute of Technology. Applicants must have a minimum 1200 SAT or 26 ACT score, strong extracurricular achievements and a B+ average. Renewable. more...

Computing Medal Scholarships
Scholarship for freshmen entering Rochester Institute of Technology. Students must be a Computing Medal winner from a participating high school. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Nathaniel Rochester Society (Nrs) Scholarships
Scholarship for full time juniors and seniors attending Rochester Institute of Technology. Applicants must have completed a minimum of 72 credit hours at RIT with a minimum 3.4 GPA. Number of awards may vary. more...

National Co-Op Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Rochester Institute of Technology. Selection is based on academic record and essay. Renewable. more...

National Merit, National Achievement & National Hispanic Scholarships
Scholarship for freshmen entering Rochester Institute of Technology. Students must be finalists or semifinalists in the National Merit, National Achivement or National Hispanic program. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

National Technical Institute For The Deaf (Ntid) Grant-In-Aid
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students enrolling in Rochester of Technologys National Institute for the Deaf. Applicants must demonstrate financial need due to insufficient support from outside sources. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

National Technology Institute For The Deaf (Ntid) Vice President & Deans Scholarship For Academic Excellence
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending National Technology Institute for the Deaf. Selection is based on academic record, recommendations, activities and the requirements for the academic program. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

National Technology Institute For The Deaf Performing Arts Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending National Technology for the Deaf who are willing to participate in performing arts. An essay must be included with application packet. Students must file the FAFSA. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Part-Time Studies Grant
Scholarship for part-time undergraduate students attending Rochester Institute of Technology enrolled in a degree program. Applicants must be enrolled for less than 12 credit hours and demonstrate financial need. Students must file the FAFSA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarships For Transfer Students
Scholarship for junior or senior transfer students who are Phi Theta Kappa members enrolling at Rochester Institute of Technology. Applicants must have earned an associate degree. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Rit Grants

Rit-Project Lead The Way Scholarships
