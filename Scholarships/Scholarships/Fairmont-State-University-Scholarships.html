Fairmont State University Scholarships


Falcon Scholarship - Fairmont State
The Falcon Scholarship is open to freshman students at Fairmont State. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and a minimum 18 ACT score to be eligible for this renewable award. Award amounts are based on ACT score, GPA and residency. more...

Maroon And White Scholarship - Fairmont State
The Maroon and White Scholarship is open to continuing full-time students at Fairmont State. You must be in good academic standing and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Stanley And Mary Beafore Scholarship
The Stanley and Mary Beafore Scholarship is open to business majors at Fairmont State. Preference will be given to students of Italian-American heritage. You must submit a paper to the FS foundation on the influence of your heritage and culture in your life. more...

Vincent And Catherine Sansalone Scholarship
The Vincent and Catherine Sansalone Scholarship is open to mathematics and science majors at Fairmont State. Preference will be given to students of Italian-American heritage. An essay is required in which you must explain the influence of your heritage and culture on your life. more...

Virginia Palmer Wellock/Richard P. Wellock Music Scholarship
The Virginia Palmer Wellock/Richard P. Wellock Music Scholarship is open to students at Fairmont State who are majoring in music. You must demonstrate musical capability, financial need and academic merit to be considered. more...

W.R. "Squibb" Wilson Athletic Scholarship
The W.R. "Squibb" Wilson Athletic Scholarship is open to members of the men's baseball team at Fairmont State. more...

Walter Phillips, Jr. Scholarship
The Walter Phillips, Jr. Scholarship is open to students at Fairmont State. You must be majoring in manufacturing engineering technology and have a minimum 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Warren R. And Ruth M. Grocott Award
The Warren R. and Ruth M. Grocott Award is open to students at Fairmont State. You must be majoring in marketing, management, or finance or have a minor in accounting to be eligible for this award. more...

Wendell Hardway Award For Excellence In Teacher Education
The Wendell Hardway Award for Excellence in Teacher Education is open to senior students at Fairmont State. You must be a teacher education major and a West Virginia resident and have a minimum 3.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

West Virginia Society Of Washington, Dc Book Scholarship
The West Virginia Society of Washington, DC Book Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen at Fairmont State. You must be a West Virginia resident or the child of a native West Virginian to be eligible for this award. more...
