Queens University of Charlotte Scholarships


Annabel Lambeth Jones Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors pursuing postsecondary studies at Queens University of Charlotte in Charlotte, North Carolina or Brevard College in Brevard, North Carolina. Selection is based on high academic merit and demonstrated leadership potential. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Athletic Scholarship - Queens University Of North Carolina
The Athletic Scholarship is open to students attending Queens University of North Carolina who are involved in athletics. Selection for this award is based on the athletic department's recommendation. more...

Community Service Grants
Scholarship for undergraduate students attending Queens College of Charlotte in the College of Arts and Sciences. Students must have been involved in various community service endeavors. An essay must be included with application packet. Recipients must participate in 100 hours of community service each semester. more...

Drama Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors enrolling at Queens University of Charlotte who plan a major or concentration in Drama. An audition is required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Scholarship for incoming freshmen pursuing studies at Queens University of Charlotte in Charlotte, North Carolina. Students must demonstrate leadership potential and have high academic merit. Lula Faye Clegg Memorial Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from the Charlotte-Mecklenburg public school system planning to attend Queens University of Charlotte in Charlotte, North Carolina majoring in Education. Students must rank in the top 10% of the class and have a strong interest in and commitment to a career in teaching. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact the colleges financial aid office or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Mecklenburg Scholars
Scholarship for Mecklenburg County, North Carolina residents who are graduating high school seniors enrolling at Queens University of Charlotte entering the College of Arts and Sciences. Students must have superior academic record, leadership, and extracurricular involvement. Renewable. more...
