Bruce Mouton Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for Lafayette or Vermilion Parish, Louisiana residents who are undergraduate (freshmen ineligible) enrolled full-time at the University of Louisiana-Lafayette Department of Chemistry majoring in Pre-Pharmacy or Nursing. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and be U.S. citizens. Applicants who are Pre-Pharmacy majors must be sophomores; Nursing majors must be juniors or seniors. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...
