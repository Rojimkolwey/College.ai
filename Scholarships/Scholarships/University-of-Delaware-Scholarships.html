University of Delaware Scholarships


B. Bradford Barnes Memorial Scholarship
Annual award for Delaware residents who are graduating high school seniors planning to attend full-time at the University of Delaware. Students must rank in the top 25% of the graduating high school class, have a minimum 1800 SAT score, and be U.S. citizens/permanent residents. Award amount is for full-tuition, fees, room, board and books. Renewable. more...

Charles L. Hebner Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for Delaware residents who are graduating high school seniors planning to attend full-time at the University of Delaware or Delaware State University majoring in the Humanities or Social Sciences. Students must rank in the top 50% of the graduating high school class, have a minimum 900 SAT score, and be U.S. citizens/permanent residents. Preference given to Political Science majors. Award amount is for full-tuition, fees, room, board, and books. Renewable. more...

Delaware Scholars Program
Scholarship for residents of Delaware who are graduating high school seniors and will be pursuing undergraduate studies in a Delaware college university or the University of Delawares Parallel Program. Applicant must have financial need, a minimum 2.5 GPA, be a U.S. citizen/legal resident, take the SAT, live with parents/guardians, and parents must also be legal residents of Delaware. Due date is in December. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. Contact high school guidance counselors or the Scholars Program by phone for more information and/or application. more...

Generation Iii Scholarship
Annual award for student who is a Delaware resident that plans to pursue a degree in the fields of architecture, engineering, business or construction. Student must attend one of the following academic institutions: University of Delaware; Delaware Technical and Community College; Delaware State University; Goldey Beacom College or Wesley College. Applicant may not be an employee of EDiS or a relative of an employee of EDiS Company. Student must have financial need and academic merit. Renewable. more...

Hagley Graduate Program
Scholarship for graduate students admitted to the University of Delaware, in the department of History, who are working towards M.A. or PhD. Student must be interested in a career as a college teacher or public historian with a focus on the history of industrialization. In addition to application, student must also submit GRE scores. Award may be renewed for 1-year for a M.A. or up to 3 years for PhD. Number of awards may vary. Contact the Department of History at U of D for application and/or more information. more...

Howard H. & Arlene M.Simon Endowed Scholarship
Scholarship for sophomores, juniors or seniors attending the University of Delaware and are enrolled in the Alfred Lerner College of Business and Economics. Selection based on academic achievement, community service and financial need. Award amount and number of awards may vary. more...
