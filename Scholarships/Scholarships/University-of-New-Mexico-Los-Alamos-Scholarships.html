University of New Mexico-Los Alamos Campus Scholarships


Agnes Finnie Grotto Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Agnes Finnie Grotto Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to non-traditional full-time students at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos who have at least a 3.0 GPA. You must be pursuing an associate's degree. You must be a New Mexico resident and a US citizen to be eligible for this award. more...

Alice H. Armstrong Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Alice H. Armstrong Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to students at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos. You must be planning to teach engineering, science and/or mathematics and have at least a 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Benito Garcia Memorial Scholarship
The Benito Garcia Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos. You must be majoring in fine arts and have at least a 3.0 GPA to qualify for this award. more...

Jim Sagel Memorial Endowment Scholarship
The Jim Sagel Memorial Endowment Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos. You must plan to earn an associate's degree in liberal arts or Southwest studies and have at least a 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Noah Rodriguez Memorial Scholarship
The Noah Rodriguez Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos. You must plan to earn an associate's degree in liberal arts and have at least a 3.0 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Unm - Los Alamos Bridge To Success Scholarship
The UNM - Los Alamos Bridge to Success Scholarship is available to full-time entering freshmen at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos. You must be a U.S. citizen, a resident of New Mexico and a graduate of a New Mexico high school to be eligible for this award. more...

Unm - Los Alamos Executive Endowment Scholarship
The UNM - Los Alamos Executive Endowed Scholarship is available to full-time entering freshmen at the University of New Mexico, Los Alamos. You must be a New Mexico resident, a US citizen and have a minimum 3.25 GPA or a minimum ACT score of 22 (or SAT equivalent) to be eligible for this award. more...
