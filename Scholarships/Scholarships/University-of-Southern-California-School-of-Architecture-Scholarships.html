University of Southern California Scholarships


Carmen Parrish Memorial Scholarship Fund
Scholarship for Northern Nevada residents who are graduating high school seniors planning to attend the University of Southern California. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Contact students guidance counselor or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Electronic Arts/William J. Byron Endowed Fund For Student Support
The Electronic Arts/William J. Byron Endowed Fund for Student Support is open to students from diverse ethnic and gender backgrounds attending the University of Southern California School of Cinematic Arts. You must be enrolled in interactive media with an emphsis in games to be eligible for this award. more...

Elizabeth Reed Annis Endowed Scholarship
The Elizabeth Reed Annis Endowed Scholarship is available to students in the University of Southern California School of Architecture. Your ancestry must be of the British Isles, regardless of your birthplace, to be eligible for this award. more...

Emery Stoops Fellowship For Graduate Studies
Annual Fellowship for Phi Delta Kappa members who are graduate students enrolled full-time at a postsecondary institution majoring in Education. Preference given to students attending the University of Southern California. An essay, personal statement, and research prospectus must be included with application packet. more...

James L. Warner Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled or planning to attend Pomona College, Whittier College, University of Southern California, Stanford University or the University of California system. Students must have financial need and be graduates of California High School, Santa Fe High School, Whittier High School, Frontier High School or Pioneer High School. Number of awards varies. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Joseph T. Desilva Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate and graduate students who are union members affiliated with the UFCW local 770 or the spouse, parent or child of a member. Students must attend the University of Southern California. Selection is based on essays, acedemic records, achievements, and oral interview. more...

Michael R. Harpster, Crna, Ms Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students who are American Association of Nurse Anesthetists (AANA) members currently attending the UNIVERSITY OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA (USC) in a Nurse Anesthesia Program. Applicants must demonstrate financial need. Selection is based on academic achievement and school and community involvement. In addition to application, students must submit a $25 application fee ($10 for each additional application when applying for more than one scholarship), college transcript from nurse anesthesia program, and proof of AANA membership. Contact the Foundation for more information and/or application; downloadable application available on website. more...

Usc Post Production Award
Scholarship for graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Southern California in a Masters of Fine Arts program majoring in Film/Video. Students must demonstrate academic ability, financial need, and professional potential. A personal statement must be included with application packet. Contact the colleges financial aid office for more information and/or application. more...

Verle L. Annis Memorial Scholarship
The Verle L. Annis Memorial Scholarship is open to students at the University of Southern California School of Architecture. You must be participating in a travel program such as the School's Saintes fall program or Milan/ Como spring program to qualify for this award. more...

Victor Regnier Endowed Scholarship
The Victor Regnier Endowed Scholarship is open to undergraduate or graduate students at the University of Southern California School of Architecture. more...

Widom, Wein, Cohen, O'leary, Terasawa Endowed Scholarship
The Widom, Wein, Cohen, O'Leary, Terasawa Endowed Scholarship is open to undergraduate or graduate students at the University of Southern California School of Architecture. You must demonstrate financial need and academic achievement, and participate in community service to qualify for this award. more...

William Landworth Memorial Prize
The William Landworth Memorial Prize is available to students in the School of Architecture at the University of Southern California. You must demonstrate exceptional design skills and financial need to be eligible for this award. more...
