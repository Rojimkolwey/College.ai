Iowa Lakes Community College Scholarship Awards
Iowa Lakes scholarships are awarded on an annual basis by an online scholarship process. Students qualifications are matched to available scholarships based on students answers to appropriate questions. In 2011-2012 Iowa Lakes awarded $880,305; the average scholarship award was $1,901 with a range of $500-6,500; 78.6% of students with completed applications received at least one scholarship. more...
