Milwaukee School of Engineering Scholarships


Jason Incorporated Scholarship
Scholarship for Redgranite, Wisconsin, or Richmond, Indiana, or Greater Dayton, Ohio area residents who are college sophomore or juniors enrolled full-time at the Milwaukee School of Engineering majoring in Mechanical, Electrical, or Industrial Engineering. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and financial need. Applicants must participate in summer internships at Milsco Manufacturing, Redgranite, Wisconsin or Osborn International, North American, Richmond, Indiana. An interview is required. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Modine Manufacturing Company Scholarship (A)
Scholarship for female college sophomores or juniors enrolled full-time at the Milwaukee School of Engineering majoring in Mechanical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering Technology. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, reside within the Midwest region of the US, and consider full-time employment with the Modine Manufacturing Company. An interview is required. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Modine Manufacturing Company Scholarship (B)
Scholarship for minority (African American, Asian American, Hispanic American, or Native American) college sophomores or juniors enrolled full-time at the Milwaukee School of Engineering majoring in Mechanical Engineering or Mechanical Engineering Technology. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, reside within the Midwest region of the US, and consider full-time employment with the Modine Manufacturing Company. An interview is required. Number of awards may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Novum Structures Llc Scholarship
Scholarship for college sophomore enrolled full-time at the Milwaukee School of Engineering majoring in Architectural Engineering with a specialty in building structural system design. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and participate in a paid summer internship at Novum Structures and willing to consider full-time employment. Number of awards may vary. An interview is required. more...
