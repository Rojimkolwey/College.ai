Siena College Scholarships


Army Reserve Officer Training Corps Scholarship
The Army Reserve Officer Training Corps Scholarship is available to students at Siena College. You must plan on seeking military service as an Army Officer to qualify for this award. more...

Dollars For Scholars Scholarship - Siena College
The Dollars for Scholars Scholarship is available to students at Siena College. You must demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Siena College will match these awards up to a maximum of $1,000 per year. more...

Empire State Association Of Two-Year College Biologists Scholarship
The Empire State Association of Two-Year College Biologists Scholarship is available to transfer students at Siena College. You must majoring in the biological sciences to qualify for this award. more...

Father Mychal Judge O.F.M. Scholarship
The Father Mychal Judge O.F.M. Scholarship is available to students at Siena College. You must be the child of a firefighter who gave his/her life trying to save others in the World Trade Center tragedy on September 11, 2001. more...

Franciscan Scholarship - Siena College
The Franciscan Scholarship is open to full-time students at Siena College. You must have a minimum 86 average, a minimum SAT/ACT score of 1130/25 and demonstrate Franciscan values and involvement in school and community activities to qualify for this award. more...

James M. Brett Scholarship
The James M. Brett Scholarship is open to male students at Siena College. You must be of Irish decent, planning a career in law and demonstrate academic ability and financial need to qualify for this award. more...

M. Dean Potts Memorial Scholarship
The M. Dean Potts Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Siena College. You must be a finance major to qualify for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Siena College
The Presidential Scholarship is open to entering full-time freshmen at Siena College. You must have complete at least 20 academic units with a minimum 90 academic average, score a minimum 1150 SAT / 25 ACT score, and receive an admissions committee nomination to be eligible for this award. more...

Vassallo Family Siena College Scholarship
The Vassallo Family Siena College Scholarship is available to full-time business majors at Siena College. You must be a resident of Bergen County, a graduate of a Catholic high school and of Italian-American heritage to qualify for this award. Participation in extracurricular activities and community service is also a consideration. more...

William And Delia O'donnell Harvey Memorial Scholarship
The William and Delia O'Donnell Harvey Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Siena College. You must be pursuing a career in business or science and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this award. Preference will be given to students with a special interest in a computer-related field. more...

William H. And Grace Cronin Gibbs Memorial Scholarship
The William H. and Grace Cronin Gibbs Memorial Scholarship is available to students at Siena College. You must be a history major and be in good academic standing to qualify for this award. more...

William P. Murray '48 Scholarship
The William P. Murray '48 Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Siena College. You must demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Preference is given to Catholic students who are graduates of Christian Brothers Academy. more...
