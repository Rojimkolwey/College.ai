The University of Tampa Scholarships


Art Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at University of Tampa majoring in Art, Art Therapy, Computer Graphics, or Graphic Design. Students must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents and demonstrate artistic talent. A portfolio is required. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Florida Bright Futures Programs Scholarship
Scholarship for Florida residents who are graduating high school seniors or GED recipients planning to attend part- or full-time at the University of Tampa. Students must demonstrate academic achievement. Due date and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Florida League For Nursing Research Grant
Scholarship for National League for Nursing and/or Florida League for Nursing members who are graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa majoring in Nursing. Students must be registered nurses planning to conduct research. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Proof of membership must be included with application packet. more...

Florida League For Nursing Scholarship
Scholarship for Florida residents who are graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa. Students must be members of the Florida League for Nursing, Florida residents for a minimum of one year, and have a minimum 3.0 GPA or B grade. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. An essay must be included with application packet. more...

Florida Resident Access Grant (Frag)
Scholarship for Florida residents who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa. Students must be Florida residents for a minimum of one year. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

Florida Student Assistance Grant (Fsag)
Scholarship for Florida residents who are undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa. Students must be Florida residents for a minimum of one year and have financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. A copy of the FAFSA must be included with application packet. more...

Government & World Affairs Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at University of Tampa majoring in Government, World Affairs, International Studies, Urban Studies, Law and Justice, or Pre-Law. Students must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents, have a minimum 3.0 GPA, demonstrate volunteer and community service. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Henderson-Donaghy Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled part- or full-time at the University of Tampa majoring in Nursing. Students must demonstrate a commitment to the values of nursing and to health care policy. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

International Baccalaureate Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend full-time at the University of Tampa. Students must have a minimum of two passes on advanced level IB exams and IB diploma. Number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

Life Science Scholarships
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at University of Tampa majoring in Biology, Biochemistry, Chemistry, Environmental Science, Marine Science (Biology or Chemistry), or Mathematics. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA, and/or a minimum 1200 SAT score or a minimum 27 ACT score, are U.S. citizens/permanent residents, and demonstrate potential and academic achievement. Number of awards may vary. more...

Mary M. Mckay Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa majoring in Nursing. Students must demonstrate academic achievement and commitment to nursing science. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Music Scholarships: Vocal, Keyboard & Instrumental
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at University of Tampa majoring in Music. Students must be U.S. citizens/permanent residents and demonstrate musical talent. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An audition is required. Renewable. more...

Part Of A Literary Community Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at University of Tampa majoring in English or Writing. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, and/or a minimum 1180 SAT score or a minimum 26 ACT score, are U.S. citizens/permanent residents, and demonstrate potential and academic achievement. Number of awards and award amount may vary. A personal statement and writing samples must be included with application packet. more...

Performing Arts Scholarships (Musical Theater)
Scholarship for incoming freshmen enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa majoring in Performing Arts (Musical Theater). Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, be U.S. citizens/permanent residents, demonstrate talent in stage movement, acting, singing, and experience in high school/community theater productions. An audition is required. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Samuel & Julia Flom Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled part- or full-time at the University of Tampa majoring in Nursing. Students must be registered nurses seeking to further their education. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Sigma Theta Tau Scholarship
Scholarship for graduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa majoring in Nursing. Students must have a minimum 3.5 GPA. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

Tampa Writing Scholars Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the University of Tampa Department of English and Writing. Students must demonstrate outstanding talent in the language arts, potential for English or writing, and be selected at a national competition. Due date and award amount may vary. more...
