University of Wisconsin-Parkside Scholarships


Albert R. And Herta A. Karwatka Memorial Scholarship
The Albert R. and Herta A. Karwatka Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time continuing students majoring in mathematics, biological science, chemistry, geology or physics at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must have a minimum 3.25 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Anna Maria Williams Endowed Scholarship
The Anna Maria Williams Endowed Scholarship is available to full - time entering freshmen at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must be majoring in pre - medicine, have a minimum GPA of 3.7 and an ACT score of 27 to be eligible for this award. In addition, you must be planning to complete four years at UW-Parkside and then seek professional degrees in medicine, osteopathic, podiatric or veterinary medicine, optometry or dentistry. more...

Art And Graphic Design Scholarship - Uw, Parkside
The Art and Graphic Design Scholarship is available to students majoring in art at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. more...

Constance M. Ferwerda Endowment For The Humanities Award
The Constance M. Ferwerda Endowment for the Humanities Award is open to students at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must be majoring in the humanities, social sciences or cultural sciences to be eligible for this award. more...

Frank And Corinne Petretti Endowed Scholarship
The Frank and Corinne Petretti Endowed Scholarship is available to continuing students enrolled in the School of Business and Technology at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must be a graduate of a public or private high school in Kenosha and have a minimum GPA of 3.0 to be eligible for this award. more...

Ralph L. And Frances A. Jaeschke Arts And Sciences Endowed Scholarship
The Ralph L. and Frances A. Jaeschke Arts and Sciences Endowed Scholarship is available to full-time students majoring in the arts or sciences at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must have a minimum 3.3 GPA, demonstrate financial need, and participate in extracurricular activities to be eligible for this renewable award. more...

Ralph L. Jaeschke Memorial Endowed Mathematics, Physics And Computer Science Scholarship
The Ralph L. Jaeschke Memorial Endowed Mathematics, Physics and Computer Science Scholarship is available to full-time students majoring in Mathematics, Physics or Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate financial need and participate in extracurricular activities to be eligible for this award. more...

William And Anna Lipman Endowed Scholarship
The William and Anna Lipman Endowed Scholarship is available to full-time entering non-traditional Jewish students at the University of Wisconsin, Parkside. You must be a resident of Kenosha County and demonstrate financial need to be eligible for this renewable award. more...
