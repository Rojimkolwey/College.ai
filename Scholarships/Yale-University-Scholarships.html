Janet Cain Sielaff Alumni Award
The Janet Cain Sielaff Alumni Award is open to graduating students at Yale School of Architecture. You must have contributed to, and fostered, school spirit to qualify for this award. more...

Moulton Andrus Award
The Moulton Andrus Award is open to graduating students at Yale School of Architecture. You must have achieved excellence in art and architecture to qualify for this award. more...

Sarah Pettit Doctoral Fellowship
The Sarah Pettit Doctoral Fellowship is open to doctoral students writing a dissertation in LGBT studies, with lesbian studies particularly encouraged. This fellowship is open to doctoral fellows coming from universities outside of Yale. Residence in New Haven and participation in LGBTS is required during the tenure of the fellowship. You must submit a project description and three letters of recommendation. more...

Wendy Elizabeth Blanning Prize
The Wendy Elizabeth Blanning Prize is open to second-year students at Yale School of Architecture. You must have shown the most promise of development in the profession to qualify for this award. more...
