Williams Baptist University Scholarships


Academic Excellence Transfer Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Academic Excellence Transfer Scholarship is available to transfer students at Williams Baptist College. To be eligible for this award, you must have completed 24 hours of college credit with a minimum GPA of 3.0. Award amount depends on applicants' status as a commuter or resident student. more...

Acteen Service Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Acteen Service Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Williams Baptist College. You must have received the Acteens Service Award to qualify for this award. more...

Art Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Art Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen who are art majors at Williams Baptist College. You must be recommended by the chair of the art department after your portfolio has been reviewed to be eligible for this award. more...

Athletic Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Athletic Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen who will be student athletes at Williams Baptist College. more...

Church Matching Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Church Matching Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen at Williams Baptist College who are active in their church. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Dean's Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen accepted at Williams Baptist College. You must have an ACT score between 22 and 23 and have a minimum high school GPA of 2.75, or be in the top 10 percent of your high school class to be considered for this award. This scholarship is renewable for four years. more...

Founder's Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Founder's Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Williams Baptist College. You must have an ACT score of at least 28 and a minimum GPA of 3.25 to be considered for this award. more...

Heritage Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Heritage Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Williams Baptist College. You must have an ACT score of at least 28 and a minimum GPA of 3.25 to be considered for this award. more...

Institutional Grant - Williams Baptist College
The Institutional Grant is available to transfer students at Williams Baptist College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and 24 hours of college credit to qualify for this award. The amount is contingent upon whether you live in college housing or are a commuter student. more...

Leadership Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Leadership Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Williams Bible College. You must have an ACT score between 19 and 22 and have a minimum high school GPA of 2.25 to be considered for this award. You must also demonstrate leadership skills. This scholarship is renewable for four years. more...

Music Talentship Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Music Talentship Scholarship is available to incoming freshmen who are music majors at Williams Baptist College. You must be recommended by the chair of the music department to be eligible for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is available to full-time transfer students at Williams Baptist College. To be eligible for this award, you must be a member of Phi Theta Kappa and have completed 24 hours of transferable college credit with a minimum GPA of 3.5. more...

President's Elite Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The President's Elite Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Williams Baptist College. You must have a minimum ACT score of 28 and a minimum 3.25 high school GPA or be the valedictorian of your high school graduating class to be considered for this award. more...

President's Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The President's Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Williams Baptist College. You must have an ACT score between 24 and 25, and have a minimum 3.0 high school GPA or be the salutatorian of your high school graduating class to be considered for this award. This scholarship is renewable for four years. more...

President's Select Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The President's Select Scholarship is open to entering freshmen at Williams Baptist College. You must have an ACT score between 26 and 27 and minimum 3.25 high school GPA or be the valedictorian of your high school graduating class to be considered for this award. more...

Summit Scholarship - Williams Baptist College
The Summit Scholarship is available to transfer students at Williams Baptist College. You must have completed 45 hours of transferable college credit with a minimum GPA of 3.0. You must also be transferring from Arkansas Northeastern College, Arkansas State University-Newport, Black River Technical College, Crowley's Ridge College, East Arkansas Community College, Ozarka College or University of Arkansas Community College in Batesville to be eligible for this award. more...
