School of Visual Arts Scholarships


Chairmans Merit Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the School of Visual Arts. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate creative and academic achievement. Due date is February 1st for incoming freshmen and March 1st for transfer students. Number of awards and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Matching Outside Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate or graduate students enrolled part- or full-time at the School of Visual Arts. The School will match 25% of any outside scholarship. Due date and number of awards may vary. more...

National Scholastic Art Award Scholarships
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors planning to attend full-time at the School of Visual Arts. Students must be the winners in the national competiton of art and design or photography. Due date and award amount may vary. Renewable. more...

Ohio Governors Youth Art Exhibition Award
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the School of Visual Arts. Students must be finalists selected by the Ohio Governors Youth Art Exhibition. Due date and number of awards may vary. Renewable. more...

School Of Visual Arts Grant
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the School of Visual Arts. Students must have financial need. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

School Of Visual Arts Scholarships
Annual award for undergraduate students enrolled at the School of Visual Arts. A FAFSA and a statement of purpose must be included with application packet. Renewable. Contact the Art Academys admissions office or Scholastic for more information and/or application. more...

Silas H. Rhodes Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at the School of Visual Arts. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and demonstrate creative and academic achievement. Due date is February 1st for incoming freshmen and March 1st for transfer students. Number of awards and award amount may vary. A slide portfolio must be included with application packet. more...
