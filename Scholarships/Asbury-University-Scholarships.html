Asbury University Scholarships


Alumni Recognition Scholarship
The Alumni Recognition Scholarship is available to new students at Asbury College. Your parents must be an alumnus of Asbury University to be eligible for this award. more...

Business Scholars Program Scholarship
The Business Scholars Program Scholarship is available to new students is designed to support Christian men and women interested in advancing the cause of Christ in the fields of business management and accounting at Asbury College. You must have a minimum test score of 24 ACT/1110 SAT, and a cumulative high school GPA of 3.0 or higher to be eligible for this award. more...

Christian Ministry Scholarship
The Christian Ministry Scholarship is available to traditional undergraduate students at Asbury College. You must have at least one parent working in paid, full-time Christian Ministry to be eligible for this award. more...

Emerging Leader-Scholar Program
The Emerging Leader-Scholar Program is available to incoming students at Asbury University. You must be a U.S. citizen, have a minimum GPA of 3.0, and a minimum 22 ACT / 1020 SAT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Howard Dayton School Of Business Endowed Scholarship
The Howard Dayton School of Business Endowed Scholarship is available to Christian students at Asbury College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0, minimum test score of 24 ACT/1110 SAT and majoring in accounting, business management, marketing, and sports management to be eligible for this award. more...

John Wesley Hughes Scholarship
The John Wesley Hughes Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Asbury College. Your selection is based on your academic records, test scores, rank in class, extracurricular activities, leadership ability, letters of recommendation, an essay and an interview. You must also qualify as a President's Scholar on Level III, National Merit/Francis Asbury Scholar, or a Kentucky Governor's Scholar on Level IV to be considered. This award covers 100 percent of tuition and is renewable for up to four years with a maintained 3.6 GPA. You must be invited to compete for this award. more...

Kentucky Governor's Scholarships
The Kentucky Governor's Scholarships is available to graduates from Kentucky high schools and who attended either the Governor's Scholars program or the Governor's School for the Arts program at Asbury College. This award is renewed for up to four years or eight semesters provided the recipient maintains a cumulative GPA of 3.30 at Asbury University. more...

Out Of State Scholarship
The Out of State Scholarship is available to traditional undergraduate students who reside within the United States, but outside the Commonwealth of Kentucky at Asbury College. This award is renewed for up to four years or eight semesters provided the recipient maintains a cumulative GPA of 3.30 at Asbury University. more...

Rogers Scholars Award
The Rogers Scholars Award is available to students who successfully completed the Rogers Scholars program at Asbury College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 or better, and an ACT of 24 or above to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable for up to four years or eight semesters provided the recipient maintains a cumulative GPA of 3.10 at Asbury University. more...

The Salvation Army Scholarship
The Salvation Army Scholarship is available to new, incoming students in the traditional, residential undergraduate program at Asbury College. You must be actively involved in The Salvation Army and submit a letter of recommendation from the Corps Officer regarding his/her participation in the Army to be eligible for this award. more...
