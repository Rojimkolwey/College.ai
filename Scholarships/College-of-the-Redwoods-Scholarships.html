College of the Redwoods Scholarships


Catherine Briggs Memorial Scholarship
The Catherine Briggs Memorial Scholarship is available to continuing students at the College of the Redwoods. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5, be enrolled in the LVN program, and be enrolled in a minimum of 10 credits to be eligible for this award. more...

Dennis Gabbert Memorial Scholarship
The Dennis Gabbert Memorial Scholarship is available to students at the College of the Redwoods. You must be majoring in music with an emphasis on piano or voice, have a minimum GPA of 2.0, and be enrolled in at least 12 credits to be eligible for this award. You must also be a Humboldt County high school graduate with preference given to graduates of Eureka High School. more...

Duane Allen Jr. Memorial Scholarship
The Duane Allen Jr. Memorial Scholarship is available to continuing students at College of the Redwoods. You must be from Del Norte, Humboldt, or Siskiyou County and have a minimum GPA of 2.0 to be eligible for this award. Preference is given to Native American students. more...

Gerald D. & Jane B. Matson Scholarship - Nursing
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Humboldt State University or College of the Redwoods majoring in Nursing. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, financial need, and be Humboldt County, California high school graduates OR long-time residents of Humboldt County. A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...

James & Rebecca Jensen-Loleta I.O.O.F. Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled at an accredited four-year postsecondary institution. Students must be Fortuna Union or Ferndale High Schools graduates or residents of either school district for a minimum of three years and have completed general education requirements at the College of the Redwoods. A personal statement must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

A personal statement must be included with application packet. more...
