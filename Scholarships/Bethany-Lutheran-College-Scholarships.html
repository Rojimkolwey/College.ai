Applied Music (Private Lesson) Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Bethany Lutheran College. Students must be interested in piano, organ, voice, or instrumental lessons. An audition is required. Number of awards and award amount may vary. An essay and a list of repertoire must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...
