Saint Joseph's College of Maine Scholarships


Distinguished Honors Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Applicants must have high academic and leadership potential as evidenced by a minimum SAT score of 1150 or higher and ACT score of 25 with a high school GPA of 3.5. Award amount is up to full tuition annually. Student must participate in Distinguished Honors Day in November. Number of awards and award amount varies. Renewable. more...

Eva Albee Kelley Scholarship
Scholarship for female undergraduate students from Lincoln County, Maine who have been accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Applicants must have demonstrated financial need. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Fortitudo Et Spes Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Applicants must have overcome adversity and been successful in school and community. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Maine Parish Scholarship
Scholarship for residents of Maine Catholic parishes who are incoming freshmen accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and a 1000 or higher SAT score or 21 ACT. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Mcauley Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and a 1550 or higher SAT score (1030 or higher SAT score taken prior to March, 2005) or 22 ACT. Candidate must have earned an exceptional record of community service and leadership. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Mercy Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen and transfer students accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. First-year students should have a successful record of accomplishment in college-prep courses, and community & extracurricular involvement. Consideration given to transfer students that have a minimum 2.5 GPA with 15 or more transferable credits. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Saint Joseph Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen and transfer students accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Applicants must have a minimum 3.0 GPA and competitive SAT I or ACT scores. Consideration given to transfer students with a minimum 3.5 GPA and with 15 or more transferable credits. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Sarah T. Burke Scholarship
Scholarship for incoming freshmen who are parishioners of St. Patricks in Lewiston, Maine and have been accepted at Saint Josephs College of Maine. Submit letter from parish priest regarding current parish involvement with application packet. Due date and number of awards varies. Renewable. more...
