Concord University Scholarships


Academic Excellence Scholarship - Concord University
The Academic Excellence Scholarship is available to students entering Concord University. You must have at least a 3.0 cumulative GPA and a minimum 25 ACT or 1140 SAT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Dean's Scholarship - Concord University
The Dean's Scholarship is available to students entering Concord University. You must have at least a 28 ACT or 1240 SAT score and a minimum 3.75 cumulative GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Director's Scholarship - Concord University
The Directors Scholarship is available to students entering Concord University. You must have at least a 3.0 GPA and an ACT score between 18 and 20 or an SAT score between 870 and 950 to be eligible for this award. more...

Entrepreneurial Scholarship - Concord University
The Entrepreneurial Scholarship is available to students at Concord University. You must be interested in owning your own business to be eligible for this award. more...

Governor's Honors Academy Scholarship - Concord University
The Governor's Honors Academy Scholarship is available to students at Concord University. You must have attended your state's Governor's Honors Academy to be eligible for this award. more...

Hugh O'brien Scholarship
The Hugh O'Brien Scholarship is available to students entering Concord University. You must have attended the Hugh O'Brien Youth Foundation camp to be eligible for this award. more...

Marsh Scholarship - Concord University
The Marsh Scholarship is available to students entering Concord University. You must have at least a 28 ACT or 1240 SAT score and a minimum 3.75 cumulative GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Mid-Atlantic Scholarship - Concord University
The Mid-Atlantic Scholarship is available to out-of-state students entering Concord University. You must have at least a 3.0 GPA and a minimum 21 ACT or 960 SAT score to be eligible for this award. more...

Presidential Scholarship - Concord University
The Presidential Scholarship is available to students entering Concord University. You must have at least a 28 ACT or 1240 SAT score and a minimum 3.75 cumulative GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Transfer Scholarship - Concord University
The Transfer Scholarship is available to students transferring into Concord University. You must have at least a 3.0 transferable GPA to be eligible for this award. more...
