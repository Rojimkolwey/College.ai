E.L. Howard Memorial Hospital Auxilary Scholarship
The E.L. Howard Memorial Hospital Auxilary Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must be majoring in nursing and have a minimum GPA of 2.0 to qualify for this award. Preference is given to Harrison County residents. more...

Fred N. Lee Memorial Scholarship
The Fred N. Lee Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and be a member of the Wildcat Basketball Team to qualify for this award. more...

Jacobs Facility Operating Service Scholarship
The Jacobs Facility Operating Service Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

James Horris And Minnie Rae Pigott Scholarship
The James Horris and Minnie Rae Pigott Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must be a graduate of Picayune Memorial High School and have a minimum GPA of 2.5 to qualify for this award. more...

Lynn Cook Hartwig Scholarship
The Lynn Cook Hartwig Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must be majoring in a health related field, have a minimum ACT score of 16 and have a minimum GPA of 2.5. You must also be a U.S. citizen and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Preference is given to employees or children of employees of Hattiesburg Clinic. more...

Marjorie Lenora White Scholarship
The Marjorie Lenora White Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must be majoring in education, have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Mississippi Association Of Supervisors Scholarship
The Mississippi Association of Supervisors Scholarship is available to entering full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must be a resident of Hancock or Lamar County, have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. more...

Troy Stockstill Memorial Scholarship
The Troy Stockstill Memorial Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must be a graduate of Pearl River Central High School, have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and demonstrate financial need to qualify for this award. Preference is given to students participating in extracurricular activities. more...

Van And Mary Lowry Scholarship
The Van and Mary Lowry Scholarship is available to full-time students at Pearl River Community College. You must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 if you are enrolled in a vocational program (2.5 for an academic program) and be a registered voter or the child of a registered voter of Beat 2, Marion County to qualify for this award. more...
