Washington University in St Louis Scholarships


Balanced Man Scholarship
The Balanced Man Scholarship is available to incoming male students at Washington University in St. Louis. You must demonstrate academic excellence, leadership skills, and a commitment to your health and well - being to be considered for this award. more...

Community College Merit Scholarship - Washington University
The Community College Merit Scholarship is open to undergraduate students at Washington University in St. Louis who are enrolled in University College. You must be a St. Louis-area community college graduate with an associates degree. You must also have a minimum GPA of 3.7 in a transferable area of interest and must apply within one year after your graduation to qualify for this award. more...

Completion Scholarship
The Completion Scholarship is available to current or returning undergraduate students at Washington University in St. Louis who are enrolled in University College. You must have exhausted all financial aid and have less than 12 credits left to graduate to be eligible for this award. more...

Dean's Fellowship - Washington University
The Dean's Fellowship is open to undergraduate and graduate students at Washington University who are enrolled in University College. You must have achieved a minimum GPA of 3.7 to qualify for this award. more...

George E. Kassabaum Scholarship
The George E. Kassabaum Scholarship is open to graduate students enrolled in the School of Architecture at Washington University in St. Louis. more...

Gertrude H. Hast Scholarship
The Gertrude H. Hast Scholarship is available to students admitted into the Post-Baccalaureate Premedical Program at Washington University in St. Louis who are enrolled in University College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 and Indiana resident to be eligible for this award. more...

James L. Sweatt Scholarship
The James L. Sweatt Scholarship is available to students admitted into the Post-Baccalaureate Premedical Program at Washington University in St. Louis who are enrolled in University College. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.5, provide two letters of recommendation, and submit an essay (addressing why diversity in the healthcare workforce matters) to be eligible for this award. more...

Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship - Washington University
The Phi Theta Kappa Scholarship is open to transfer, undergraduate students at Washington University in St. Louis who are enrolled in University College. You must be a transfer of a St. Louis- area community college, have a minimum GPA of 3.5, and be a member of Phi Theta Kappa to qualify for this award. more...

Ralph P. Ranft Scholarship
The Ralph P. Ranft Scholarship is open to returning graduate students enrolled in the School of Architecture at Washington University in St. Louis. To qualify for this award, you must have completed the 400 level of the architectural design sequence at Washington University or received placement as a new student at the 500 level or above. more...

Roland W. Bockhorst Scholarship
The Roland W. Bockhorst Scholarship is open to returning graduate students enrolled in the School of Architecture at Washington University in St. Louis. more...

School Full-Tuition Award - Washington University In St. Louis
The School Full-Tuition Award is open to incoming graduate students enrolled in the School of Architecture at Washington University in St. Louis. Selection is based on academic record, strength of portfolio, and professional promise. more...

University College Graduate Scholarships - Washington University
The University College Graduate Scholarship is open to graduate students at Washington University in St. Louis who are enrolled in University College. You must demonstrate financial need and promise of academic success to qualify for this award. more...

William B.K. And Helen Watkins Memorial Scholarship
The William B.K. and Helen Watkins Memorial Scholarship is open to returning graduate students enrolled in the School of Architecture at Washington University in St. Louis. more...
