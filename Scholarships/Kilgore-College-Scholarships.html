Kilgore College Scholarships


Addie Mae Lloyd Scholarship Award
Annual award for college freshmen enrolled full-time at Kilgore College Biology Department majoring in Life Sciences or a Health realted field. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA in biology courses, completed a minimum of 12 credit hours but no more than 30, and completed one biology course at Kilgore College, and are enrolled in a second course. Nonrenewable. more...

Bob Young Go Texan Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Kilgore College Agricultural and Environmental Science Department. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and have completed one year at Kilgore College (24 credit hours). Award amount may vary. more...

Charles E. & Addie Mae Lloyd Scholarship Award
Annual award for college sophomores enrolled full-time at Kilgore College Biology Department majoring in Biology or Biology or Health realted field. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, completed a minimum of two biology courses at Kilgore College, and are enrolled in a third course. more...

Cheerleader Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Kilgore College. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA with a minimum of 12 credit hours or be graduating high school seniors. Applicants will be required to try-out. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Choral-Vocal Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Kilgore College. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA, completed a minimum of 12 credit hours, participate in at least one ensemble, demonstrate musical accomplishments, and be enrolled in a minimum of two non-music academic classes. An audition is required. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. more...

Communications Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Kilgore College. Students who are graduating high school seniors must have a minimum 21 ACT score or a minimum 900 SAT score, and have a 90 average; current Kilgore College students must have a minimum 2.75 GPA on at least 12 credit hours, be active members of The Flare or Ranger staff, and have a minimum 3.25 GPA in Communications courses. Number of awards and award amount may vary. more...

Counselors Scholarship

Dance Scholarships
Scholarship for undergraduate students enrolled full-time at Kilgore College. Students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA and have a minimum of 12 credit hours. An audition is required. Deadlines and award amounts may vary. Renewable. more...

Dr. & Mrs. J.E. Watkins Scholarship Award
Annual award for Gregg, Panola, Rusk, or Smith County, Texas residents who are college freshmen or sophomores enrolled full-time at Kilgore College Biology Department majoring in Pre-Med, Biology, Health Sciences or closely realted fields. Students must have a minimum 3.0 GPA, enroll in biology and chemistry courses, and plan to transfer to a four-year institution. Applicants who are graduating high school seniors must rank in the top 25% of the graduating class. Nonrenewable. more...
