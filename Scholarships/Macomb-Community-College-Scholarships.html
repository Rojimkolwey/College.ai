Today, a second generation of family members shares this tradition of quality, service and value for its customers in both Clinton and Shelby Township. To honor their commitment to the community, the Vitale family has pledged to support the students at the Macomb Culinary Institute at Macomb Community College through the establishment of this Culinary Scholarship.
