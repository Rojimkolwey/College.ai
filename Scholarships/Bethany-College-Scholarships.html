Bethany College Scholarships


Lutheran Leaders Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time Bethany College (Kansas) undergraduate students who are members of the Lutheran Church and have demonstrated leadership and service to their high school, church or community. Students must continue to be involved in leadership and service in the Bethany College community. High School students must have a 3.0 GPA and a minimum ACT score of 21 or rank in the top 50% of their graduating class. Number of awards varies. Renewable. more...

Partners In Scholarship
Scholarship for undergraduate students (freshmen ineligible) attending Bethany College (Kansas) who are working on a research project. Selection based on research topic. Due date and number of awards varies. more...

Performance Awards (Art, Music,Theater)
Scholarship for incoming, full-time freshmen acceptd to Bethany College (Kansas) who have talent in Art, Music, Theatre or Athletics. Applicants must have a 2.5 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 19 or SAT score of 910 OR rank in the top 50% of their graduating high school class. Students must be recommended by Bethany faculty or coaches based on students ability, talent and/or portfolio. Award amount varies upon recommendation of faculty or coaches. Renewable. more...

Performance Awards (Athletics)
Scholarship for incoming, full-time freshmen accepted to Bethany College (Kansas) who will be participate in the schools athletic program. Applicants must have a 2.5 GPA, a minimum ACT score of 19 or SAT score of 910 OR rank in the top 50% of their graduating high school class. Students must be recommended by Bethany faculty or coaches based on students ability, talent and/or portfolio. Award amount varies upon recommendation of faculty or coaches. Renewable. more...
