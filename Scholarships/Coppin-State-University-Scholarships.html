Coppin State University Scholarships


Blue Freshmen Merit Award - Coppin State University
The Blue Freshmen Merit Award is awarded to first-time, full-time freshmen at Coppin State University. To qualify for this award, you must have a minimum high school GPA of 2.5 and a minimum combined SAT score of 900, and your CSU placement exam must indicate that no more than one refresher course is required. This award is renewable for up to eight semesters. more...

Blue Transfer Merit Award - Coppin State University
The Blue Transfer Merit Award is awarded to transfer students at Coppin State University. To qualify for this award, you must transfer from a community college with a minimum 2.5 GPA and you must have successfully completed at least 25 credits. This award is renewable for up to six semesters. more...

Eagle Scholarship - Coppin State University
The Eagle Scholarship is available to first - semester, first - year students at Coppin State University. You must have a minimum GPA of 3.0 in high school and an SAT score of at least 1590 to qualify for this award. more...

Gold Freshmen Merit Award - Coppin State University
The Gold Freshmen Merit Award is awarded to first-time, full-time freshmen at Coppin State University . To qualify for this award, you must have a minimum high school GPA of 2.5 and a minimum combined SAT score of 950, and your CSU placement exam must indicate that no more than one refresher course is required. This award is renewable for up to eight semesters. more...

Gold Transfer Merit Award - Coppin State University
The Gold Transfer Merit Award is awarded to transfer students at Coppin State University. To qualify for this award, you must transfer from a community college with a minimum 2.5 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 950 to be eligible for this award. This award is renewable for up to four semesters. more...

Golden Eagle Scholarship - Coppin State University
The Golden Eagle Scholarship is open to incoming freshmen students at Coppin State University. You must have at least a 3.2 GPA and a minimum SAT score of 1820 to be eligible for this award. more...

Maxie Collier Scholarship
The Maxie Collier Scholarship is open to nursing, pre-med, psychology and social work majors at Coppin State University. You must be a freshman or sophomore with a minimum GPA of 3.0. You must also pursue a career in mental health in the State of Maryland Mental Health System and be willing to commit to the undergraduate/graduate levels of training to qualify for this award. more...

Upper Division Scholarship - Coppin State University
The Upper Division Scholarship is available to full - time upper division students at Coppin State University. You must have between 45 to 60 credit hours, have a minimum GPA of 3.5, and have completed English Composition I and II to qualify for this award. more...
