Marymount Manhattan College Scholarships


21St Century Scholarship - Marymount Manhattan College
The 21st Century Scholarship is available to full- time degree- seeking freshmen and transfer students at Marymount Manhattan College. You must be a non- US citizen or permanent resident and demonstrate academic achievement with a minimum 2.5 GPA to be eligible for this award. more...

Community College Honor Society Scholarship - Marymount Manhattan College
The Community College Honor Society Scholarship is available to full- time, degree- seeking students entering Marymount Manhattan College. You must be transferring from a community college and be a member of Phi Theta Kappa, Alpha Beta Gamma, or another recognized community college honor society to be eligible for this award. more...

Diplomatic Scholarship - Mmc
The Diplomatic Scholarship is available to full- time, degree- seeking freshmen and transfer students at Marymount Manhattan College. You must be a member, or the spouse or child of a member, of a foreign mission or consulate, or the United Nations to be eligible for this award. You must also have an academic average of at least a B and TOEFL score of at least 550. more...

Margaret Mary Connolly Memorial Scholarship
The Margaret Mary Connolly Memorial Scholarship is available to female entering freshmen majoring in education at Marymount Manhattan College. You must have graduated from a Catholic high school, excel academically, and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Mohony Prowse Scholarship
The Mohony Prowse Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Marymount Manhattan College. You must be a graduate of a New York City Catholic High School and demonstrate academic ability and financial need to be considered for this award. more...

Sister Dymphna Leonard Scholarship
The Sister Dymphna Leonard Scholarship is available to entering freshmen at Marymount Manhattan College. You must demonstrate financial need and require MMC's small supportive community to thrive academically to be considered for this award. You must also submit two teacher recommendations. more...

Sister Edmund Harvey Scholarship
The Sister Edmund Harvey Scholarship is available to entering freshmen and transfer students at Marymount Manhattan College. You must be the child of an alumni to be eligible for this award. Freshmen must have a minimum 3.0 GPA; transfer students must have a minimum 2.5 GPA. more...

Sister Regina Shelly Scholarship
The Sister Regina Shelly Scholarship is available to female entering freshmen and transfer students at Marymount Manhattan College. You must have graduated from a Catholic high school in Brooklyn, New York and demonstrate financial need to be considered for this award. more...
