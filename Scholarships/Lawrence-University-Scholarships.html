Lawrence University Scholarships


Elmer & Margaret Otte Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from Kaukauna High School planning to attend Lawrence University in Appleton, Wisconsin. Students must hav a minimum 3.0 GPA. Due date and number of awards may vary. Contact students guidance counselor or the Foundation for more information and/or application. more...

Infant Welfare Circle-F.J. Sensenbrenner Scholarship
Scholarship for graduating high school seniors from Appleton, Wisconsin high schools planning to attend Lawrence University, Marquette University, St. Norbert College, or an institution within the University of Wisconsin system. Students must rank in the top 20% of the graduating class. Selection is based on financial need, scholastic achievement, and leadership. An interview will be required. Number of awards may vary. A copy of parents income tax return must be included with application packet. Renewable. more...

More Light! Scholarship
The More Light! Scholarships are available to entering students at Lawrence University. To be considered, you must demonstrate outstanding school - based and / or community - focused engagement, service and leadership; outstanding international service and / or study experiences; or participate in activities that contribute to environmental sustainability and care of the earth. more...

Thompson-Thiex Memorial Scholarship
Scholarship for full-time undergraduate students attending Lawrence University majoring in Organ Performance. If no organ majors apply scholarship may be awarded to a Music Education major. Contact the university for more information and/or application. more...
