Avila University is a private, co-educational, Catholic university in Kansas City, Missouri, United States, founded in 1916 by the Sisters of St. Joseph of Carondelet. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. It has an enrollment of about 1,000 students. The university offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in a variety of disciplines.
