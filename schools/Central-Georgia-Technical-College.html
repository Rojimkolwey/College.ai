Central Georgia Technical College

Warner Robins, GA
80 Cohen Walker Drive, Warner Robins, GA 31088
478-757-3400

Central Georgia Technical College is a public, two-year technical college serving a thirteen-county service area in central Georgia. The College is part of the Technical College System of Georgia and is accredited by the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools to award associate degrees, diplomas, and technical certificates of credit. Central Georgia Technical College is accredited by the Council on Occupational Education.

The College offers over one hundred programs of study leading to associate degrees, diplomas, and technical certificates of credit. These programs are designed to prepare students for careers in business and industry, health care, information technology, and the skilled trades. The College also offers adult education and continuing education programs to meet the needs of the community.

Central Georgia Technical College is committed to providing quality education and training that prepare students for successful careers and responsible citizenship. The College is dedicated to the economic development of the region through workforce training and economic development programs.

Central Georgia Technical College has campuses in Warner Robins, Milledgeville, and Dublin. The College also has centers in Fort Valley, Perry, and Sandersville.

Admission Requirements

Central Georgia Technical College (CGTC) is a public two-year technical college in Georgia, United States. It is part of the Technical College System of Georgia and accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. CGTC offers over 50 associate degrees, diplomas, and technical certificates of credit in business, health, industrial, and public service technologies.

To be eligible for admission to CGTC, applicants must:

1. Be a high school graduate or possess a GED certificate

2. Submit an application for admission

3. Take the placement test, unless exempt

4. Meet with an advisor

5. Register for classes

Admission to CGTC is open to all qualified applicants. However, priority for admission and registration is given to those who have completed the admission requirements and submitted all required documentation prior to the published deadlines.
