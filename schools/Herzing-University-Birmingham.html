Herzing University-Birmingham

Birmingham, AL
280 West Valley Ave, Birmingham, AL 35209
205-916-2800

Herzing University-Birmingham is a private, for-profit university in Birmingham, Alabama. It is a branch campus of Herzing University, which is based in Wisconsin. The university offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees in business, healthcare, and technology.

Herzing University-Birmingham was founded in 1987 as a branch of Herzing Institute. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. It is also a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.

The university offers a variety of programs in business, healthcare, and technology. Associate's degrees are offered in medical assisting, business administration, and computer science. Bachelor's degrees are offered in business administration, healthcare management, and information technology. Master's degrees are offered in business administration and healthcare management.

Herzing University-Birmingham has a student-to-faculty ratio of 15-to-1. The average class size is 20 students. The university offers online and evening classes.

The university is located in the historic Five Points South district of Birmingham. The district is home to a variety of restaurants, bars, and shops. The university is also located near the University of Alabama at Birmingham and Samford University.
