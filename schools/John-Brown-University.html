John Brown University

Siloam Springs, AR
2000 W University St, Siloam Springs, AR 72761
479-524-9500

John Brown University is a private, interdenominational, Christian university in Siloam Springs, Arkansas. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in a variety of fields, including business, communication, education, engineering, nursing, and more. Founded in 1919, John Brown University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The university is also a member of the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities.

Admission Requirements

John Brown University is a private, interdenominational, Christian university located in Siloam Springs, Arkansas. The university offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs.

Admission to John Brown University is based on a holistic review of the applicant's academic record, test scores, extracurricular involvement, and personal essay. The admissions committee looks for students who have demonstrated academic excellence, leadership potential, and a commitment to service.

John Brown University has a rolling admissions policy, meaning that applications are reviewed and decisions are made on a rolling basis. The earlier an applicant applies, the better their chances of being admitted.
