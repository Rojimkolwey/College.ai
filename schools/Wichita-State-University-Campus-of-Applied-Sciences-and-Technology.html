Wichita State University-Campus of Applied Sciences and Technology

Wichita, KS
4004 N Webb Rd. Bldg 100, Wichita, KS 67226
316-677-9400

Wichita State University-Campus of Applied Sciences and Technology (WSU-CASAT) is a public university located in Wichita, Kansas, United States. It is one of the three campuses of Wichita State University. WSU-CASAT was established in 1965 as the Wichita Area Technical School. It became a part of Wichita State University in 1987. WSU-CASAT offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in applied sciences and technology. The university has over 4,000 students enrolled in its programs.

WSU-CASAT is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university's programs are also accredited by various professional organizations. WSU-CASAT has been ranked as one of the best universities in the Midwest by The Princeton Review. The university is also a member of the Kansas Board of Regents.

WSU-CASAT offers a variety of programs in the fields of engineering, computer science, business, and health sciences. The university also has a Cooperative Education Program which allows students to gain work experience in their field of study. WSU-CASAT has a strong commitment to research and provides its students with opportunities to participate in research projects. The university has a number of research centers, including the Center for Advanced Manufacturing, the Center for Entrepreneurship, and the Center for Intelligent Maintenance Systems.

WSU-CASAT is located in the heart of Wichita's Innovation Quarter. The university is adjacent to the Wichita Transit Center and the Wichita Amtrak station. WSU-CASAT is also within walking distance of a number of restaurants, shops, and entertainment venues.

Once students have met the basic requirements, they will need to submit an application, transcripts, and any required test scores. The admissions committee will review the applications and make a decision based on the student's academic record and potential.

With a commitment to excellence, the Campus of Applied Sciences and Technology provides students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen field.
