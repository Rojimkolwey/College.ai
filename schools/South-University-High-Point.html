South University-High Point is a private university in High Point, North Carolina. It is a branch campus of South University. The university offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees in business administration, criminal justice, healthcare management, and nursing.

South University-High Point was founded in 1997 as High Point University. The university was acquired by South University in 2007. The campus is located on a 100-acre site in the Piedmont Triad region of North Carolina. The university enrolls over 1,000 students.

The university offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. The undergraduate programs include business administration, criminal justice, healthcare management, and nursing. The graduate programs include business administration, healthcare management, and nursing.

The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. The business programs are accredited by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs. The nursing programs are accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education.

The university has a variety of student organizations, including the Student Government Association, the Student Nurses Association, and the Business Club. The university also offers a variety of intramural sports.

The university is committed to providing a quality education at an affordable price. The university offers a variety of scholarships and financial aid programs.
