The University of Wisconsin-Parkside is a public university located in Kenosha, Wisconsin. The university is part of the University of Wisconsin System and offers bachelor's and master's degrees. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university has an enrollment of 4,959 students. The university's athletic teams compete in the NCAA Division II and are members of the Great Lakes Intercollegiate Athletic Conference. The university is home to the Ranger Center, a state-of-the-art fitness and recreation facility. The university also has a planetarium, an art gallery, and a performing arts center. The University of Wisconsin-Parkside is a beautiful campus located on the shores of Lake Michigan. The university has a strong commitment to academic excellence and student success.
