Wyoming Universities and Colleges


Wyoming is a state full of adventure and opportunity, and that extends to higher education as well. The Equality State has a wide array of colleges and universities offering an array of programs, degrees, and concentrations.

The University of Wyoming, located in Laramie, is the state’s flagship institution. It is a public research university, and one of the oldest in the West. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate degrees, including a number of professional and doctoral degrees. The university is also home to several research institutes, and it has a branch campus in Rock Springs.

Western Wyoming Community College, located in Rock Springs, is a two-year public college offering associate degrees and certificate programs. The college also offers a wide range of online courses and programs, and it is home to the Wyoming Institute for Technology and Information (WITI).

Central Wyoming College is a two-year public college located in Riverton. It offers associate degrees and certificate programs in a variety of disciplines, including business, healthcare, and information technology.

Laramie County Community College is a two-year public college located in Cheyenne. The college offers a wide range of degree and certificate programs, as well as online courses and programs.

Sheridan College is a two-year public college located in Sheridan. The college offers a variety of degree and certificate programs, as well as online courses and programs.

Casper College is a two-year public college located in Casper. The college offers associate degrees and certificate programs in a variety of disciplines, as well as online courses and programs.

Lincoln University, located in Cheyenne, is a private four-year university offering a variety of undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate degrees. The university also offers online courses and programs.

Northwest College is a two-year public college located in Powell. The college offers a variety of associate degrees and certificate programs, as well as online courses and programs.

Wyoming Catholic College, located in Lander, is a private four-year college offering a variety of undergraduate and graduate degrees. The college also offers online courses and programs.

The University of Wyoming, Western Wyoming Community College, Central Wyoming College, Laramie County Community College, Sheridan College, Casper College, Lincoln University, Northwest College, and Wyoming Catholic College are all great options for those looking to pursue a higher education in Wyoming. Whether you are looking for a traditional degree program, an online course, or a certificate program, Wyoming’s colleges and universities have something to offer.
