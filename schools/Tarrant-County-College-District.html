Tarrant County College District is a public community college district serving Tarrant County, Texas. The district is headquartered in Fort Worth and has eight campuses throughout the county. Tarrant County College District was established in 1965 as a two-year public community college district. The district has grown steadily over the past 50 years and now serves over 70,000 students each year.
