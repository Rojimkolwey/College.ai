Ozarka College is a two-year institution of higher education located in the beautiful Ozarks of north central Arkansas. The College offers associate degrees and certificates in a variety of academic and technical areas. Ozarka College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association.

The College opened its doors in 1975 as a technical institute offering two-year degrees and has since grown to become a comprehensive community college serving the needs of the citizens in the eight counties of the College’s primary service area: Baxter, Boone, Carroll, Marion, Newton, Searcy, Van Buren, and Washington.
