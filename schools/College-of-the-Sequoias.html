The College of the Sequoias is a public community college in Visalia, California. It is part of the California Community Colleges System and offers associate's degrees and certificates in over 70 academic and vocational programs. The college also has a campus in Hanford, California.

The college was founded in 1926 as the Visalia Junior College. It was renamed the College of the Sequoias in 1957. The college moved to its current location in 1960. The Hanford campus was established in 1962.

The college serves the cities of Visalia, Hanford, and the surrounding communities. It has an enrollment of over 11,000 students.

The college offers a variety of academic and vocational programs. Associate's degrees can be earned in arts, sciences, and applied sciences. The college also offers certificates in a variety of vocational and technical areas.

The college has a number of student clubs and organizations, including the Associated Students of the College of the Sequoias, which represents the student body in college governance. The college also has an active athletics program, with men's and women's teams in a variety of sports.
