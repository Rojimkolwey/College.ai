Morningside College is a small, private, liberal arts college located in Sioux City, Iowa. The college was founded in 1894 by the Methodist Episcopal Church and is affiliated with the United Methodist Church. The college offers bachelor's degrees in over 40 majors and minors, as well as several master's degrees and doctoral programs. Morningside is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college has an enrollment of approximately 1,500 students and a faculty of over 100. The student-to-faculty ratio is 14-to-1.
