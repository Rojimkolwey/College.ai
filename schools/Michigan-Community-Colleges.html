Michigan Community Colleges


Michigan’s community colleges offer an array of educational opportunities for students of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities. The state’s 28 public community colleges provide a wide range of academic programs, from certificates and associate degrees to transfer programs, apprenticeships, and workforce training.

The mission of Michigan’s community colleges is to provide quality, affordable, and accessible education to all residents. Community colleges offer a variety of academic programs, including general education and career and technical education. General education courses provide students with the fundamental knowledge and skills needed to pursue a higher education. Career and technical education courses provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to enter the workforce or pursue further education in a specific field.

Michigan’s community colleges also provide a variety of support services to help students succeed. These services include job placement assistance, academic advising, tutoring, and financial aid. Community colleges also offer a variety of student activities and clubs, allowing students to become involved in their college community and explore interests outside of the classroom.

In addition to providing educational opportunities, Michigan’s community colleges also play an important role in the economic development of the state. Community colleges are involved in workforce development initiatives, helping to create and maintain a qualified and skilled workforce. Community colleges also work with local businesses to create partnerships that benefit both the college and the business.

Michigan’s community colleges serve an important role in the state’s educational landscape. They provide an affordable and accessible option for students of all ages, backgrounds, and abilities. From certificates and associate degrees to transfer programs, apprenticeships, and workforce training, Michigan’s community colleges offer a variety of educational opportunities and support services to help students succeed.
