Louisiana Community Colleges


Louisiana is home to 14 community colleges, each offering a range of educational opportunities to its students. These colleges provide a variety of associate degrees, certificates, and career-focused programs. They also offer non-credit and continuing education courses, as well as workforce training and educational programs.

The Louisiana Community and Technical College System (LCTCS) is the governing body that oversees the state’s community colleges. LCTCS provides a number of resources and services to help students succeed, such as financial aid, student support, and academic advisement.

Louisiana’s community colleges are committed to providing quality educational programs and services that are designed to meet the needs of the state’s diverse population. They offer a variety of degree and certificate programs in fields such as business, healthcare, engineering, and the arts. The colleges also provide a number of career-focused programs, enabling students to gain the necessary skills and knowledge to pursue their chosen career path.

In addition to traditional academic programs, Louisiana’s community colleges offer a range of workforce training and continuing education courses. These programs provide students with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the modern workplace.

Louisiana’s community colleges are committed to providing students with the highest quality educational opportunities. Through their diverse programs and services, these colleges are helping to create a brighter future for the state’s citizens.
