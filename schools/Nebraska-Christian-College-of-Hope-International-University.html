Nebraska Christian College of Hope International University

Papillion, NE
12550 S. 114th St., Papillion, NE 68046
402-935-9400

Nebraska Christian College (NCC) is a small, private, Christian college located in the city of Papillion, Nebraska. The college was founded in 1968 as a two-year institution and began offering four-year degrees in 1987. NCC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities. The college offers bachelor's degrees in Bible and Theology, Business, Education, Nursing, and Psychology, as well as a Master of Arts in Education. Nebraska Christian College is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that equips students for lives of service and leadership. The college's core values include faith, excellence, community, and stewardship. NCC's campus is located just outside of Omaha, Nebraska, and the college's student body is made up of approximately 500 students from across the United States.

Admission Requirements

Nebraska Christian College is committed to providing an excellent education grounded in biblical truth. We are a Christ-centered community that equips students to fulfill their God-given potential. The admissions process at Nebraska Christian College is designed to help us get to know you and your story. We want to know what God has done in your life and how He has prepared you for the next step in your journey.

We are looking for students who have a heart for God and a desire to grow in their faith. We want students who are excited about learning and are ready to engage in rigorous academic study. We are looking for students who will contribute to the life of our campus community and who are ready to take advantage of all that Nebraska Christian College has to offer.

The first step in the admissions process is to submit an application. You can apply online or request a paper application from the admissions office. Once we receive your application, we will review your academic records and test scores to get a sense of your academic preparedness. We will also consider your personal essay and your letters of recommendation.

If you meet the academic requirements for admission, we will invite you to campus for an interview. The interview is an opportunity for us to get to know you better and for you to ask any questions you may have about Nebraska Christian College.

Once we have all of the information we need, we will make a decision on your admission. We will notify you of our decision via mail. If you are admitted to Nebraska Christian College, we will also send you information about how to enroll and what to do next.

We are excited to get to know you and to help you take the next step in your journey.
