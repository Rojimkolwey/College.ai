Indian Capital Technology Center-Muskogee

Muskogee, OK
2403 N 41st St E, Muskogee, OK 74403-1889
918-687-6383

The Indian Capital Technology Center (ICTC) is a public, two-year career and technology education center located in Muskogee, Oklahoma. The ICTC serves high school students from 11 surrounding school districts as well as adults in the community. The ICTC offers over 50 different programs in a variety of career and technical areas, such as agriculture, business and marketing, health sciences, information technology, manufacturing, and welding.

The ICTC is accredited by the Oklahoma State Board of Career and Technology Education and is a member of the Oklahoma Association of Technology Centers. The ICTC is also recognized by the National Center for Construction Education and Research and the National Institute for Automotive Service Excellence.

The ICTC has a strong commitment to providing quality education and training that leads to successful careers for its students. The ICTC staff is highly qualified and dedicated to helping students reach their full potential. The ICTC offers a variety of services to its students, including academic advising, career counseling, job placement assistance, and financial aid.

The ICTC is an important part of the Muskogee community and is committed to providing quality education and training that leads to successful careers for its students.
