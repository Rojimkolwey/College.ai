Charter College

Vancouver, WA
17200 S.E. Mill Plain Boulevard, Suite 100, Vancouver, WA 98683-7575
360-448-2000

Founded in 1985, Charter College is a leading post-secondary career training institution specializing in providing quality, hands-on education in a wide range of in-demand career fields. With over 30 locations across the Western United States, Charter College offers a unique and affordable education path for busy, working adults.

Charter College's mission is to provide quality, hands-on education that prepares students for successful careers in high-demand industries. The school offers a wide range of programs in fields such as healthcare, business, technology, and trades.

Charter College is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC), and all programs are designed to meet the needs of busy, working adults. The school offers flexible class schedules, online learning options, and career services to help students succeed.

Charter College is committed to helping students reach their career goals. The school's faculty and staff are passionate about helping students succeed and are dedicated to providing a supportive and positive learning environment.

Whether you're looking to start a new career or advance in your current field, Charter College can help you reach your goals. With over 30 years of experience in providing quality education, Charter College is the right choice for busy, working adults.

Admission Requirements

Charter College is a private for-profit college with locations across the United States. The school offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees in a variety of fields, including business, healthcare, and technology.

To be eligible for admission to Charter College, applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent. Some programs may have additional requirements, such as minimum GPA or standardized test scores.

Once admitted, students must complete an enrollment agreement and pay a deposit to secure their spot in the incoming class. After that, they will work with an advisor to create a personalized degree plan.

Charter College is committed to helping students succeed, and offers a variety of services to support them, including tutoring, academic advising, and career services. The school also has a flexible schedule that allows students to take classes online, in person, or a combination of both.
