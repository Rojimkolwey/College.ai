Louisiana State University-Eunice

Eunice, LA
2048 Johnson Hwy, Eunice, LA 70535
337-457-7311

Louisiana State University-Eunice is a public university located in Eunice, Louisiana. It is part of the Louisiana State University System and offers associate's and bachelor's degrees. The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges.

The university was founded in 1964 as a two-year institution and became a four-year university in 1974. The university is located on a 200-acre campus in Eunice, Louisiana. The campus includes academic buildings, administrative offices, a library, a student center, a gymnasium, and athletic fields.

The university offers associate's degrees in arts, science, and applied science. The bachelor's degree programs include business administration, criminal justice, education, and nursing. The university also offers a cooperative education program and an online degree completion program.

The university has an enrollment of 2,500 students. The student body is diverse, with students from all over Louisiana and the United States. The university has a student-to-faculty ratio of 18-to-1.

The university is committed to providing a quality education at an affordable price. The average cost of tuition and fees for Louisiana residents is $5,764 per year. The university offers financial aid to help students meet the cost of tuition and fees.

The university is located in the heart of Cajun country. The university offers students the opportunity to experience the Cajun culture and learn about the Cajun language. The university is also home to the Eunice Museum, which houses exhibits on the history and culture of the Cajun people.
