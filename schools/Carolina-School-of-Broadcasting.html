Carolina School of Broadcasting

Charlotte, NC
3435 Performance Rd, Charlotte, NC 28214
704-395-9272

Carolina School of Broadcasting in Charlotte, North Carolina has been training students in the art of broadcasting since 1968. The school offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers all aspects of the broadcasting industry, from on-air performance to behind-the-scenes production. Carolina School of Broadcasting is dedicated to helping its students find success in their chosen career field, and its graduates have gone on to work at some of the most prestigious radio and television stations in the country. The school's experienced faculty members are all working professionals in the broadcasting industry, and they bring a wealth of knowledge and experience to the classroom. Carolina School of Broadcasting is committed to providing its students with the skills and training they need to succeed in the competitive world of broadcasting.
