Luzerne County Community College

Nanticoke, PA
1333 South Prospect Street, Nanticoke, PA 18634-3899
570-740-0200

Luzerne County Community College (LCCC) is a two-year, public community college located in Nanticoke, Pennsylvania. LCCC offers associate's degrees, certificate programs, and adult basic education and literacy programs. LCCC also offers a variety of continuing education and workforce development programs.

LCCC was founded in 1966 as the first community college in Pennsylvania. LCCC has grown significantly since its founding, and now serves over 10,000 students each year. LCCC is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education.

LCCC offers a wide variety of academic programs, including over 50 associate's degree programs and over 20 certificate programs. LCCC also offers a variety of continuing education and workforce development programs.

LCCC is committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality education and training opportunities to the residents of Luzerne County and the surrounding region. LCCC is an active member of the community, and works collaboratively with local businesses and organizations to meet the needs of the community.
