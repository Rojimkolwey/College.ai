Mt. San Antonio College (Mt. SAC) is a public community college in Walnut, California. The college is one of the 112 community colleges in the California Community Colleges System (CCCS) and one of the largest community colleges in the state with over 46,000 students.

Mt. SAC offers over 140 Associate's degree and certificate programs as well as transfer programs to four-year colleges and universities. The college also has a strong athletics program and is home to the Mt. SAC Relays, one of the largest and most prestigious track and field events in the country.

Admission to Mt. SAC is open to all high school graduates and students who have completed the equivalent of high school (e.g. GED). The college does not have an admission test but placement tests are required for students who want to enroll in English, Math, and/or ESL courses.

Mt. SAC is committed to helping all students succeed and has a number of programs and services in place to support student success. These include academic counseling, tutoring, and financial aid.
