Grady Health System Professional Schools

Atlanta, GA
80 Jesse Hill Jr Drive, SE, Atlanta, GA 30303-3050
404-616-3506

Grady Health System Professional Schools is a non-profit institution located in Atlanta, Georgia. The school offers a variety of health care programs including nursing, medical, dental, and pharmacy. The school is committed to providing quality education and training to its students so they can provide the best care possible to their patients. The school has a long history of serving the community and is dedicated to its mission of providing quality health care to all.

Admission Requirements

Grady Health System Professional Schools are committed to providing quality education to their students. In order to be admitted into one of their programs, applicants must meet the following requirements:

A minimum cumulative grade point average (GPA) of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale is required for all applicants.

All applicants must complete an application form and submit it to the school's admissions office.

All applicants must submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended.

All applicants must submit a non-refundable application fee.

All applicants must complete an entrance exam, such as the Test of Essential Academic Skills (TEAS) or the Health Careers Aptitude Test (HCAT).

All applicants must participate in an interview with a member of the school's admissions committee.

Once all of the above requirements have been met, the applicant will be considered for admission into the desired program.
