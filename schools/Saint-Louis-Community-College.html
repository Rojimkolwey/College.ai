Saint Louis Community College (STLCC) is a public, two-year institution of higher education located in the city of St. Louis, Missouri, United States. STLCC serves nearly 60,000 students annually and offers more than 1,000 courses in over 100 program areas. The College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Saint Louis Community College, applicants must submit a completed application form, high school transcript, and ACT or SAT scores. Applicants must also have a minimum 2.0 GPA and meet the College Placement Test (CPT) requirements.
