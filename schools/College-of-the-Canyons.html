The College of the Canyons is a community college located in the Santa Clarita Community College District in Santa Clarita, California. It was founded in 1969 and is accredited by the Western Association of Schools and Colleges. The college offers over 130 associate's degree and certificate programs. The college has an enrollment of over 22,000 students. The college has a student-to-faculty ratio of 24:1. The college offers a variety of student services, including academic advising, tutoring, and career services. The college also has a variety of clubs and organizations, including the Associated Students of College of the Canyons, which represents the student body. The college is governed by a seven-member Board of Trustees, which is elected by the community college district. The college has two campuses, the Valencia campus, which is the main campus, and the Canyon Country campus. The college also has a satellite campus in the city of Santa Clarita.
