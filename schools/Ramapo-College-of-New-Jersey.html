Admission Requirements

Ramapo College of New Jersey is a public liberal arts college in Mahwah, New Jersey. Founded in 1969, Ramapo College offers bachelor's degrees in the arts, business, humanities, social sciences, and sciences, as well as master's degrees in business and education and a Doctor of Education in Educational Leadership. The college is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education.

To be eligible for admission to Ramapo College, applicants must have graduated from an accredited high school or earned a GED. They must also submit official transcripts, SAT or ACT scores, and a $50 application fee. International students must also submit TOEFL or IELTS scores.

Ramapo College has a rolling admissions policy, meaning that applications are reviewed as they are received. However, applicants are encouraged to apply early as space in some programs is limited. Priority is given to applications received by February 1 for the fall semester and November 1 for the spring semester.
