New Hampshire Institute for Therapeutic Arts

Hudson, NH
153 Lowell Rd, Hudson, NH 03051
603-882-3022

The New Hampshire Institute for Therapeutic Arts is a massage therapy school located in Hudson, New Hampshire. The school offers a variety of massage therapy programs, as well as other health and wellness services. The school is accredited by the Commission on Massage Therapy Accreditation (COMTA) and is a member of the American Massage Therapy Association (AMTA). The school's mission is to provide students with the knowledge and skills necessary to become successful massage therapists. The school offers a variety of programs, including a Certificate in Massage Therapy, an Associate's Degree in Massage Therapy, and a Bachelor's Degree in Massage Therapy. The school also offers a variety of continuing education courses.
