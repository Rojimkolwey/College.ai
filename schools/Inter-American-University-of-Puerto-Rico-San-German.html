Inter American University of Puerto Rico-San German (IAUPRSG) is a private, coeducational institution of higher learning located in the municipality of San German, Puerto Rico. It is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The university offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of fields, including business, education, engineering, and nursing.

IAUPRSG was founded in 1912 as the Colegio de San German by a group of educators and civic leaders. The college was originally located in the town of San German, but moved to its current location in the municipality of San German in the early 1950s. In the late 1960s, the college began to offer a wider range of degree programs and was renamed the Inter American University of Puerto Rico.

The university is organized into six academic units: the School of Arts and Sciences, the School of Business Administration, the School of Education, the School of Engineering, the School of Nursing, and the School of Continuing Education. The university also operates a campus in the city of Ponce.

IAUPRSG offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate degree programs. Undergraduate programs are offered in fields such as business, education, engineering, and nursing. Graduate programs are offered in fields such as business administration, education, and engineering.

The university is committed to providing a quality education to its students. It offers a variety of student services, including academic advising, career counseling, and tutoring. The university also has a strong commitment to research and offers a variety of research facilities and resources.
