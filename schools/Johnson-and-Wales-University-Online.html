Johnson & Wales University-Online

Providence, RI
8 Abbott Park Pl, Providence, RI 02903
401-598-1000

Founded in 1914, Johnson & Wales University is a private, nonprofit, accredited institution with four campuses located in Providence, Rhode Island; North Miami, Florida; Denver, Colorado; and Charlotte, North Carolina. The university offers associate, bachelor’s, and master’s degrees in business, culinary arts, hospitality, education, and technology. In addition to its online programs, Johnson & Wales University also offers a variety of distance learning options including hybrid and blended learning formats.

Johnson & Wales University’s online programs are designed to provide students with the flexibility to complete their coursework around their busy schedules. The university offers a variety of online degrees at the undergraduate and graduate level, as well as a number of certificate programs. Students can choose from programs in business, culinary arts, hospitality, education, and technology.

Johnson & Wales University’s online programs are accredited by the same accrediting bodies as the university’s on-campus programs. The university is committed to providing students with the same quality education whether they are learning on-campus or online.

Johnson & Wales University’s online students have access to the same resources as on-campus students including academic advising, career services, library services, and technical support. Online students also have the opportunity to participate in extracurricular activities and clubs, and to connect with other online students through the university’s online community.

Admission Requirements

Johnson & Wales University-Online is a regionally accredited, online university that offers bachelor's and master's degrees in business, culinary arts, hospitality, and more. Admission requirements vary by program, but all applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent. Some programs may also require SAT or ACT scores, and all applicants must submit transcripts from all previous colleges and universities attended.
