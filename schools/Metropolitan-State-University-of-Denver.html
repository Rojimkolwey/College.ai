Metropolitan State University of Denver (MSU Denver) is a public university in Denver, Colorado. MSU Denver is the largest institution of higher education in Colorado and the only urban public university in the Denver metropolitan area. It is located in the heart of downtown Denver, on a 122-acre campus, and enrolls more than 18,000 students in over 140 undergraduate and graduate programs.

MSU Denver offers a wide variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, as well as a unique dual-degree program that allows students to earn a bachelor's and a master's degree in four years. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.

MSU Denver is committed to providing a quality education at an affordable price. In addition to offering a variety of scholarships and financial aid options, the university has a tuition guarantee program that allows students to lock in their tuition rate for four years.

MSU Denver is also committed to serving the community. The university offers a variety of programs and services that are designed to meet the needs of the community, including a community college, a business incubator, and a variety of continuing education and professional development programs.
