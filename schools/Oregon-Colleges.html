Oregon Universities and Colleges


Oregon is home to a wide variety of higher education institutions, ranging from large public universities to small private colleges. The Oregon University System (OUS) is the state's largest public higher education system, and includes seven universities. The University of Oregon, Oregon State University, and Portland State University are the three largest universities in the system.

The University of Oregon, located in Eugene, is the state's flagship university. It is home to a number of highly ranked academic programs, such as its business, law, and architecture schools. Oregon State University, located in Corvallis, is the state's largest university and is known for its engineering, agricultural, and science programs. Portland State University is the most urban of the OUS universities, and is home to a diverse range of programs, including its popular business and engineering schools.

In addition to the OUS universities, Oregon is also home to a number of private colleges and universities. These include the University of Portland, Lewis & Clark College, Willamette University, and Reed College. The University of Portland is a Catholic university located in the city of Portland and is known for its nursing, business, and engineering programs. Lewis & Clark College, located in Portland, is a liberal arts college that is home to a number of highly ranked academic programs. Willamette University, located in Salem, is a private liberal arts college that is known for its law school and business school. Reed College, located in Portland, is a small liberal arts college that is home to a number of highly ranked academic programs.

Oregon also offers a number of community colleges, technical schools, and career colleges. These schools offer a variety of programs, from certificate and associate degree programs to more specialized technical and career-oriented programs. Oregon also has a number of online colleges and universities that offer degree and certificate programs.

Overall, Oregon offers a wide variety of higher education options, from large public universities to small private colleges and universities. Whether you're looking for a large university experience or a small liberal arts college, Oregon has something to offer.
