Three Rivers College

Poplar Bluff, MO
2080 Three Rivers Blvd, Poplar Bluff, MO 63901
573-840-9600

Three Rivers College is a two-year community college located in Poplar Bluff, Missouri. The college offers associate's degrees and certificate programs in a variety of fields, including business, healthcare, and liberal arts. Three Rivers College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college was founded in 1968 and has an enrollment of approximately 4,000 students. Three Rivers College is governed by a seven-member Board of Trustees, which is appointed by the Governor of Missouri. The college is led by President Dr. Wesley Payne. Three Rivers College is committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality education to its students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans. Three Rivers College also has a robust distance learning program, which offers online and hybrid courses.

Admission Requirements

Three Rivers College (TRC) is a public, two-year institution of higher education located in Poplar Bluff, Missouri. TRC serves an eight-county region in Southeast Missouri and offers over 50 different degree and certificate programs. TRC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

The admissions process at Three Rivers College is designed to be simple and straightforward. All students are welcome, and TRC does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, or disability.

All students must complete an application for admission and submit all required transcripts and test scores. TRC has an open admissions policy, meaning that all students who meet the minimum requirements for admission will be accepted.

TRC has rolling admissions, meaning that applications are reviewed and decisions are made on a rolling basis throughout the year. Students are encouraged to apply early to ensure the best possible chance of admission.
