About Illinois

The state of Illinois is located in the Midwestern region of the United States. It is the 6th most populous state in the country with a population of over 12 million people. The state capital is Springfield and the largest city is Chicago. Illinois is known for its diverse landscape which includes forests, prairies, and wetlands. The state is also home to several national parks and monuments. The economy of Illinois is based on agriculture, manufacturing, and service industries. The state is a major transportation hub with a large network of roads and railways. Illinois is also home to several community colleges.
