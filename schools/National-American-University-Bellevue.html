National American University-Bellevue

Bellevue, NE
3604 Summit Plaza Drive, Bellevue, NE 68123-1065
402-972-4250

National American University-Bellevue is a for-profit university located in Bellevue, Nebraska. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees in business, healthcare, and information technology. The university has an enrollment of over 1,200 students. The university is owned by National American University Holdings, Inc.



Quick Facts

Type: Private for-profit, 4-year or above

Main Website: https://www.national.edu/locations/campuses/bellevue/

Financial Aid: https://www.national.edu/admissions-financial/financial-aid/federal-financial-aid/

Admission Requirements

National American University-Bellevue requires that all students have a high school diploma or equivalent. They must also submit an application, transcripts, and a non-refundable application fee. International students must also submit proof of English proficiency.
