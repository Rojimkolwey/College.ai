Del Mar College

Corpus Christi, TX
101 Baldwin Blvd., Corpus Christi, TX 78404-3897
361-698-1200

Del Mar College is a public community college in Corpus Christi, Texas. The college offers associate degrees, certificates, and continuing education courses in a variety of subject areas.

Del Mar College was founded in 1935 as a junior college by the Corpus Christi Independent School District. The college was originally located in the old Corpus Christi High School building. In its early years, the college offered two-year programs leading to an Associate of Arts degree.

In the 1950s, the college began to expand its programs and facilities. A new campus was built in 1959, and the college began to offer four-year programs leading to a Bachelor of Arts degree. In the 1970s, the college added a number of technical and vocational programs.

Today, Del Mar College enrolls over 12,000 students in credit and non-credit programs. The college offers over 100 associate degree and certificate programs. Del Mar College is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.

Del Mar College is an important part of the Corpus Christi community. The college provides affordable, accessible education and training that helps students achieve their goals. The college also serves as a cultural and economic engine for the region, providing a variety of cultural and recreational events and activities.

Admission Requirements

Del Mar College (DMC) is a public two-year community college in Corpus Christi, Texas. DMC is part of the Texas A&M University System. The college offers associate degrees, certificates, and continuing education courses.

DMC has an open admissions policy, meaning that any student who meets the minimum requirements for admission will be accepted into the college. The minimum requirements for admission are as follows:

- Must be a high school graduate or have a GED

- Must be 18 years of age or older

- Must have a 2.0 GPA or higher

Once students have met the minimum requirements for admission, they will need to complete an application for admission. The application can be found on the DMC website. Once the application has been completed, students will need to submit it to the Office of Admissions along with any required documents.

Once the Office of Admissions has received the completed application and required documents, they will review the application and determine if the student has been accepted into the college. If the student is accepted, they will be sent an acceptance letter and will be able to register for classes.
