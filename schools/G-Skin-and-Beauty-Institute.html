G Skin & Beauty Institute

Oakbrook, IL
1200 Harger Road, Suite 100, Oakbrook, IL 60523
815-786-7266

G Skin & Beauty Institute is a beauty school in Schaumburg, Illinois. The school offers a variety of beauty and cosmetology programs, as well as esthetician and nail technician programs. The school is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Cosmetology Arts and Sciences. The school's programs are designed to prepare students for a career in the beauty industry. The school offers a variety of courses that cover topics such as hair design, skin care, makeup, and nail care. The school also offers a variety of services to its students, including a salon, spa, and retail store.
