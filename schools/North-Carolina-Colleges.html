North Carolina Universities and Colleges


North Carolina is home to a wide variety of colleges and universities, offering a variety of educational opportunities for students. The University of North Carolina system is the largest public university system in the state, and consists of 16 campuses across the state. These include the flagship campus at Chapel Hill, as well as North Carolina State University in Raleigh, Appalachian State University in Boone, and East Carolina University in Greenville. The North Carolina Community College System is the second largest public higher education system in the state, and includes 58 campuses spread across the state. These community colleges offer associate degrees, certificates, and diplomas in a variety of fields.

In addition to the public university and community college systems, North Carolina is home to a variety of private colleges and universities. These include Davidson College, a highly selective liberal arts college in Davidson; Duke University, a prestigious research university in Durham; and Wake Forest University, a private research university in Winston-Salem. There are also several historically black colleges and universities located in North Carolina, including North Carolina Central University in Durham, Shaw University in Raleigh, and Johnson C. Smith University in Charlotte.

North Carolina is also home to a variety of specialized schools, such as the North Carolina School of the Arts in Winston-Salem, the North Carolina Maritime Academy in Morehead City, and the North Carolina School of Science and Mathematics in Durham. There are also several religious institutions in the state, including Campbell University in Buies Creek, Gardner-Webb University in Boiling Springs, and Piedmont International University in Winston-Salem.

No matter what kind of educational program a student is looking for, North Carolina has something to offer. From the public university and community college systems to the private colleges and specialized schools, North Carolina has a wide variety of educational opportunities available to students.
