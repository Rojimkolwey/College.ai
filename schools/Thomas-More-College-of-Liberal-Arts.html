Thomas More College of Liberal Arts

Merrimack, NH
6 Manchester St, Merrimack, NH 03054
603-880-8308

Thomas More College of Liberal Arts is a small, private, Catholic college located in Merrimack, New Hampshire. The college was founded in 1978 by the late Bishop John J. McCormack, and its mission is to provide a liberal arts education rooted in the Catholic intellectual tradition. The college offers bachelor's degrees in a variety of majors, including theology, philosophy, history, English, and political science. In addition to its academic programs, the college also offers a number of extracurricular activities, including a student-run newspaper, an a cappella group, and a campus ministry program. Thomas More College is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges.
