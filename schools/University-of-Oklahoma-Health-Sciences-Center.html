The University of Oklahoma-Health Sciences Center (OUHSC) is a public university in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. OUHSC is one of three comprehensive health centers in the state of Oklahoma and is the only one with a medical school. The OUHSC campus is located on 110 acres (0.45 km2) in the northeast corner of the Oklahoma Health Center, which is adjacent to the State Capitol and the Oklahoma State Fairgrounds.

OUHSC offers programs in the health sciences at the undergraduate, graduate, and professional levels. The OU College of Medicine is the only medical school in the state of Oklahoma. The OU School of Community Medicine is the state's only public school of medicine. The OU College of Nursing is the state's only public college of nursing. The OU School of Allied Health is the state's only public college of allied health.

The OU College of Medicine is accredited by the Liaison Committee on Medical Education. The OU School of Community Medicine is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Graduate Medical Education.
