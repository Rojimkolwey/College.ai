Eastern New Mexico University-Roswell Campus

Roswell, NM
52 University Blvd, Roswell, NM 88202-6000
575-624-7000

Eastern New Mexico University-Roswell Campus is a two-year public college located in Roswell, New Mexico. ENMU-R offers associate degrees and certificates in a variety of academic and vocational fields. The campus is part of the Eastern New Mexico University system, which includes four other campuses in Portales, Ruidoso, Carlsbad, and Hobbs.

ENMU-R was founded in 1958 as a branch campus of Eastern New Mexico University. The campus originally offered only night classes, but daytime classes were added in the 1960s. In the 1970s, the campus began offering a wider range of programs, including vocational and technical programs. In the 1980s, the campus underwent a major expansion, adding a new library, student center, and classroom buildings.

Today, ENMU-R offers over 30 associate degree and certificate programs. The campus is home to over 2,000 students, most of whom are from the Roswell area. ENMU-R is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

ENMU-R offers a variety of student services, including academic advising, tutoring, career services, and counseling. The campus also has an active student life, with over 30 clubs and organizations. ENMU-R athletics include men's and women's basketball, cross country, and track and field.

Admission Requirements

Eastern New Mexico University-Roswell Campus is a two-year community college located in Roswell, New Mexico. The school offers Associate of Arts and Associate of Science degrees, as well as technical and vocational programs.

Admission to Eastern New Mexico University-Roswell Campus is open to all students who have a high school diploma or equivalent. There are no minimum GPA or test score requirements. However, students are encouraged to submit their transcripts and test scores to the school for placement purposes.

Once admitted, students must complete an orientation program and meet with an academic advisor to plan their course of study. Classes at Eastern New Mexico University-Roswell Campus are offered on a semester basis.
