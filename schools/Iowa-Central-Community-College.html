Iowa Central Community College

Fort Dodge, IA
One Triton Circle, Fort Dodge, IA 50501
515-576-7201

Iowa Central Community College (ICCC) is a public community college in Fort Dodge, Iowa. It is part of the Iowa Community College System. ICCC offers associate's degrees, diplomas, and certificates in over 50 areas of study. The college has four campuses in Fort Dodge, Webster City, and Storm Lake, Iowa.

ICCC was established in 1966 as Iowa's first community college. The college has grown significantly since then, and now serves over 8,000 students each year. ICCC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

ICCC is committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality education to its students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options to help students pay for their education. ICCC also has a number of programs and services to support students' success, including tutoring, advising, and career services.

ICCC is an active member of the community, and partners with local businesses and organizations to provide students with real-world experience. The college also offers a variety of community education and enrichment programs.

Iowa Central Community College is a great option for students seeking an affordable, accessible, and high-quality education. The college offers a variety of programs and services to support students' success, and is an active member of the community.

Admission Requirements

Iowa Central Community College is a two-year institution of higher education located in the state of Iowa. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. Iowa Central offers over 100 programs of study and awards both associate degrees and certificates.

Admission to Iowa Central Community College is open to any student who has a high school diploma or equivalent. Students must submit an application, transcripts, and a non-refundable application fee. International students must also submit proof of English proficiency. Once all materials have been received, the Admissions Office will review the file and make a decision.

Students who are admitted to Iowa Central Community College will be required to complete a New Student Orientation prior to registering for classes. During orientation, students will learn about the college's policies and procedures, as well as their rights and responsibilities as a student. Orientation is mandatory for all new students and must be completed before classes begin.
