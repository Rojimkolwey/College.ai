Indiana County Technology Center

Indiana, PA
441 Hamill Rd, Indiana, PA 15701-1465
724-349-6700

Indiana County Technology Center (ICTC) is a public, career and technical education school that serves the residents of Indiana County, Pennsylvania. ICTC offers a variety of programs in the areas of business, health, trade and industry, and STEM (science, technology, engineering and mathematics). ICTC also has a strong adult education program, which offers courses in GED preparation, English as a Second Language (ESL) and Adult Basic Education (ABE).

ICTC was founded in 1966, and has been serving the community for over 50 years. ICTC is accredited by the Commission on Secondary Schools of the Middle States Association of Colleges and Schools. ICTC is also a member of the Pennsylvania Association of Career and Technical Educators (PACTE) and the National Association of Career and Technical Education (NACTE).

ICTC offers a wide range of programs and services to meet the needs of Indiana County residents. ICTC’s programs are designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the workforce. ICTC’s programs are also designed to meet the needs of businesses and industries in Indiana County. ICTC’s programs and services include:

Business programs: ICTC offers programs in business administration, accounting, and computer applications. ICTC’s business programs prepare students for careers in business and industry.

Health programs: ICTC offers programs in nursing, medical office assistant, and dental assistant. ICTC’s health programs prepare students for careers in healthcare.

Trade and industry programs: ICTC offers programs in welding, automotive technology, and construction technology. ICTC’s trade and industry programs prepare students for careers in the trades.

STEM programs: ICTC offers programs in engineering, computer science, and mathematics. ICTC’s STEM programs prepare students for careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.

Adult education programs: ICTC offers courses in GED preparation, ESL, and ABE. ICTC’s adult education programs provide adults with the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the workforce.

ICTC is committed to providing the residents of Indiana County with the best possible education and training. ICTC’s programs are affordable and convenient, and ICTC’s faculty and staff are dedicated to helping students succeed.
