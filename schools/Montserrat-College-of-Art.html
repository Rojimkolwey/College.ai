Montserrat College of Art is a small, private, non-profit college of visual and applied arts located in Beverly, Massachusetts. The college offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of arts disciplines, as well as a post-baccalaureate certificate program. Founded in 1970, Montserrat is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges and the National Association of Schools of Art and Design. The college's campus is situated on 25 acres (10 hectares) of land in the historic downtown Beverly and features several buildings that are listed on the National Register of Historic Places.

Montserrat College of Art's mission is to prepare its students for professional careers in the visual and applied arts. The college offers a comprehensive education that combines studio-based training with liberal arts coursework. Students have the opportunity to study with internationally recognized faculty and to participate in a variety of extracurricular activities, including exhibitions, lectures, and workshops. The college also offers a number of study abroad programs.

The college offers a variety of degree programs that prepare students for careers in the visual and applied arts, including painting, sculpture, photography, graphic design, illustration, and printmaking. In addition to its degree programs, the college offers a post-baccalaureate certificate program in art education.

Montserrat College of Art's campus is located in the historic downtown Beverly, Massachusetts. The college's 25-acre (10-hectare) campus features several buildings that are listed on the National Register of Historic Places, including the 18th-century Georgian mansion that serves as the college's main administration building.
