Admission Requirements

Admission to Imperial Valley College (IVC) is open to all high school graduates and adults 18 years of age or older who can benefit from instruction. There are no entrance examinations. However, students are required to complete an assessment process to determine their level of skills in English and mathematics. This process is designed to help students and counselors select courses that match the student's skill level.

IVC uses a placement test called the Accuplacer for English and math. The test is not pass/fail. Rather, it is used to determine the student's level of skills in these subject areas. Based on the results of the test, the student is placed in the appropriate English and/or math course(s).
