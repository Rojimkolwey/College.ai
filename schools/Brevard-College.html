Brevard College is a small, private, liberal arts college located in the mountains of Western North Carolina. The college was founded in 1853 by a group of men who wanted to provide an education for the young men of the area. The college is affiliated with the United Methodist Church. The campus is located on 150 acres of land and includes several buildings, a library, and a chapel. The college offers a variety of degree programs, including a bachelor's degree in business administration, a bachelor's degree in education, and a bachelor's degree in liberal arts. The college also offers a master's degree in business administration. The college has an enrollment of approximately 1,000 students.
