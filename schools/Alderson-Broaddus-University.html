Alderson Broaddus University is a private, four-year institution of higher education located in Philippi, West Virginia, USA. The university was founded in 1871 as Alderson Academy and Broaddus College, making it the oldest continuously operating institution of higher education in West Virginia. The school's name was changed to Alderson-Broaddus College in 1932, and then to Alderson Broaddus University in 2013. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of disciplines.

Alderson Broaddus University is committed to providing a liberal arts education that develops students intellectually, spiritually, and physically. The university offers a variety of programs and majors that prepare students for success in their chosen field of study. The university also offers a number of extracurricular activities and organizations that allow students to explore their interests and develop their leadership skills.

Alderson Broaddus University is located in the historic town of Philippi, West Virginia. The town is home to a number of businesses and organizations, as well as a variety of cultural and recreational opportunities. The university is situated on a beautiful campus that features a variety of academic, residential, and athletic facilities.

Admission Requirements

Alderson Broaddus University is a private, four-year institution located in Philippi, West Virginia. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs.

Undergraduate programs:

Alderson Broaddus University offers a variety of undergraduate programs. The university has a rolling admissions policy, which means that applications are accepted throughout the year. There is no application fee.

To be eligible for admission, applicants must submit the following:

-A completed application form

-Official high school transcripts

-Official college transcripts (if applicable)

-SAT or ACT scores

-Two letters of recommendation

-A personal essay

-A non-refundable deposit of $200

Graduate programs:

Alderson Broaddus University also offers a variety of graduate programs. Admission requirements vary by program.
