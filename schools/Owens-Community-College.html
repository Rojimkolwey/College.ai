Owens Community College

Perrysburg, OH
30335 Oregon Rd, Perrysburg, OH 43551
567-661-7000

Owens Community College is a public two-year institution of higher education in Toledo, Ohio. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. Owens Community College was established in 1965 and serves over 30,000 students annually. The college offers over 100 programs of study and awards associate degrees and certificates. Owens Community College is committed to providing accessible and affordable education to students in northwest Ohio. The college has campuses in Toledo and Findlay, Ohio, as well as online learning opportunities. Owens Community College is a member of the Ohio Association of Community Colleges and the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.

Admission Requirements

Owens Community College is a public, two-year institution of higher education that is part of the Ohio public college and university system. Owens Community College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college has an open-admission policy, meaning that any student who has a high school diploma or equivalent is eligible to enroll.

Owens Community College offers over 100 degree and certificate programs. The most popular programs at Owens are in the areas of business, health care, and engineering. Owens also has a strong liberal arts and sciences program.

To be eligible for admission to Owens Community College, students must submit an application, transcripts, and test scores. Owens uses a holistic approach to admissions, meaning that the admissions committee will consider all aspects of the student's application, including academic achievement, personal circumstances, and motivation.

Owens Community College is committed to student success and offers a variety of services to help students succeed in college. These services include academic advising, tutoring, and career services. Owens also has a strong commitment to diversity and inclusion and offers a variety of programs and resources to support students from all backgrounds.
