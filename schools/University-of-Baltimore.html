The University of Baltimore (UB) is a public university in Baltimore, Maryland. Founded in 1925, UB is a member of the University System of Maryland. The university offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in business, law, public affairs, and the liberal arts.

The University of Baltimore is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The school's business programs are accredited by the Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business. The University of Baltimore School of Law is accredited by the American Bar Association.

The University of Baltimore was founded in 1925 as the Baltimore branch of the University of Maryland. The school was renamed the University of Baltimore in 1975. The university is located in downtown Baltimore, near the Inner Harbor.
