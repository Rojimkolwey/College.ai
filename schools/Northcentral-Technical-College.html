Northcentral Technical College

Wausau, WI
1000 Campus Dr, Wausau, WI 54401-1899
715-675-3331

Northcentral Technical College (NTC) is a public technical college in Wausau, Wisconsin. The college offers more than 200 associate degree, technical diploma, and certificate programs in business, health, manufacturing, and other career fields. NTC also has a campus in Antigo, Wisconsin, which offers a limited selection of programs.

NTC was founded in 1912 as the Wausau School of Commerce. The school became a two-year college in 1966 and was renamed Northcentral Technical Institute. The college began offering four-year degrees in 1974 and was renamed Northcentral Technical College. NTC is part of the Wisconsin Technical College System.

NTC has five schools: School of Agriculture, School of Business and Information Technology, School of Health Sciences, School of Manufacturing and Construction, and School of Transportation and Public Safety. The college also has a Center for Adult Education, which offers programs for adults who want to earn a high school diploma or GED, learn English as a second language, or take college courses.

NTC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college’s nursing program is accredited by the Accreditation Commission for Education in Nursing. The college’s radiography program is accredited by the Joint Review Committee on Education in Radiologic Technology.

NTC has an enrollment of more than 7,000 students. The college’s student-to-faculty ratio is 15-to-1.

NTC offers many services to its students, including academic advising, tutoring, career services, and disability services. The college also has a library, a fitness center, and a child care center.

NTC is located in Wausau, Wisconsin, a city of about 39,000 people. The city is located in central Wisconsin, about halfway between Milwaukee and Green Bay. Wausau is a regional center for healthcare, manufacturing, and education.

NTC is committed to diversity and inclusion. The college offers a variety of programs and services to support students from all backgrounds.

NTC is a great choice for students who want to start or continue their education in a supportive and affordable environment. The college offers many programs and services to help students succeed.

Admission Requirements

Northcentral Technical College (NTC) is a public, two-year technical college located in Wausau, Wisconsin. NTC offers over 200 degree and certificate programs in a variety of technical and career fields.

Admission to NTC is open to all high school graduates and GED holders. Prospective students must submit a completed application, official high school transcripts or GED scores, and placement test scores. Placement tests are not required for students who have already completed college-level coursework in English or math.

Once admitted, students must meet with an academic advisor to develop a degree plan. NTC also offers a variety of support services to help students succeed, including tutoring, academic advising, and career counseling.
