East West College of Natural Medicine

Sarasota, FL
3808 N. Tamiami Tr, Sarasota, FL 34234-0000
941-355-9080

East West College of Natural Medicine (EWCNM) is a non-profit, accredited institution located in Sarasota, Florida, offering a Master of Science in Oriental Medicine (MSOM) and a Doctor of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (DAOM) degree. The college also offers a Bachelor of Science in Health Sciences (BSHS) degree and a certificate program in Integrative Health Studies (IHS).

The EWCNM curriculum is based on the traditional medical systems of China and Japan and includes coursework in acupuncture, Chinese herbs, Tuina (Chinese medical massage), Qi Gong, diet and nutrition, and Western medical sciences. The college also offers clinical training in both outpatient and inpatient settings.

EWCNM is accredited by the Accreditation Commission for Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (ACAOM) and is a member of the American Association of Colleges of Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (AACOM).

Admission Requirements

East West College of Natural Medicine is a private, for-profit institution specializing in the field of naturopathic medicine. The college is located in Sarasota, Florida and was founded in 1992. The college offers a Doctor of Naturopathic Medicine (ND) degree, as well as Master's degrees in Acupuncture and Oriental Medicine (MAOM) and in Ayurvedic Sciences (MAS). The ND program at East West College of Natural Medicine is four years in length and includes coursework in the basic and clinical sciences, as well as naturopathic theory and practice. The MAOM and MAS programs are each three years in length and include coursework in the respective field of study, as well as in Chinese or Sanskrit language, respectively.

Admission to East West College of Natural Medicine is competitive and based on the applicant's undergraduate GPA, letters of recommendation, and personal essay. The college does not require the completion of a specific undergraduate degree for admission into the ND program, but preference is given to applicants with a degree in the sciences. The MAOM and MAS programs each require the completion of a bachelor's degree prior to admission. In addition to the above requirements, all applicants must submit official transcripts, a resume, and a non-refundable application fee. International applicants must also submit proof of English proficiency.
