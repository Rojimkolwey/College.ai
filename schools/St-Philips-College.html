St Philip's College is a college in the city of Newcastle upon Tyne in the North East of England. The college offers a wide range of academic and vocational courses and is a centre of excellence for engineering and construction. The college has strong links with industry and the local community and provides students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen careers.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to St Philip's College, applicants must first meet the general admission requirements of the University of Texas at Austin. These requirements include:

- Being a graduate of an accredited high school

- Submitting an official high school transcript

- Completing the SAT or ACT

In addition to the general requirements, St Philip's College has specific admission requirements that must be met in order to be considered for admission. These requirements include:

- Completing an application for admission

- Submitting official high school transcripts

- Completing the SAT or ACT

- Submitting a $50 application fee

Once these requirements have been met, applicants will be reviewed for admission to the college. Admission to St Philip's College is competitive, and preference will be given to applicants who have a strong academic record and who demonstrate financial need.
