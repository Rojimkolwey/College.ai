About New Mexico

New Mexico is a state located in the southwestern United States. It is bordered by Colorado to the north, Oklahoma and Texas to the east, and Mexico to the south. With an area of 121,593 square miles, it is the fifth-largest state in the US. The state's capital is Santa Fe, and its largest city is Albuquerque.

New Mexico is home to a diverse array of cultures and peoples. The state is home to the Navajo Nation, the largest Native American tribe in the US. It also has a large Hispanic population, as well as a significant number of people of European descent.

The state's economy is based largely on agriculture, tourism, and the oil and gas industry. New Mexico is also home to a number of military bases, including the White Sands Missile Range.

New Mexico has a wide variety of landscapes, from the arid desert of the Chihuahuan Desert to the mountains of the Rocky Mountains. The state also contains a number of National Parks, including Carlsbad Caverns National Park and the Petroglyph National Monument.
