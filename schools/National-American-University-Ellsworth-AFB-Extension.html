National American University-Ellsworth AFB Extension

Ellsworth Air Force Base, SD
1000 Ellsworth Street, Suite 2400-B, Ellsworth Air Force Base, SD 57706-4800
605-718-6550

National American University-Ellsworth AFB Extension is a regionally accredited, non-profit university offering flexible schedules and affordable tuition to working adults. The university is conveniently located on Ellsworth Air Force Base, just minutes from downtown Rapid City.

NAU-Ellsworth AFB Extension offers associate, bachelor’s, and master’s degrees in business, healthcare, and information technology. The university also offers a variety of certificate programs.

Classes are taught by experienced professionals who are experts in their field. The small class sizes allow for individualized attention and interaction with classmates.

The university is committed to providing a quality education to its students. The faculty and staff are dedicated to helping students reach their academic and professional goals.

NAU-Ellsworth AFB Extension is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.

Admission Requirements

National American University-Ellsworth AFB Extension is a regionally accredited, military-friendly university that offers flexible and affordable degree programs that fit the busy lifestyles of working adults. Our admissions requirements are designed to help you get started on your educational journey.

To be eligible for admission to National American University-Ellsworth AFB Extension, you must:

-Be at least 18 years of age

-Have a high school diploma or equivalent

-Be a U.S. citizen or have a valid visa

If you meet these requirements, you can apply for admission by submitting an online application. Once your application has been received, a member of our admissions team will review your qualifications and contact you to discuss next steps.

We understand that returning to school can be a big decision, and we’re here to help you every step of the way. Contact us today to learn more about our admissions requirements and how we can help you achieve your educational goals.
