Institute of Lutheran Theology

Brookings, SD
910 4th Street, Brookings, SD 57006
605-692-9337

The Institute of Lutheran Theology is a seminary that trains men and women to become pastors and other church leaders. It was founded in 2001 by Dr. John T. Pless, who serves as its president. The Institute is located in St. Louis, Missouri, and offers a four-year program leading to a Master of Divinity degree. The Institute is accredited by the Association of Theological Schools.

The Institute of Lutheran Theology is committed to providing a high-quality theological education that is affordable and accessible to all. It offers a rigorous academic program that is grounded in the Lutheran tradition and equips its students to serve the Church in a variety of ways. The Institute also has a strong commitment to social justice, and its students are involved in a variety of service projects both in the St. Louis community and around the world.

The Institute of Lutheran Theology is an exciting and innovative seminary that is preparing men and women to be leaders in the Church in the 21st century.
