New England Tractor Trailer Training School of CT-Bridgeport

Bridgeport, CT
510 Barnum Ave, Bridgeport, CT 06608
203-368-9069

New England Tractor Trailer Training School of CT-Bridgeport (NETTTS-Bridgeport) is a premier truck driving school in Bridgeport, Connecticut. Since our founding in 1974, we have been committed to providing the highest quality education and training to our students. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for a successful career in the trucking industry.

NETTTS-Bridgeport offers both Class A and Class B CDL training, as well as a variety of specialized courses. Our Class A program is designed to prepare students for a career as a professional truck driver. Students in our Class B program will learn the skills necessary to operate smaller vehicles, such as box trucks and dump trucks. In addition to our CDL training programs, we also offer a variety of specialized courses, such as Hazmat training, Tanker training, and Doubles/Triples training.

We are proud to be one of the only schools in the country to offer a complete range of truck driving services. In addition to our training programs, we also offer a full-service truck rental and leasing company, a truck repair and maintenance facility, and a truck wash and detail center. We are dedicated to providing our students with the best possible education and training, and our complete range of services allows us to do just that.

If you are interested in a career in the trucking industry, we encourage you to contact us today to learn more about our programs. We are confident that we can provide you with the education and training you need to be successful in this exciting industry.
