Mayo Clinic College of Medicine and Science

Rochester, MN

507-284-3208

Mayo Clinic College of Medicine and Science (MCCMS) is a private medical school located in Rochester, Minnesota. It is a component of Mayo Clinic and is affiliated with the larger Mayo Clinic system. MCCMS is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

The college offers a Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree, a Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) in basic biomedical sciences, and a Master of Science in Biomedical Sciences (MSBS) degree. The MD program is a four-year program that leads to eligibility for medical licensure. The PhD program is a research-focused program that takes an average of five to six years to complete. The MSBS program is a two-year, non-thesis program that is designed for working professionals.

MCCMS has a diverse student body, with students coming from all 50 states and more than 100 countries. The college is committed to promoting diversity and inclusion in all aspects of its operations.

MCCMS offers a rigorous curriculum that is designed to prepare students for successful careers in medicine and science. The college has a strong focus on research, and students have the opportunity to participate in cutting-edge research projects. MCCMS also offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including clubs, organizations, and service opportunities.

The college is located on the Mayo Clinic campus in Rochester, Minnesota. The campus is home to a variety of state-of-the-art facilities, including a library, a museum, and a simulating center. MCCMS students have access to all of the resources and amenities of the Mayo Clinic system.
