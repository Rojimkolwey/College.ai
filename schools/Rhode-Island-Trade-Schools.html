About Rhode Island

Rhode Island, the smallest state in the US, is located in the New England region of the country. The state is known for its sandy beaches, maritime history, and vibrant nightlife. The capital city of Providence is home to a number of community colleges, as well as a thriving arts and culture scene. Rhode Island's other major cities include Newport, Warwick, and East Providence. The state is also home to a number of smaller towns and villages, each with their own unique charm. Rhode Island's economy is largely based on tourism, agriculture, and manufacturing. The state is also home to a number of major corporations, including Textron, Hasbro, and CVS Health.
