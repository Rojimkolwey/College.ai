New England Tractor Trailer Training School of Massachusetts

North Andover, MA
1600 Osgood Street #1570, North Andover, MA 01845-1048
978-965-2969

New England Tractor Trailer Training School of Massachusetts in North Andover, Massachusetts is a private, for-profit school that specializes in training students to become professional truck drivers. The school was founded in 1970 and is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC). The school offers both full-time and part-time programs, and students can choose to attend classes during the day or evening. The school also offers online courses for students who cannot attend classes in person.

The full-time program at New England Tractor Trailer Training School of Massachusetts takes four weeks to complete, and the part-time program takes eight weeks. The curriculum for both programs includes classroom instruction and behind-the-wheel training. Classroom instruction covers topics such as trucking regulations, map reading, and basic maintenance. Behind-the-wheel training gives students the opportunity to practice driving in a truck under the supervision of a licensed truck driver.

After completing the program, students are eligible to take the Massachusetts Commercial Driver's License (CDL) exam. Upon passing the exam, students will receive their CDL and will be able to work as professional truck drivers.
