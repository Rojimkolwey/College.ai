Linfield University-McMinnville Campus is a small, private university located in McMinnville, Oregon. The university was founded in 1858 and is affiliated with the Presbyterian Church (USA). The university offers a liberal arts education and has an undergraduate enrollment of approximately 1,000 students. The university is accredited by the Northwest Association of Schools and Colleges.

Linfield University-McMinnville Campus is located in the Willamette Valley, approximately 30 miles southwest of Portland. The university is situated on a 55-acre campus in the city of McMinnville. The campus includes several academic and administrative buildings, a library, a student center, and a residence hall.

Linfield University-McMinnville Campus offers a liberal arts education with a strong emphasis on the humanities and social sciences. The university offers bachelor's degrees in a variety of majors, including English, History, Political Science, Psychology, and Sociology. The university also offers a master's degree in Education.

Linfield University-McMinnville Campus has a strong commitment to service learning and offers a variety of opportunities for students to get involved in the community. The university also has a strong athletics program and competes in the NCAA Division III Northwest Conference.
