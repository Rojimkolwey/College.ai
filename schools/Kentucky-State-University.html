Kentucky State University (KSU) is a public university in Frankfort, Kentucky. It is a land-grant university and part of the Kentucky state university system. KSU was founded in 1886 as a Normal School to train black teachers. It became a state college in 1952 and a university in 1966. KSU is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. The university offers bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees in over 70 academic programs. The university's athletics teams are known as the Thorobreds and compete in the NCAA Division II.

KSU was founded in 1886 as the State Normal School for Colored Persons. The school was created in response to the 1879 state law that required separate schools for black and white students. The Normal School was the first public institution of higher education for black students in Kentucky. The school's mission was to train black teachers. The school's first class graduated in 1890. In 1952, the school became a state college and began offering bachelor's degrees. In 1966, the school became a university and began offering graduate degrees.

The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. KSU offers over 70 undergraduate and graduate programs. The university's athletics teams are known as the Thorobreds and compete in the NCAA Division II. KSU has a marching band, the Marching Thorobreds, which performs at university and community events.

Admission Requirements

Admission requirements for undergraduate students at Kentucky State University vary depending on whether the student is a freshman, transfer, or international student.

For freshman students, the minimum high school GPA required for admission is 2.0. However, the average high school GPA of students who are admitted is 3.31. The average SAT score of admitted students is 1060, and the average ACT score is 21.

Transfer students must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 in order to be considered for admission. However, the average GPA of admitted students is 3.14.

International students must have a minimum TOEFL score of 500 in order to be considered for admission.
