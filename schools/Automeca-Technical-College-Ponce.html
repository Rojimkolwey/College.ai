Automeca Technical College-Ponce

Ponce, PR
Calle Villa No.452, Ponce, PR 00731
787-840-7880

Automeca Technical College-Ponce is a technical college located in Ponce, Puerto Rico. The college offers programs in automotive technology, collision repair, and diesel technology. The college has been in operation since 1972 and has been accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC). The college has an enrollment of approximately 500 students.
