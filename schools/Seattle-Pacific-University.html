Seattle Pacific University

Seattle, WA
3307 3rd Ave W, Seattle, WA 98119-1997
206-281-2000

Founded in 1891, Seattle Pacific University is a private Christian university in Seattle, Washington. Affiliated with the Free Methodist Church, Seattle Pacific University emphasizes academic excellence, spiritual growth, and service. The university offers more than 60 undergraduate majors and programs, as well as graduate programs in business, education, counseling, nursing, and theology.

Located just minutes from downtown Seattle, Seattle Pacific University’s campus is situated on Queen Anne Hill, one of the city’s most vibrant and desirable neighborhoods. The campus is also home to a variety of unique and beautiful architecture, including the historic First Free Methodist Church and the newly constructed Welcome Center.

With a student-to-faculty ratio of just 11:1, Seattle Pacific University is committed to providing a personal and supportive learning environment. The average class size is just 20 students, ensuring that each student receives the individual attention they need to succeed.

Seattle Pacific University is accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities. The university’s nursing program is also accredited by the Commission on Collegiate Nursing Education.

In addition to academic excellence, Seattle Pacific University is also committed to spiritual growth and service. The university offers a variety of opportunities for students to get involved in service projects, both on and off campus. The Office of Spiritual Life also offers a variety of programs and resources to help students grow in their faith.

Whether you’re looking for an excellent education, a supportive community, or opportunities to grow in your faith, Seattle Pacific University has something to offer.

Admission Requirements

Seattle Pacific University is a private university located in Seattle, Washington. The university was founded in 1891 and is affiliated with the Free Methodist Church. The university has an undergraduate enrollment of 3,183 and a graduate enrollment of 1,209. The university offers bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees.

The admission requirements for undergraduate students are as follows:

1. A completed application form

2. A non-refundable application fee of $50

3. Official high school transcripts or GED scores

4. Official college transcripts (if applicable)

5. SAT or ACT scores

6. Two letters of recommendation

7. A personal essay

8. An interview (optional)

The admission requirements for graduate students are as follows:

1. A completed application form

2. A non-refundable application fee of $50

3. Official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended

4. A resume

5. Three letters of recommendation

6. A personal statement

7. An interview (optional)

8. Additional requirements for specific programs

Seattle Pacific University is committed to providing an excellent education to all students. The university has a strong academic program and offers a variety of extracurricular activities. The admission requirements are designed to ensure that only the most qualified students are admitted to the university.
