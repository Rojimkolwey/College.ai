Rowan College at Burlington County (RCBC) is a public community college in Burlington County, New Jersey. It is part of the Rowan University system. The college was founded in 1966 as Burlington County College (BCC). It became a four-year institution in 2014 and was renamed Rowan College at Burlington County.

RCBC offers over 50 academic programs leading to associate degrees and certificates. The college also offers a number of continuing education and workforce development programs.

RCBC is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The college's main campus is located in Mount Laurel, New Jersey. The college also has campuses in Pemberton and Willingboro.

RCBC is committed to providing an affordable, high-quality education to the residents of Burlington County. The college offers a variety of financial aid options to help students pay for college.

Rowan College at Burlington County is an open and inclusive community that values diversity. The college is committed to creating a safe and welcoming environment for all students, faculty, and staff.

Admission Requirements

Rowan College at Burlington County (RCBC) is a public community college in Mount Laurel Township, New Jersey. It is part of the Rowan University system. The college offers associate's degrees, certificates, and non-credit courses.

Admission to RCBC is open to all high school graduates and GED holders. The college does not have any specific admission requirements, but recommends that applicants submit a completed application, official high school transcripts, and SAT or ACT scores. Applicants who are not high school graduates or GED holders may be eligible for admission if they meet the college's assessment requirements.

Once admitted, students must complete a new student orientation and meet with a college advisor to develop a degree plan. Degree-seeking students must then register for classes and pay tuition. Non-degree-seeking students are not required to complete a degree plan or register for classes, but may do so if they wish.
