Ohio University is a large, public university located in Athens, Ohio. The university was founded in 1804, making it one of the oldest universities in the United States. The university has an enrollment of over 35,000 students, making it one of the largest universities in Ohio. The university is divided into 11 different colleges, offering a wide variety of majors and degrees. Ohio University is known for its strong academic programs, beautiful campus, and vibrant student life. The university is home to a number of Greek organizations, as well as a thriving sports scene. Ohio University is a great choice for students looking for a large, public university with a strong academic reputation.
