Monongalia County Technical Education Center

Morgantown, WV
1000 Mississippi Street, Morgantown, WV 26501-6841
304-291-9240

Monongalia County Technical Education Center is a public school located in Morgantown, West Virginia. The school offers a variety of technical and trade programs for students in grades 9-12. Programs offered include automotive technology, carpentry, cosmetology, culinary arts, electrical technology, HVAC technology, information technology, masonry, plumbing, and welding. The school also offers a variety of academic courses to prepare students for post-secondary education. Monongalia County Technical Education Center is accredited by the West Virginia Department of Education and the Council on Occupational Education.
