Alaska Bible College

Palmer, AK
248 E Elmwood Ave, Palmer, AK 99645
907-745-3201

Alaska Bible College is a private, non-profit college located in Glennallen, Alaska. It is the only accredited college in the state of Alaska. The college was founded in 1957 by a group of missionaries from the United States. The college's mission is to prepare students for Christian service in Alaska and around the world. Alaska Bible College offers Associate's and Bachelor's degrees in Bible, Theology, and Ministry. The college is accredited by the Association for Biblical Higher Education. Alaska Bible College is a member of the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities.

Admission Requirements

Alaska Bible College is a four-year accredited college located in Glennallen, Alaska. The college offers degrees in Bible and Theology, as well as a unique two-year Wilderness Leadership program. Admission requirements for Alaska Bible College include a high school diploma or equivalent, a 2.0 GPA or higher, and passing scores on the ACT or SAT. The college also requires two letters of recommendation and a personal essay. For more information on admission requirements and the application process, visit the college's website.
