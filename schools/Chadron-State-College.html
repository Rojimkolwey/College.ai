Chadron State College is a public college in Chadron, Nebraska. The college is part of the Nebraska State College System. The college offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees.

Admission to Chadron State College is open to all students who meet the minimum requirements. All students must submit an application, transcripts, and test scores. The college does not have an application fee.
