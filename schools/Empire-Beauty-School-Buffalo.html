Empire Beauty School-Buffalo

Cheektowaga, NY
2190 Walden Avenue, Cheektowaga, NY 14225-5177
800-920-4593

Empire Beauty School-Buffalo in Cheektowaga, New York offers a cosmetology education that can be completed in as little as 10 months. The school provides students with the opportunity to learn from experienced professionals in the field, and to gain the skills and knowledge necessary to pass the New York State Board exams. The curriculum at Empire Beauty School-Buffalo includes courses in hair cutting, styling, coloring, and chemical services, as well as nail care, skin care, and makeup application. The school also offers a variety of business-related courses to help students prepare for a career in the cosmetology industry. Upon graduation, students will be prepared to sit for the New York State Board exams, and will be eligible to obtain a cosmetology license.
