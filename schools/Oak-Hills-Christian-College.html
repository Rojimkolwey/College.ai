Oak Hills Christian College (OHCC) is a private, non-profit college located in Bemidji, Minnesota. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and offers bachelor's degrees in Bible and Theology, Business, Education, and Intercultural Studies, as well as a Master of Arts in Biblical Studies. OHCC is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that equips students to serve and lead in their communities and around the world. The college offers small class sizes, personal attention from faculty, and opportunities to get involved in campus life through clubs, organizations, and athletics.

Admission Requirements

Oak Hills Christian College is a private, four-year liberal arts college located in Bemidji, Minnesota. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college offers bachelor's degrees in Bible and Theology, Business Administration, Education, and Intercultural Studies, as well as a Master of Arts in Biblical Studies. The college has an enrollment of approximately 500 students.

The admissions process at Oak Hills Christian College is designed to be personal and individualized. The college seeks to enroll students who have a desire to grow spiritually and academically, and who are committed to living their lives in accordance with the teachings of the Bible. The admissions process begins with the submission of an online application, followed by an interview with a member of the admissions staff. Once the student's application is complete, the admissions committee will review the file and make a decision regarding admission.

Students who are interested in attending Oak Hills Christian College are encouraged to visit the campus and meet with a member of the admissions staff. The college also offers a variety of financial aid options to help make college affordable for all students.
