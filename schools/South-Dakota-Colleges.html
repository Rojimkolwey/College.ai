South Dakota Universities and Colleges


South Dakota is home to a wide variety of colleges and universities, offering a variety of educational opportunities for its citizens. The University of South Dakota is the state's flagship university, located in Vermillion. It is a comprehensive public research university with a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional degree programs. The University of South Dakota School of Medicine is the only medical school in the state, located in Sioux Falls. South Dakota State University is the state's largest university, located in Brookings. It is a comprehensive public research university with a wide range of undergraduate, graduate, and professional degree programs. South Dakota State University is home to the state's only College of Veterinary Medicine. South Dakota School of Mines & Technology is located in Rapid City and is a public research university with a focus on engineering, science, and technology.

In addition to these four public universities, South Dakota is also home to a number of private colleges and universities. Dakota Wesleyan University is a private liberal arts college located in Mitchell. Mount Marty College is a Catholic liberal arts college located in Yankton. The University of Sioux Falls is a private, Christian university located in Sioux Falls. Other private institutions include Oglala Lakota College, Bellevue University, and the University of Mary.

South Dakota also has a number of two-year community colleges and technical schools, including Southeast Technical Institute, Western Dakota Technical Institute, Mitchell Technical Institute, Lake Area Technical Institute, and Sinte Gleska University. These schools offer a variety of associate's degree and certificate programs.

No matter what your educational goals may be, South Dakota has something to offer. With its wide range of institutions, there is a college or university that can help you reach your goals.
