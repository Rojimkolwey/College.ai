Penrose Academy

Scottsdale, AZ
13402 North Scottsdale Road Suite B160, Scottsdale, AZ 85254-4065
480-222-9540

Penrose Academy is a private, for-profit cosmetology school located in Scottsdale, Arizona. The school offers programs in cosmetology, esthetics, and nail technology. The programs are designed to prepare students for state licensing exams and for careers in the cosmetology industry.

Penrose Academy was founded in 1985 by two cosmetologists, Rosemary and Peter Penrose. The school was originally located in Tempe, Arizona, but moved to Scottsdale in 1990. The school is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts and Sciences (NACCAS).

The cosmetology program at Penrose Academy is a 1,600-hour program that includes classroom instruction and hands-on training in hair cutting, hair coloring, hair styling, skin care, and nail care. The esthetics program is a 600-hour program that includes instruction in facials, makeup, and waxing. The nail technology program is a 300-hour program that includes instruction in manicures, pedicures, and nail art.

Penrose Academy also offers a number of advanced training programs for students who want to further their education and career in the cosmetology industry. These programs include hair extensions, hair color correction, and advanced skin care.

The school has a campus salon, which is open to the public, and a student-run salon, which is open to students, faculty, and staff. The school also offers a number of career services to help students find jobs after graduation.
