Pearl River Community College

Poplarville, MS
101 Hwy 11 N, Poplarville, MS 39470
601-403-1000

Pearl River Community College (PRCC) is a public community college in Poplarville, Mississippi. It was founded in 1909 and is part of the Mississippi Community College System. The college serves Pearl River, Stone, and George counties and offers Associate's degrees, certificates, and workforce training.

PRCC offers over 80 programs of study and is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. The college has four campuses: the Poplarville Campus, the Hancock Campus in Waveland, the Forrest County Center in Hattiesburg, and the Rankin Campus in Brandon.

PRCC is home to the Mississippi School of the Arts, a residential high school for the visual and performing arts. The college also has a strong athletics program, with teams competing in baseball, basketball, cross country, golf, rodeo, soccer, softball, tennis, and track and field.

PRCC has a long history of serving the community and providing students with the education and training they need to succeed. The college is committed to academic excellence and to meeting the needs of its students, faculty, and staff.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Pearl River Community College is open to any student who has a high school diploma or GED. There are no minimum GPA or test score requirements. However, students must complete an application and submit all required transcripts and test scores.

PRCC has an open-admission policy, meaning that any student who meets the above requirements will be admitted to the college. However, some programs may have additional requirements, such as a minimum GPA or specific course requirements.

PRCC offers a variety of programs, including associate's degrees, certificate programs, and workforce development programs. There is something for everyone at PRCC!

Whether you're looking to transfer to a four-year university or enter the workforce after graduation, Pearl River Community College can help you reach your goals. With affordable tuition and a variety of programs to choose from, PRCC is a great choice for anyone looking to further their education.
