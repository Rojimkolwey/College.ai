Mid Michigan College

Harrison, MI
1375 S Clare Ave, Harrison, MI 48625-9447
989-386-6622

Mid Michigan College is a public community college in Harrison, Michigan. The college was established in 1966 and serves over 8,000 students. Mid Michigan College offers over 70 academic programs and degrees in a variety of fields, including business, healthcare, and manufacturing. The college has campuses in Harrison, Mt. Pleasant, and Alma, Michigan. Mid Michigan College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

Admission Requirements

Mid Michigan College is a public community college in central Michigan. The college has an open admissions policy, meaning that any student who has a high school diploma or equivalent is eligible to enroll. However, there are a few programs that have additional admission requirements.

The college offers over 60 associate degree and certificate programs, as well as a number of transfer options. Associate degrees can be earned in arts, sciences, and applied sciences, while certificates are available in a variety of technical and career-oriented fields. The college also offers a number of distance learning options, including online and hybrid courses.

Mid Michigan College has a strong commitment to student success, and offers a number of support services to help students succeed in college. These services include academic advising, tutoring, and career counseling. The college also has a number of student organizations and clubs, as well as a vibrant campus life.
