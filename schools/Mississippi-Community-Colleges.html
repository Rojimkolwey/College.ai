Mississippi Community Colleges


Since its inception in 1915, Mississippi's community college system has evolved and grown to become an integral part of the state's educational landscape. With fifteen two-year colleges throughout the state, Mississippi's community colleges offer a wide variety of degree and certificate programs to meet the educational needs of all students. From career and technical education to university transfer programs, Mississippi's community colleges provide students with the opportunity to get a quality education close to home.

In addition to a variety of academic programs, Mississippi's community colleges also offer a wide range of student services, including academic advising, career counseling, financial aid, and student support. Many of the colleges also provide services such as tutoring, library resources, and job placement assistance.

The community colleges of Mississippi are also committed to providing students with access to higher education. Through the Mississippi Virtual Community College, students can take courses online, allowing them to complete their degree or certificate program from the comfort of their own home. The college's dual enrollment program also allows high school students to take college courses for both high school and college credit.

In addition to their academic and student services, Mississippi's community colleges also provide students with a variety of extracurricular activities. From student organizations and clubs to athletics and intramural sports, students can get involved in activities that help them build leadership skills and gain experience outside of the classroom.

Mississippi's community college system has become an integral part of the state's educational landscape and is committed to providing quality education and services to all students. With a wide range of degree and certificate programs, student services, and extracurricular activities, Mississippi's community colleges offer students the opportunity to get a quality education close to home.
