Chippewa Valley Technical College is a public technical college in Wisconsin. It was founded in 1912 and is part of the Wisconsin Technical College System. The college has campuses in Eau Claire, Menomonie, and River Falls, and serves an eight-county area in west-central Wisconsin.

The college offers more than 200 programs in business, health, manufacturing, agriculture, design, and other technical fields. CVTC is one of the largest technical colleges in Wisconsin, with an enrollment of more than 12,000 students. The college also offers a variety of continuing education and training programs for businesses and individuals.

CVTC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college is also accredited by a number of professional organizations, including the Accreditation Commission for Education in Nursing, the National League for Nursing, and the American Dental Association.
