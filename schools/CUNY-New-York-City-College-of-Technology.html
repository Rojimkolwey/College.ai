CUNY New York City College of Technology

Brooklyn, NY
300 Jay St, Brooklyn, NY 11201
718-260-5500

CUNY New York City College of Technology (City Tech) is the designated college of technology of The City University of New York, currently offering both baccalaureate and associate degrees in more than 50 technological fields. It is the largest public college of technology in the Northeast and one of the largest in the United States.

City Tech is an internationally recognized leader in technological education. The College was ranked the fourth best public college in the nation for undergraduate education by Washington Monthly in its September/October 2010 issue. In addition, City Tech was ranked the number one public college in the nation for adult learners by Washington Monthly in its May/June 2011 issue.

City Tech is located at 300 Jay Street in Downtown Brooklyn, occupying the entire block bounded by Jay, Tillary, Willoughby, and Johnson Streets. The College’s main campus is within walking distance of many subways and the Long Island Rail Road.

City Tech offers associate and baccalaureate degrees in more than 50 areas of study, including architecture, the humanities, the social sciences, the natural sciences, the arts, business, and health sciences. The College also offers a wide range of certificate programs.

City Tech’s student body is among the most diverse in the CUNY system, with students hailing from more than 150 countries and speaking more than 100 languages. The College’s faculty is also highly diverse, with more than 50 percent of full-time faculty members identifying as people of color.

City Tech is committed to providing an affordable, high-quality education to all of its students. The College’s tuition and fees are among the lowest of any public college in New York State. City Tech also offers a wide range of financial aid and scholarship opportunities to help make college affordable for all students.

City Tech is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The College’s School of Technology is accredited by the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology (ABET).

City Tech is a vibrant and dynamic community of more than 18,000 students and 1,500 faculty and staff members. The College is constantly evolving to meet the ever-changing needs of its students and the ever-changing demands of the workforce.

City Tech is an essential part of the economic and social fabric of New York City. The College’s graduates are employed in every sector of the city’s economy, and City Tech is a key driver of the city’s economic growth.

City Tech is a special place – a place where students from all backgrounds can come together to learn, to grow, and to succeed.

Admission Requirements

CUNY New York City College of Technology is a public college located in New York City. It is a part of the City University of New York system. The college offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees in a variety of technical and professional fields.

Admission to CUNY New York City College of Technology is open to all students who meet the minimum requirements. There is no specific GPA or test score requirement for admission. However, all applicants must submit a completed application, transcripts, and any required supplemental materials.

CUNY New York City College of Technology offers a variety of financial aid options to help students cover the cost of tuition and other expenses. Scholarships, grants, loans, and work-study programs are available. Students are encouraged to apply for financial aid as early as possible.
