Albion College

Albion, MI
611 E Porter St, Albion, MI 49224
517-629-1000

Albion College is a small, private liberal arts college located in Albion, Michigan. The college was founded in 1835 and is affiliated with the United Methodist Church. Albion College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college offers bachelor's degrees in over 30 majors and has a student-to-faculty ratio of 13:1. The average class size is 19 students. Albion College has a strong commitment to service learning and offers many opportunities for students to get involved in the community. The college also has a strong athletics program, competing in the NCAA Division III.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Albion College is based on a holistic review of each student's application. We consider all aspects of the student's record, including academic achievement, standardized test scores, involvement in extracurricular activities, community service, and leadership experience. We also take into account the student's essay, recommendations, and overall fit with the Albion community.

There is no minimum GPA or test score required for admission, but we do expect students to have demonstrated strong academic performance in a challenging college preparatory curriculum. The average GPA of our incoming first-year students is 3.6, and the average ACT score is 26.

We encourage all students to apply for admission, regardless of their financial circumstances. We are committed to meeting 100% of the demonstrated financial need of all admitted students through a combination of need-based grants, loans, and work-study.
