The University of West Alabama is a public university located in Livingston, Alabama. It was founded in 1835 as Livingston Female Academy and is the oldest state-supported institution of higher learning in Alabama. The university enrolls approximately 4,000 students from 36 states and 19 countries. The University of West Alabama offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of disciplines through its five colleges: Business, Education, Liberal Arts, Natural Sciences and Nursing, and Social Sciences. The University of West Alabama is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award degrees at the baccalaureate and master's levels.
