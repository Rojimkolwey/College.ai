Columbia International University

Columbia, SC
7435 Monticello Rd, Columbia, SC 29203-1513
800-777-2227

Founded in 1923, Columbia International University is a private, non-profit institution located in Columbia, South Carolina. The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. Columbia International University offers associate, bachelor's, master's, and doctorate degrees in a variety of disciplines, including business, education, counseling, and ministry. The university has a student-to-faculty ratio of 13:1 and an average class size of 15 students. Columbia International University is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that equips students for leadership and service in the global community. The university's core values include excellence, integrity, diversity, and community. Columbia International University's campus is situated on 100 acres of land in the historic city of Columbia. The university is home to the CIU Art Gallery, the CIU Library, and the CIU Tennis Center. The CIU Athletic Department offers a variety of intercollegiate sports, including baseball, basketball, cross country, golf, soccer, and tennis.

Admission Requirements

Columbia International University is a private, non-profit institution located in Columbia, South Carolina. The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. Columbia International University offers associate, bachelor, and master degrees, as well as certificate programs.

Admission to Columbia International University is based on the submission of a completed application, high school transcripts, and SAT or ACT scores. The university also requires a personal essay and a letter of recommendation. For international students, the university requires proof of English proficiency. Columbia International University has a rolling admissions policy and accepts applications on a rolling basis.
