Rivier University

Nashua, NH
420 South Main St, Nashua, NH 03060
603-888-1311

Rivier University is a private university located in Nashua, New Hampshire. It is a Catholic university founded in 1933 by the Sisters of the Presentation of Mary. The university offers undergraduate and graduate programs.

Rivier University's mission is to provide a transformative education that engages students in critical thinking, fosters collaborative learning, and prepares them for ethical leadership and service in a global society. The university is committed to academic excellence, personal development, and social responsibility.

Rivier University offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. Undergraduate programs include business, education, nursing, and liberal arts. Graduate programs include business, education, nursing, and counseling.

Rivier University has a strong commitment to service. The university offers a variety of service-learning opportunities, such as the Rivier Bonner Leader Program, which provides students with the opportunity to serve the community while also developing leadership skills.

Rivier University is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges. The university is also a member of the Association of Catholic Colleges and Universities.
