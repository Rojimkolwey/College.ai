Remington College-Memphis Campus is a two-year, for-profit college located in Memphis, Tennessee. The college is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC). The college offers associate's degrees and diploma programs in the medical, business, and cosmetology fields.

Remington College-Memphis Campus was founded in 1987 as a branch of Heald College. The college was acquired by Corinthian Colleges, Inc. in 2010 and renamed Remington College-Memphis Campus. The college is located in a two-story building on Poplar Avenue in Memphis.

The college offers associate's degrees and diploma programs in the medical, business, and cosmetology fields. The medical programs include medical assistant, medical billing and coding, and pharmacy technician. The business programs include business administration and accounting. The cosmetology program includes courses in hair design, skin care, and nail care.

The college has a student population of approximately 500 students. The student-to-faculty ratio is 20-to-1. The college offers small class sizes and hands-on training. The average class size is 12 students.

Remington College-Memphis Campus is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC). The college is approved by the Tennessee Higher Education Commission (THEC) and is a member of the National Association of Career Colleges and Schools (NACCAS).

Remington College-Memphis Campus has a graduation rate of 62%. The college's job placement rate is 90%. The college has an open admissions policy and does not require entrance exams for admission.
