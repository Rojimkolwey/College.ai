Wofford College

Spartanburg, SC
429 N Church St, Spartanburg, SC 29303-3663
864-597-4000

Wofford College is a small, private, liberal arts college in Spartanburg, South Carolina. Founded in 1854, Wofford is one of the oldest colleges in the state. The college is known for its strong academic programs, beautiful campus, and friendly students and faculty.

Wofford offers a traditional liberal arts curriculum with a strong focus on writing and critical thinking skills. The college also offers a number of unique programs, including a nationally recognized study abroad program, an environmental studies program, and a competitive honors program.

Wofford's campus is located in the heart of downtown Spartanburg. The college's buildings are a mix of historic and modern architecture, and the campus is home to a number of beautiful gardens and green spaces.

Wofford students are a close-knit community, and the college offers a number of opportunities for students to get involved on campus. The college has a strong athletics program, and students can also get involved in student government, clubs and organizations, and service projects.

If you are looking for a small, close-knit college community with a strong academic program, Wofford College is the place for you.
