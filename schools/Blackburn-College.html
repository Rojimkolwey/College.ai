Blackburn College

700 College Ave, Carlinville, IL 62626
217-854-3231

Blackburn College is a small, private, liberal arts college located in Carlinville, Illinois, United States. The college was founded in 1837 by the Presbyterian Church and is affiliated with the Presbyterian Church (USA). Blackburn is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college offers bachelor's degrees in business administration, education, and the liberal arts, as well as a master's degree in education.

Blackburn College is committed to providing a quality education at an affordable price. The college has a unique financing model that allows students to graduate with little or no debt. Blackburn College is also known for its innovative approach to education, which includes a focus on experiential learning.

Admission Requirements

Blackburn College is committed to providing an academically challenging, yet supportive environment that empowers our students to reach their fullest potential. We are proud to offer a unique curriculum that integrates academic and career-focused coursework, allowing our students to gain the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in college and in their future careers.

In order to be admitted to Blackburn College, students must first complete an online application. Once the application is received, students will be contacted by a member of our Admissions team to schedule an interview. During the interview, students will have the opportunity to discuss their academic goals and interests, as well as their career aspirations.

Once the interview is complete, students will be asked to submit their official high school transcript and SAT/ACT scores. Students who have not yet taken the SAT/ACT may still be considered for admission if they have strong grades and a demonstrated commitment to their academic goals.

After all required materials have been received, the Admissions Committee will review the application and make a decision. Students who are admitted to Blackburn College will be notified of their acceptance and will be asked to submit a deposit to secure their spot in the incoming class.
