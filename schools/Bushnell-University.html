Bushnell University is a private university located in Eugene, Oregon. The university was founded in 1867 and is the oldest institution of higher education in the state of Oregon. The university is named after its founder, Asa Bushnell, who was a pioneer in the state of Oregon. The university offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, as well as a law school.

Bushnell University is accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities. The university's campus is located on a 100-acre site in the city of Eugene. The campus is home to a variety of academic and administrative buildings, as well as a library, a gymnasium, and a chapel.

The university's academic programs are divided into five colleges: the College of Arts and Sciences, the College of Business, the College of Education, the College of Engineering, and the College of Law. The university also offers a variety of online programs.

Bushnell University's student body is diverse, with students from all 50 states and more than 60 countries represented. The university has a strong commitment to international education and offers a variety of study abroad programs.

Bushnell University is a comprehensive university that offers a wide range of academic programs and opportunities for students to get involved in the community. The university has a long history of academic excellence and is committed to providing a quality education to all of its students.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Bushnell University, students must first complete an application and submit all required documentation. Once the application is complete, students will be contacted for an interview. The interview is an important part of the admissions process, as it allows the admissions committee to get to know the student on a personal level.

In order to be considered for admission, students must have a strong academic record, as well as extracurricular involvement. Bushnell University looks for students who have demonstrated leadership qualities and a commitment to service. The admissions committee also takes into consideration the student's personal essay, letters of recommendation, and test scores.

Bushnell University is committed to enrolling a diverse student body, and offers a variety of scholarships and financial aid options to make college affordable for all students. For more information on admission requirements and the admissions process, please visit the Bushnell University website.
