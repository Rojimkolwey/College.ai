Bryant & Stratton College-Virginia Beach

Virginia Beach, VA
301 Centre Pointe Dr, Virginia Beach, VA 23462
757-499-7900

In 1854, Bryant & Stratton College was founded in Buffalo, New York, making it one of the oldest continually operating colleges in the United States. The college was founded by John Bryant and Henry Stratton as a business school. The college expanded its curriculum over the years to include programs in the liberal arts and sciences, and today offers associate's and bachelor's degrees as well as professional certificates. The college has campuses in New York, Ohio, Virginia, Wisconsin, and online.

Bryant & Stratton College-Virginia Beach is located in the heart of Virginia Beach, just minutes from the oceanfront. The campus offers a variety of programs, including business, accounting, criminal justice, and medical assisting. The college also offers a number of online programs.

The college is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education.
