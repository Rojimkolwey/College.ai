Weatherford College

Weatherford, TX
225 College Park Drive, Weatherford, TX 76086-5699
800-287-5471

Weatherford College is a two-year community college located in Weatherford, Texas. The college offers associate degrees and certificate programs in a variety of academic and technical fields. Weatherford College is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges.

Weatherford College was founded in 1869 as a private college. The college became a public institution in 1909 and was renamed Weatherford Junior College. The college moved to its current location in 1965. In 1986, the college was renamed Weatherford College.

Weatherford College enrolls over 4,000 students each semester. The college offers a variety of student services, including academic advising, tutoring, and career counseling. The college also operates a child care center and a food pantry.

Weatherford College is a member of the Texas Association of Community Colleges and the American Association of Community Colleges. The college is governed by a seven-member Board of Trustees. The college's president is Dr. T.J. Flores.
