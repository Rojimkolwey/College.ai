Founded in 1974, Huntsville Bible College is a non-profit, private institution of higher learning located in Huntsville, Alabama. The college is accredited by the Commission on Colleges of the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools to award Associate and Bachelor degrees. The mission of Huntsville Bible College is to prepare men and women for effective Christian service through biblically-based education.

The college offers Associate degrees in Biblical Studies, Christian Ministry, and Christian Counseling, as well as a Bachelor of Arts in Biblical Studies. The curriculum is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills necessary to effectively minister to others in a variety of settings.

Huntsville Bible College is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that is affordable and accessible to all students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans, to help make college affordable.

Huntsville Bible College is located in Huntsville, Alabama, a city with a rich history and a vibrant cultural scene. The city is home to a number of museums, art galleries, and historical sites, as well as a variety of restaurants and shops. Huntsville is also home to the Huntsville Botanical Garden, the U.S. Space & Rocket Center, and the Huntsville Museum of Art.
