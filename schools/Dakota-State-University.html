Dakota State University is a public university in Madison, South Dakota. It was founded in 1881 as the Dakota Business College, and it became a four-year institution in 1941. The university offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of fields, and it is home to the Beacom Institute of Technology. Dakota State University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.
