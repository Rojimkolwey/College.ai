Columbia Basin College (CBC) is a community college in the Columbia Basin region of Washington state. Founded in 1963, CBC provides higher education and job training in a variety of fields.

CBC offers over 80 degree and certificate programs, as well as professional development and continuing education courses. The college has an enrollment of over 6,000 students, and its campus is located in the city of Pasco.

CBC is accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities. The college is also a member of the Washington State Board for Community and Technical Colleges.

In addition to its academic programs, CBC also offers a variety of student services, including career counseling, tutoring, and financial aid. The college also has a strong commitment to community engagement, and its faculty and staff are actively involved in a variety of community initiatives.

CBC is a vital part of the Columbia Basin region, and its impact is felt throughout the community. The college is committed to providing quality education and training opportunities for its students, and to making a positive difference in the lives of the people it serves.
