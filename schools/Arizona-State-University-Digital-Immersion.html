Arizona State University Digital Immersion

Scottsdale, AZ
1475 N Scottsdale Rd, Scottsdale, AZ 85257
855-278-5080

Arizona State University's Digital Immersion program is an intensive, hands-on learning experience that gives students the opportunity to gain practical experience in the field of digital media. The program is designed to prepare students for careers in the ever-changing field of digital media, and provides them with the skills and knowledge necessary to be successful in the industry. The program is divided into four main areas of focus: design, development, production, and management. Each area of focus provides students with the opportunity to learn about the latest trends and technologies in the field, and to gain practical experience through projects and internships. The program also offers a variety of electives, allowing students to tailor their learning experience to their specific interests. Upon completion of the program, students will have a strong portfolio of work to show prospective employers, and will be prepared to enter the workforce as knowledgeable and skilled digital media professionals.

Arizona State University Digital Immersion Academy is a public charter school that offers a unique educational experience for students in grades 6-12. The school is located in the heart of downtown Phoenix, Arizona and offers a rigorous academic program that is focused on preparing students for success in college and careers. The school offers a unique blend of online and offline learning, and students have the opportunity to take advantage of the latest technology to customize their learning experience. The school has a strong commitment to providing a safe and supportive learning environment, and all students are expected to follow the school's code of conduct. Admission to the school is open to all students who meet the minimum age requirements, and there are no entrance exams or other requirements. The school does not charge tuition, and all students are eligible for financial aid.
