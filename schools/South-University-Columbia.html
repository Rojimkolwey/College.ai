South University-Columbia

Columbia, SC
9 Science Court, Columbia, SC 29203
803-799-9082

Founded in 1899, South University is a private, nonprofit institution with a long history of driving student success.

Located in Columbia, South Carolina, the university offers a range of undergraduate and graduate programs in the liberal arts and sciences, business and management, nursing and health professions, and pharmacy.

With a student-centered approach to education, South University provides a supportive environment that encourages students to reach their full potential. The university’s experienced faculty members are committed to helping students achieve their academic and professional goals.

South University’s campus in Columbia is situated in the historic Five Points district, just minutes from downtown. The university is also within driving distance of the beach and the mountains.

Admission Requirements

South University-Columbia's admissions requirements are designed to ensure that students have the academic and personal qualifications necessary to succeed in our rigorous program. All applicants must have a high school diploma or equivalent, and must submit official transcripts as well as SAT or ACT scores. In addition, all applicants must complete an interview with a member of the admissions committee.

Successful applicants will demonstrate strong academic performance, personal motivation, and a commitment to the field of healthcare. We are looking for students who are excited to learn and who are eager to make a difference in the lives of others. If you have what it takes to be a South University-Columbia student, we encourage you to apply today.
