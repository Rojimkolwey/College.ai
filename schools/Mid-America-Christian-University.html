Mid-America Christian University (MACU) is a private, non-profit, Christian university in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. The school was founded in 1953 and offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees. MACU is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The university's athletic teams compete in the NAIA and are known as the Evangels.

MACU's campus is located on nearly 100 acres in southwest Oklahoma City. The university offers a variety of on-campus housing options, including traditional residence halls, apartments, and townhomes. There are also a number of off-campus housing options available in the surrounding community.

The university offers a variety of academic programs, including business, education, liberal arts, nursing, and ministry. MACU also offers a number of online programs. The university's most popular majors include business, psychology, and education.

MACU is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that equips students for leadership and service. The university offers a number of extracurricular activities, including intramural sports, student organizations, and service opportunities. MACU also offers a number of study abroad programs.

Admission Requirements

Mid-America Christian University (MACU) is a private, Christian university in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. MACU was founded in 1953 as Central Christian College of Oklahoma. The university offers associate, bachelor's, and master's degrees in a variety of fields.

MACU is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and the Oklahoma State Regents for Higher Education. The university is also a member of the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities.

MACU has an open admissions policy, meaning that any student who meets the minimum requirements for admission will be accepted into the university.

The minimum requirements for admission to MACU are as follows:

- A high school diploma or equivalent

- A minimum 2.0 GPA

- SAT or ACT scores (if applicable)

- A completed application for admission

Students who do not meet the minimum requirements for admission may still be considered for admission on a case-by-case basis.

Once admitted, students must complete an orientation program before beginning their coursework at MACU.
