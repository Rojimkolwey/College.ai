Denmark Technical College (DTC) is located in Denmark, South Carolina and is a two-year, public, historically black college (HBCU). It is part of the South Carolina Technical College System. DTC was founded in 1947 as a trade school for African American students. It became a junior college in 1963 and began offering four-year degrees in 1974. The college is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC).
