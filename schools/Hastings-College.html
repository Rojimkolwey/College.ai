Hastings College is a private, four-year liberal arts college located in Hastings, Nebraska, United States. The college was founded in 1882 by a group of men and women determined to provide a quality education for young people in central Nebraska. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

The college's campus is located on 120 acres (49 ha) of land in the heart of Hastings. The college has an enrollment of approximately 1,100 students, most of whom are from Nebraska. The student-to-faculty ratio is 11:1 and the average class size is 20 students.

Hastings College offers more than 50 majors and minors in the liberal arts and sciences. The college also offers a number of pre-professional programs, including pre-law, pre-med, and pre-dental.

Hastings College also offers a number of scholarships and financial aid programs to help make college affordable for all students.
