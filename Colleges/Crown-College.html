Crown College is a small, private, Christian college located in St. Bonifacius, Minnesota. The college was founded in 1916 by a group of Swedish-American immigrants. The college's mission is to provide a Christ-centered education that equips students for lives of service and leadership. The college offers a variety of programs, including business, education, nursing, and liberal arts. Crown College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The college has an enrollment of approximately 1,000 students.
