Iowa Universities and Colleges


Iowa is home to a wide variety of colleges and universities, offering a variety of educational opportunities for students of all backgrounds. The state’s public universities, including the University of Iowa, Iowa State University, and the University of Northern Iowa, are among the most respected higher education institutions in the Midwest. Private institutions such as Drake University, Buena Vista University, and the University of Dubuque also offer a wide range of degree programs.

In addition to traditional four-year universities, Iowa also offers a variety of two-year and technical schools. These institutions provide students with the opportunity to gain the skills and knowledge they need to pursue a career in a variety of fields. Iowa Central Community College, Des Moines Area Community College, and Hawkeye Community College are just a few of the two-year colleges located in the state. These schools offer associate degrees, certificates, and diplomas in a variety of disciplines.

Iowa is also home to a number of specialized institutions. The University of Iowa College of Medicine is the only medical school in the state, while the University of Iowa Carver College of Medicine is one of the nation’s top-ranked medical schools. The University of Iowa College of Law is the state’s only law school, and the University of Iowa College of Dentistry is the only dental school in the state.

In addition to its traditional universities and colleges, Iowa is home to a number of online universities. These institutions offer students the opportunity to pursue a degree from the comfort of their own home. Iowa State University, the University of Iowa, and Kaplan University are just a few of the many online universities located in the state.

No matter what type of degree program a student is looking for, Iowa has an institution that can meet their needs. From its renowned public universities to its specialized institutions and online universities, Iowa provides its students with a wealth of educational opportunities.
