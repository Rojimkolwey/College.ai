Missouri Western State University

Saint Joseph, MO
4525 Downs Dr, Saint Joseph, MO 64507
816-271-4200

Missouri Western State University is located in Saint Joseph, Missouri. The school was founded in 1915 as a two-year institution and became a four-year university in 1969. Missouri Western offers over 70 undergraduate programs and 19 graduate programs. The school is divided into five colleges: the College of Arts and Sciences, the College of Business and Professional Studies, the College of Education, the College of Health Professions, and the Honors College. Missouri Western is a member of the NCAA Division II and the Mid-America Intercollegiate Athletics Association. The school's athletic teams are known as the Griffons. Missouri Western is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Missouri Western State University, applicants must submit an application, transcripts, SAT or ACT scores, and a nonrefundable application fee. Missouri Western State University accepts applications on a rolling basis, meaning that there is no deadline to apply. However, applicants are encouraged to submit their materials as early as possible to ensure that they are considered for admission.

Missouri Western State University does not have a minimum GPA or SAT/ACT score requirement for admission. However, applicants with strong academic records are more likely to be admitted than those with weaker records. Missouri Western State University takes a holistic approach to admissions, meaning that they consider all aspects of an applicant's file when making a decision. In addition to academic factors, the admissions committee also takes into account an applicant's extracurricular involvement, letters of recommendation, and personal essay.

Missouri Western State University is committed to enrolling a diverse student body and offers a variety of scholarships and financial aid programs to help make college affordable for all students. For more information on admission requirements and the application process, please visit the Missouri Western State University website.
