Admission Requirements

Admission to Quincy University is based on a holistic review of each student's application. The Admissions Committee looks at a variety of factors when making a decision for admission, including high school GPA, class rank, standardized test scores, and extracurricular involvement. In addition to the above-mentioned factors, the Admissions Committee also looks at each student's essay, letters of recommendation, and overall motivation for attending Quincy University.

All students interested in attending Quincy University must submit an application for admission, along with all required supporting materials, by the appropriate deadline. Incomplete applications will not be reviewed by the Admissions Committee.
