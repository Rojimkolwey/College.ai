The University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center (UTSW) is one of the largest medical schools in the United States. It is located in the city of Dallas, Texas. The medical school is part of the University of Texas System.

UTSW offers a variety of medical degree programs, including MD, PhD, and MD/PhD. The medical school also has a highly ranked residency program.

UTSW is home to a number of research institutes, including the Center for Alzheimer's and Neurodegenerative Diseases, the Center for Autism and Developmental Disabilities, and the Center for Molecular Imaging.

The medical school has a strong commitment to patient care and offers a variety of clinical services, including cancer care, cardiology, and transplantation.
