College of Our Lady of the Elms

Chicopee, MA
291 Springfield St, Chicopee, MA 01013
413-594-2761

The College of Our Lady of the Elms is a private, Catholic college located in Chicopee, Massachusetts. It was founded in 1947 by the Sisters of St. Joseph of Springfield. The college offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of disciplines. The campus is situated on 110 acres of land and features several buildings, including a chapel, library, and student center. The college is known for its strong academic programs, beautiful campus, and supportive community.
