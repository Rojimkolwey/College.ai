Catholic Distance University

Charles Town, WV
300 S. George Street, Charles Town, WV 25414
888-254-4238

Founded in 1985, Catholic Distance University (CDU) is an accredited, online Catholic university offering associate, bachelor’s, master’s, and certificate programs in a flexible, affordable, and convenient format. CDU’s mission is to provide quality higher education that is accessible and affordable to all, regardless of location or circumstance.

CDU’s programs are designed for working adults and busy families who want to further their education but cannot commit to a traditional on-campus program. All courses are offered online, and students can complete their degree at their own pace.

CDU is accredited by the Distance Education Accreditation Commission (DEAC), and all of its programs are approved by the Catholic Church. CDU is also a member of the Association of Catholic Colleges and Universities (ACCU).

CDU offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, including business, education, theology, and liberal arts. Its most popular programs are its business and education programs, which are both accredited by the DEAC.

CDU’s business programs prepare students for careers in management, marketing, accounting, and other business-related fields. The education programs prepare students to become teachers, administrators, and other education professionals.

CDU’s theology programs offer a comprehensive study of the Catholic faith, and its liberal arts programs provide a well-rounded education in the humanities and social sciences.

CDU’s students come from all over the world, and its faculty members are experts in their respective fields. The university offers a supportive and friendly environment, and its staff is dedicated to helping students succeed.

Whether you are looking to further your education, change careers, or simply learn more about your faith, Catholic Distance University is the perfect place for you.

Admission Requirements

Catholic Distance University (CDU) is a Catholic online university offering degree programs and courses for adult learners. CDU is accredited by the Distance Education Accrediting Commission (DEAC).

CDU’s adult learners come from all over the world and from a variety of backgrounds. Some are returning to school after many years, some are taking their first college course, and others are looking to complete a degree they started elsewhere.

There are no specific admission requirements for CDU’s degree programs. However, all students must complete a free online application and submit transcripts from all previous college coursework.

CDU’s admissions team will review each application and determine if the applicant meets the general admission requirements. Once an applicant is accepted, they will be contacted by a member of the admissions team to discuss next steps.
