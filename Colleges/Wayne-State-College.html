Wayne State College

Wayne, NE
1111 Main St, Wayne, NE 68787
402-375-7000

Wayne State College (WSC) is a public college in Wayne, Nebraska, United States. It is part of the Nebraska State College System. WSC was founded in 1910 and offers more than 50 undergraduate majors and 20 graduate programs. The college is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission.

WSC is located in the city of Wayne, Nebraska, which is about 50 miles northwest of Omaha. The college campus consists of more than 50 buildings, including the College of Education, the College of Business, and the College of Arts and Sciences.

The college offers a wide variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. Undergraduate programs include business, education, liberal arts and sciences, and nursing. Graduate programs include business administration, education, and nursing.

The college has a strong commitment to academic excellence and student success. Wayne State College is dedicated to providing a quality education at an affordable price. The college offers a variety of financial aid options to help students pay for their education.

Wayne State College is a great choice for students who are looking for a quality education at an affordable price. The college offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, and the faculty is dedicated to helping students succeed.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Wayne State College is open to all students who have graduated from an accredited high school or who have successfully completed the General Educational Development test. Admission to the college does not guarantee admission to a specific program of study.

All new students are required to complete an application for admission and submit all required transcripts and test scores. International students must also submit proof of English proficiency.

Applicants who have not yet completed high school or who have not taken the GED will be evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Admission to the college in this instance does not guarantee admission to a specific program of study.

Students who have been previously enrolled at another college or university must submit official transcripts from all institutions attended. Wayne State College uses a cumulative grade point average (GPA) of 2.0 on a 4.0 scale for transfer students.
