Messiah University

Mechanicsburg, PA
One University Avenue, Mechanicsburg, PA 17055
717-766-2511

Messiah University is a private Christian college in Mechanicsburg, Pennsylvania. The school was founded in 1909 by the Brethren in Christ Church. The university enrolls approximately 3,000 undergraduate and graduate students. The campus is situated on 500 acres of land.

The university offers over 80 undergraduate majors and programs, as well as several graduate degrees. The most popular majors at Messiah University include Business, Education, Nursing, and Psychology. The university is also home to the Center for Professional and Continuing Education, which offers non-credit courses and certificate programs.

Messiah University is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The university is a member of the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities.

Messiah University is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that equips students for lives of service and leadership in the church and the world. The university offers a variety of opportunities for students to get involved on campus, including over 100 student organizations, intramural sports, and service-learning projects.

The university has a strong commitment to global engagement, with over 60% of students participating in study abroad programs. Messiah University also offers a variety of online degree programs and courses, making it possible for students to earn a degree from anywhere in the world.
