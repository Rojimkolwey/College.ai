Pacific Islands University is a private, non-profit university located in the U.S. state of Hawaii. The university is accredited by the Accrediting Commission for Senior Colleges and Universities of the Western Association of Schools and Colleges. It offers bachelor's and master's degrees in business administration, education, and liberal arts, as well as a doctorate in education.

Founded in 2001, Pacific Islands University is committed to providing quality education to students from the Pacific region. The university has a strong focus on cultural diversity, and its student body is made up of students from over 30 different countries.

Pacific Islands University is located on the island of Oahu, in the city of Honolulu. The university's campus is situated on the beautiful Diamond Head crater, offering stunning views of the Pacific Ocean.

The university offers a variety of student services and facilities, including a library, computer lab, and a student center. Pacific Islands University also has a strong commitment to community service, and its students are involved in a variety of projects and programs that benefit the local community.

Admission Requirements

Pacific Islands University (PIU) is a private, non-profit university located in Hawaii, United States. It is accredited by the WASC Senior College and University Commission. The university offers bachelor's and master's degrees in business, education, liberal arts, and natural sciences.

PIU has a rolling admissions policy, which means that applications are reviewed and evaluated as they are received. There is no deadline to apply, but applicants are encouraged to submit their materials as early as possible.

To be considered for admission, applicants must submit the following materials:

-A completed application form

-Official transcripts from all colleges and universities previously attended

-A personal statement

-Two letters of recommendation

-A non-refundable application fee

Applicants will be notified of the admissions decision within two weeks of the receipt of all required materials.
