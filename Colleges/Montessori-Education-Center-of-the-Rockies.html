Montessori Education Center of the Rockies

Boulder, CO
4745 Walnut Street, Boulder, CO 80301
303-494-3002

The Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is a private, independent school serving children from infancy through eighth grade. Founded in 1974, the school is located on a beautiful, wooded campus in Boulder, Colorado. The school is accredited by the American Montessori Society and is a member of the Association of Colorado Independent Schools.

The Montessori philosophy is based on the belief that all children have an innate desire to learn. The Montessori method is an educational approach that emphasizes hands-on, individualized learning experiences. Children are free to move about the classroom and choose from a variety of activities that interest them. The Montessori teacher serves as a guide, helping each child to progress at his or her own pace.

The curriculum at the Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is designed to meet the individual needs of each child. The school offers a wide range of academic and enrichment classes, including art, music, Spanish, and physical education. In addition, the school provides a variety of extracurricular activities, such as sports, clubs, and service projects.

The Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is committed to providing a high-quality education to all of its students. The school’s faculty and staff are highly qualified and dedicated to their work. The school’s small class sizes allow for individualized attention, and the school’s low student-to-teacher ratio ensures that each child receives the individualized attention he or she needs.

The Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is a special place where children can learn, grow, and thrive. The school’s nurturing environment and commitment to excellence provide the perfect setting for each child to reach his or her full potential.

Admission Requirements

Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is a private, coeducational day school located in Boulder, Colorado, serving children from pre-kindergarten through eighth grade. The school is accredited by the National Association of Montessori Schools.

Admission to Montessori Education Center of the Rockies is based on a child's readiness for the Montessori program, as well as space availability. The school does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, creed, or national origin.

Children must be at least 3 years old and potty-trained to be eligible for admission to the pre-kindergarten program. For kindergarten through eighth grade, admission is based on space availability and an assessment of the child's readiness for the Montessori program.

The Montessori Education Center of the Rockies does not have a formal admissions process. Families interested in enrolling their child at the school are encouraged to schedule a tour and observation.
