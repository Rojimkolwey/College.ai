Fisher College is a small, private college located in Boston, Massachusetts. The college was founded in 1903 by Myron T. Fisher, and it specializes in business and liberal arts programs. The college has an enrollment of approximately 1,000 students, and its campus is located in the heart of Boston's financial district. The college is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges.

Fisher College offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs, including business, accounting, economics, finance, management, marketing, and liberal arts. The college also offers a number of online and distance learning programs. Fisher College is committed to providing a quality education at an affordable price, and it offers a number of scholarships and financial aid programs to help students meet their educational goals.
