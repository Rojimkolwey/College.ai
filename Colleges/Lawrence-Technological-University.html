Founded in 1932, Lawrence Technological University is a private university located in Southfield, Michigan, United States. The university offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctoral degrees in various fields of study.

Lawrence Tech was founded as the Lawrence Institute of Technology by Russell E. Lawrence. The university was established in order to provide quality education in the fields of engineering, science, and architecture.

The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. It is also a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools.

Lawrence Tech offers more than 100 undergraduate and graduate programs in various fields of study. The university is divided into six colleges: the College of Architecture and Design, the College of Arts and Sciences, the College of Business and Information Technology, the College of Engineering, the College of Education, and the College of Graduate Studies.

The university has a student population of more than 4,000. The student-to-faculty ratio is 15:1 and the average class size is 20.

The university has a strong commitment to research and offers many opportunities for students to get involved in research projects. The university has a number of research centers and institutes, including the Center for Advanced Vehicle Systems, the Center for Intelligent Manufacturing Systems, the Center for Intelligent Transportation Systems, the Center for Materials Research, the Center for Nano-scale Science and Technology, and the Clean Energy Research Center.

Lawrence Tech is home to a number of student organizations and clubs, including the American Society of Civil Engineers, the American Society of Mechanical Engineers, the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, the National Society of Black Engineers, and the Society of Women Engineers.
