Sullivan University is a private, for-profit university based in Louisville, Kentucky, United States. It was founded in 1962 by A.O. Sullivan and his son, A.W. Sullivan. The university has six campuses in Kentucky and one in Ohio, and offers associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees in a variety of disciplines.

Sullivan University is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award associate's, bachelor's, and master's degrees. The university is also a member of the Kentucky Association of Private Colleges and Universities.

The university's main campus is located in Louisville's Highlands neighborhood. The campus is spread out over several city blocks and includes several buildings, including the A.O. Sullivan Building, which houses the school's administrative offices, classrooms, and library; the E.P. True Jr. School of Business; and the Sullivan College of Technology and Design. The Louisville campus also includes the Sullivan University System Administration Building, which houses the offices of the university's president and other system administrators.

The university's Lexington campus is located in the suburb of Lexington-Fayette. The campus includes the E.P. True Jr. School of Business, the Lexington School of Nursing, and the Lexington campus library.

The university's Fort Knox campus is located on the grounds of the United States Army post of the same name. The campus offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in a variety of disciplines, including business, criminal justice, and nursing.

The university's Cincinnati campus is located in the suburb of Sharonville. The campus offers bachelor's and master's degrees in business and nursing.

The university's Bowling Green campus is located in the city of Bowling Green. The campus offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in business and nursing.

The university's Online & Continuing Education campus offers a variety of online and evening programs for students who cannot attend classes on one of the university's campuses.

Sullivan University has a total enrollment of over 7,000 students, including over 5,000 undergraduate students and over 2,000 graduate students. The university's student body is majority female, with women accounting for 60% of the total enrollment. The university's faculty is also majority female, with women accounting for 55% of the total faculty.

The university's athletics teams are known as the Sullivan Patriots. The Patriots compete in the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA) and are members of the Mid-South Conference. The university fields teams in a variety of sports, including baseball, basketball, cross country, golf, soccer, softball, tennis, and volleyball.
