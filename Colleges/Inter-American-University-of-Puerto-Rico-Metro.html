Inter American University of Puerto Rico-Metro (IAUPRM) is a private, non-profit institution of higher learning located in the metropolitan area of Puerto Rico. It is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. IAUPRM offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of fields, including business, education, engineering, and nursing. The university also has a law school and a medical school.

IAUPRM was founded in 1912 as the Inter American Normal School. The school was founded by a group of Puerto Rican educators who wanted to provide quality education to the people of Puerto Rico. The school's first class graduated in 1913. In1919, the school was renamed the Inter American University of Puerto Rico. The university has grown significantly since its founding, and now has over 10,000 students enrolled in its various programs.

IAUPRM is committed to providing a quality education to its students. The university has a strong focus on research and offers a number of research opportunities for its students. IAUPRM is also committed to serving the community, and offers a number of community service programs. The university is an active member of the Puerto Rican community and is involved in a number of civic and cultural activities.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Inter American University of Puerto Rico-Metro Campus, students must meet the following requirements:

-Be a high school graduate or have a GED

-Submit a completed application

-Pay the application fee

-Submit official high school and/or college transcripts

-Submit SAT or ACT scores (for students applying for undergraduate programs)

-Submit two letters of recommendation

-Submit a personal essay

Once all of the required materials have been submitted, the admissions committee will review the application and determine whether or not the student meets the criteria for admission. If the student is admitted, they will be notified of their acceptance and will be given further instructions on how to complete their enrollment.
