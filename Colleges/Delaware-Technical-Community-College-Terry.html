Delaware Technical Community College-Terry

Dover, DE
100 Campus Drive, Dover, DE 19901
302-857-1000

Delaware Technical Community College is a public, two-year institution of higher education that serves the residents of Delaware. The college has campuses in each of the three counties of the state, with the main campus located in Dover. The college offers associate degrees and certificate programs in a variety of academic and technical disciplines.

Delaware Tech was established in 1966 as a vocational-technical school to provide training in specific occupations. In 1971, the school became a community college and began offering academic programs leading to the associate degree. The college has grown steadily over the years and now enrolls over 12,000 students.

Delaware Tech is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The college offers a wide range of programs and services to its students. These include academic advising, career counseling, tutoring, and job placement assistance. The college also has an active athletics program, with teams competing in intercollegiate sports.

Delaware Tech is an affordable option for students seeking a quality education. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans. Delaware Tech also offers a number of flexible payment plans to make college more affordable.
