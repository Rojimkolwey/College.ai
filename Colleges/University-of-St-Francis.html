Founded in 1920, the University of St. Francis is a Catholic, Franciscan university located in Joliet, Illinois. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and offers bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees in a variety of fields. The university's mission is to provide a values-based education that prepares students for leadership and service in a global society. The university has a student-centered approach to education and offers a variety of programs and services to support students' academic, personal, and professional development. The university is committed to social justice and community engagement, and offers a variety of service-learning and volunteer opportunities for students. The university also has a strong athletics program and competes in the National Association of Intercollegiate Athletics (NAIA).
