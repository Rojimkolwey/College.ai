Illinois Institute of Technology

Chicago, IL
10 West 35th Street, Chicago, IL 60616
312-567-3000

Illinois Institute of Technology (IIT) is a private research university in Chicago, Illinois. Founded in 1890, it is the second oldest institute of technology in the United States. IIT is a highly ranked engineering school and is especially well known for its architecture, computing, and engineering programs.

IIT was founded as the Armour Institute of Technology in 1890. The school was established with a donation from Philip Danforth Armour, Sr., a Chicago businessman and philanthropist. The Armour Institute was initially focused on providing education in the fields of science and engineering.

In 1940, the school was renamed the Illinois Institute of Technology to reflect its expanded focus on technology. The school began to grow rapidly in the postwar years, adding new programs and facilities.

Today, IIT is a comprehensive university with six colleges and institutes. The university offers undergraduate and graduate degrees in a wide range of fields, including architecture, business, computing, engineering, humanities, science, and law.

IIT is a member of the Association of Independent Technological Universities. The university is also a research partner of the Argonne National Laboratory, a U.S. Department of Energy facility.

IIT is a highly selective university, with an acceptance rate of just over 30%. The university has an enrollment of about 5,800 students, of whom about one-third are international students.

IIT's campus is located on the southwest side of Chicago, in the Bronzeville neighborhood. The campus covers more than 150 acres and includes several historic buildings.

IIT is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university's engineering programs are accredited by the Accreditation Board for Engineering and Technology.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Illinois Institute of Technology is highly competitive. In order to be considered for admission, students must submit an application, transcripts, test scores, and a personal essay.

Illinois Institute of Technology is a leading research university that provides an exceptional education to students from around the world. The university is committed to excellence in teaching, research, and service, and seeks to attract and retain the best students, faculty, and staff.

IIT offers a rigorous academic program that prepares students for success in a global economy. The university offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in engineering, science, architecture, business, design, and law.

IIT is located in the heart of Chicago, one of the world’s great cities. The university is situated on a beautiful campus that is home to a diverse community of students, faculty, and staff.

IIT is an Equal Opportunity/Affirmative Action employer. The university does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, disability, or veteran status.

IIT is committed to providing a safe and secure environment for all members of the university community. The university has a comprehensive safety and security program that includes 24-hour patrols, emergency response teams, and a network of security cameras.
