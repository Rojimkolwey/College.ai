Goodwin University is a private, nonprofit institution of higher education located in East Hartford, Connecticut. The University offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs in business, education, health sciences, and liberal arts. Goodwin University is accredited by the New England Commission of Higher Education.

Founded in 1999, Goodwin University was established to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality education to students in the Greater Hartford region. The University is named after its founders, Robert and Diane Goodwin, who are committed to making higher education accessible to all.

Goodwin University is committed to providing a supportive and nurturing environment for all students. The University offers a variety of services and resources to help students succeed, including academic advising, tutoring, and career services. Goodwin University also offers a number of scholarships and financial aid programs to help make college more affordable.

Goodwin University is dedicated to preparing students for successful careers. The University offers a number of programs and services to help students develop the skills and knowledge needed to succeed in the workforce. Goodwin University also partners with a number of businesses and organizations to provide students with internship and job opportunities.

Goodwin University is committed to making a difference in the community. The University offers a number of programs and services to help meet the needs of the community. Goodwin University also partners with a number of community organizations to provide volunteer opportunities for students.

Admission Requirements

Goodwin University is a comprehensive institution that offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. The university is located in East Hartford, Connecticut and is accredited by the New England Association of Schools and Colleges (NEASC).

Undergraduate students wishing to apply to Goodwin University must submit a completed application, official high school transcripts, and SAT or ACT scores. The university does not have a minimum GPA or test score requirement, but the admissions committee does consider both when making admissions decisions. In addition to academic factors, the admissions committee also takes into account an applicant's personal statement, letters of recommendation, and extracurricular activities when making a decision.

Graduate students wishing to apply to Goodwin University must submit a completed application, official transcripts from all previous colleges and universities attended, and GRE or GMAT scores. The admissions committee will also consider an applicant's personal statement, letters of recommendation, and professional experience when making a decision.
