Georgetown College is a small, private, liberal arts college located in Georgetown, Kentucky. The college was founded in 1829 by the Kentucky Baptist Convention and is affiliated with the Southern Baptist Convention. Georgetown College is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools. The college offers Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science degrees in more than 40 majors and programs. Georgetown College is committed to providing a quality education at an affordable price. The college's average class size is 20 students, and the student-to-faculty ratio is 11-to-1. Georgetown College has a strong commitment to service and leadership. The college offers a number of service-learning opportunities, and students can get involved in leadership roles through the Student Government Association, Student Ambassadors, and other organizations.
