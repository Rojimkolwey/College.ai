Admission Requirements

Colorado Mountain College (CMC) is an accredited two-year and four-year institution with eleven locations in Western Colorado. CMC offers over 150 degree and certificate programs, as well as online and distance learning options.

To be eligible for admission to CMC, students must have a high school diploma or equivalent. GED scores are also accepted. CMC does not require SAT or ACT scores for admission. However, students who do not submit SAT or ACT scores may be asked to complete a placement test to determine course eligibility.

CMC has an open admissions policy and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, disability, or age in its programs or activities.
