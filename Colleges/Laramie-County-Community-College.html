Laramie County Community College (LCCC) is a public community college in Cheyenne, Wyoming. Founded in 1968, LCCC is the largest community college in the state of Wyoming, serving over 10,000 students each year. LCCC offers over 50 degree and certificate programs, as well as a variety of continuing education and workforce development courses.

LCCC is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and is a member of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools. The college is also accredited by the Wyoming Department of Education and the Accrediting Commission for Community and Junior Colleges.

LCCC is committed to providing accessible, affordable, and quality education to all students. The college offers a variety of financial aid options, including grants, scholarships, and loans, to help students pay for their education. LCCC also offers a variety of services to help students succeed, including tutoring, advising, and career services.

LCCC is located in Cheyenne, Wyoming, just off of Interstate 80. The college is situated on a beautiful, 300-acre campus that includes a variety of academic, athletic, and residential buildings. LCCC also has a satellite campus in Laramie, Wyoming, which offers a limited selection of courses and programs.
