Founded in 1839, Framingham State University is a public university located in Framingham, Massachusetts. The university offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of disciplines, as well as doctorates in education and nursing practice.

The university offers a variety of programs and services to help students succeed, including academic advising, tutoring, and career services.
