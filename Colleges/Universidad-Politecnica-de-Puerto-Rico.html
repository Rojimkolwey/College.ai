Hato Rey, PR

787-622-8000

Founded in 1903, the Universidad Politecnica de Puerto Rico (Polytechnic University of Puerto Rico, also known as PUPR) is a private, non-profit higher education institution located in the city of San Juan, Puerto Rico. It is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education. The university offers undergraduate and graduate programs in engineering, architecture, business and management.

As the oldest and largest engineering school in Puerto Rico, PUPR has contributed significantly to the development of the island's economy and infrastructure. Many of Puerto Rico's top business and government leaders are alumni of the university.

PUPR is committed to providing a world-class education to its students. The university has state-of-the-art facilities and equipment, and its faculty members are experts in their respective fields. PUPR graduates are highly sought-after by employers, and the university has a strong track record of placing its students in top jobs.

PUPR is an institution of higher learning dedicated to the advancement of science and technology and their application to the problems of society. The University offers a wide variety of programs at the undergraduate and graduate levels.

Admission Requirements

Universidad Politecnica de Puerto Rico (UPPR) is a public university located in San Juan, Puerto Rico. It was founded in 1966 and is part of the University of Puerto Rico system. It is the largest engineering school in Puerto Rico and offers bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees in various engineering disciplines.

To be eligible for admission to UPPR, students must have completed high school or the equivalent, with a minimum GPA of 2.0. They must also submit SAT or ACT scores, as well as two letters of recommendation and a personal essay. Admission is competitive and based on a holistic review of the applicant's file.

UPPR is committed to providing a quality education to all of its students. It offers small class sizes, experienced faculty, and state-of-the-art facilities. The university also has a strong focus on research and offers many opportunities for students to get involved.
