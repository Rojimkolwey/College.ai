Texas Universities and Colleges


Texas is home to many well-known and respected colleges and universities, with a variety of educational opportunities ranging from top-tier research institutions to small liberal arts colleges. The University of Texas System is the largest public university system in the United States and is composed of 14 institutions, including the flagship University of Texas at Austin. Other well-known public universities in the state include Texas A&M University, the University of North Texas, and the University of Houston. Private institutions in Texas include Baylor University, Southern Methodist University, and Rice University.

The state also has a number of smaller public and private colleges and universities, as well as two-year community colleges. Texas is home to the University of Texas at El Paso, the only public university in the United States located on the U.S.-Mexico border. Other public institutions include the University of Texas at San Antonio, Texas State University, and the University of Texas at Dallas. Private institutions in the state include Texas Christian University, Southwestern University, and St. Mary's University.

Texas is also home to a number of community colleges, technical schools, and vocational schools. These schools provide career and technical training, as well as associate degrees and certifications. Texas is also home to a number of specialty schools, including the Texas Culinary Academy, the Texas Academy of Mathematics and Science, and the Texas School for the Blind and Visually Impaired.

In addition to its traditional colleges and universities, Texas also has a number of online schools and programs, including Texas A&M University-Corpus Christi and the University of North Texas System. These online programs provide students with the opportunity to earn a degree or certification without having to attend classes in person.

No matter what kind of educational experience a student is looking for, they can find it in Texas. With its wide variety of colleges and universities, Texas offers something for everyone.
