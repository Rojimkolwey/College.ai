Campbell University

Buies Creek, NC
143 Main Street, Buies Creek, NC 27506
800-334-4111

Founded in 1887, Campbell University is a private, comprehensive institution located in the heart of North Carolina, offering undergraduate and graduate degrees. The university is made up of six schools and colleges: the School of Business, the School of Education, the School of Engineering, the School of Law, the School of Medicine, and the Divinity School. With an enrollment of over 6,000 students, Campbell University is committed to providing a personal, Christian education that prepares students for leadership and service. The university offers over 100 undergraduate majors, as well as graduate programs in business, education, engineering, law, medicine, and divinity. Campbell University is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges.

Admission Requirements

Admission to Campbell University is based on a holistic review of each applicant. The Admissions Committee considers all aspects of an applicant's file, including academic achievement, standardized test scores, leadership and service, and letters of recommendation.

Campbell University is committed to enrolling a diverse student body, and the Admissions Committee looks for evidence of academic and personal achievement in each applicant. The Committee also looks for evidence of a commitment to service and leadership, as well as a strong work ethic.

Campbell University has a rolling admissions policy, and applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. Applicants are encouraged to submit their applications as early as possible.
