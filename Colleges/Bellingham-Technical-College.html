Bellingham Technical College

Bellingham, WA
3028 Lindbergh Ave, Bellingham, WA 98225-1599
360-752-7000

Bellingham Technical College (BTC) is a public technical college in Bellingham, Washington. BTC offers associate degrees, professional certificates, and continuing education in over 50 programs. BTC is accredited by the Northwest Commission on Colleges and Universities (NWCCU).

BTC was founded in 1941 as a vocational school to serve the needs of the local community and economy. Since then, BTC has grown and expanded its programs and services to meet the changing needs of students and the community. BTC is committed to providing quality education and training that prepares students for successful careers and lifelong learning.

BTC offers a variety of programs that lead to associate degrees, professional certificates, and continuing education. Programs are offered in areas such as business, health care, information technology, manufacturing, and more. BTC also offers a variety of services to support students, including academic advising, career services, tutoring, and more.

BTC is located in Bellingham, Washington, a city of about 80,000 people located in the northwest corner of the state. Bellingham is a beautiful city with a vibrant downtown, a thriving arts and culture scene, and a variety of outdoor activities. BTC is just a short drive from Seattle, Vancouver, and the Canadian border.

Admission Requirements

Bellingham Technical College (BTC) is a public two-year technical college in Bellingham, Washington. BTC offers over 50 programs in a variety of fields, including business, healthcare, trades, and technology.

Admission to BTC is open to all high school graduates and GED holders. Applicants must submit an online application, as well as transcripts and test scores. BTC does not have an application fee.

BTC offers a variety of financial aid options, including grants, loans, and scholarships. Students must complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) in order to be considered for financial aid.

BTC has a rolling admissions policy, meaning that applications are reviewed and accepted on a rolling basis throughout the year. Applicants are encouraged to apply early.
