Augsburg University

Minneapolis, MN
2211 Riverside Ave, Minneapolis, MN 55454
612-330-1000

Founded in 1869, Augsburg University is a private, coeducational institution located in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Augsburg University is affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America and offers more than 50 undergraduate and graduate programs. The university is committed to providing an education that is both rigorous and relevant to the real world.

Augsburg University has a long history of serving students from a variety of backgrounds. The university was founded by Norwegian immigrants who had a strong commitment to social justice. Augsburg University has always been committed to providing an education that is accessible and affordable.

Augsburg University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university has been recognized for its commitment to academic excellence. Augsburg University is ranked as one of the top universities in the Midwest by U.S. News & World Report.

Augsburg University is a vibrant community of more than 3,000 students from more than 50 countries. The university is committed to providing a supportive and inclusive environment for all students. Augsburg University is dedicated to preparing students for lives of leadership and service.

Admission Requirements

Augsburg University is a private, coeducational university located in Minneapolis, Minnesota, USA. The school was founded in 1869 by German immigrants as Augsburg Seminary and was one of the first institutions of higher learning in the state of Minnesota. The university is affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America and is open to students of all faiths. The school's mission is to "provide an education that equips students for lives of leadership and service in a global society."

Augsburg University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The school offers bachelor's degrees in over 50 majors, as well as master's degrees in business, education, nursing, and theology. The school also has a Doctor of Nursing Practice program.

To be eligible for admission to Augsburg University, students must have a high school diploma or equivalent. The university does not have a minimum GPA requirement, but does consider grades and class rank when making admissions decisions. Students must also submit SAT or ACT scores. The university offers both need-based and merit-based scholarships, as well as grants and loans.
