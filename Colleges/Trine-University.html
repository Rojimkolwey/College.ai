Trine University is a private university in Angola, Indiana. It was founded in 1884 as Tri-State Normal College, and later became Trine University. The university offers bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees in a variety of fields.

Trine University was founded in 1884 as Tri-State Normal College. The school was created in order to provide teachers for the growing number of schools in the tri-state area of Indiana, Ohio, and Michigan. The college was originally located in Angola, Indiana, and was later moved to its current location in Fort Wayne, Indiana. The school became Trine University in 2008.

The university offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. Undergraduate programs are offered in fields such as business, engineering, education, and health sciences. Graduate programs are offered in fields such as business, engineering, and education.
