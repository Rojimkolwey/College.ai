Arizona Christian University

Glendale, AZ
1 W. Firestorm Way, Glendale, AZ 85306-3216
602-489-5300

Arizona Christian University is a private, non-profit, Christian university located in Phoenix, Arizona. The university was founded in 1960 by a group of pastors and laymen from the First Baptist Church of Phoenix. The university's stated mission is "to prepare its students to serve Christ in the Church and in the world by providing an excellent education in the liberal arts and sciences grounded in a biblical worldview."

The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and offers bachelor's degrees in over 40 majors, as well as master's degrees in business, education, and counseling. The university has an enrollment of over 1,400 students, with a student-to-faculty ratio of 13:1.

The university is committed to providing its students with an excellent education grounded in a biblical worldview. In addition to academic rigor, the university offers opportunities for students to grow spiritually and to serve the community. The university offers a variety of on-campus and online programs, as well as a robust athletics program.
