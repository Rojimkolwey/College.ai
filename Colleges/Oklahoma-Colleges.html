Oklahoma Universities and Colleges


Oklahoma is home to a wide variety of colleges and universities, ranging from public universities to private liberal arts colleges. The University of Oklahoma (OU) is the state’s flagship university and is known for its excellent programs in engineering, business, and the sciences. The University of Tulsa (UT) is another well-known institution of higher learning in Oklahoma and offers a wide range of academic programs. Oklahoma State University (OSU) is the state’s land-grant university and is well-known for its agricultural and veterinary sciences programs.

In addition to these larger universities, Oklahoma is also home to several smaller private liberal arts colleges. Oklahoma Baptist University is a Christian college located in Shawnee and offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. Oklahoma City University is another private liberal arts college in the state and offers a range of academic programs. Oral Roberts University is a private Christian university in Tulsa and is renowned for its programs in the health sciences and business.

Oklahoma is also home to several community colleges and technical schools. Tulsa Community College is the largest community college in the state and offers a wide range of academic and technical programs. Oklahoma City Community College is another popular school in the state and offers a variety of academic and vocational programs. There are also several tribal colleges in the state, including Bacone College and the College of the Muscogee Nation.

Oklahoma is a great place to pursue a college education, with a wide range of schools and programs to choose from. Whether you’re looking for a large public university or a small private liberal arts college, Oklahoma has something for everyone.
