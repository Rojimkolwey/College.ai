Augustana University is a small, private university located in Sioux Falls, South Dakota. The university was founded in 1860 by Swedish immigrants and is affiliated with the Evangelical Lutheran Church in America. Augustana offers a liberal arts education and has been ranked as one of the top regional universities in the Midwest by U.S. News & World Report. The university enrolls approximately 2,000 students and has a student-to-faculty ratio of 13:1.
