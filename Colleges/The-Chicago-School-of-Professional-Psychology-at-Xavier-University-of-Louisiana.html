The Chicago School of Professional Psychology at Xavier University of Louisiana is a private, for-profit, doctoral-level university located in New Orleans, Louisiana. The school is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges (SACSCOC) to award degrees at the doctoral level. The school offers programs in clinical psychology, counseling psychology, school psychology, and organizational psychology. The school also offers a joint doctoral program in clinical psychology and counseling psychology.

The school was founded in 1974 as the Institute for Psychological Studies. The school was renamed The Chicago School of Professional Psychology at Xavier University of Louisiana in 2009. The school is a member of the National Association of Schools of Psychology (NASP). The school is also a member of the American Psychological Association (APA).

The school's clinical psychology program is accredited by the Commission on Accreditation of the American Psychological Association (APA). The school's counseling psychology program is accredited by the American Psychological Association (APA). The school's organizational psychology program is accredited by the Society for Industrial and Organizational Psychology (SIOP).

The school's clinical psychology program is designed to train students to become licensed psychologists. The program includes coursework in psychopathology, assessment, diagnosis, and treatment. The program also includes practicum and internship experiences.

The school's counseling psychology program is designed to train students to become licensed counselors. The program includes coursework in human development, counseling theories and techniques, research methods, and professional ethics. The program also includes practicum and internship experiences.

The school's organizational psychology program is designed to train students to become licensed psychologists who specialize in organizational psychology.
