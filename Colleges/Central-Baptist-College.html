Central Baptist College

Conway, AR
1501 College Avenue, Conway, AR 72034
501-329-6872

Central Baptist College is a private, four-year liberal arts college located in Conway, Arkansas, United States. The college is affiliated with the Arkansas Baptist State Convention. Central Baptist College was founded in 1952 as Central Baptist Bible Institute by the Arkansas Baptist State Convention. The college moved to its current location in Conway in 1955. In 2007, the college began offering its first graduate program, a Master of Arts in Christian Studies. In 2010, the college's name was changed to Central Baptist College.

Central Baptist College offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate programs. Undergraduate programs include Bible, business, communication arts, education, music, and ministry. Graduate programs include the Master of Arts in Christian Studies and the Master of Business Administration.

Central Baptist College is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and the Arkansas Department of Education. The college is a member of the Association of Christian Colleges and Universities and the Council for Christian Colleges and Universities.

Central Baptist College is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that prepares students for leadership and service. The college offers a variety of opportunities for students to get involved in campus life, including athletics, clubs and organizations, and service projects. Central Baptist College is dedicated to equipping students to make a difference in the world for Christ.

Admission Requirements

Central Baptist College is a private, four-year college located in Conway, Arkansas. The college is affiliated with the Arkansas Baptist State Convention. Central Baptist College offers Bachelor of Arts and Bachelor of Science degrees in a variety of majors.

Admission to Central Baptist College is open to all students who meet the minimum requirements for admission. The minimum requirements for admission are a 2.0 GPA and a 21 on the ACT. Central Baptist College does not have an application fee.

Once students have been admitted to Central Baptist College, they will need to complete a New Student Orientation program. This program is designed to help students transition to college life and to help them become familiar with the college's policies and procedures.
