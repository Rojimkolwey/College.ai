Admission Requirements

Southeast Missouri Hospital College of Nursing and Health Sciences is a two-year institution that offers an Associate of Science in Nursing degree. The college is located in Cape Girardeau, Missouri.

Admission to the college is competitive, and preference is given to applicants who have completed prerequisite coursework at a regionally accredited institution with a cumulative GPA of 2.5 or higher.

Prospective students must also submit an application, a non-refundable $50 application fee, official transcripts, and a personal essay.

The college offers a traditional nursing program as well as an accelerated nursing program for students who already have a bachelor's degree in another field. The accelerated program can be completed in as little as 16 months.

Both programs prepare students to take the NCLEX-RN exam and become licensed registered nurses. Upon graduation, students are eligible to apply for positions in a variety of healthcare settings, including hospitals, clinics, long-term care facilities, and home health agencies.
