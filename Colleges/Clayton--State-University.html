Clayton State University

Morrow, GA
2000 Clayton State Boulevard, Morrow, GA 30260-0285
678-466-4000

Clayton State University, located in Morrow, Georgia, is a public institution that was founded in 1969. The school has a student body of over 6,000 students, and offers bachelor's and master's degrees in a variety of disciplines. Clayton State is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges. The university's campus is located just south of Atlanta, and its proximity to the city provides students with access to a variety of internship and job opportunities. Clayton State also has a strong commitment to community engagement, and offers its students a number of service-learning and volunteer opportunities.
