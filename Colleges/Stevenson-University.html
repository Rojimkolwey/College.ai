Stevenson University

Stevenson, MD
1525 Greenspring Valley Rd, Stevenson, MD 21153-0641
410-486-7000

Stevenson University is a private, coeducational university located in Stevenson, Maryland. The university has four schools: the School of the Arts, the School of Business and Leadership, the School of Education, and the School of Graduate and Professional Studies. The university offers more than 50 undergraduate majors and programs, as well as 20 graduate programs and degrees.

Founded in 1947, Stevenson University is committed to providing students with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in their chosen field. The university offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including clubs and organizations, sports, and Greek life. Stevenson University is also home to a number of unique facilities, including the Inscape Art Gallery, the Caves Sports and Recreation Complex, and the Owings Mills Campus Observatory.

Located just outside of Baltimore, Maryland, Stevenson University is situated on a picturesque campus that spans more than 100 acres. The university is accredited by the Middle States Commission on Higher Education and is a member of the Association of Independent Colleges and Universities of Maryland.

Admission Requirements

Stevenson University is a private, independent, coeducational university located in the Greenspring Valley area of Baltimore County, Maryland. The university has four campuses: two in Stevenson and two in Owings Mills. The university offers bachelor's and master's degrees in business, education, the liberal arts, and the sciences.

The admission requirements for Stevenson University vary depending on the program to which you are applying. For most programs, you will need to submit an application, transcripts, and test scores. Some programs may also require additional materials, such as a portfolio or essay.

The admissions process at Stevenson University is designed to be personal and individualized. The admissions counselors are here to help you every step of the way and will work with you to create a custom plan that fits your needs.
