Admission Requirements

Arizona State University's Campus Immersion program is a unique opportunity for students to get a taste of college life while still in high school. The program is open to all high school juniors and seniors, and allows them to take up to two college-level courses per semester. There is no application fee, and students can apply online.

To be eligible for the program, students must have a minimum GPA of 2.5 and must take a placement exam to determine which courses they are eligible to take. The placement exam is offered at no cost to the student, and can be taken at any of the three ASU campuses.

Once students have been accepted into the program, they will be able to register for classes and pay tuition just like any other college student. Financial aid is available for those who qualify, and students can also take advantage of on-campus housing and dining options.
