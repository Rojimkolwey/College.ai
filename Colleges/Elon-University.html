Elon University

Elon, NC
100 Campus Drive, Elon, NC 27244-2010
336-278-2000

Elon University is a private university in North Carolina that was founded in 1889. The university has an enrollment of about 6,000 students. The university is known for its strong academic programs, particularly in the fields of business and law. The university's business school is ranked among the top 50 business schools in the country. The university's law school is also highly ranked, and has produced many successful lawyers and judges. The university is located in the city of Elon, which is about 30 miles from Raleigh, the state capital. The university is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools.
