North Dakota Universities and Colleges


North Dakota has a wide variety of colleges and universities, offering a range of educational opportunities for students of all ages. From two-year community colleges to four-year universities, North Dakota has a school to fit any student’s needs.

The University of North Dakota (UND) in Grand Forks is the state’s flagship university. It is the largest university in North Dakota and offers a wide range of degrees, from bachelor’s to doctorate, in fields such as business, engineering, nursing, and the sciences. UND also has a strong athletic program and is home to the Fighting Hawks, the school’s NCAA Division I team.

North Dakota State University (NDSU) in Fargo is the state’s second-largest university. It offers a variety of bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degrees in fields such as business, engineering, and the sciences. In addition, NDSU is home to the Bison, the school’s NCAA Division I team.

The North Dakota University System (NDUS) is a system of 11 public colleges and universities throughout the state, including the two universities mentioned above. NDUS schools offer a variety of two-year and four-year degrees in fields such as business, engineering, nursing, and the sciences.

In addition to the schools within the NDUS, there are also several independent colleges and universities in North Dakota, such as Bismarck State College, Dickinson State University, and Mayville State University. These schools offer a variety of undergraduate and graduate degrees, as well as certificate and diploma programs.

North Dakota also has several private colleges and universities, such as the University of Mary and the University of Jamestown. These schools offer a variety of bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degrees in fields such as business, engineering, nursing, and the sciences.

No matter what kind of degree you’re looking for, North Dakota has a school to fit your needs. From two-year community colleges to four-year universities, North Dakota has a wide variety of colleges and universities offering a range of educational opportunities.
