American Public University System

Charles Town, WV
111 W Congress St, Charles Town, WV 25414
877-468-6268

American Public University System is a leading provider of online higher education. It offers more than 200 programs and degrees across a broad range of disciplines. American Public University System is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. American Public University System is a wholly owned subsidiary of American Public Education, Inc. (APEI).

American Public University System was founded in 1991 by Dr. Wallace E. Boston as American Military University (AMU). AMU was established to provide quality higher education to the men and women of the United States Armed Forces.

In 2002, APUS was established as a separate, non-profit institution, and the name was changed to American Public University System.

Today, APUS serves a global community of more than 100,000 students. The university offers associate, bachelor’s, master’s, and doctorate degrees, as well as certificates, in a wide variety of subject areas.

American Public University System is committed to providing affordable, accessible, and high-quality online education. The university offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans, to help students meet their educational goals.

American Public University System is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. The university is also a member of the Distance Education Accreditation Commission (DEAC) and the Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to American Public University System, applicants must submit a completed application form, along with all required supporting documentation. This includes official transcripts, proof of English proficiency (if applicable), and a non-refundable application fee.

Once the admissions office has received all required materials, the application will be reviewed for completeness and minimum eligibility requirements. If the applicant meets these requirements, their file will be forwarded to the appropriate academic department for review.

The academic department will make a determination on whether or not the applicant meets the departmental requirements for admission. If they do, the applicant will be offered admission to the university. If not, the applicant will be denied admission.

American Public University System has rolling admissions, meaning that there is no set deadline for applying. However, it is recommended that applicants submit their materials as early as possible to ensure that they are considered for admission.
