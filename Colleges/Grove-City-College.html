Grove City College

Grove City, PA
100 Campus Dr, Grove City, PA 16127-2104
724-458-2000

Founded in 1876, Grove City College is a private, Christian institution of higher education located in Grove City, Pennsylvania. The college offers a liberal arts education with over 130 undergraduate programs in the arts, humanities, sciences, and social sciences. The college is committed to providing a Christ-centered education that equips students for faithful and fruitful service to God and neighbor. The college offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including athletics, clubs, and organizations, and students can also choose to study abroad. The college has a strong commitment to service and equips students to be leaders in their communities and in the world.

Admission Requirements

In order to be admitted to Grove City College, students must submit an application along with high school transcripts, SAT or ACT scores, and a non-refundable $35 application fee. International students must also submit proof of English proficiency. The admissions committee reviews applications on a rolling basis and typically makes decisions within two weeks. Grove City College does not consider race, color, national origin, sex, or disability in its admissions process.
