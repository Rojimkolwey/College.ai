Founded in 1877, Regis University is a private, Catholic university in Denver, Colorado. The university is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission. Regis offers undergraduate degrees in liberal arts, business, and nursing, as well as graduate degrees in business, education, nursing, and health professions. The university is comprised of five colleges: the College of Contemporary Liberal Studies, the Rueckert-Hartman College for Health Professions, the College of Computer & Information Sciences, the College of Business & Economics, and the College of Nursing. The university's campus is located in the historic Northwest Denver neighborhood, and the university's main campus is situated on 80 acres of land. The university also has a satellite campus in Loveland, Colorado.

Admission Requirements

Regis University is a private, Catholic institution of higher learning located in Denver, Colorado. The university was founded in 1877 by the Society of Jesus (the Jesuits) and today is one of 28 Jesuit colleges and universities in the United States. Regis University is accredited by the Higher Learning Commission and offers undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate degrees.

Undergraduate Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission to Regis University, applicants must:

Be a graduate of an accredited high school

Submit an official high school transcript

Submit official ACT or SAT scores

Complete the Regis University application for admission

Pay the non-refundable $50 application fee

Graduate Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission to a graduate program at Regis University, applicants must:

Have a bachelor's degree from an accredited institution

Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended

Complete the Regis University application for admission

Pay the non-refundable $50 application fee

Doctoral Admission Requirements

To be eligible for admission to a doctoral program at Regis University, applicants must:

Have a master's degree from an accredited institution

Submit official transcripts from all colleges and universities attended

Complete the Regis University application for admission

Pay the non-refundable $50 application fee

International Admission Requirements

In addition to the above requirements, applicants who are not citizens or permanent residents of the United States must also:

Submit a completed Certificate of Finances form

Submit official TOEFL or IELTS scores (if English is not the applicant's native language)

Submit a copy of their passport

Regis University welcomes applications from all qualified students and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, national origin, sex, disability, or age in its programs and activities. The university is committed to providing an inclusive and welcoming environment for all members of the community.
