Auburn University at Montgomery (AUM) is a public university in Montgomery, Alabama. It is part of the Auburn University system. AUM was established in 1967 as a two-year upper-level institution and began offering bachelor's degrees in 1973. The university now offers a variety of undergraduate and graduate degrees.

AUM is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award bachelor's, master's, and doctoral degrees. The university is organized into five colleges: the College of Business, the College of Education, the College of Liberal Arts, the College of Nursing and Health Sciences, and the College of Science and Mathematics.

AUM has an enrollment of over 5,000 students, representing 43 states and 43 countries. The university offers a variety of student organizations and clubs, as well as intramural sports. Auburn University at Montgomery is a member of the NCAA Division II and competes in the Gulf South Conference.

The university's campus is located on a 1,000-acre (4.0 km2) site in east Montgomery. The campus includes academic buildings, residence halls, a library, a student center, a stadium, and an arena.

Admission Requirements

Auburn University at Montgomery is a public university in Montgomery, Alabama. The school was founded in 1967 as Auburn Montgomery College, a two-year branch of Auburn University. In 2003, the school became a four-year institution and was renamed Auburn University at Montgomery. The school is part of the Auburn University System.

Auburn University at Montgomery is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools Commission on Colleges to award baccalaureate and master's degrees.

The school has an open admissions policy, meaning that any student who has graduated from high school or earned a GED is eligible to enroll. However, the school does have minimum GPA and SAT/ACT score requirements for admission into certain programs.

Auburn University at Montgomery offers over 90 undergraduate and graduate degree programs in a variety of fields, including business, education, nursing, and more. The school also has a number of research centers and institutes, as well as an Honors College for high-achieving students.
