Jefferson County Dubois Area Vocational Technical Practical Nursing Program

Reynoldsville, PA
576 Vo-Tech Road, Reynoldsville, PA 15851
814-653-8420

The Jefferson County Dubois Area Vocational Technical Practical Nursing Program in Reynoldsville Pennsylvania is a two year program that provides students with the opportunity to earn their Practical Nursing Diploma. The program is designed to prepare students for a career in nursing, and provides them with the skills and knowledge necessary to provide quality care to patients. The program is accredited by the Pennsylvania State Board of Nursing, and is approved by the National League for Nursing. The program is offered at the Reynoldsville campus of the Jefferson County Dubois Area Vocational Technical School.
