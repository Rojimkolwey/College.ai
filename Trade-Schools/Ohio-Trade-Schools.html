About Ohio

Ohio is a state located in the Midwestern United States. Ohio is the 34th largest state by area and the 10th most populous state. The state's capital and largest city is Columbus. Ohio is bordered by Lake Erie to the north, Pennsylvania to the east, West Virginia to the southeast, Kentucky to the southwest, Indiana to the west, and Michigan to the northwest. Ohio is historically known as the "Buckeye State" after its Ohio buckeye trees, and Ohioans are also known as "Buckeyes".

The state has a diverse economy with an emphasis on manufacturing, agriculture, mining, and healthcare. Ohio is home to numerous community colleges, including the Ohio State University, the University of Cincinnati, and Case Western Reserve University. Ohio is also home to many professional sports teams, including the Cleveland Browns, the Cincinnati Bengals, and the Columbus Blue Jackets.
