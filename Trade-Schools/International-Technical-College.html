International Technical College

San Juan, PR
104 Loaiza Cordero St, San Juan, PR 00918-3325
787-767-8389

The International Technical College (ITC) in San Juan, PR is a two-year technical college that offers associate degrees and technical certificates in a variety of fields. ITC is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC) and is a member of the Puerto Rico Association of Private Colleges and Universities (PRAPCU). ITC was founded in 1972 and has an enrollment of over 1,000 students. ITC offers programs in Automotive Technology, Business Administration, Computer Science, Cosmetology, Culinary Arts, Electrical Technology, HVAC/R Technology, Industrial Maintenance Technology, and Welding Technology. ITC also offers a variety of non-technical programs such as English as a Second Language (ESL), GED Preparation, and Adult Basic Education (ABE). ITC is located in the heart of the financial and business district of San Juan and is easily accessible by public transportation. ITC is committed to providing quality education and training that leads to employment and career advancement. ITC has a strong commitment to the community and provides a variety of services to businesses and individuals. ITC is an affordable option for those seeking a quality education and has a variety of financial aid options available.
