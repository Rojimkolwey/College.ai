Old Town Barber College-Wichita

Wichita, KS
1211 E Douglas, Wichita, KS 67211-1605
316-264-4891

Old Town Barber College has been teaching the art of barbering in Wichita, Kansas since 1924. The college is located in the historic Old Town district of downtown Wichita. The college offers a two-year program that leads to a diploma in barbering. The curriculum includes courses in haircutting, shaving, hair design, color, chemistry, anatomy, bacteriology, and shop management. The college also offers a one-year program that leads to a certificate in barbering. The curriculum for the one-year program is the same as the two-year program, but is condensed into one year.

Old Town Barber College is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts and Sciences. The college is a member of the National Association of Barber Boards of America and the Kansas State Board of Cosmetology.

The college has a long history of producing successful graduates who go on to have successful careers in the barbering industry. Many of the graduates of Old Town Barber College have gone on to open their own barber shops, and some have even gone on to become instructors at the college.

Old Town Barber College is committed to providing its students with the best possible education so that they can be successful in their chosen career. The college has a staff of experienced and knowledgeable instructors who are dedicated to helping their students succeed. The college also has a well-equipped facility that includes a spacious classroom, a large barber shop, and a student lounge.

If you are interested in a career in barbering, Old Town Barber College is the place to start. The college offers a comprehensive education that will prepare you for a successful career in the barbering industry.
