Central Career Institute LLC

South Plainfield, NJ
126 Corporate Blvd, South Plainfield, NJ 07080-2408
908-412-8600

Central Career Institute LLC in South Plainfield New Jersey is a private, for-profit career training institute that offers programs in a variety of medical and business-related fields. The school is accredited by the Accrediting Council for Independent Colleges and Schools. Central Career Institute LLC offers programs in medical billing and coding, medical office administration, business administration, and accounting. The school also offers a variety of online courses. Central Career Institute LLC is a small school with a student-to-faculty ratio of 15-to-1. The average class size is 10 students.
