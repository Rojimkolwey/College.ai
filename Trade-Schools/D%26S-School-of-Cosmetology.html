Waterford, MI
4180 Elizabeth Lake Road, Waterford, MI 48328
248-738-3860

D&S School of Cosmetology in Waterford Michigan is a top-notch cosmetology school that offers a wide range of services to its students. The school has been in business for over 20 years and has a well-established reputation in the cosmetology industry. The school offers a variety of courses that cover everything from hair styling and cutting to makeup application and nail care. The school also offers a wide range of services to its students, including a full-service salon, a spa, and a retail store. The school is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Cosmetology Arts and Sciences and is a member of the Michigan Cosmetology Association. The school is also a member of the Better Business Bureau.
