The Salon Professional Academy-Georgetown

Georgetown, TX
901 South IH 35, Georgetown, TX 78626-5470
512-868-8079

The Salon Professional Academy-Georgetown in Georgetown, Texas, is a beauty school that offers a wide range of services and programs to its students. The school offers cosmetology, esthetics, and nail care programs, as well as a variety of other courses. The Salon Professional Academy-Georgetown also offers a wide range of services to its clients, including hair, nails, and makeup services. The school is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Cosmetology Arts and Sciences.
