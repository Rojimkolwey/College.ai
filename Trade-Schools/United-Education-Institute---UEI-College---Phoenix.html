United Education Institute - UEI College - Phoenix

Phoenix, AZ

United Education Institute - UEI College - Phoenix in Phoenix, Arizona is a two-year, for-profit college that offers associate's degree and diploma programs in business, criminal justice, and healthcare. The college is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC).

UEI College - Phoenix opened its doors in 2007 in response to the growing demand for quality post-secondary education in the Phoenix metropolitan area. The college provides a supportive and affordable education to a diverse student population.

The college offers a variety of programs that prepare students for successful careers in industries that are in high demand in the Phoenix area. The business program offers associate's degrees in business administration and accounting, while the criminal justice program offers degrees in law enforcement and corrections. The healthcare program offers degrees in medical assisting and dental assisting.

UEI College - Phoenix is committed to providing its students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their chosen careers. The college's faculty members are experienced professionals who are dedicated to helping students reach their full potential.

The college offers a variety of services to its students, including career services, academic advising, and tutoring. The college also has a robust student life program that offers a variety of activities and events.

UEI College - Phoenix is an affordable option for students who are looking for a quality education that will prepare them for a successful career. The college's tuition is significantly lower than the tuition at many other colleges and universities in the area.

UEI College - Phoenix is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC). The college's accreditation ensures that its programs meet the highest standards of quality and integrity.
