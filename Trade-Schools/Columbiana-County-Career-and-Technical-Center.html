Columbiana County Career and Technical Center

Lisbon, OH
9364 St Rt 45, Lisbon, OH 44432
330-424-9561

The Columbiana County Career and Technical Center is a public career and technical education center located in Lisbon, Ohio. The center serves the students of Columbiana County and offers programs in a variety of career and technical fields. The programs offered at the center are designed to prepare students for careers in the workforce or for further education at the post-secondary level. The center also offers adult education and training programs for those who wish to improve their skills or learn new ones. The Columbiana County Career and Technical Center is committed to providing quality career and technical education to its students and to the community.
