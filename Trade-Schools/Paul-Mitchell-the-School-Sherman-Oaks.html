Paul Mitchell the School-Sherman Oaks

Sherman Oaks, CA
15301 Ventura Blvd Unit P2, Sherman Oaks, CA 91403
800-761-8006

Paul Mitchell the School-Sherman Oaks is located in Sherman Oaks, California. The school offers a wide range of programs to choose from, including cosmetology, esthetics, and barbering. The school also offers a variety of services to the community, such as haircuts, hair color, and nail services. The school is accredited by the National Accrediting Commission of Career Arts and Sciences. The school has a staff of over 100 instructors and a student-to-teacher ratio of 10-to-1. The average class size is 20 students. The school offers a variety of financial aid options, including scholarships, grants, and loans. The school also offers a job placement program.
