Orion Institute

Perrysburg, OH
340 Three Meadows Drive, Perrysburg, OH 43551
419-874-4496

Orion Institute is a private, for-profit institution located in Perrysburg, Ohio. It was founded in 2006 by Dr. Richard Orion, who also serves as its president. The school offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in business, healthcare, and information technology. It is accredited by the Distance Education Accrediting Commission (DEAC). As of 2016, Orion Institute had an enrollment of approximately 500 students.

Orion Institute was founded with the goal of providing quality, affordable education to working adults. The school offers flexible scheduling and accelerated degree programs that can be completed in as little as 18 months. Orion Institute is committed to providing students with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in their chosen career field.

The business program at Orion Institute offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in business administration. The healthcare program offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in medical coding, medical billing, and healthcare management. The information technology program offers associate's and bachelor's degrees in computer science and information systems.

Orion Institute is accredited by the Distance Education Accrediting Commission (DEAC). The DEAC is a recognized accrediting body by the US Department of Education.
