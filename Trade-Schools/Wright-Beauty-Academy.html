Wright Beauty Academy

Battle Creek, MI
492 Capital Ave SW, Battle Creek, MI 49015
269-964-4016

Wright Beauty Academy is a beauty school located in Battle Creek, Michigan. The school offers a variety of programs in cosmetology, esthetics, and nail technology. The programs are designed to prepare students for careers in the beauty industry. The school offers a hands-on approach to learning, with a focus on practical experience. Students have the opportunity to learn from experienced instructors and to use the latest equipment and techniques. The school also offers a variety of services to the community, including hair, skin, and nail care.
