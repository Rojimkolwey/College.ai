Randolph Technical Center

Elkins, WV
200 Kennedy Drive, Elkins, WV 26241-9527
304-636-9195

Randolph Technical Center is a public career and technical education center located in Elkins, West Virginia. It is part of the Randolph County School District and serves students in grades 11 and 12. The center offers programs in a variety of career and technical areas, including automotive technology, construction technology, cosmetology, culinary arts, health sciences, and information technology.

The center’s mission is to provide students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in their chosen career or technical field. The center’s faculty and staff are committed to helping students reach their full potential and providing them with the resources they need to be successful.

The center’s programs are designed to prepare students for careers in high-demand, high-skill industries. The programs are rigorous and provide students with the opportunity to learn the latest skills and techniques in their chosen field. The center’s programs are also flexible, allowing students to complete their program of study in a way that fits their schedule and needs.

The center’s faculty and staff are experienced professionals who are dedicated to helping students succeed. The faculty and staff work closely with students to ensure that they are prepared for their chosen career or technical field. The center also has a strong network of industry partners who provide students with internships, job shadowing, and other opportunities to gain real-world experience.

The Randolph Technical Center is accredited by the Council on Occupational Education. The center’s programs are also approved by the West Virginia State Board of Education.

The Randolph Technical Center is an important resource for the community. The center provides students with the skills and knowledge they need to be successful in their chosen career or technical field. The center also provides the community with a skilled workforce that is prepared to meet the needs of local businesses and industries.
