Francis Tuttle Technology Center

Oklahoma City, OK
12777 N Rockwell Ave, Oklahoma City, OK 73142-2789
405-717-7799

The Francis Tuttle Technology Center is one of the largest and most comprehensive technology centers in the state of Oklahoma. It offers programs in a wide variety of technical and career fields, including computer science, engineering, and health care. The technology center also provides adult education and continuing education courses, and is home to a variety of research and development facilities.

The Francis Tuttle Technology Center was established in 1959 as the Oklahoma County Vocational-Technical School. It was renamed in honor of its founder, Francis Tuttle, in 1967. The technology center is located on a campus of more than 200 acres in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma. It is accredited by the Commission of the Council on Occupational Education.

The Francis Tuttle Technology Center offers more than 100 programs of study in a variety of technical and career fields. Programs are offered at the certificate, diploma, and associate degree levels. The technology center also provides adult education and continuing education courses.

The Francis Tuttle Technology Center is home to a variety of research and development facilities, including the Oklahoma Manufacturing Alliance, the Oklahoma Statewide Virtual Library, and the Oklahoma Center for the Advancement of Science and Technology. The technology center also houses the Francis Tuttle Job Corps Center, which provides training and education to low-income young adults.
