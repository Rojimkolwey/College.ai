Ruben's Five Star Academy

Lake Wales, FL
104 State Road 60 West, Lake Wales, FL 33853-4409
863-241-0287

Ruben's Five Star Academy is a private school located in Lake Wales, Florida. The school has a student population of approximately 100 students in grades K-12. The school is accredited by the Southern Association of Colleges and Schools (SACS) and is a member of the National Association of Private Schools (NAPS). The school's curriculum is college preparatory and the school offers a variety of extracurricular activities, including sports, clubs, and community service opportunities. The school's campus is located on a beautiful, 100-acre lakefront property and the school is known for its small class sizes and individualized attention to each student.
