GoodFellas Barber College

Little Rock, AR
4306 Asher Avenue, Little Rock, AR 72204
501-707-7983

Since its founding in 2001, GoodFellas Barber College in Little Rock, Arkansas has been producing some of the best barbers in the country. The college offers a comprehensive curriculum that covers all aspects of the barbering trade, from haircutting and styling to shaving and beard care. GoodFellas graduates are in high demand, and many go on to open their own successful barber shops. The college is also known for its close-knit community, and many students form lifelong friendships while attending GoodFellas.
